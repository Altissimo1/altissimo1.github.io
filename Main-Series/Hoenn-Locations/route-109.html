Route 109

Access to:
North: Slateport City
West: Route 108

Utility:
Surf is required to travel to Route 108 in the west and access a Heart Scale, a PP Up, Tuber Gwen, Swimmer♀ Alice, Swimmer♂ David, Young Couple Mel & Paul, and Fisherman Carter. In Ruby and Sapphire, it is required to access Tuber Carmen. In Emerald, it is required to access Tuber Austina and Bird Keeper Elijah. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Access to:

West: Route 108
South (via soaring in the sky): Mirage Forest

Utility:

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

  • Wild Tentacool have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Wingull have a 50% chance of holding a Pretty Wing.
  • Wild Pelipper have a 50% chance of holding a Pretty Wing and a 5% chance of holding a Lucky Egg.
