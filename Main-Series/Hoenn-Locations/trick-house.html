Trick House

Access to:
Exit: Route 110

Utility:
Various HMs are required to complete the puzzles here; see below for more details.

Access to:
Any exit: Route 110

Utility:
Various HMs are required to complete the puzzles here; see below for more details.

The Trick House consists of three rooms: the entrance room, a maze room, and the end room.

In the entrance room, the Trick Master is hiding somewhere. The player must locate him for him to open the way to the maze room.

In the maze room, the player must navigate a maze with items and Trainers to find the entrance to the end room. There are actually eight different maze rooms, with each successive maze room becoming accessible as the player gains additional Gym Badges or defeats the Elite Four. HMs are required to complete most of the maze rooms.

In the maze room, the player must navigate a maze with items and Trainers to find the entrance to the end room. There are actually six different maze rooms. HMs are required to complete most of the maze rooms, meaning the player cannot complete all the maze rooms immediately; they will need to collect HMs and their corresponding Gym Badges to be able to navigate the rooms.

In the end room, the Trick Master will give the player a reward for completing the maze, and then allow the player to leave the Trick House.

Once a maze is completed, it cannot be re-accessed. This means that any items not picked up or any Trainers not battled become inaccessible once the player finishes the maze. The player can leave the maze before finishing it with no issues, though.

Details on each puzzle are given below. First are the items that can be obtained in the entrance and end rooms.
