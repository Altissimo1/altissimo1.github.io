S.S. Tidal

Access to:
Departs from: Slateport City and Lilycove City
Travels to: Battle Tower (Ruby and Sapphire), Battle Frontier (Emerald), Southern Island, Navel Rock (Emerald), Birth Island (Emerald), and Faraway Island (Emerald)

Places of interest:
The second cabin from the left near the entrance on 1F, Cabin 2, contains a bed for healing the player's Pokémon. Sleeping in the bed will cause the ship to arrive.
