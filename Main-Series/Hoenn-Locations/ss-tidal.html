S.S. Tidal

Access to:
Departs from: Slateport City and Lilycove City
Travels to: Battle Tower (Ruby and Sapphire), Battle Frontier (Emerald), Southern Island, Navel Rock (Emerald), Birth Island (Emerald), and Faraway Island (Emerald)

Places of interest:
One cabin contains a bed for healing the player's Pokémon. Sleeping in the bed will cause the ship to arrive.
