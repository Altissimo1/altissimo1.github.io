Dewford Town

Access to:
North: Route 106
East: Route 107

Places of interest:
Dewford Hall: Speaking to the boy outside the Hall will change the trendy phrase that people in the Dewford Hall discuss, which also changes Feebas' location on Route 119.
Dewford Gym: The second Gym. It specializes in Fighting-types.

Utility:
Surf is required to travel to Route 106 or 107. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Access to:
North: Route 106
East: Route 107
West (via soaring in the sky): Mirage Island

Pokémon

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

  • Wild Tentacool have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Wingull have a 50% chance of holding a Pretty Wing.
  • Wild Pelipper have a 50% chance of holding a Pretty Wing and a 5% chance of holding a Lucky Egg.
