Dewford Town

Access to:
North: Route 106
East: Route 107

Places of interest:
Dewford Hall: Speaking to the boy outside the Hall will change the trendy phrase that people in the Dewford Hall discuss, which also changes Feebas' location on Route 119.
Dewford Gym: The second Gym. It specializes in Fighting-types.

Utility:
Surf is required to travel to Route 106 or 107. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Access to:
North: Route 106
East: Route 107
West (via soaring in the sky): Mirage Island
The portal in the sky where the player battles Dialga, Palkia, and Giratina is south of Dewford Town.
