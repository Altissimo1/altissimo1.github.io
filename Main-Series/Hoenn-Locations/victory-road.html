Victory Road

Access to:
Any exit: Ever Grande City

Utility:
Strength and Rock Smash are required to traverse the area and access a Full Restore on B1F.
Surf is required to traverse the area and access an Elixir. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Flash is recommended.

Access to:
Any exit: Ever Grande City

Utility:
Rock Smash is required to traverse the area.
Strength is required to traverse the area and access TM35 Flamethrower on B1F.
Surf is required to traverse the area and access a Full Heal, a Max Repel, an Elixir, an Ultra Ball, a Max Elixir. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Surf and Strength are required to access a PP Up on B1F and TM29 Psychic in Ever Grande City.
Surf and Waterfall are required to access TM81 X-Scissor on 2F.
Flash is recommended.

Pokémon

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

  • Wild Aron and Lairon have a 5% chance of holding a Hard Stone.
  • Wild Hariyama have a 5% chance of holding a King's Rock.
  • Wild Mawile have a 5% chance of holding an Iron Ball.
  • Wild Sableye have a 5% chance of holding a Wide Lens.
  • Wild Tentacool and Tentacruel have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Luvdisc have a 50% chance of holding an Heart Scale.
  • Wild Goldeen have a 5% chance of holding a Mystic Water.
