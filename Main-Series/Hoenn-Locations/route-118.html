Route 118

Access to:
North: Route 119
West: Mauville City
East: Route 123
Potentially accessible on the route: Terra Cave (Emerald only)

Utility:
Surf is required to cross the route and access the eastern half. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
