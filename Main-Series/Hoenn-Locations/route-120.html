Route 120

Access to:
West: Fortree City
East: Route 121
Accessible on the route: Scorched Slab
Accessible on the route: Ancient Tomb

Utility:
Cut is required to access a Rare Candy and six Aspear Berries.
Surf is required to access the Scorched Slab, as well as a Rare Candy and a Full Heal. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
The Devon Scope is required to battle five Kecleon and access the Scorched Slab and a Nest Ball.

Access to:

Utility:
Cut is required to access a Rare Candy, four Aspear Berries, four Wepear Berries, and four Pinap Berries.

Pokémon

  • Wild Kecleon have a 5% chance of holding a Persim Berry.
  • Wild Linoone have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Sitrus Berry.
  • Wild Zigzagoon have a 5% chance of holding an Oran Berry.
  • Wild Poochyena and Mightyena have a 5% chance of holding a Pecha Berry.

Walking

Swarm

During a swarm, the player has a 50% chance of encountering the swarming Pokémon, and a 50% chance that their encounter is taken from the normal encounter table, effectively halving all normal encounter rates. Swarms can be shared between players by mixing records.

To activate a swarm, the player must first defeat the Elite Four. Every battle the player engages in after that has approximately a 0.5% chance to start a swarm. The player must learn about the swarm on the TV the following day to activate it, and it will last until the end of the next day.

Potentially due to a bug, only one swarm can ever be generated on a single save file without record mixing.

Pokémon

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

  • Wild Linoone have a 50% chance of holding a Potion and a 5% chance of holding a Max Revive.
  • Wild Oddish and Gloom have a 5% chance of holding an Absorb Bulb.
  • Wild Absol have a 5% chance of holding a Life Orb.
  • Wild Surskit have a 5% chance of holding Honey.
  • Wild Masquerain have a 5% chance of holding Silver Powder.
  • Wild Tentacool have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Goldeen have a 5% chance of holding a Mystic Water.
