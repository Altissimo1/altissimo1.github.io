Southern Island

Access to:
Accessible from the area (via the S.S. Tidal): Lilycove City

Access to:
The first time the player visits, they arrive from Route 118.
Accessible from the area (via the S.S. Tidal): Slateport City

Accessing this area requires the Eon Ticket, which was only available via an e-Reader card distributed in 2003, or by mixing records with a copy of Ruby, Sapphire, or Emerald that has obtained the Eon Ticket.

Accessing this area after the initial visit with Steven requires the Eon Ticket, which was only available via download or StreetPass between 2014 and 2015, or by communicating with a player who has it via StreetPass.
