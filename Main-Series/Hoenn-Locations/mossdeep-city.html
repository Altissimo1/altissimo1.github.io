Mossdeep City

Access to:
North: Route 125
West: Route 124
South: Route 127

Places of interest:
Poké Mart
The boy in the house west of the Pokémon Center will tell the player where their Secret Base is.
In Ruby and Sapphire, there is a house with a door on the back wall. Trainers can be battled here if the player has scanned e-Reader cards.
Mossdeep Space Center
Mossdeep Gym: The seventh Gym. It specializes in Psychic-types and Double Battles.
Mossdeep Game Corner: In Emerald, a Game Corner located in the city allows players to play minigames with other owners of FireRed, LeafGreen, and Emerald, using the Wireless Adapter.
Move Tutor: In Emerald, a Black Belt near the western shore can teach one Pokémon Dynamicpunch.

Utility:
Surf is required to access Mossdeep City the first time, as well as to travel to Route 124, 125, or 127. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Access to:
North: Route 125
West: Route 124
South: Route 127

Places of interest:
Poké Mart
There is a house with a door on the back wall. If the player scans QR codes corresponding to special Secret Bases, they can access those Bases through this door. Scanning Shigeru Ohmori's Base's code and speaking to him will reward the player with a Regice Doll.
Mossdeep Space Center
Mossdeep Gym: The seventh Gym. It specializes in Psychic-types and Double Battles.

Utility:
Surf is required to access Mossdeep City the first time, as well as to travel to Route 124, 125, or 127. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
The Devon Scope is required to battle Kecleon in the Space Center.
