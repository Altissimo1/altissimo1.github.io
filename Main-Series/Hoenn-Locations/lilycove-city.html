Lilycove City

Access to:
West: Route 121
East: Route 124
Accessible from the city: Team Magma/Aqua Hideout
Accessible from the city (via the S.S. Tidal): Slateport City, Battle Tower (Ruby and Sapphire), Battle Frontier (Emerald), Southern Island, Navel Rock (Emerald), Birth Island (Emerald), and Faraway Island (Emerald)

Places of interest:
Lilycove Department Store
Contest Hall: In Ruby and Sapphire, this Contest Hall hosts Master Contests. In Emerald, this Contest Hall hosts all Contests. For a list of Contest opponents, check out this page.
Cove Lily Motel: After defeating the Elite Four, Game Freak employees can be found on the second floor. One will give the player a diploma if they have 200 Hoenn Pokédex Pokémon - all of them excluding Jirachi and Deoxys - caught in the Pokédex, and another if they have most of the National Pokédex Pokémon caught in the Pokédex. In Ruby and Sapphire, the Pokémon not needed for the National Pokédex diploma are Mew, Chikorita (due to a bug), Celebi, Jirachi, and Deoxys, and in Emerald, they are Mew, Ho-Oh, Lugia, Celebi, Jirachi, and Deoxys.
Lilycove Museum: If the player wins Master Contests with at least 800 points, an artist here will complete a painting of the Pokémon that won the contest. If the player is able to get paintings for all five categories, the player can receive a Glass Ornament.
Harbor: After the player defeats the Elite Four, the S.S. Tidal in the port can be used to travel to Slateport City, the Battle Tower or Frontier, and the event-exclusive islands Southern Island, Navel Rock, Birth Island, and Faraway Island.
Pokémon Trainer Fan Club
Move Deleter
Pokémon Center Ladies: A lady will appear in the Pokémon Center in Emerald, depending on the player's ID number. She can be replaced with another of the ladies if the player mixes records with another copy of Emerald.
Move Tutor: In Emerald, a tutor on the Lilycove Department Store roof can teach one Pokémon Substitute.

Utility:
Surf is required to travel to Route 124 and the Team Magma/Aqua Hideout. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
