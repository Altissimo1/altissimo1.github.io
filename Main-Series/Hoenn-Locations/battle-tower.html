Battle Tower (Ruby and Sapphire)

This page is specifically about the Battle Tower as it appears in Ruby and Sapphire. For information about the Emerald Battle Tower, see this page.

Access to:
Accessible from the area (via the S.S. Tidal): Slateport City, Lilycove City

The Battle Tower is a Battle facility where the goal is to defeat Trainers without losing. Winning a certain number of battles in the row can give the player rewards, which are described below.

The following restrictions and rules apply to battles conducted in the Battle Tower:

  • The player will not receive money, and their Pokémon will not receive experience. Instead, the player will receive a prize after each round of 7 battles completed. The list of items that can be received is shown below.
  • The player cannot enter two Pokémon of the same species, or holding the same held item. Opposing Trainers will never use two Pokémon of the same species, or holding the same held item.
  • The player's Pokémon will be automatically healed after every battle.
  • Items used or swapped (via Trick, Thief, Knock Off, or Covet) during the battle will be given back to the Pokémon after the battle.
  • Battles are conducted in rounds of 7.
  • Set Mode is enforced.
  • In Level 50 mode, Pokémon higher than level 50 cannot be entered.
  • The moves Return and Frustration, when used by the opponent, will always have their maximum power.
  • Losing a battle will reset the player's streak to 0. Turning off the game mid-battle, or mid-round without saving, will count as a loss and reset the streak.
  • Pokémon encountered in battles will not be added to the Pokédex.
  • All opposing Trainers will use Pokémon that can be found in the Hoenn Pokédex. The player, however, can use any Pokémon in the National Pokédex, except for those shown in the table below.
  • All battles are Single Battles.
  • Enemy Pokémon will always take their Nature, Item, moves, and EVs from predetermined 'sets'.
  • Enemy Pokémon that can have two Abilities will always have a random Ability. Enemy Pokémon that can be male or female will have their gender randomly determined.
  • Soul Dew will have no effect if entered.
  • Some Pokémon cannot be entered altogether, and will never be encountered on an opponent's team:
Mewtwo Mew Lugia Ho-Oh Celebi Kyogre
Groudon Rayquaza Jirachi Deoxys Eggs

Reward Items

The following items can be obtained as a reward for completing 7 battles in a row, so long as the player's total streak is 35 wins or fewer. A random item will be selected.

Protein Iron Calcium
Carbos Zinc HP Up

The following items can be obtained as a reward when the player's total streak is 42 wins or higher.

Leftovers White Herb Quick Claw
Mental Herb Brightpowder Choice Band
King's Rock Focus Band Scope Lens
