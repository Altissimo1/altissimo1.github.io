Pokémon

Gift

All Fossils can be revived on the second floor of Devon Corporation. However, Dome, Armor, and Plume Fossils can only be found naturally in Omega Ruby, while Helix, Skull, and Cover Fossils can only be found naturally in Alpha Sapphire. Old Amber can be found in both games. A Root and Claw Fossil can be found in both games, but only one may be chosen. Jaw and Sail Fossils cannot be found in either game, and must be transferred from X or Y.
