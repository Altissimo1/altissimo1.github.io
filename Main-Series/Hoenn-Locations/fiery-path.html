Pokémon

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

  • Wild Koffing have a 5% chance of holding a Smoke Ball.
  • Wild Grimer have a 5% chance of holding Black Sludge.
  • Wild Torkoal have a 5% chance of holding Charcoal.
  • Wild Machop have a 5% chance of holding a Focus Band.
  • Wild Roggenrola have a 50% chance of holding an Everstone and a 5% chance of holding a Hard Stone.
  • Wild Diglett have a 5% chance of holding Soft Sand.
