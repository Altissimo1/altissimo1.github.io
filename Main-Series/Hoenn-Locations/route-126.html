Route 126

Access to:
North: Route 124
East: Route 127
Accessible on the route: Sootopolis City

Utility:
Surf is required to access and traverse this route. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Dive is required to access all items on this route, Swimmer♀ Nikki (Ruby/Sapphire) or Brenda (Emerald), and Sootopolis City. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Dive.

Access to:
North: Route 124
East: Route 127
Accessible on the route: Sootopolis City
The Secret Islet is accessible in the south with the use of Dive, containing one Secret Spot.

Utility:
Surf is required to access and traverse this route. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Dive is required to access all items on this route, Swimmer♀ Nikki, Scuba Diver Yutaka, Scuba Diver Tristan, and Free Diver Jillian, Sootopolis City, and the Secret Islet. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Dive.

Pokémon

  • Wild Clamperl have a 5% chance of holding a Blue Shard.
  • Wild Chinchou have a 5% chance of holding a Yellow Shard.
  • Wild Relicanth have a 5% chance of holding a Green Shard.

Pokémon

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

  • Wild Tentacool and Tentacruel have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Pelipper have a 50% chance of holding a Pretty Wing and a 5% chance of holding a Lucky Egg.
  • Wild Chinchou, Lanturn, and Relicanth have a 5% chance of holding a Deep Sea Scale.
  • Wild Clamperl have a 50% chance of holding a Pearl and a 5% chance of holding a Big Pearl.
