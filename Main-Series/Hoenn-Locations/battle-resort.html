Battle Resort

Access to:
Accessible from the area (via the S.S. Tidal): Slateport City and Lilycove City

Places of interest:
The Battle Maison can be found here.
Day Care
Stats judge
A Parasol Lady south of the Day Care can change the background music in the outer area of the Battle Resort.
A Triathlete on a bike southeast of the Day Care must be spoken to in order to get both Bikes from Rydel's Cycles.
Move Tutors: The people west of the Pokémon Center can teach Pokémon moves. The list is given below.

Utility:
Surf can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Pokémon

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

  • Wild Tentacool and Tentacruel have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Pelipper have a 50% chance of holding a Pretty Wing and a 5% chance of holding a Lucky Egg.
