Route 111

Access to:
West (northern half): Route 113
West (southern half): Route 112
South: Mauville City
Accessible in the desert: Desert Ruins
Accessible in the desert, one time only: Mirage Tower (Emerald only)
Accessible on the route: Trainer Hill (Emerald only)

Places of interest:
The lady in the rest house in the northern section will heal the player's Pokémon when spoken to.

Utility:
Rock Smash is required to access the northern section, the desert, and most of the southern section.
The Mach Bike is required to access Black Belt Daisuke in the northern section.
Surf is required to access an HP Up in the southern section.
The Go-Goggles are required to access and traverse the entire desert area.
