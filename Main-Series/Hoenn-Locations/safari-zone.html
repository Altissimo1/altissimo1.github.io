Safari Zone

Access to:
Exit: Route 121

Utility:
The Mach Bike is required to access Area 3.
The Acro Bike is required to access Area 4.
Surf is required to access a Max Revive in Area 2 and TM22 Solarbeam in Area 3. In Emerald, Surf is also required to access a Big Pearl in Area 5. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Rock Smash can be used to encounter wild Pokémon in Areas 4 and 6.

Access to:
Exit: Route 121

Utility:
The Acro Bike is required to access the long grass patch in Area 3 and the grass patch in Area 4, as well as an Absolite and Rare Candy in Area 3 and a Calcium in Area 4.
The Mach Bike is required to access the long grass patch in Area 2 and the grass patch in Area 3, as well as a Protein in Area 2 and a Nugget and Big Pearl in Area 3.
The Acro Bike and Surf are required to access the long grass patch in Area 1, as well as a Calcium in Area 1 and a Max Revive in Area 2.
The Mach Bike and Surf are required to access the long grass patch in Area 4, as well as an Ultra Ball and TM22 Solar Beam in Area 4.
The Acro Bike, Mach Bike, and Surf are required to access TM53 Energy Ball in Area 1 and TM93 Wild Charge in Area 4.
Surf can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Pokémon

  • Wild Girafarig have a 5% chance of holding a Persim Berry.
  • Wild Doduo and Dodrio have a 5% chance of holding a Sharp Beak.
  • Wild Pikachu have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Light Ball.
  • Wild Miltank always hold Moomoo Milk.
  • Wild Geodude have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
  • Wild Shuckle always hold an Oran Berry.

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

  • Wild Gloom have a 5% chance of holding an Absorb Bulb.
  • Wild Pikachu have a 5% chance of holding a Light Ball.
