Abandoned Ship

In Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire, this area is replaced with Sea Mauville.

Access to:
Exit: Route 108

Utility:
Surf is required to access and navigate the area.
Dive is required to access several rooms and items, including the Room 1, 2, 4, and 6 keys, TM18 Rain Dance, a Water Stone, a Luxury Ball, and the Scanner.
