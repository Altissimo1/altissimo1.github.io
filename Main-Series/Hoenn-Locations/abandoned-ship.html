Abandoned Ship

In Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire, this area is replaced with Sea Mauville.

Access to:
Exit: Route 108

Utility:
Surf is required to access and navigate the area. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Dive is required to access several rooms and the Room 1, 2, 4, and 6 keys, TM18 Rain Dance, a Water Stone, a Luxury Ball, and the Scanner.
