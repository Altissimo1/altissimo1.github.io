Fortree City

Access to:
West: Route 119
East: Route 120

Places of interest:
Poké Mart
Secret Base decoration shop
Fortree Gym: The sixth Gym. It specializes in Flying-types.
Move Tutor: In Emerald, the old man in the house northwest of the Gym can teach one Pokémon Sleep Talk.

Utility:
The Devon Scope is required to clear Kecleon, which in turn is required to access the Gym.
