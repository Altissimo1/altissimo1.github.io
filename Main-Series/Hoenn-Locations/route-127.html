Route 127

Access to:
North: Mossdeep City
West: Route 126
South: Route 128
Potentially accessible on the route: Marine Cave (Emerald only)

Utility:
Surf is required to access and traverse this route. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Dive is required to access a Carbos, a Star Piece, a Heart Scale, an HP Up, and a Red Shard. In Emerald, Dive is also required to access Marine Cave.
