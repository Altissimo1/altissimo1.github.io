Jagged Pass

Access to:
Northern exit: Mt. Chimney
Southern exit: Route 112
Accessible on the route: Team Magma Hideout (only in Emerald)

Utility:
The Acro Bike is required to traverse the area from bottom to top.

Access to:
Northern exit: Mt. Chimney
Southern exit: Route 112

Utility:
The Acro Bike is required to access TM43 Flame Charge, a Super Repel, and an X Defense.
The Acro Bike or Mach Bike is required to access TM69 Rock Polish.

Pokémon

  • Wild Numel always hold a Rawst Berry.

  • Wild Machop have a 5% chance of holding a Focus Band.
