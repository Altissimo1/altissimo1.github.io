Route 103

Access to:
North: Altering Cave (only in Emerald)
South: Oldale Town
East: Route 110

Utility:
Cut is required to access a Guard Spec., Cheri Berries, and Leppa Berries on the east side of the route. In Emerald, it is required to access Black Belt Rhett and Guitarist Marcos.
Surf is required to cross from the west side to the east side and vice versa. In Emerald, it is required to access Swimmer♀ Isabelle and Swimmer♂ Pete. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Pokémon

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

  • Wild Zigzagoon have a 50% chance of holding a Potion and a 5% chance of holding a Revive.
  • Wild Wingull have a 50% chance of holding a Pretty Wing.
  • Wild Tentacool have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Pelipper have a 50% chance of holding a Pretty Wing and a 5% chance of holding a Lucky Egg.
  • Wild Chatot have a 5% chance of holding a Metronome.
