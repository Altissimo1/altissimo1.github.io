Utility:

Access to:
Exit: Route 134

Utility:
Surf and Dive are required to access this area.
Dig is required to open the second room.

Using Dig in front of the Braille in the first room is required to open the second room.

In the second room, the player must put a Relicanth in their first party slot and a Wailord in their last slot and read the Braille to open the Island Cave, Desert Ruins, and Ancient Tomb. In Emerald, Relicanth must instead be in the last slot and Wailord must instead be in the first.

Pokémon

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

  • Wild Tentacool have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Horsea have a 5% chance of holding a Dragon Scale.
