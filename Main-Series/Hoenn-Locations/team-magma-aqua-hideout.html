Team Magma/Aqua Hideout

This page is about the location in Lilycove City. For the Team Magma Hideout located in Jagged Pass in Emerald, see this page.

This location is called Team Magma Hideout in Ruby and Omega Ruby, and Team Aqua Hideout in Sapphire, Emerald, and Alpha Sapphire. Both locations are similar enough that I think they can be combined.

In Ruby and Sapphire only, this location closes after being cleared, meaning any items not picked up are made permanently inaccessible.

Access to:
Exit from the entrance: Lilycove City

Utility:
Surf is required to access this area.

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

  • Wild Tentacool have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Staryu have a 50% chance of holding Stardust and a 5% chance of holding a Star Piece.
