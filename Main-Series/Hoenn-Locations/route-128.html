Route 128

Access to:
North: Route 127
South: Route 129
East: Ever Grande City
Accessible on the route: Seafloor Cavern

Utility:
Surf is required to access and traverse this route. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Dive is required to access the Seafloor Cavern, as well as a Protein and a Pearl.

Access to:
North: Route 127
South: Route 129
East: Ever Grande City
Accessible on the route: Seafloor Cavern

Utility:
Surf is required to access and traverse this route. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Dive is required to access the Seafloor Cavern, as well as a Stone Plate, a Draco Plate, a Pixie Plate, an Adamant Orb, a Protein, a Pearl, and Free Diver Mayu. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Dive.

Pokémon

  • Wild Luvdisc have a 50% chance of holding a Heart Scale.
  • Wild Corsola have a 5% chance of holding a Red Shard.

Pokémon

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

  • Wild Tentacool and Tentacruel have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Pelipper have a 50% chance of holding a Pretty Wing and a 5% chance of holding a Lucky Egg.
  • Wild Luvdisc have a 50% chance of holding a Heart Scale.
  • Wild Corsola have a 5% chance of holding a Luminous Moss.
  • Wild Chinchou, Lanturn, and Relicanth have a 5% chance of holding a Deep Sea Scale.
  • Wild Clamperl have a 50% chance of holding a Pearl and a 5% chance of holding a Big Pearl.
