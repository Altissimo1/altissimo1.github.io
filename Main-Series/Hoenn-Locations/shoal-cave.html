Shoal Cave

Access to:
Exit from the Entrance room: Route 125

Utility:
Surf is required to access and traverse this area. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Access to:
Exit from the Entrance room: Route 125

Places of interest:
Eevee will evolve into Glaceon in the Ice room due to the Icy Rock.

Utility:
Surf is required to access and traverse this area. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
The Mach Bike is required to access TM79 Frost Breath in the Lower room during low tide.
Rock Smash can be used to encounter wild Pokémon and find random items.

This cave has two stages, corresponding to high tide and low tide. During high tide, the player can access high cliffs using Surf. During low tide, the player can access lower levels of the cave. The tide depends on the time of day. From 9:00 to 2:59 (AM and PM), it is high tide; from 3:00 to 8:59 (AM and PM), it is low tide.

During high tide, only the Entrance and Inner rooms are accessible.

Pokémon

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

  • Wild Snorunt have a 5% chance of holding a Snowball.
  • Wild Tentacool and Tentacruel have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Graveler have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
