Mirage Mountain

Access to:
All Mirage Mountains can only be accessed from soaring in the sky. Their location on the map is given below, in relation to each individual mountain.

Utility:
The Acro Bike is required to access a Protein in Spot #1 and TM84 Poison Jab in Spot #2.
Rock Smash can be used to find items and encounter wild Pokémon in Spots #3 and #6.
The Mach Bike is required to access a Rare Candy in Spot #5.

Every day, the game picks one random Mirage Spot (Mirage Island, Mirage Forest, Mirage Cave, Mirage Mountain, or Crescent Isle) to be accessible. More can be made available on a given day by interacting with other players through StreetPass. There are eight of each of Mirage Island, Mirage Forest, Mirage Cave, and Mirage Mountain, located in different areas; despite having the same name, their Pokémon and items differ, meaning the player must visit all eight to get everything. Details of all eight Mirage Mountains are given below.

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

Spot #1

This area is located west of Route 104.

A Protein can be found in the southern area, requiring the Acro Bike. West of the grass patch, a hidden Green Shard, Yellow Shard, or Deep Sea Scale can be found.

  • Wild Kricketune have a 5% chance of holding a Metronome.

Walking

Spot #2

This area is located north of Lilycove City.

TM84 Poison Jab can be found across the eastern bridge, requiring the Acro Bike. In the southeastern corner of the grass patch, a hidden Green Shard, Yellow Shard, or Deep Sea Scale can be found.

  • Wild Kricketune have a 5% chance of holding a Metronome.

Walking

Spot #3

This area is located north of Route 125.

A Zinc can be found in the northwest, requiring Rock Smash. Northeast of the grass patch, a hidden Green Shard, Yellow Shard, or Deep Sea Scale can be found.

Fossils can be found here with Rock Smash. The Old Amber can be found in both games. In Omega Ruby, Dome Fossils, Armor Fossils, and Plume Fossils can be found. In Alpha Sapphire, Helix Fossils, Skull Fossils, and Cover Fossils can be found.

  • Wild Audino have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Sitrus Berry.
  • Wild Happiny have a 50% chance of holding an Oval Stone and a 5% chance of holding a Lucky Egg.
  • Wild Graveler have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
  • Wild Crustle have a 5% chance of holding a Hard Stone.
