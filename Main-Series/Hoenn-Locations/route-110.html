Route 110

Access to:
North: Mauville City
West: Route 103
South: Slateport City
Accessible on the route: Trick House
Accessible on the route: New Mauville

Utility:
Surf is required to access New Mauville, as well as a Rare Candy. Wild Pokémon can be encountered here with Surf.
The Mach Bike or Acro Bike is required to access Cycling Road.
