Route 110

Access to:
North: Mauville City
West: Route 103
South: Slateport City
Accessible on the route: Trick House
Accessible on the route: New Mauville

Utility:
Surf is required to access a Rare Candy and the entrance to New Mauville. Wild Pokémon can be encountered here with Surf.
The Mach Bike or Acro Bike is required to access Cycling Road.

Access to:

Utility:
Surf is required to access New Mauville, as well as a Rare Candy and an Elixir. Wild Pokémon can be encountered here with Surf.
The Mach Bike or Acro Bike is required to access Seaside Cycling Road.

Pokémon

  • Wild Gulpin have a 5% chance of holding a Big Pearl.
  • Wild Zigzagoon have a 5% chance of holding an Oran Berry.
  • Wild Poochyena have a 5% chance of holding a Pecha Berry.

Pokémon

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

  • Wild Zigzagoon have a 50% chance of holding a Potion and a 5% chance of holding a Revive.
  • Wild Gulpin have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Sitrus Berry.
  • Wild Oddish have a 5% chance of holding an Absorb Bulb.
  • Wild Wingull have a 50% chance of holding a Pretty Wing.
  • Wild Plusle and Minun have a 5% chance of holding a Cell Battery.
  • Wild Tentacool have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Pelipper have a 50% chance of holding a Pretty Wing and a 5% chance of holding a Lucky Egg.
  • Wild Trubbish have a 5% chance of holding Black Sludge.
  • Wild Chatot have a 5% chance of holding a Metronome.
