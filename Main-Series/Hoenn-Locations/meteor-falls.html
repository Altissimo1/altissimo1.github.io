Meteor Falls

Access to:
Eastern exit on 1F (front): Route 114
Southern exit on 1F (front): Route 115

Utility:
Surf can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.
Surf and Waterfall are required to access TM23 Iron Tail and a PP Up on 1F (front), as well as 1F (back) and all of B1F.

Access to:
Eastern exit on 1F (front): Route 114
Southern exit on 1F (front): Route 115

Places of interest:
Move Tutor: After finishing the Delta Episode, Zinnia's grandmother on 1F will teach Dragon Ascent to the player's Rayquaza.

Utility:
Surf is required to access a Great Ball on 1F. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Surf and Waterfall are required to access a Stardust and a PP Max on 1F (front), as well as 1F (back) and all of B1F.

Pokémon

  • Wild Solrock have a 5% chance of holding a Sun Stone.
  • Wild Lunatone have a 5% chance of holding a Moon Stone.
  • Wild Bagon have a 5% chance of holding a Dragon Scale.

Pokémon

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

  • Wild Solrock have a 50% chance of holding Stardust and a 5% chance of holding a Sun Stone.
  • Wild Lunatone have a 50% chance of holding Stardust and a 5% chance of holding a Moon Stone.
  • Wild Bagon and Druddigon have a 5% chance of holding a Dragon Fang.
  • Wild Goldeen have a 5% chance of holding Mystic Water.
  • Wild Clefairy have a 5% chance of holding a Moon Stone.
