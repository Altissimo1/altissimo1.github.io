Route 123

Access to:
North: Route 122
West: Route 118

Utility:
Cut is required to access a Rare Candy and Psychic Cameron. In Emerald, Cut is also required to access a PP Up, Ninja Boy Jonas, and Parasol Lady Kayley.

Pokémon

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

  • Wild Linoone have a 50% chance of holding a Potion and a 5% chance of holding a Max Revive.
  • Wild Shuppet have a 5% chance of holding a Spell Tag.
  • Wild Gloom have a 5% chance of holding an Absorb Bulb.
  • Wild Pelipper have a 50% chance of holding a Pretty Wing and a 5% chance of holding a Lucky Egg.
  • Wild Surskit have a 5% chance of holding Honey.
  • Wild Masquerain have a 5% chance of holding Silver Powder.
  • Wild Goldeen have a 5% chance of holding a Mystic Water.
  • Wild Wingull have a 50% chance of holding a Pretty Wing.

The Berry Master gives away two Berries per day, from the below list.

Pomeg Berry Kelpsy Berry Qualot Berry Hondew Berry Grepa Berry

Berry Master's Wife

Once a day, the player can choose to tell her a special phrase, and receive a Berry. There are five special phrases that the player can tell her in order to receive a rare Berry. Each of the rare Berries can only be obtained once. The normal Berries she can give out, and the rare Berries and their associated phrases, are given below.

Berry Master's Wife

Once a day, the player can choose to tell her a special phrase, and receive a Berry. She will prompt the player with part of a phrase, and if the correct word is given to fill in her phrase, she will give the player a rare Berry. Each of the rare Berries can only be obtained once.
