Route 123

Access to:
North: Route 122
West: Route 118

Utility:
Cut is required to access a Rare Candy and Psychic Cameron. In Emerald, Cut is also required to access a PP Up, Ninja Boy Jonas, and Parasol Lady Kayley.
Surf is required to access most of the eastern part of the route from Route 122.

Access to:
North: Route 122
West: Route 118

Utility:
Cut is required to access a PP Up, a Revival Herb, six Qualot Berries, six Grepa Berries, six Tamato Berries, Psychic Cameron, Street Thug Hannibal, Ace Trainer Julie, Fisherman Timin, Fisherman Fisher, and Fisherman Finley.
Surf is required to access most of the eastern part of the route from Route 122.

Pokémon

  • Wild Kecleon have a 5% chance of holding a Persim Berry.
  • Wild Zigzagoon have a 5% chance of holding an Oran Berry.
  • Wild Linoone have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Sitrus Berry.
  • Wild Shuppet and Duskull have a 5% chance of holding a Spell Tag.
  • Wild Poochyena and Mightyena have a 5% chance of holding a Pecha Berry.

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

Berry Master's Wife

Once a day, the player can choose to tell her a special phrase, and receive a Berry. She will prompt the player with part of a phrase, and if the correct word is given to fill in her phrase, she will give the player a rare Berry. Each of the rare Berries can only be obtained once. The normal Berries she can give out, and the rare Berries and their associated phrases, are given below. The words must be entered in all capitals.
