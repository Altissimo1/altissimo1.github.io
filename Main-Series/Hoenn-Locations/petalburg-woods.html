Petalburg Woods

Access to:
Any exit: Route 104

Utility:
Cut is required to access the eastern part of the forest, which contains the girl who gives the Miracle Seed, two Tinymushrooms, a Great Ball, and an X Attack.

Access to:
Any exit: Route 104
East (via soaring in the sky): Trackless Forest

Places of interest:
Eevee will evolve into Leafeon here due to the Moss Rock in the northeast.

Utility:
Cut is required to access the eastern part of the forest, which contains the girl who gives the Miracle Seed, two Tiny Mushrooms, a Great Ball, an X Attack, and a Scizorite.

Pokémon

  • Wild Zigzagoon have a 5% chance of holding an Oran Berry.
  • Wild Poochyena have a 5% chance of holding a Pecha Berry.

Walking

Pokémon

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

  • Wild Zigzagoon have a 50% chance of holding a Potion and a 5% chance of holding a Revive.
  • Wild Wurmple have a 50% chance of holding a Pecha Berry and a 5% chance of holding Bright Powder.
  • Wild Shroomish and Paras have a 50% chance of holding a Tiny Mushroom and a 5% chance of holding a Big Mushroom.
