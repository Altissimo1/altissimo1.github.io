Route 104

Access to:
North: Rustboro City
South: Route 105
East: Petalburg City
Accessible on the route: Petalburg Woods

Utility:
Cut or Surf is required to access an X Accuracy in the northern half.
Surf is required to freely travel to Route 105 from the southern half and to access a PP UP in the northern half. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
