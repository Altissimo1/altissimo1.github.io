Mirage Forest

Access to:
All Mirage Forests can only be accessed from soaring in the sky. Their location on the map is given below, in relation to each individual forest.

Utility:
Rock Smash can be used to find items and encounter wild Pokémon in Spot #1.
Cut is required to access a Green Shard in Spot #7 and an Iron in Spot #8.

Every day, the game picks one random Mirage Spot (Mirage Island, Mirage Forest, Mirage Cave, Mirage Mountain, or Crescent Isle) to be accessible. More can be made available on a given day by interacting with other players through StreetPass. There are eight of each of Mirage Island, Mirage Forest, Mirage Cave, and Mirage Mountain, located in different areas; despite having the same name, their Pokémon and items differ, meaning the player must visit all eight to get everything. Details of all eight Mirage Forests are given below.

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

Spot #1

This area is located west of Route 105.

A Max Elixir can be found in the northeast area of the forest. In the western area, a hidden Tiny Mushroom, Big Mushroom, or Razor Claw can be found.

Fossils can be found here with Rock Smash. The Old Amber can be found in both games. In Omega Ruby, Dome Fossils, Armor Fossils, and Plume Fossils can be found. In Alpha Sapphire, Helix Fossils, Skull Fossils, and Cover Fossils can be found.

  • Wild Happiny have a 50% chance of holding an Oval Stone and a 5% chance of holding a Lucky Egg.
  • Wild Graveler have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.

Spot #2

This area is located south of Route 109.

A Full Restore can be found in the southwest. Between the grass and the ledge in the south, a hidden Tiny Mushroom, Big Mushroom, or Razor Claw can be found.

  • Wild Audino have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Sitrus Berry.

Spot #3

This area is located north of Route 111.

TM74 Gyro Ball can be found in the southwest corner. Northwest of the grass patch, a hidden Tiny Mushroom, Big Mushroom, or Razor Claw can be found.

  • Wild Kricketune have a 5% chance of holding a Metronome.

Spot #4
