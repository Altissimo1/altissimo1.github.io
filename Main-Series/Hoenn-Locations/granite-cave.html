Access to:
Exit on 1F: Route 106

Utility:
Flash is recommended on B1F and B2F.
The Mach Bike is required to access a Repel, a Rare Candy, and an Everstone on B2F.
Rock Smash can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Pokémon

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

  • Wild Makuhita have a 5% chance of holding a Black Belt.
  • Wild Abra have a 5% chance of holding a Twisted Spoon.
  • Wild Geodude have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
  • Wild Aron and Nosepass have a 5% chance of holding a Hard Stone.
  • Wild Mawile have a 5% chance of holding an Iron Ball.
  • Wild Sableye have a 5% chance of holding a Wide Lens.
