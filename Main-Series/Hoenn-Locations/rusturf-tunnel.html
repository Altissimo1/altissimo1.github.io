Rusturf Tunnel

Access to:
Western exits: Route 116
Eastern exit: Verdanturf Town

Utility:
Rock Smash is required to break the rocks in the center, allowing the player to get HM04 Strength from Wanda's boyfriend. Using Rock Smash here permanently removes the rocks, allowing the player to travel through the whole tunnel.

Access to:
Western exits: Route 116
Eastern exit: Verdanturf Town

Utility:
Rock Smash is required to break the rocks in the center, allowing the player to get an Aggronite from Wanda's boyfriend. Using Rock Smash here permanently removes the rocks, allowing the player to travel through the whole tunnel. Wild Pokémon and random items can also be found here with Rock Smash.

Pokémon

  • Wild Whismur have a 5% chance of holding a Chesto Berry.
