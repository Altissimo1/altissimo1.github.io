Access to:
Exit: Route 111

Utility:
Strength is required to open Regirock's room.

The player must complete the puzzle at the Sealed Chamber in order for this cave to open.

Inside the cave, in Ruby and Sapphire, the player must go two tiles east and two tiles south from the Braille inscription and use Strength. In Emerald, the player must instead go two tiles west and two south, and use Rock Smash.
