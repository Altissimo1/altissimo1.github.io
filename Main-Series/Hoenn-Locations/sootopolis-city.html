Sootopolis City

Access to:
Accessible from the Dive spot: Route 126
Accessible in the city: Cave of Origin

Places of interest:
Poké Mart
Sootopolis Gym: The eighth Gym. It specializes in Water-types.
Move Tutor: In Emerald, a Trainer in the Pokémon Center can teach one Pokémon Double-Edge.

Utility:
Surf is required to access Sootopolis City the first time and traverse the city, as well as to travel to Route 126. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Dive is required to traverse Sootopolis City the first time, as well as to travel to Route 126.

Kiri's Berries

A little girl named Kiri will give the player two Berries every day, except when abnormal weather is present, or (in Emerald) when Groudon and Kyogre are fighting. One Berry will always be a Figy or Iapapa berry, and the other will come from the below list.

Pomeg Berry Kelpsy Berry Qualot Berry Hondew Berry Grepa Berry
Tamato Berry Cornn Berry Magost Berry Rabuta Berry Nomel Berry
