Faraway Island

This area is only accessible in Emerald.

Access to:
Accessible from the area (via the S.S. Tidal): Lilycove City

Accessing this area requires the Old Sea Map, which was only distributed in Japan and Taiwan in 2005 and 2006. The Old Sea Map can be obtained with hacks or cheats, and Faraway Island itself is accessible in Emerald with the use of a glitch.

Pokémon

The player must track down Mew in the tall grass to battle it.
