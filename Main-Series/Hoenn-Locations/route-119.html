Route 119

Access to:
South: Route 118
East: Fortree City

Places of interest:
Weather Institute: A bed on the first floor will allow the player to heal their Pokémon.

Utility:
Surf is required to access a Max Ether, an Ultra Ball, a Leaf Stone, a Zinc, and Fisherman Eugene (Ruby/Sapphire) or Chris (Emerald). Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
The Acro Bike is required to access a Calcium.
Surf and Waterfall are required to access four Hondew Berries.
Surf, Waterfall, and the Acro Bike are required to access a Rare Candy. In Emerald, they are also required to access a Nugget.
The Devon Scope is required to battle two Kecleon.

Feebas Tiles

Six of the water tiles on this route will contain Feebas. The six tiles are randomly generated when the game begins, and can be randomly generated again by changing the trendy phrase in Dewford Town.

When fishing using any Rod on a 'Feebas tile', there is a 50% chance to encounter Feebas and a 50% chance that the encounter is taken from the normal encounter table, effectively halving all normal encounter rates.

Pokémon

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

  • Wild Linoone have a 50% chance of holding a Potion and a 5% chance of holding a Max Revive.
  • Wild Oddish and Gloom have a 5% chance of holding an Absorb Bulb.
  • Wild Tentacool have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Wingull have a 50% chance of holding a Pretty Wing.
  • Wild Pelipper have a 50% chance of holding a Pretty Wing and a 5% chance of holding a Lucky Egg.
  • Wild Carvanha and Sharpedo have a 5% chance of holding a Deep Sea Tooth.
