Route 115

Access to:
South: Rustboro City
Accessible on the route: Meteor Falls
Potentially accessible on the route: Terra Cave (Emerald only)

Utility:
Surf is required to access most of the route, including TM01 Focus Punch, Kelpsy Berries, Expert Timothy, and Black Belt Koichi. In Emerald, Surf is required to access a Heal Powder, Ninja Boy Jaiden, Triathlete Kyra, Battle Girl Helene, and Psychic Alix. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Rock Smash is required to access a Great Ball.
The Mach Bike and Surf are required to access an Iron.
