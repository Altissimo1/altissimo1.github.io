Route 115

Access to:
South: Rustboro City
Accessible on the route: Meteor Falls
Potentially accessible on the route: Terra Cave (Emerald only)

Utility:
Surf is required to access most of the route, including TM01 Focus Punch, Kelpsy Berries, Expert Timothy, and Black Belt Koichi. In Emerald, Surf is required to access a Heal Powder, Ninja Boy Jaiden, Triathlete Kyra, Battle Girl Helene, and Psychic Alix. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Rock Smash is required to access a Great Ball and Bluk Berries.
The Mach Bike and Surf are required to access an Iron.

Access to:
South: Rustboro City
Accessible on the route: Meteor Falls
West (via soaring in the sky): Mirage Cave

Utility:
Surf is required to access a PP Up, a Heal Powder, Bluk Berries, Kelpsy Berries, Black Belt Koichi, Expert Timothy, and Ruin Maniac Hayes. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
The Mach Bike and Surf are required to access an Iron.

Pokémon

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

  • Wild Wingull have a 50% chance of holding a Pretty Wing.
  • Wild Tentacool have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Pelipper have a 50% chance of holding a Pretty Wing and a 5% chance of holding a Lucky Egg.
  • Wild Clefairy have a 5% chance of holding a Moon Stone.
