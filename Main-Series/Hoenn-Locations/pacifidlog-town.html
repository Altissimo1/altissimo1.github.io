Pacifidlog Town

Access to:
West: Route 132
East: Route 131

Places of interest:
A man in a house on the east side will alert the player when Mirage Island is visible.
Move Tutor: In Emerald, a tutor in the Pokémon Center can teach one Pokémon Explosion.

Utility:
Surf is required to access Pacifidlog the first time, as well as to travel to Route 131 or 132. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Utility:
