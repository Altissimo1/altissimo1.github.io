Pacifidlog Town

Access to:
West: Route 132
East: Route 131

Places of interest:
A man in a house on the east side will alert the player when Mirage Island is visible.
Move Tutor: In Emerald, the southeastern man in the Pokémon Center can teach one Pokémon Explosion.

Utility:
Surf is required to access Pacifidlog Town the first time, as well as to travel to Route 131 or 132. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Access to:
West: Route 132
East: Route 131
South (via soaring in the sky): Mirage Island

Utility:
Surf is required to access Pacifidlog Town the first time, access the Big Pearl and Kangaskhanite, and travel to Route 131 or 132. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Pokémon

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

  • Wild Tentacool and Tentacruel have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Pelipper have a 50% chance of holding a Pretty Wing and a 5% chance of holding a Lucky Egg.
