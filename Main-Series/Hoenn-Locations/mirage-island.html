Mirage Island

Access to:
Technically part of Route 130

Utility:
Surf is required to access Mirage Island.

Access to:
All Mirage Islands can only be accessed from soaring in the sky. Their location on the map is given below, in relation to each individual island.

Utility:
The Mach Bike is required to access a Max Elixir in Spot #4.
Rock Smash can be used to find items and encounter wild Pokémon in Spots #6 and #8.
Cut is required to access a Rare Candy in Spot #7.

A man in the southeastern house in Pacifidlog Town can be spoken to to reveal if Mirage Island is visible.

Every Pokémon has a personality value which controls some aspects of the Pokémon, such as its gender, Ability, shiny status, and in the case of Spinda, its spot pattern. Every day, the game will generate a value between 0 and 65535 and compare this to the personality values of the Pokémon in the player's party. If a match is found - which on a single Pokémon is a 1/65536 chance - Mirage Island will be visible and accessible. The more Pokémon the player has, the more likely it is that a match will be found, but Mirage Island will only be visible if a matching Pokémon is actively in the player's party.

Every day, the game picks one random Mirage Spot (Mirage Island, Mirage Forest, Mirage Cave, Mirage Mountain, or Crescent Isle) to be accessible. More can be made available on a given day by interacting with other players through StreetPass. There are eight of each of Mirage Island, Mirage Forest, Mirage Cave, and Mirage Mountain, located in different areas; despite having the same name, their Pokémon and items differ, meaning the player must visit all eight to get everything. Details of all eight Mirage Islands are given below.

Pokémon that can be obtained via the DexNav as Hidden Pokémon can be up to 29 levels higher than their levels here: any individual Hidden Pokémon can be up to 10 levels higher than their natural highest level, and an additional 19 levels can be added by chaining hidden encounters.

Pokémon

Walking

Spot #1

This area is located west of Route 104.

An Ether can be found in the center of the island. On the southwest corner of the lower platform, a hidden Pearl, Big Pearl, or Razor Fang can be found.

  • Wild Venomoth have a 5% chance of holding a Shed Shell.

Walking

Spot #2

This area is located west of Dewford Town.

A Big Pearl can be found south of the tree. On the north side of the tree, a hidden Pearl, Big Pearl, or Razor Fang can be found.

  • Wild Venomoth have a 5% chance of holding a Shed Shell.

Walking

Spot #3

This area is located north of Route 113.

A Star Piece can be found in the crater. North of the crater, a hidden Pearl, Big Pearl, or Razor Fang can be found.

Walking (Spot 3)

Spot #4

This area is located north of Route 124.

A Max Elixir can be found atop the steep slope, requiring the Mach Bike. North of the grass area, a hidden Pearl, Big Pearl, or Razor Fang can be found.

  • Wild Venomoth have a 5% chance of holding a Shed Shell.
  • Wild Persian have a 5% chance of holding a Quick Claw.

Walking (Spot 4)

Spot #5

This area is located north of Route 125.

TM91 Flash Cannon can be found atop the ladder. In the ruined building, a hidden Pearl, Big Pearl, or Razor Fang can be found.

Walking (Spot 5)

Spot #6

This area is located south of Pacifidlog Town.

TM90 Substitute can be found on the volcano's rim, requiring Rock Smash. Above the stairs leading to the rim, a hidden Pearl, Big Pearl, or Razor Fang can be found.

Fossils can be found here with Rock Smash. The Old Amber can be found in both games. In Omega Ruby, Dome Fossils, Armor Fossils, and Plume Fossils can be found. In Alpha Sapphire, Helix Fossils, Skull Fossils, and Cover Fossils can be found.

  • Wild Audino have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Sitrus Berry.
  • Wild Graveler have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.

Walking (Spot 6)

Rock Smash (Spot 6)

Spot #7

This area is located south of Route 132.

A Rare Candy can be found in the north, requiring Cut. At the northern part of the island, a hidden Pearl, Big Pearl, or Razor Fang can be found.

  • Wild Ditto have a 50% chance of holding Quick Powder and a 5% chance of holding Metal Powder.

Walking (Spot 7)

Spot #8

This area is located south of Route 134.

TM66 Payback can be found in the east, requiring Rock Smash. To the northeast, a hidden Pearl, Big Pearl, or Razor Fang can be found.

Fossils can be found here with Rock Smash. The Old Amber can be found in both games. In Omega Ruby, Dome Fossils, Armor Fossils, and Plume Fossils can be found. In Alpha Sapphire, Helix Fossils, Skull Fossils, and Cover Fossils can be found.

  • Wild Venomoth have a 5% chance of holding a Shed Shell.
  • Wild Maractus have a 5% chance of holding a Miracle Seed.
  • Wild Graveler have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
