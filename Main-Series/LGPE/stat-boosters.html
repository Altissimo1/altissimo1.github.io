Let's Go Pikachu and Let's Go Eevee Stat Booster Locations

The following table contains the location(s) of stat boosters, including Candies, Rare Candy, PP Ups, PP Maxes, Bottle Caps, and Gold Bottle Caps in Let's Go Pikachu and Let's Go Eevee.

Catching Wild Pokémon

Catching wild Pokémon can reward the player with Candies. The type of Candy the player will receive is dependent on what the Pokémon's EV yield is in other Pokémon games. The mapping of stats to Candy is as follows:

HP Health Candy
Attack Mighty Candy
Defense Tough Candy
Special Attack Smart Candy
Special Defense Courage Candy
Speed Quick Candy

The higher the player's Catch Combo, the more likely it is that the player will receive Candy L and Candy XL for their captures, and the more likely it is that the player will receive higher amounts of Candy.

Transferring Pokémon to Professor Oak

Professor Oak will reward the player with Candy for transferring Pokémon to him. For every Pokémon transferred, he will provide one Candy. The type of Candy obtained this way is whatever Candy the Pokémon may yield when caught in the wild.

If 50 individuals of a single species are transferred to Professor Oak, he will give the player one candy that is compatible with that Pokémon's species. For example, 50 Bulbasaur, Ivysaur, or Venusaur transferred will yield one Bulbasaur Candy.

GO Park

If the player has 25 of the same species in the GO Park, they can play a minigame. Completing this minigame in the time limit will reward the plaayer with random stat Candies.

Poké Ball Plus

If a Pokémon is sent for a stroll in the Poké Ball Plus, the player can receive two Candies for every 5000 EXP gained.

Day Care

When the player retrieves a Pokémon left at the Day Care, they will receive an item, depending on the number of steps taken since depositing the Pokémon. Rare Candies are among the possible items, and have the following chances of being obtained.

Steps taken Chance
1500 1%
3000 5%
5000 10%
8000 20%

Celadon Game Corner

The Game Corner has six spots where hidden items can be found daily. Each spot can spawn a Berry, a Candy (and its L and XL forms), PP Up, PP Max, Bottle Cap, and Gold Battle Cap. The chances of finding each item are shown below.

Item Chance
Berry 50%
Candy 30%
Candy L 10%
Candy XL 5%
PP Up 2%
PP Max 1%
Bottle Cap 1%
Gold Bottle Cap 1%

All of the items except for Berries are stat boosters. The following table gives the six item spots and their associated Candy type.

Location Candy Type
In the southwest corner Health Candy
On the fire extinguisher on the western wall Mighty Candy
