Let's Go Pikachu and Let's Go Eevee Poké Ball Locations

The following table contains the location(s) of all Poké Ball types in Let's Go Pikachu and Let's Go Eevee.

Poké Marts

All Poké Marts sell Poké Balls, Great Balls, and Ultra Balls, although Great Balls and Ultra Balls must be unlocked by obtainining 1 Badge and 4 Badges, respectively.

The player will receive a Premier Ball for every ten Poké Balls, Great Balls, or Ultra Balls they purchase at once.

Celadon Department Store only sells Poké Balls, Great Balls, and Premier Balls (for buying ten Poké or Great Balls).

A Man in Caves

A man in Mt. Moon, Rock Tunnel, and Seafoam Islands will give the player 10 Poké Balls if they have fewer than 10 in their Bag. He can be spoken to anytime the player has fewer than 10 Poké Balls to receive more.

Trainers

With a few exceptions, all Trainers in the game will give the player three Poké Balls after they are defeated in battle. The exceptions are:

  • Trainers in Gyms, including the Gym Leader, will never give Poké Balls.
  • Team Rocket Grunts, Jessie and James, Team Rocket Admin Archer, and Giovanni will never give Poké Balls.
  • The Elite Four, the Rival, Blue, Red, Green, and Mina will never give Poké Balls.
  • Scientists, Coach Trainers, and Master Trainers will never give Poké Balls.
  • Campers and Picnickers give two Great Balls instead of three Poké Balls.
  • Ace Trainers give five Ultra Balls instead of three Poké Balls.

Cerulean Cave 2F

Cerulean Cave 2F has eleven spots where hidden items can be found. All spots are 'glowing' and have a 10%-30% (depending on the spot) chance of regenerating for every 256 steps the player takes. Five of the spots yield items in the Poké Ball family. The exact locations of each spot can be found here, in the 'Cerulean Cave Locations' tab.
