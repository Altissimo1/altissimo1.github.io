Let's Go Pikachu and Let's Go Eevee Evo Item Locations

The following tables contains the location of all evolutionary items in Let's Go Pikachu and Let's Go Eevee.

Celadon Department Store - 4F

Fire, Thunder, Water, Leaf, and Ice Stones can be purchased on 4F of Celadon Department Store for 5000 each.
