Let's Go Pikachu and Let's Go Eevee Clothing Locations

The following table contains the location of all clothing objects, for both the player and the Partner Pokémon, in Let's Go Pikachu and Let's Go Eevee.

Clothing Sets

Full clothing sets are obtained from various people throughout the game. Each clothing set has clothes that can be worn by both the player character and their Partner Pokémon. Clothing sets contain a Cap, Top, Shorts, Shoes, and a Bag for the player character, and a Tee and (usually) Cap for the Partner Pokémon.
