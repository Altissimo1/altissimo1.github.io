Let's Go Pikachu and Let's Go Eevee Move Locations

The following table contains every TM, Move Tutor, and Move Relearner location in Let's Go Pikachu and Let's Go Eevee.
All TM moves and the Move Relearner have the same locations in all versions of Let's Go Pikachu and Let's Go Eevee. The Move Tutors have the same location in both games, but teach different moves to different Pokémon depending on if the game is Let's Go Pikachu or Let's Go Eevee.

Move Relearner

The Move Relearner, Madame Memorial, is located at Indigo Plateau. She can make a Pokémon remember a single move from its level-up movepool in exchange for a single Heart Scale. Heart Scales can be found in the following locations:

  • Cerulean City: The player will receive five from the Bike Maniac for listening to his descriptions of Bikes.
  • Route 20: One can be found east of Picnicker Irene on the western half of the route.
  • Seafoam Islands: Two can be found on 1F and three on B2F. All five are hidden and therefore can regenerate.
  • The player's Partner Pikachu or Eevee can sometimes present one to the player as a gift.

Move Tutors

The only Move Tutors in Let's Go are those that can teach your Partner Pikachu or Partner Eevee special moves. There are three of them total: one in Cerulean City, one in Celadon City, and one in Fuchsia City. All three Move Tutors can teach all available moves, but each Move Tutor has a specific set of moves they are tied to, and will only teach the other moves after the player has interacted with the Move Tutor that those moves are attached to.
