Route 32

Access to:
North: Violet City
West: Ruins of Alph
Accessible on the route: Union Cave

Places of interest:
Fisher Ralph will call to inform the player of Qwilfish swarms if his number is registered in the Pokégear.
Pokémon Center

Utility:
Surf can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.
Cut is required to obtain TM05 Roar from a man on the western plateau.

Access to:
North: Violet City
West: Ruins of Alph
Accessible on the route: Union Cave

Places of interest:
Pokémon Center
After obtaining the Plain Badge, Cameron appears here and can take photos on Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Saturdays.

Utility:
Surf is required to access a Heart Scale. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.
Cut is required to access a Super Potion and obtain TM05 Roar from a man on the western plateau.
Rock Smash is required to access a Shell Bell.

Pokémon

  • Wild Quagsire and Heracross can flee.

Pokémon

  • Wild Whismur have a 5% chance of holding a Chesto Berry.
  • Wild Linoone have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Sitrus Berry.
  • Wild Buizel have a 5% chance of holding a Wacan Berry.
  • Wild Rattata have a 5% chance of holding a Chilan Berry.
  • Wild Poliwhirl have a 5% chance of holding a King's Rock.
  • Wild Tentacool, Tentacruel, and Qwilfish have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
