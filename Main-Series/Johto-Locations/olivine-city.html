Olivine City

Access to:
North: Route 39
West: Route 40
Accessible in the city: Olivine Lighthouse
Accessible from the city: Vermilion City (via the S.S. Aqua)

Places of interest:
Port: The S.S. Aqua in the port can be used to travel to Vermilion City in Kanto. The service to travel from Olivine City to Vermilion City is available only on Mondays and Fridays.
Poké Mart
Olivine Gym: The sixth gym. It specializes in Steel-types.

Utility:
Surf can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Access to:
North: Route 39
West: Route 40
Accessible in the city: Glitter Lighthouse
Accessible from the city: Vermilion City (via the S.S. Aqua)

Places of interest:
Port: The S.S. Aqua in the port can be used to travel to Vermilion City in Kanto. The service to travel from Olivine City to Vermilion City is available only on Mondays and Fridays.
Poké Mart
Olivine Gym: The sixth gym. It specializes in Steel-types.
Jasmine appears in the Café between 1:00 PM and 2:00 PM after the player has defeated the Elite Four. She will give the player her Pokégear number when spoken to.
Sabrina appears in the harbor on Fridays after the player has defeated the Elite Four. She will give the player her Pokégear number when spoken to. The player must have the S.S. Ticket to access the harbor.

Utility:
Surf is required to access TM57 Charge Beam. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Rock Climb is required to access a hidden Rare Candy.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:

Pokémon

  • Wild Staryu have a 23% chance of holding Stardust and a 2% chance of holding a Star Piece.
  • Wild Shellder have a 23% chance of holding a Pearl and a 2% chance of holding a Big Pearl.
