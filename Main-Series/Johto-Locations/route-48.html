Route 48

Access to:
North: Safari Zone Gate
South: Route 47

Places of interest:
Cameron appears here and can take photos on Sundays and Fridays.

Utility:
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:

Pokémon

  • Wild Farfetch'd have a 5% chance of holding a Stick.
  • Wild Fearow have a 5% chance of holding a Sharp Beak.
  • Wild Girafarig have a 5% chance of holding a Persim Berry.
  • Wild Diglett have a 5% chance of holding a Soft Sand.
  • Wild Growlithe and Vulpix always hold a Rawst Berry.
