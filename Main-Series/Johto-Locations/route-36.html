Route 36

Access to:
North: Route 37
West: National Park
South: Route 35 (western exit)
South: Ruins of Alph (eastern exit)
East: Violet City

Places of interest:
In Crystal, Suicune appears east of the entrance to National Park after seeing it on Route 42.

Utility:
Cut is required to access Route 35 without going through National Park.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.
The Squirtbottle is needed to battle the Sudowoodo blocking the path. Clearing Sudowoodo is required to access Ecruteak City.

Pokémon

  • Wild Growlithe and Vulpix have a 25% chance of holding a Burnt Berry.
  • Wild Butterfree have a 2% chance of holding Silverpowder.
  • Wild Beedrill have a 2% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
