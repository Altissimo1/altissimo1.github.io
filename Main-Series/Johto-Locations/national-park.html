Access to:
South: Route 35
East: Route 36

Places of interest:
The Bug-Catching Contest is held here every Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday. After obtaining the National Pokédex, each day's contest will have a different pool of Pokémon, with Tuesday's contest keeping the same pool as the pre-National Pokédex contest.
Cameron appears in the southern gate and can take photos on Sundays and Saturdays. He also appears in the park itself and can take photos on Sundays, Mondays, Wednesdays, and Saturdays.
After obtaining Bugsy's Pokégear number, Cameron can take photos of the player with him here on Fridays.

Utility:
Rock Climb is required to access a Shiny Stone and the special Headbutt trees.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Pokémon

  • Wild Butterfree have a 2% chance of holding Silverpowder.
  • Wild Beedrill have a 2% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Paras have a 23% chance of holding a Tinymushroom and a 2% chance of holding a Big Mushroom.

Pokémon

  • Wild Sunkern have a 5% chance of holding a Coba Berry.
  • Wild Butterfree have a 5% chance of holding SilverPowder.
  • Wild Beedrill have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Paras have a 50% chance of holding a TinyMushroom and a 5% chance of holding a Big Mushroom.
  • Wild Kricketot and Kricketune have a 5% chance of holding a Metronome.
  • Wild Beautifly and Dustox have a 5% chance of holding a Shed Shell.
  • Wild Combee always hold Honey.
  • Wild Cherubi have a 5% chance of holding a Miracle Seed.
