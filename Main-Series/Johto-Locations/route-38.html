Route 38

Access to:
West: Route 39
East: Ecruteak City

Places of interest:
In Gold and Silver, Schoolboy Chad will call to inform the player of Snubbull swarms if his number is registered in the Pokégear.

Utility:
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Access to:
West: Route 39
East: Ecruteak City

Utility:
Rock Climb is required to access a Lax Incense, an HP Up, and the special Headbutt tree, by using Rock Climb on Route 39.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Pokémon

  • Wild Magnemite have a 2% chance of holding a Metal Coat.
  • Wild Miltank have a 25% chance of holding Moomoo Milk.
  • Wild Farfetch'd have a 2% chance of holding a Stick.
  • Wild Butterfree have a 2% chance of holding Silverpowder.
  • Wild Beedrill have a 2% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Magnemite and Snubbull can flee.
