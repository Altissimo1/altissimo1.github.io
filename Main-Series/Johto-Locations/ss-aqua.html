S.S. Aqua

Access to:
Travels from Olivine City to Vermilion City on Mondays and Fridays, and Vermilion City to Olivine City on Wednesdays and Sundays.

Places of interest:
The westernmost cabin on the northern row of rooms on the upper deck contains a bed for healing the player's Pokémon and a PC. Sleeping in the bed in any voyage after the first one will cause the ship to arrive.

Captain

The first time the Captain is spoken to on each voyage, he will give the player one of the elemental Plates. When all sixteen Plates have been received, he will give the player Nuggets.
