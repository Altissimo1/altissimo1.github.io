Route 44

Access to:
West: Mahogany Town
Accessible on the route: Ice Path

Places of interest:
In Gold and Silver, Fisher Wilton will call to inform the player of Remoraid swarms if his number is registered in the Pokégear.

Utility:
Surf is required to access the grass patch, a Max Revive, and an Elixer. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.
