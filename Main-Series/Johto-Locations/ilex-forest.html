Access to:
North: Route 34

Places of interest:
Move Tutor: A man here can teach Headbutt to Pokémon.
Bringing a special event Celebi here as the first Pokémon in the party will trigger an event where the player can engage in some extra content involving the Rival and Giovanni.
After obtaining the Plain Badge, Cameron appears here and can take photos on Sundays, Tuesdays, and Saturdays.

Utility:
Cut is required to pass between the northern and southern areas.
Surf is required to access a Big Mushroom. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.
