Union Cave

Access to:
1F: Route 32 (northern exit)
1F: Route 33 (southern exit)
Exits on B1F: Ruins of Alph

Utility:
Surf is required to access all of B2F, the Ruins of Alph exits, and much of B1F, including all four Trainers. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Surf and Strength are required to access the southern exit to the Ruins of Alph on B1F.

Access to:
1F: Route 32 (northern exit)
1F: Route 33 (southern exit)
Exits on B1F: Ruins of Alph

Utility:
Surf is required to access a Big Pearl on 1F and a Revive, as well as all four Trainers, on B1F. It is also required to access all of B2F and the Ruins of Alph exits. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Surf and Strength are required to access the southern exit to the Ruins of Alph on B1F.

Pokémon

  • Wild Geodude have a 2% chance of holding an Everstone.
  • Wild Staryu have a 23% chance of holding a Stardust and a 2% chance of holding a Star Piece.
  • Wild Quagsire can flee.
