Route 47

Access to:
North: Route 48
East: Cliff Edge Gate
Accessible on the route: Cliff Cave
Accessible on the route: Embedded Tower

Utility:
Surf is required to access a Pearl. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Surf and Waterfall are required to access the grass patch, the special Headbutt trees, and a White Flute.
Surf and Rock Climb are required to access the Embedded Tower, a Wave Incense, and a hidden Stardust.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:

Pokémon

  • Wild Ditto have a 50% chance of holding a Quick Powder and a 5% chance of holding a Metal Powder.
  • Wild Miltank always hold Moomoo Milk.
  • Wild Farfetch'd have a 5% chance of holding a Stick.
  • Wild Whismur have a 5% chance of holding a Chesto Berry.
  • Wild Linoone have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Sitrus Berry.
  • Wild Buizel have a 5% chance of holding a Wacan Berry.
  • Wild Raticate have a 5% chance of holding a Chilan Berry.
  • Wild Fearow have a 5% chance of holding a Sharp Beak.
  • Wild Tentacool and Beedrill have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Staryu have a 50% chance of holding Stardust and a 5% chance of holding a Star Piece.
  • Wild Butterfree have a 5% chance of holding Silverpowder.
  • Wild Chinchou and Lanturn have a 5% chance of holding a DeepSeaScale.
  • Wild Shellder have a 50% chance of holding a Pearl and a 5% chance of holding a Big Pearl.
