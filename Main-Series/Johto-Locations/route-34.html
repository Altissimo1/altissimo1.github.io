Route 34

Access to:
North: Goldenrod City
South: Ilex Forest

Places of interest:
Pokémon Day Care: In international versions of Pokémon Crystal, the player can receive the Odd Egg here.

Utility:
Surf is required to access a Rare Candy, a Soft Sand, and Cooltrainers Irene, Jenn, and Kate. In Crystal, Surf is also required to access a Nugget. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Access to:
North: Goldenrod City
South: Ilex Forest

Places of interest:
Pokémon Day Care
After obtaining the Plain Badge, Cameron can take photos of the player with the Day-Care Man here on Wednesdays and Thursdays.

Utility:
Surf is required to access a Nugget, a Rare Candy, a Power Herb, and Ace Trainers Irene, Jenn, and Kate. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Pokémon

  • Wild Staryu have a 23% chance of holding a Stardust and a 2% chance of holding a Star Piece.
  • Wild Butterfree have a 2% chance of holding Silverpowder.
  • Wild Beedrill have a 2% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Snubbull can flee.

Odd Egg (International Crystal only)

The Odd Egg will hatch into into one of the following Pokémon. It will always be female (except for Tyrogue), and will always know Dizzy Punch.
If it is not shiny, its IVs will be all 0's, and it will have a Hidden Power type of Fighting with 31 power.
If it is shiny, its IVs will be 2/10/10/10, and it will have a Hidden Power type of Grass with 49 power.
The hatched Pokémon has a higher chance than normal of being shiny. The percentages of how likely any given combination of Pokémon & shininess is are given below.
