Route 43

Access to:
North: Lake of Rage
South: Mahogany Town

Places of interest:
Going through the gate before clearing Team Rocket HQ costs 1000.

Utility:
Surf can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.
Surf and Cut are required to access a Bitter Berry.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Access to:

Pokémon

  • Wild Sentret have a 2% chance of holding a Berry.
  • Wild Farfetch'd have a 2% chance of holding a Stick.
  • Wild Furret have a 23% chance of holding a Gold Berry and a 2% chance of holding a Berry.
  • Wild Heracross can flee.
