Cliff Cave

Access to:
All exits: Route 47

Utility:
After the National Pokédex is obtained, Rock Smash can be used to encounter wild Pokémon and find random items.

Pokémon

  • Wild Geodude and Graveler have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
  • Wild Bronzor and Steelix have a 5% chance of holding a Metal Coat.
  • Wild Chingling have a 5% chance of holding a Colbur Berry.

Walking

Rock Smash

Items

HeartGold and SoulSilver Items
Item Location Notes
Ultra Ball On 3F, on the wall south of the southeasternmost floor tile Hidden.

Rock Smash

The following items can be found sometimes by breaking rocks.

Rock Smash Items
Max Ether Pearl Big Pearl Rare Bone

In HeartGold, Claw Fossils, Red Shards, and Yellow Shards can be found here, while in SoulSilver, Root Fossils, Blue Shards, and Green Shards can be found.
