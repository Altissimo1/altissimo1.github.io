Lottery Corner

The Lucky Channel station broadcasts an ID number, which changes once per week. If one of the player's Pokémon's IDs matches the number (partially or fully), the player will receive a prize.

Match Prize
Last two digits PP Up
Last three or four digits Exp. Share
All digits Master Ball

Buena's Password

In Crystal only, Buena's show airs on channel 10.5. She gives out a password between 6 PM and 12 AM. Speaking to, or calling, Buena and providing the correct password gives the player one point. Prizes can be purchased from Buena with the points. If the player obtains 30 points, speaking to Buena will register her phone number in the Pokégear.

Prize Points
Ultra Ball 2
Full Restore 2
Nugget 3
Rare Candy 3
Protein 5
Iron 5
Carbos 5
Calcium 5
HP Up 5

Buena's Password

Buena's show airs on channel 10.5. She gives out a password between 6 PM and 12 AM. Speaking to, or calling, Buena and providing the correct password gives the player one point. Prizes are automatically awarded when the player reaches specific point thresholds. If the player obtains 30 points, speaking to Buena will register her phone number in the Pokégear, and the player will receive a new Blue Card, allowing them to get the prizes again.

Poké Ball 1
Full Restore 3
Protein 5
Iron 10
Carbos 15
Nugget 20
Rare Candy 25
HP Up 30
