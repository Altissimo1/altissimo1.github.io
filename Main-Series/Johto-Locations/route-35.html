Route 35

Access to:
North: National Park
North: Route 36
South: Goldenrod City

Places of interest:
Bug Catcher Arnie will call to inform the player of Yanma swarms if his number is registered in the Pokégear.

Utility:
Surf is required to access a Mysteryberry. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.
Cut is required to access Route 36 directly.

Access to:
North: National Park
North: Route 36
South: Goldenrod City
West: Pokéathlon Dome (west through the southern gate)

Places of interest:
Cameron appears here and can take photos on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays.

Utility:
Surf is required to access the Apricorn tree and a Nugget. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.
Cut is required to access Route 36 directly.

Pokémon

  • Wild Growlithe have a 25% chance of holding a Burnt Berry.
  • Wild Butterfree have a 2% chance of holding Silverpowder.
  • Wild Beedrill have a 2% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Snubbull can flee.

Gift

All Versions
Pokémon Level Notes
Spearow 10 Obtained from the guard in the southern gate. It holds a Flower Mail.
A Pokémon holding its Mail can be given to a man on Route 31 in exchange for TM50 Nightmare. He will keep the Pokémon he is given permanently.

Pokémon

  • Wild Whismur have a 5% chance of holding a Chesto Berry.
  • Wild Linoone have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Sitrus Berry.
  • Wild Buizel have a 5% chance of holding a Wacan Berry.
  • Wild Abra have a 5% chance of holding a TwistedSpoon.
  • Wild Ditto have a 50% chance of holding a Quick Powder and a 5% chance of holding a Metal Powder.
  • Wild Yanma have a 5% chance of holding a Wide Lens.
