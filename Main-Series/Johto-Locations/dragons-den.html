Dragon's Den

Access to:
Exit on 1F: Blackthorn City

Utility:
Surf is required to access and traverse this area. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Whirlpool is required to access a Dragon Fang, a Max Elixer, and a Revive. In Crystal, Whirlpool is also required to access the back of the Dragon Shrine.

Access to:
Exit on 1F: Blackthorn City

Places of interest:
Clair appears here between 6:00-9:59 AM (06:00-09:59) after defeating her and Lance here in a Multi Battle. She will give the player her Pokégear number when spoken to. After her Pokégear number is obtained, she will appear here between 4:00-6:59 PM (16:00-18:59), and speaking to her will enable the player to take a picture with her.
After completing the quiz, Cameron can take photos of the player with the Dragon Master here on Mondays and Thursdays.

Utility:
Surf is required to access and traverse this area. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Whirlpool is required to access a Dragon Fang and the back of the Dragon Shrine.

Pokémon

  • Wild Dratini and Dragonair have a 2% chance of holding a Dragon Scale.
  • Wild Dratini and Dragonair can flee.
