Lake of Rage

Access to:
South: Route 43

Utility:
Surf is required to access the Red Gyarados and obtain the Red Scale. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Cut is required to access Wesley, Cooltrainer Aaron, a Rare Candy, TM10 Hidden Power, and TM43 Detect. In Gold and Silver, Cut is also required to access a Max Ether. In Crystal, Cut is also required to access an Elixer.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Access to:
South: Route 43

Places of interest:
On Wednesdays, after clearing the Team Rocket HQ, the water level drops, changing which items and Trainers are accessible.
Pryce appears between 6:00-9:59 AM (06:00-09:59) after the player has defeated the Elite Four. He will give the player his Pokégear number when spoken to.

Utility:
Surf is required to access the Red Gyarados and obtain the Red Scale, as well as to access a Red Flute, TM43 Secret Power, TM10 Hidden Power, a pair of Choice Specs, a Max Potion, and a Full Heal. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Cut is required to access a Max Revive and a Full Restore.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Pokémon

  • Wild Butterfree have a 2% chance of holding Silverpowder.
  • Wild Beedrill have a 2% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
