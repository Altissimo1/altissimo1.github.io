Mt. Mortar

Access to:
Exits on 1F (front): Route 42

Places of interest:
In Gold and Silver, Hiker Parry on Route 45 will call to inform the player of Marill swarms on 1F (front) if his number is registered in the Pokégear.

Utility:
In Gold and Silver, Flash is recommended.
Strength is required to access a Hyper Potion and an Escape Rope. In Crystal, Strength is also required to access an Ultra Ball and a Nugget.
Surf is required to access a Hyper Potion. In Gold and Silver, Surf is also required to access a Full Heal. In Crystal, Surf is also required to access a Full Restore, a Max Ether, a PP Up, and a Carbos. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Surf and Waterfall are required to access the entire 2F floor, as well as a hidden Max Repel, a Max Revive, and Blackbelt Kiyo. In Gold and Silver, Surf and Waterfall are also required to access a Max Revive. In Crystal, Surf and Waterfall are also required to access a Max Potion, an Iron, and Super Nerd Hugh.
In Crystal, Strength, or Surf and Waterfall are required to access Super Nerd Markus.

Access to:
Exits on 1F (front): Route 42

Utility:
Rock Climb is required to access a Revive, an Ultra Ball, a Protector, a Rare Candy, a Full Incense, and a Nugget.
Strength is required to access a Max Repel, an Ultra Ball, a Max Potion, an Escape Rope, and a Nugget.
Surf is required to access a Full Restore, a Mex Ether, a Hyper Potion, a PP Up, a hidden Ultra Ball, and a Carbos. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Surf and Waterfall are required to access the entire 2F floor, as well as a hidden Ether, an Iron, an Iron Ball, a Max Revive, a hidden Max Revive, Super Nerd Hugh, and Blackbelt Kiyo.
Strength, Rock Climb, or Surf and Waterfall are required to access Super Nerd Markus.
