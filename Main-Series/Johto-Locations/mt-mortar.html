Mt. Mortar

Access to:
Exits on 1F (front): Route 42

Places of interest:
In Gold and Silver, Hiker Parry on Route 45 will call to inform the player of Marill swarms on 1F (front) if his number is registered in the Pokégear.

Utility:
In Gold and Silver, Flash is recommended.
Strength is required to access a Hyper Potion and an Escape Rope on 1F (back). In Crystal, Strength is also required to access an Ultra Ball and a Nugget on 1F (back).
Surf is required to access a Hyper Potion on B1F. In Gold and Silver, Surf is also required to access a Full Heal on B1F. In Crystal, Surf is also required to access a Full Restore, a Max Ether, a PP Up, and a Carbos on B1F. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Surf and Waterfall are required to access the entire 2F floor, as well as a Max Repel and a Max Revive on 1F (back), and a Max Revive and Blackbelt Kiyo on B1F. In Crystal, Surf and Waterfall are also required to access a Max Potion and an Iron on B1F.
In Crystal, Strength, or Surf and Waterfall are required to access Super Nerd Markus on 1F.

Access to:
Exits on 1F (front): Route 42

Utility:
Rock Climb is required to access a Revive, an Ultra Ball, a Protector, a Rare Candy, a Full Incense, and a Nugget. Rock Climb is also required to access a Dubious Disc on Route 42.
Strength is required to access a Max Repel, an Ultra Ball, a Max Potion, an Escape Rope, and a Nugget.
Surf is required to access a Full Restore, a Max Ether, a Hyper Potion, a PP Up, a hidden Ultra Ball, and a Carbos. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Surf and Waterfall are required to access the entire 2F floor, as well as an Ether, an Iron, an Iron Ball, two Max Revives, Super Nerd Hugh, and Blackbelt Kiyo.
Strength, Rock Climb, or Surf and Waterfall are required to access Super Nerd Markus.
