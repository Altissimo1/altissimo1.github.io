Cianwood City

Access to:
East: Route 41

Places of interest:
In Crystal, Suicune appears in the northernmost part of the city.
Photo Studio: Players can print pictures of their Pokémon, stats, and Mail with a Game Boy Printer.
Pharmacy
Poké Seer: In Crystal, a lady in the northernmost house can tell the player where, when, and at which level a Pokémon in the player's party was caught.
Cianwood Gym: The fifth Gym. It specializes in Fighting-types.

Utility:
Surf is required to access Cianwood City the first time, as well as to travel to Route 41. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Rock Smash is required to access a Revive and a Max Ether. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Rock Smash.
Strength is required to navigate the Cianwood Gym.

Access to:
West: Cliff Edge Gate
East: Route 41

Places of interest:
Suicune appears in the northernmost part of the city.
Cameron's house: Players can take photos with their team for the Photo Album.
Pharmacy
Cianwood Gym: The fifth Gym. It specializes in Fighting-types.
Chuck's wife will give the player Chuck's Pokégear number when spoken to after the player has defeated the Elite Four.

Utility:
Surf is required to access Cianwood City the first time, as well as to travel to Route 41. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Rock Smash is required to access a Revive. Wild Pokémon and random items can also be found here with Rock Smash.

Pokémon

  • Wild Staryu have a 23% chance of holding Stardust and a 2% chance of holding a Star Piece.
  • Wild Shuckle have a 25% chance of holding a Berry.
