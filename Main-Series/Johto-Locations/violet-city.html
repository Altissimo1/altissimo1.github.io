Violet City

Access to:
West: Route 36
South: Route 32
East: Route 31
Accessible in the city: Sprout Tower

Places of interest:
Poké Mart
Earl's Pokémon Academy
Violet Gym: The first Gym. It specializes in Flying-types.

Utility:
Surf is required to access a PP Up and a Rare Candy. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Cut is required to access a Hyper Potion.
