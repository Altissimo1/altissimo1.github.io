Route 45

Access to:
North: Blackthorn City
South: Route 46
Accessible on the route: Dark Cave (Route 45 side)

Places of interest:
In Gold and Silver, Hiker Parry will call to inform the player of Marill swarms in Mt. Mortar if his number is registered in the Pokégear.

Utility:
Surf is required to access a PP Up. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Access to:
North: Blackthorn City
South: Route 46
Accessible on the route: Dark Cave (Route 45 side)

Places of interest:
Cameron appears here and can take photos on Wednesdays and Saturdays.

Utility:
Rock Climb is required to access a PP Max.
Surf can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Pokémon

  • In the Japanese and Korean versions of Gold and Silver, Teddiursa appears instead of Phanpy in Silver, and Phanpy appears instead of Teddiursa in Gold.
  • Wild Geodude and Graveler have a 2% chance of holding an Everstone.
  • Wild Dratini and Dragonair have a 2% chance of holding a Dragon Scale.
  • Wild Dratini, Dragonair, Phanpy, and Teddiursa can flee.

Pokémon

  • Wild Whismur have a 5% chance of holding a Chesto Berry.
  • Wild Linoone have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Sitrus Berry.
  • Wild Buizel have a 5% chance of holding a Wacan Berry.
  • Wild Geodude and Graveler have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
  • Wild Phanpy have a 5% chance of holding a Passho Berry.

Pokégear

In Crystal, the player can call Blackbelt Kenji in the afternoons if they have his number. Sometimes, he will ask for the player to visit him. If the player does so, he will give the player a PP Up.
Also in Crystal, Hiker Parry will sometimes offer an Iron after he is defeated in his second rematch.
