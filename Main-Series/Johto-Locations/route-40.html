Route 40

Access to:
North: Battle Tower (only in Crystal)
South: Route 41
East: Olivine City

Utility:
Surf is required to traverse the route and travel to Route 41. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Rock Smash is required to access a Hyper Potion. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Rock Smash.
