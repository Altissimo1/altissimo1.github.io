Pokémon

  • In Japanese Gold and Silver, the two encounter slots that have Jigglypuff (one 4% slot and one 1% slot, a total of 5%) are instead level 4 Rattata, putting Rattata's combined rate at 25% in the morning and day and 55% at night.
  • Wild Geodude have a 2% chance of holding an Everstone.
  • Wild Phanpy can flee.
