Route 41

Access to:
North: Route 40
West: Cianwood City
Accessible on the route: Whirl Islands

Utility:
Surf is required to access and traverse this route. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Whirlpool is required to access a hidden Max Ether and the Whirl Islands.
