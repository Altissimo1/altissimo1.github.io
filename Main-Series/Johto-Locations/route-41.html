Route 41

Access to:
North: Route 40
West: Cianwood City
Accessible on the route: Whirl Islands

Utility:
Surf is required to access and traverse this route. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Whirlpool is required to access a Max Ether and the Whirl Islands.

Access to:
North: Route 40
West: Cianwood City
Accessible on the route: Whirl Islands

Utility:
Surf is required to access and traverse this route. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Whirlpool is required to access a Max Ether, four Stardusts, and the Whirl Islands.

Pokémon

  • Wild Shellder have a 23% chance of holding a Pearl and a 2% chance of holding a Big Pearl.

Pokémon

  • Wild Chinchou and Lanturn have a 5% chance of holding a DeepSeaScale.
  • Wild Shellder have a 50% chance of holding a Pearl and a 5% chance of holding a Big Pearl.
