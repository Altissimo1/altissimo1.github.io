Utility:
Surf is required to collect a Mystic Water from a man on the island, as well as to access a Nugget by Surfing from Route 30. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Surf and Rock Climb are required to access a Nugget and the special Headbutt trees.
