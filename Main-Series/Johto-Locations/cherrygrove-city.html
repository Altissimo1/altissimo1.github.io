Cherrygrove City

Access to:
North: Route 30
East: Route 29

Places of interest:
Poké Mart

Utility:
Surf is required to collect a Mystic Water from an man on the island. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Access to:

East: Route 29

Places of interest:
Poké Mart

Utility:
