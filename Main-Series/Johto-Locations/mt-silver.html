Mt. Silver

The outside area is also known as Silver Cave.

Access to:
East: Route 28

Places of interest:
Pokémon Center

Utility:
Flash is recommended inside the mountain.
Cut is required to access TM47 Steel Wing and a Rare Candy on Route 28.
Surf is required to access a Full Restore outside the mountain. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Surf and Waterfall are required to access several rooms, their items, and the Pokémon within inside the mountain.

Access to:
East: Route 28

Places of interest:
Pokémon Center
Cameron appears outside and can take photos on Wednesdays and Fridays. He also appears inside on Thursdays.

Utility:
Cut is required to access TM47 Steel Wing, TM35 Flamethrower, and a Rare Candy on Route 28.
Surf is required to access a Reaper Cloth outside the mountain. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Surf and Waterfall are required to access several rooms, their items, and the Pokémon within, including Moltres.
Rock Climb is required to traverse most of the cave area.
Rock Smash is required to access a Max Potion, a Calcium, and an Iron. Random items can also be found here with Rock Smash.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon in the outside area and on the lower mountainside.

Pokémon

  • In the Japanese and Korean versions of Gold and Silver, Ursaring appears instead of Donphan in Silver, and Donphan appears instead of Ursaring in Gold.
  • Wild Sneasel have a 2% chance of holding a Quick Claw.
  • Wild Dodrio have a 2% chance of holding a Sharp Beak.
  • Wild Poliwhirl have a 2% chance of holding a King's Rock.
  • Wild Graveler have a 2% chance of holding an Everstone.
  • Wild Magmar have a 25% chance of holding a Burnt Berry.
  • Wild Misdreavus have a 2% chance of holding a Spell Tag.
  • Wild Parasect have a 23% chance of holding a Tinymushroom and a 2% chance of holding a Big Mushroom.
  • Wild Tangela and Quagsire can flee.
