Blackthorn City

Access to:
North: Ice Path
South: Route 45
Accessible in the city: Dragon's Den

Places of interest:
Move Deleter
Poké Mart
Blackthorn Gym: The eighth Gym. It specializes in Dragon-types.

Utility:
Surf is required to access the Dragon's Den. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Strength is required to navigate the Blackthorn Gym.

Move Tutor: A tutor here can teach Frenzy Plant, Blast Burn, or Hydro Cannon to fully-evolved starter Pokémon with maximum friendship.
