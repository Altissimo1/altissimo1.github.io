Move Deleter
Poké Mart
Blackthorn Gym: The eighth Gym. It specializes in Dragon-types.

Utility:
Surf is required to access the Dragon's Den. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Strength is required to navigate the Blackthorn Gym.

Access to:

Places of interest:
Move Deleter
Move Reminder: In exchange for a Heart Scale, the Move Reminder can teach a Pokémon a move that it was capable of learning at a lower level than its current level, but which is not currently in its moveset.
Move Tutor: An old lady in the western house can teach Draco Meteor to Dragon-type Pokémon with maximum friendship.
Move Tutor: An old man in the western house can teach Frenzy Plant, Blast Burn, or Hydro Cannon to fully-evolved starter Pokémon with maximum friendship.

Blackthorn Gym: The eighth Gym. It specializes in Dragon-types.
Cameron appears here and can take photos on Sundays and Tuesdays.
