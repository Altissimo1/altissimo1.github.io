Access to:
North: Route 35

Accessible in the city: Goldenrod Underground
Accessible in the city: Goldenrod Radio Tower
Accessible from the city: Saffron City (via the Magnet Train)

Places of interest:
Pokémon Communication Center: In the original cartridge release of Crystal in Japan, the GS Ball could be obtained here, the Egg Ticket could be exchanged for the Odd Egg, and online trading was possible. All of these features were moved or removed in subsequent releases.

Goldenrod Department Store Rooftop Lottery

The player can play the lottery multiple times per day. The prize has roughly a 55% chance of being a Berry, a 35% chance of being a Poké Ball, and a 10% chance of being a TM. Which TM or Poké Ball is received depends on the day of the week. Berries are pulled from a random pool regardless of the day.
