Goldenrod City

Access to:
North: Route 35
South: Route 34
Accessible in the city: Goldenrod Underground
Accessible in the city: Goldenrod Radio Tower
Accessible from the city: Saffron City (via the Magnet Train)

Places of interest:
Pokémon Communication Center: In the original cartridge release of Crystal in Japan, the GS Ball could be obtained here, the Egg Ticket could be exchanged for the Odd Egg, and online trading was possible. All of these features were moved or removed in subsequent releases.

Move Tutor: Bill's father appears outside the Game Corner every Wednesday and Saturday after defeating the Elite Four. He can teach one of Flamethrower, Ice Beam, and Thunderbolt every day that he is present for 4000 coins.
Goldenrod Game Corner
Goldenrod Gym: The third gym. It specializes in Normal-types.

Places of interest:
Global Terminal: The Global Terminal could be used to access online features, including trading Pokémon, viewing Battle Videos, or sending photos of in-game data. The official servers used to facilitate these trades have since been shut down, but fan servers exist.

Goldenrod Game Corner: In the Japanese versions of HeartGold and SoulSilver, the Game Corner contains slot machines similar to those used in previous games. In international releases, the slot machines are replaced with the Voltorb Flip game.
Goldenrod Gym: The third gym. It specializes in Normal-types.
Goldenrod Department Store
Whitney appears on Goldenrod Department Store 6F between 12:00-3:59 PM (12:00-15:59) after the player has defeated the Elite Four. She will give the player her Pokégear number when spoken to. After her Pokégear number is obtained, she will appear here between 6:00-8:59 PM (18:00-20:59), and speaking to her will enable the player to take a picture with her.

Goldenrod Department Store Rooftop Lottery

The player can play the lottery multiple times per day. The prize has roughly a 55% chance of being a Berry, a 35% chance of being a Poké Ball, and a 10% chance of being a TM. Which TM or Poké Ball is received depends on the day of the week. Berries are pulled from a random pool regardless of the day.
