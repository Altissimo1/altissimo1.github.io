Goldenrod Tunnel

Known as Goldenrod Underground in Gold, Silver, and Crystal.

Access to:
Either exit on 1F: Goldenrod City
Accessible in the area: Goldenrod Department Store basement

Places of interest:
Barber shop: On all days except Mondays, one of two brothers can give the player's Pokémon a haircut, increasing its friendship and Beauty.
Bargain shop
Herb shop

Access to:
Either exit on 1F: Goldenrod City
Accessible in the area: Goldenrod Department Store basement

Places of interest:
Barber shop: On all days except Mondays, one of two brothers can give the player's Pokémon a haircut, increasing its friendship.
Bargain shop
Herb shop
Photo booth: A photo booth at the north allows players to dress up their Pokémon using Accessories and photograph them, and allows for players to take photos of their team. A nearby photo gallery allows players to view their photos and those of other players with whom they have mixed records. A Hiker in the photo gallery will allow players to enter an Accessory drawing to win Accessories.
The camera here can be used to take a photo of the player character when they are dressed in the Team Rocket uniform.
