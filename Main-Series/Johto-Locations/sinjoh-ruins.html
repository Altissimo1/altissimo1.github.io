Sinjoh Ruins

Access to:
Entry: Ruins of Alph (only if the player enters the Ruins Research Center with an Arceus from an event or from the Hall of Origin as their first Pokémon)
A man with an Abra will teleport the player to New Bark Town when spoken to.

Pokémon

The player can only access the Sinjoh Ruins with an Arceus obtained in an event, or one obtained from the Hall of Origin. Upon entering, the player will be able to choose one of the following Pokémon to receive. After receiving it, if the player enters the Sinjoh Ruins with an Arceus from the other type of distribution (ex. the player's first visit was with an event Arceus and their second is with a Hall of Origin Arceus), they will be able to choose one of the Pokémon that they did not choose the first time.
As Arceus was never distributed through the Hall of Origin event, the only way to obtain two Pokémon here is by using cheats to capture the Hall of Origin Arceus.
