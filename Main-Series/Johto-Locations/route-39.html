East: Route 38

Utility:

Access to:
South: Olivine City
East: Route 38

Places of interest:
Cameron appears here and can take photos on Sundays and Thursdays.
After obtaining Lt. Surge's Pokégear number and completing the trade with him in Saffron City, Cameron can take photos of the player with him here on Fridays and Saturdays.

Utility:
Rock Climb is required on this route to access the special Headbutt tree on Route 38.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Pokémon

  • Wild Magnemite have a 2% chance of holding a Metal Coat.
  • Wild Miltank have a 25% chance of holding Moomoo Milk.
  • Wild Farfetch'd have a 2% chance of holding a Stick.
  • Wild Butterfree have a 2% chance of holding Silverpowder.
  • Wild Beedrill have a 2% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Magnemite can flee.

  • Wild Farfetch'd have a 5% chance of holding a Stick.
  • Wild Miltank always hold Moomoo Milk.
