Route 42

Access to:
West: Ecruteak City
East: Mahogany Town
Accessible on the route: Mt. Mortar

Places of interest:
In Crystal, Suicune appears in the grove with the Apricorn trees after seeing it in Cianwood City. Cut is required to trigger Suicune to run away.

Utility:
Surf is required to access a hidden Max Potion and a Super Potion, as well as to navigate the route without going through Mt. Mortar. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Surf and Cut are required to access the Apricorn trees and Suicune.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Access to:
West: Ecruteak City
East: Mahogany Town
Accessible on the route: Mt. Mortar

Places of interest:
Suicune appears in the grove with the Apricorn trees after seeing it in Cianwood City. Cut is required to trigger Suicune to run away.

Utility:
Surf is required to access a Max Potion and a Super Potion, as well as to navigate the route without going through Mt. Mortar. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
