Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl Home

Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl were originally released in 2021 for the Nintendo Switch. They are remakes of Diamond and Pearl. The original cartridges and digital downloads are able to trade and battle with each other.

Compatible Pokémon can be transferred into Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl from the Pokémon HOME application for the Nintendo Switch. Compatible Pokémon can be transferred into HOME from the Switch games Let's Go Pikachu & Let's Go Eevee, Sword & Shield, Legends: Arceus, and Scarlet & Violet, and from the mobile application Pokémon GO.

Using the Pokémon Bank application for the 3DS and connecting that application with Pokémon HOME, Pokémon from previous games can be transferred into Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl. Pokémon can be sent into Bank from Ruby, Sapphire, & Emerald, FireRed & LeafGreen, Colosseum, XD: Gale of Darkness, Diamond, Pearl, & Platinum, HeartGold & SoulSilver, Black & White, Black 2 & White 2, X & Y, Omega Ruby & Alpha Sapphire, Sun & Moon, Ultra Sun & Ultra Moon, Red, Green, Blue, & Yellow (for the 3DS Virtual Console), and Gold, Silver, & Crystal (for the 3DS Virtual Console), meaning any compatible Pokémon originating in one of these games can be ultimately transferred into Brilliant Diamond or Shining Pearl.

Except for Spinda, Pokémon can also be moved from Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl into Pokémon HOME, from where they can be accessed by Nintendo Switch games with which they are compatible. Let's Go Pikachu and Let's Go Eevee cannot receive Pokémon from Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl, however, even if the species is part of those games' Kanto Pokédex.

The first 493 Pokémon in the National Pokédex are obtainable in these games.

Pokémon HOME contains a Sinnoh Pokédex that consists of 150 Pokémon, corresponding with the Sinnoh Pokédex that can be completed in Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl. If the player is able to complete this Pokédex in Pokémon HOME (which requires depositing or trading Pokémon such that the player has had one of all 150 Pokémon in HOME at some point, with all 150 originating from Brilliant Diamond or Shining Pearl), they can receive a shiny Manaphy.
