How Many Pokémon Can You Get with Only Pokémon Shining Pearl?

Assuming you do not use any glitches, the answer is 433. Three Mythical Pokémon (Mew, Jirachi, and Arceus) can also be obtained if the player has access to Let's Go Pikachu/Eevee, Sword/Shield, and Legends: Arceus save data, respectively. The Professor Oak challenge guide for Brilliant Diamond can be found here, although I've made a shorter list of encounters below in case anything happens to the external guide.

The list of Pokémon you cannot get without trading, which equals 433 species, is as follows:

Trade Evolutions (16) - 16 total
Alakazam, Golem, Machamp, Gengar, Politoed, Slowking, Rhyperior, Kingdra, Scizor, Electivire, Magmortar, Porygon2, Porygon-Z, Dusknoir, Huntail, and Gorebyss evolve by trade. Without the ability to trade, they are unobtainable.
Version Exclusives (35) - 51 total
The following Pokémon cannot be obtained without the use of glitches or trading: Cranidos, Bastiodon, Murkrow, Honchkrow, Stunky, Skuntank, Dialga, Caterpie, Weedle, Beedrill, Ekans, Arbok, Growlithe, Arcanine, Seel, Dewgong, Elekid, Electabuzz, Scyther, Raikou, Entei, Suicune, Gligar, Gliscor, Kecleon, Ho-Oh, Seedot, Nuzleaf, Walrein, Mawile, Zangoose, Solrock, Larvitar, Shelgon, and Tyranitar.
Mythical/Event (9) - 60 total
Outside of glitches, Mew, Celebi, Jirachi, Deoxys, Phione, Manaphy, Darkrai, Shaymin, and Arceus are only obtainable via events. Mew, Jirachi, and Arceus can be obtained with additional save data on the same system, though.
