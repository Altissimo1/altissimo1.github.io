Battle Tower Boss Trainers

In Master Class, the last battle of every set of 7 is against a pre-determined Boss Trainer. Which Boss Trainer (or Trainers, in Doubles) the player faces is dependent on which Rank the player is on. Each Boss Trainer has three Pokémon rosters associated with them. Those rosters are shown below.

This page does not include data on the Regular Class battles against Palmer; those teams can be found on the Battle Tower page.
