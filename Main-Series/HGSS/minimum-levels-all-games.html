HeartGold and SoulSilver Minimum Levels (All Games)

This is a list of the lowest level at which you can obtain every Pokémon in HeartGold and SoulSilver. It does not contain levels attainable via glitches. However, it does assume you have access to all of Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald, FireRed, LeafGreen, Colosseum, XD, Diamond, Pearl, Platinum, HeartGold, SoulSilver, and the Pokéwalker.

This page was based off a spreadsheet sent to me by QuentinQuonce of Smogon. HeartGold and SoulSilver information has been collated with my existing HGSS Minimum Levels page, but Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald, Colosseum, XD, Diamond, Pearl, and Platinum information has not yet been fully compiled. I will further review this page for accuracy and add links when I add locations information for those games.

In the table, Pokémon with "Any Gen 4" listed as their game can be obtained in Diamond, Pearl, Platinum, HeartGold, or SoulSilver.
