HeartGold and SoulSilver Time Events

HeartGold and SoulSilver use the DS' internal clock to track the passage of time and days of the week. Several events in HeartGold and SoulSilver utilize the clock. Some events only occur on certain days of the week, some Pokémon and Trainers can only be battled at certain times of day, and some events are available only once a day, becoming available again after the clock passes midnight. This page provides information about the events that are tied to the clock.

Daily Events

These events require the start of a new day, but do not require a specific time of day or day of the week otherwise.

  • Apricorns can be collected once a day.
  • If Kurt is given an Apricorn to make a custom Poké Ball with, he will have it available for the player the following day.
  • The Lottery Corner in Goldenrod Radio Tower can be played once a day.
  • The Shuckle that the player can receive from Kirk can be returned to him the following day.
  • A Trainer can be battled in the Viridian City Trainer House once every day.
  • After defeating the Elite Four, the player can tune in to Professor Oak's Pokémon Talk to hear which Pokémon is currently Swarming. The species that is Swarming changes every day.
  • The old man on Route 16 will teach the player a new trendy phrase once a day.
  • The girl in the northeastern house in Olivine City will give the player three random Seals once per day.
  • At midnight, the counters on Safari Zone areas increase by 1 for the purposes of determining how long the area has been active.

All of the above events can be obtained repeatedly by setting the DS' clock to 11:59 PM (23:59) and allowing it to roll over to the next day. However, Safari Zone areas cannot be leveled up repeatedly this way, as the game will freeze the clock for the Safari Zone for 24 hours after the time is changed.

Time Events

These events require a specific time of day, but do not require a specific day of the week.

The three times of day that the game uses are Morning, Day, and Night. Morning lasts from 4:00 AM-9:59 AM (04:00-09:59), Day lasts from 10:00 AM-7:59 PM (10:00-19:59), and Night lasts from 8:00 PM-3:59 AM (20:00-03:59).

Many Pokémon can only be found during one of these three times, but I have not compiled the full list here as it's pretty extensive.

Morning

  • Eevee will evolve into Espeon if leveled up.
  • Budew will evolve into Roselia if leveled up.
  • Riolu will evolve into Lucario if leveled up.
  • Happiny will evolve into Chansey if leveled up with an Oval Stone.

Day

  • Eevee will evolve into Espeon if leveled up.
  • Budew will evolve into Roselia if leveled up.
  • Riolu will evolve into Lucario if leveled up.
  • Happiny will evolve into Chansey if leveled up with an Oval Stone.

Night

  • Eevee will evolve into Umbreon if leveled up.
  • Chingling will evolve into Chimecho if leveled up.
  • Sneasel will evolve into Weavile if leveled up with a Razor Claw.
  • Gligar will evolve into Gliscor if leveled up with a Razor Fang.
  • Policeman Keith and Policeman Dirk will battle the player.
  • The Spell Tag can only be obtained at night.

Other Specific Times

  • The Variety Channel on the radio, broadcast on channel 4 (lower-right station), plays Pokémon Search Party for an hour every three hours, starting at 12:00 AM (0:00), such that the hours beginning with 12, 3, 6, or 9 will have this program.
  • The Variety Channel will play Serial Radio Drama for an hour every three hours, starting at 1:00 AM (01:00), such that the hours beginning with 1, 4, 7, or 10 will have this program.
  • The Variety Channel will play Buena's Password for an hour every three hours, starting at 2:00 AM (02:00), such that the hours beginning with 2, 5, 8, or 11 will have this program.
  • Between 12:00 PM and 12:59 PM (12:00-22:59), Blackbelt Kenji can be called and may ask for the player to visit him, at which time he may rematch the player.
  • Daisy will massage the player's Pokémon between 3:00-3:59 PM (15:00-15:59).
  • After defeating the Elite Four, Whitney can be found on Goldenrod Department Store 6F between 12:00-3:59 PM (12:00-15:59) and will give the player her Pokégear number when spoken to.
  • After defeating the Elite Four, Jasmine can be found at the Olivine Café between 1:00-1:59 PM (13:00-13:59) and will give the player her Pokégear number when spoken to.
  • After defeating the Elite Four, Pryce can be found at the Lake of Rage between 6:00-9:59 AM (06:00-09:59) and will give the player his Pokégear number when spoken to.
  • After defeating her and Lance in a Multi Battle at the Dragon's Den, Clair can be found there between 6:00-9:59 AM (06:00-09:59) and will give the player her Pokégear number when spoken to.
  • After obtaining all 16 Badges, Brock can be found in Diglett's Cave between 12:00-2:59 PM (12:00-14:59) and will give the player his Pokégear number when spoken to.
  • After obtaining all 16 Badges, Misty can be found on Route 25 between 4:00-5:59 PM (16:00-17:59) and will give the player her Pokégear number when spoken to.
  • After obtaining all 16 Badges and catching Zapdos, Lt. Surge can be found on Route 10 between 9:00-11:59 AM (09:00-11:59) and will give the player his Pokégear number when spoken to, if the player is walking with a Pikachu.
  • After obtaining all 16 Badges, Janine can be found in the gate south of Victory Road between 4:00-5:59 PM (16:00-17:59) and will give the player her Pokégear number when spoken to.
  • After obtaining Whitney's Pokégear number, Cameron can take photos of the player with her on 6F of the Goldenrod Department Store between 6:00-8:59 PM (18:00-20:59).
  • After obtaining Jasmine's Pokégear number, Cameron can take photos of the player with her in Olivine Café between 12:00-12:59 PM (12:00-12:59).
  • After obtaining Clair's Pokégear number, Cameron can take photos of the player with her in the Dragon's Den between 4:00-6:59 PM (16:00-18:59).
  • After obtaining Brock's Pokégear number, Cameron can take photos of the player with him in Diglett's Cave between 5:00-7:59 PM (17:00-19:59).
  • After obtaining Misty's Pokégear number, Cameron can take photos of the player with her on Route 25 between 2:00-3:59 PM (14:00-15:59), so long as the player has a Water-type as their first Pokémon.
  • After obtaining Janine's Pokégear number, Cameron can take photos of the player with her in the gate south of Victory Road between 6:00-7:59 PM (18:00-19:59).
