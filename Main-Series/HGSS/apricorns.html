HeartGold and SoulSilver Apricorn Locations

The following tables contain the location(s) of all Apricorns in HeartGold and SoulSilver.

Pokéathlon Dome

Apricorns can be purchased at the Pokéathlon Dome for 200P apiece. The table below gives the days of the week that each Apricorn is available. All Apricorns that are sold here can be purchased before obtaining the National Dex.

Item Days
Red Apricorn Sunday, Monday, Friday
Blu Apricorn Sunday, Monday, Wednesday
Blk Apricorn Sunday, Wednesday, Saturday
Grn Apricorn Monday, Friday, Saturday
Ylw Apricorn Tuesday, Thursday, Friday
Pnk Apricorn Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
Wht Apricorn Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday

In the Overworld

The Apricorn Box, which is required to carry Apricorns, is obtained from the man in the southern house on Route 30.

All Apricorns regenerate daily.
