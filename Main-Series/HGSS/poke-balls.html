HeartGold and SoulSilver Poké Ball Locations

The following tables contain the location(s) of all Poké Ball types in HeartGold and SoulSilver.

Poké Marts

Poké Balls can only be purchased at the Cherrygrove Poké Mart after first receiving them from Ethan or Lyra on Route 29.

All Poké Marts (that is, locations actually called 'Poké Mart', not just shops) sell Poké Balls, Great Balls, and Ultra Balls. Great Balls are only sold after obtaining 3 Badges, and Ultra Balls after 5 Badges. Buying ten or more Poké Balls at a time will also reward the player with a Premier Ball.

In addition to the normal Poké Balls sold by these Marts, many Poké Marts also have additional types of Poké Balls sold by the upper vendor in the Mart. These types are listed below.

Pickup

Pokémon with the Pickup ability can find the following items:

  • Great Ball: 10% chance between levels 1-30, 30% chance between levels 31-40
  • Ultra Ball: 4% chance between levels 1-20, 10% chance between levels 21-80, 30% chance between levels 81-90

Fisherman Wilton

Fisherman Wilton on Route 44 may call to offer a Poké Ball, Great Ball, or Ultra Ball if his number is registered in the Pokégear.
