How Many Pokémon Can You Get Without Evolving or Breeding?

In Mystery Dungeon, I often strive to recruit as many Pokémon as possible at the evolutionary stage I want them at, no matter their spawn rate, and evolve only the ones I can't get otherwise. These pages outline what Pokémon can be obtained with a similar approach to HeartGold and SoulSilver.

Completing the Pokédex (Using All Gen 4 Games)

The four Regis (Regirock, Regice, Registeel, and Regigigas) either require transfer from Ruby, Sapphire, or Emerald, or the support of event distributions. All other Pokémon can be obtained in the five Gen 4 games.
