HeartGold and SoulSilver Photography

In various areas throughout the game, the player can find Cameron the photographer on certain days of the week. Upon speaking with him, he will offer to take a photo of the player and their Pokémon. These photos, along with the date they were taken, are saved in the player's Photo Album and can be viewed on their PC.

Additionally, a handful of NPCs will offer to take a photo with the player when spoken to, and Cameron will appear and take the photo if the player agrees.

This page lists all locations and days where Cameron or an NPC that can take photos can be found.
