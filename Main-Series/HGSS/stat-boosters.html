HeartGold and SoulSilver Stat Booster Locations

The following lists and table contain the locationn(s) of all Stat Boosters (HP Up, PP Up, PP Max, Rare Candy, Protein, Carbos, Calcium, Iron, Zinc) in HeartGold and SoulSilver.

Pokégear

Several Trainers may offer a stat-boosting item after the player defeats them in a rematch. These trainers are:

Trainer Item
Picnicker Erin Calcium
Youngster Joey HP Up
Sailor Huey Protein
Bird Keeper Vance Carbos
Black Belt Kenji PP Up
Hiker Parry Iron

Pickup

Pokémon with the Pickup ability can find the following items:

  • Rare Candy: 4% chance between levels 21-40, 10% chance between levels 41-100
  • PP Up: 4% chance between levels 81-100

Goldenrod and Celadon Department Stores & Safari Zone Gate

Some stat boosters can be purchased from the vendor on 4F in Goldenrod Department Store, from the right vendor on 5F in Celadon Department Store, and from the southeastern shop at the Safari Zone Gate.

Item Price
HP Up 9800
Protein 9800
Iron 9800
Calcium 9800
Zinc 9800
Carbos 9800

Pokéathlon Dome

Some stat boosters can be purchased at the Pokéathlon Dome. The table below gives the days of the week and the price. All stat boosters that are sold here can be purchased before obtaining the National Dex.

Item Price Days
Rare Candy 2000P Monday, Saturday
PP Up 1000P Tuesday, Thursday

Buena's Password

Some stat boosters can be obtained at the Goldenrod Radio Tower by reaching specific point totals on the Blue Card. The list is given below.

  • Protein: 5 Points
  • Iron: 10 Points
  • Carbos: 15 Points
  • Rare Candy: 25 Points
  • HP Up: 30 Points

Pokéwalker

Some stat boosters can be found in Pokéwalker courses. The list is given below.

  • PP Up - Rugged Road, Suburban Area, Stormy Beach
  • Calcium - Treehouse
  • Iron - Scary Cave
  • Rare Candy - Sinnoh Field, Sightseeing

Battle Frontier

Some stat boosters can be purchased from the left vendor at the Battle Frontier in exchange for Battle Points. The stat boosters are part of the group "Training Items #1".

Item Price

Calcium 1BP
Iron 1BP
