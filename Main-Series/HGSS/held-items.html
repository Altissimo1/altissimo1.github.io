HeartGold and SoulSilver Held Item Locations

The following lists and table contain the location of all items that can have an effect on Pokémon when held, except for Berries (their locations can be found here. This page does not include Mail locations.

Pickup

Pokémon with the Pickup ability can find the following items:

  • King's Rock: 1% chance between levels 11-30
  • Iron Ball: 1% chance between levels 41-60
  • Leftovers: 1% chance between levels 81-100

S.S. Aqua Captain

After the first voyage on the S.S. Aqua, speaking with the captain on subsequent voyages will give the player one of the 16 elemental Plates that they do not already have.

In-Game Trade

The Machop that the player can receive in a trade in Goldenrod Department Store holds a Macho Brace.
The Steelix that the player can receive in a trade in Olivine Gym holds a Soothe Bell.
The Dodrio that the player can receive in a trade in Blackthorn City holds a Smoke Ball.
The Magneton that the player can receive in a trade in the Power Plant holds a Metal Coat.
