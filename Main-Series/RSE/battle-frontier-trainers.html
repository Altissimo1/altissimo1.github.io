Battle Frontier Trainers

In the Battle Frontier, enemy Trainers - their Trainer class and name - are pulled from a pre-existing list of 300 Trainers. Every Trainer in this list has a number, an IV value, and a Pokémon roster associated with them. The number determines which sets of 7 battles the player may face this Trainer as an opponent in; lower numbers will appear in earlier battles than higher numbers. The IV value will determine what IVs the Pokémon belonging to this Trainer can possess. The enemy Trainer's three Pokémon will be a random selection of Pokémon from their roster, although they will never use two Pokémon of the same species or two Pokémon with the same held item.

  • 1-100
  • 101-120
  • 121-140
  • 141-160
  • 161-180
  • 181-200
  • 201-220
  • 221-300

The numbers after the Pokémon's names refer to set numbers, explained in more detail on the Battle Tower Pokémon page.

Battle Tower Group Mechanics

Trainer groups each have a specific IV value associated with them. Pokémon that belong to a Trainer will receive the IVs of the Trainer's group. The IV values of the groups are given below:

  • 1-100: 3IV
  • 101-120: 6IV
  • 121-140: 9IV
  • 141-160: 12IV
  • 161-180: 15IV
  • 181-200: 18IV
  • 201-220: 21IV
  • 221-300: 31IV

Due to a bug, all Trainers except Tucker in the Battle Dome will always have 3IV.
