Ruby, Sapphire, and Emerald Berry Locations

The following tables contain the location(s) of all Berries in Ruby, Sapphire, and Emerald.

Wild Pokémon

Some wild Pokémon can hold Berries. If the player captures the Pokémon, or uses Thief, Covet, Trick, or Switcheroo, they can obtain the item. The list of wild Pokémon with held Berries is as follows:

  • Wild Zigzagoon have a 5% chance of holding an Oran Berry.
  • Wild Linoone have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Sitrus Berry.
  • Wild Pikachu have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry.
  • Wild Shuckle always hold an Oran Berry.
  • Wild Poochyena and Mightyena have a 5% chance of holding a Pecha Berry.
  • Wild Numel and Vulpix always hold a Rawst Berry.
  • Wild Spinda, Whismur, and Loudred have a 5% chance of holding a Chesto Berry.
  • Wild Skitty have a 5% chance of holding a Leppa Berry.
  • Wild Kecleon and Girafarig have a 5% chance of holding a Persim Berry.

Altering Cave

The wild Pokémon that can appear in Altering Cave are specifically coded to have a 5% chance of holding rare Berries, regardless of what their regular encounter data is. Their held Berries are as follows:

  • Wild Mareep have a 5% chance of holding a Ganlon Berry.
  • Wild Pineco have a 5% chance of holding a Apicot Berry.
  • Wild Teddiursa and Stantler have a 5% chance of holding a Petaya Berry.
  • Wild Smeargle have a 5% chance of holding a Salac Berry.

People in the overworld

Random people in some areas of the overworld will give away Berries when spoken to. Most of them will give away Berries once a day. Their locations, and possible Berries, are listed below.

  • The easternmost girl in the Pretty Petal shop can give away one Cheri, Chesto, Pecha, Rawst, Aspear, Leppa, Oran, or Persim Berry.
  • A man south of Lanette's house on Route 114 can give away one Razz, Bluk, Nanab, Wepear, or Pinap Berry.
  • The Berry Master on Route 123 can give away a random two of Pomeg, Kelpsy, Qualot, Hondew, Grepa, Tamato, Cornn, Magost, Rabuta, or Nomel Berries.
  • The Berry Master's wife on Route 123 can give away a Cheri, Chesto, Pecha, Rawst, Aspear, Leppa, Oran, Persim, Lum, or Sitrus Berry for any random phrase. For special phrases, she will give away special Berries one time only: Spelon for GREAT BATTLE, Pamtre for CHALLENGE CONTEST, Watmel for OVERWHELMING LATIAS, Durin for COOL LATIOS, and Belue for SUPER HUSTLE.
  • A Beauty on the southwest part of Route 120 will give away a Berry depending on the player's Trainer ID. If it ends in 0 or 5, the player gets a Figy Berry; 1 or 6 gets Wiki, 2 or 7 gets Mago, 3 or 8 gets Aguav, and 4 or 9 gets Iapapa.
  • A man in the northeast part of Lilycove City can give away one Cheri, Chesto, Pecha, Rawst, Aspear, Leppa, Oran, Persim, Lum, or Sitrus Berry.
  • Kiri in the southwest part of Sootopolis City can give away one Pomeg, Kelpsy, Qualot, Hondew, Grepa, Tamato, Cornn, Magost, Rabuta, or Nomel Berry, and she will also give away a second Berry, which will always be a Figy or Iapapa Berry.

In-Game Trade

In Emerald, the Seedot that the player can receive in a trade in Rustboro City holds a Chesto Berry.
