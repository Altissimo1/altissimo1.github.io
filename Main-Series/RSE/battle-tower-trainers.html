Battle Tower Trainers

In the Battle Tower, enemy Trainers - their Trainer class and name - are pulled from a pre-existing list of 100 Trainers. Every Trainer in this list has a number, an IV value, and a Pokémon roster associated with them. The number determines which sets of 7 battles the player may face this Trainer as an opponent in; lower numbers will appear in earlier battles than higher numbers. The IV value will determine what IVs the Pokémon belonging to this Trainer can possess. The enemy Trainer's three Pokémon will be a random selection of Pokémon from their roster, although they will never use two Pokémon of the same species or two Pokémon with the same held item.

The same 100 Trainers are used for both Level 50 and Level 100 Modes, and have the same IVs and battle appearances in both modes. Their rosters, however, are different.

  • Level 50: 1-10
  • Level 50: 11-20
  • Level 50: 21-30
  • Level 50: 31-40
  • Level 50: 41-50
  • Level 50: 51-60
  • Level 50: 61-70
  • Level 50: 71-80
  • Level 50: 81-100
  • Level 100: 1-10
  • Level 100: 11-20
  • Level 100: 21-30
  • Level 100: 31-40
  • Level 100: 41-50
  • Level 100: 51-60
  • Level 100: 61-70
  • Level 100: 71-80
  • Level 100: 81-100

The numbers after the Pokémon's names refer to set numbers, explained in more detail on the Battle Tower Pokémon page.

Battle Tower Group Mechanics

Trainer groups each have a specific IV value associated with them. Pokémon that belong to a Trainer will receive the IVs of the Trainer's group. The IV values of the groups are given below:

  • 1-20: 6IV
  • 21-30: 9IV
  • 31-40: 12IV
  • 41-50: 15IV
  • 51-60: 18IV
  • 61-70: 21IV
  • 71-100: 31IV

The battles in which each trainer group can appear are given below.

Battle(s) Trainer Group(s)
