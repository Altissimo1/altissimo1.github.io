Battle Tent (Fallarbor)

The Fallarbor Battle Tent, located in Fallarbor Town, is a battle facility accessible only in Emerald. This facility is made to resemble the Battle Arena in the Battle Frontier, but in this facility, the player only needs to win three battles, and all the opponents use only Pokémon in the Hoenn Pokédex.

In this facility, neither the player nor the opponent is allowed to switch Pokémon, and Pokémon will always be sent out in the order they are listed in the party. The two Pokémon on the field will engage in three turns' worth of combat. At the end of three turns, if neither Pokémon has fainted, the game will judge which Pokémon performed better using criteria listed below. The winning Pokémon stays on the field for the next round, and the losing Pokémon faints.

If either Pokémon faints before three turns are up, the next Pokémon will be sent out, and the next round begins.

If the player's highest-leveled Pokémon is 30 or lower, the opposing Pokémon will always be level 30. If the player's highest-leveled Pokémon is higher than 30, the opposing Pokémon will match the player's highest-leveled Pokémon.
