Battle Tower (Battle Frontier)

The Battle Tower is one of seven facilities located in the Battle Frontier, accessible only in Emerald. The Frontier Brain here is Anabel.

Rewards

The player can get the following rewards for victories here. The two Shield items can be used as Secret Base decorations.

Battle Tower Rewards
Wins Required Reward Notes
50 Silver Shield Obtained from Scott.
56 Winning Ribbon Only in Level 50 mode. The Winning Ribbon is only given to the Pokémon entered into the Battle Tower.
56 Victory Ribbon Only in Open Level mode. The Victory Ribbon is only given to the Pokémon entered into the Battle Tower.
100 Gold Shield Obtained from Scott.

Modes

The Battle Tower has options for Single Battles and Double Battles. Unique to the Battle Tower is the mode of Multi Battles. Multi Battles can be conducted with either an AI-controlled partner Trainer or a second player with their own copy of Emerald.

The player can enter three Pokémon in Singles, four in Doubles, and two in Multi (with the AI partner or the other player providing another two).

Mechanics

Mechanics that apply to every Facility are on the Battle Frontier page. Here are mechanics specific to the Battle Tower:

  • Two Pokémon of the same species cannot be entered. The exception is in Multi Battles, where Trainers from this list may have a chance of using the same species as you. Trainers that are based off friends you have mixed records with don't seem to be capable of using the same species as you, though.
  • Two Pokémon holding the same item cannot be entered, except in Multi Battles, where they must be selected by different Trainers. Even then, using two of the same item is not allowed for two real-life players linking their copies of Emerald.
  • Items cannot be used during battle.
  • The player's Pokémon will be automatically healed after every battle.
  • Battles are conducted in rounds of 7. The player can save between battles in a round, but will not be able to do anything except complete the rest of the round when they load the game. The player can do anything between rounds without it affecting their streak.
  • Items used or swapped (via Trick, Thief, Covet, or Knock Off) during the battle will be given back to the Pokémon after the battle.

Frontier Brain

The Frontier Brain, Anabel, appears first after 34 battles and again after 69. After defeating her the second time, she will appear every 35 battles with her Gold Symbol team. Battle Points

In Singles, the player will receive 1 Point for winning the first round of seven battles, 2 for the second round, etc. to a maximum of 15 Points per round. An additional 10 Points are added every time the player faces and defeats Anabel.

In Doubles, the player will receive 2 Points for winning the first round of seven battles, 3 for the second round, etc. to a maximum of 15 Points per round.

In Multis, the player(s) will receive 3 Points for winning the first round of seven battles, 4 for the second round, etc. to a maximum of 15 Points per round.
