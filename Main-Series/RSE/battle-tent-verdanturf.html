Battle Tent (Verdanturf)

The Verdanturf Battle Tent, located in Verdanturf Town, is a battle facility accessible only in Emerald. This facility is made to resemble the Battle Palace in the Battle Frontier, but in this facility, the player only needs to win three battles, and all the opponents use only Pokémon in the Hoenn Pokédex.

In this facility, the only action the player can make during a battle is switching their Pokémon. If the player chooses to attack, their Pokémon will decide which move to use based on their nature and current HP, or may choose not to use a move. The same logic applies to opposing Pokémon's move selection. More details can be found on the Battle Palace page itself.

If the player's highest-leveled Pokémon is 30 or lower, the opposing Pokémon will always be level 30. If the player's highest-leveled Pokémon is higher than 30, the opposing Pokémon will match the player's highest-leveled Pokémon.
