Ruby, Sapphire, and Emerald Held Item Locations

The following lists and table contain the location of all items that can have an effect on a Pokémon when held, including Berries. This page does not include Mail locations, nor locations of Berries that have no held effect.

Pickup

In Ruby and Sapphire, Pokémon with the Pickup ability have a 1% chance of picking up King's Rocks.

In Emerald, the following items can be obtained at the listed levels:

  • King's Rock: 1% between levels 11-30
  • White Herb: 1% between levels 41-60
  • Leftovers: 1% between levels 81-100

Battle Tower

In Ruby and Sapphire's Battle Tower, the player can receive a Leftovers, White Herb, Quick Claw, Mental Herb, Brightpowder, Choice Band, King's Rock, Focus Band, or Scope Lens as a reward for completing a set of 7 battles, so long as the player's total streak is 42 wins or more.

Battle Frontier

In Emerald, several held items can be purchased at the rightmost counter of the Exchange Service Corner in the Battle Frontier. These items are purchased with Battle Points (BP).

Wild Pokémon

Some wild Pokémon can have held items. If the player captures the Pokémon, or uses Thief, Covet, or Trick, they can obtain the item. The list of wild Pokémon with held items or Berries is as follows:

  • Wild Zigzagoon have a 5% chance of holding an Oran Berry.
  • Wild Linoone have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Sitrus Berry.
  • Wild Pikachu have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Light Ball.
  • Wild Shuckle always hold an Oran Berry.
  • Wild Sandshrew have a 5% chance of holding a Quick Claw.
  • Wild Trapinch have a 5% chance of holding Soft Sand.
  • Wild Cacnea and Roselia have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Geodude and Graveler have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
  • Wild Poochyena and Mightyena have a 5% chance of holding a Pecha Berry.
  • Wild Numel and Vulpix always hold a Rawst Berry.
  • Wild Spinda, Whismur, and Loudred have a 5% chance of holding a Chesto Berry.
  • Wild Skitty have a 5% chance of holding a Leppa Berry.
  • Wild Abra have a 5% chance of holding a Twistedspoon.
  • Wild Kecleon and Girafarig have a 5% chance of holding a Persim Berry.
  • Wild Shuppet, Banette, Duskull, and Dusclops have a 5% chance of holding a Spell Tag.
  • Wild Horsea and Bagon have a 5% chance of holding a Dragon Scale.
  • Wild Aron and Lairon have a 5% chance of holding a Hard Stone.
  • Wild Magnemite and Magneton have a 5% chance of holding a Metal Coat.
  • Wild Koffing have a 5% chance of holding a Smoke Ball.
  • Wild Ditto have a 5% chance of holding Metal Powder.
  • Wild Doduo and Dodrio have a 5% chance of holding a Sharp Beak.
  • Wild Hariyama have a 5% chance of holding a King's Rock.

The Latias or Latios that can be found at the event-only Southern Island always holds Soul Dew.

Altering Cave

The wild Pokémon that can appear in Altering Cave are specifically coded to have a 5% chance of holding rare Berries, regardless of what their regular encounter data is. Their held Berries are as follows:

  • Wild Mareep have a 5% chance of holding a Ganlon Berry.
  • Wild Pineco have a 5% chance of holding a Apicot Berry.
  • Wild Teddiursa and Stantler have a 5% chance of holding a Petaya Berry.
  • Wild Aipom and Shuckle have a 5% chance of holding Berry Juice.
  • Wild Smeargle have a 5% chance of holding a Salac Berry.

If the player's Pokémon uses Thief, Covet, or Trick on the enemy Pokémon, and in the case of Thief and Covet does not have a held item, the user Pokémon will be able to obtain the enemy Pokémon's held item permanently. Berries

Random people in some areas of the overworld will give away Berries when spoken to. Most of them will give away Berries once a day. Their locations, and possible Berries, are listed below.

  • The easternmost girl in the Pretty Petal shop can give away one Cheri, Chesto, Pecha, Rawst, Aspear, Leppa, Oran, or Persim Berry.
  • The Berry Master's wife on Route 123 can give away a Cheri, Chesto, Pecha, Rawst, Aspear, Leppa, Oran, Persim, Lum, or Sitrus Berry for any random phrase.
  • A Beauty on the southwest part of Route 120 will give away a Berry depending on the player's Trainer ID. If it ends in 0 or 5, the player gets a Figy Berry; 1 or 6 gets Wiki, 2 or 7 gets Mago, 3 or 8 gets Aguav, and 4 or 9 gets Iapapa.
  • A man in the northeast part of Lilycove City can give away one Cheri, Chesto, Pecha, Rawst, Aspear, Leppa, Oran, Persim, Lum, or Sitrus Berry.
  • Kiri in the southwest part of Sootopolis City will give away two Berries, the second of which will always be a Figy or Iapapa Berry.
