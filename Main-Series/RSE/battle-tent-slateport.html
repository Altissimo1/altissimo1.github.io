Battle Tent (Slateport)

The Slateport Battle Tent, located in Slateport City, is a battle facility accessible only in Emerald. This facility is made to resemble the Battle Factory in the Battle Frontier, but in this facility, the player only needs to win three battles, and all the opponents use only Pokémon in the Hoenn Pokédex.

In this facility, before the player faces any Trainers, they will be provided a draft of six Pokémon, taken from the list below. Genders and abilities for these Pokémon are random. The player must select their Pokémon from the list of six. After every battle, the player will be given the option to trade one of their Pokémon for one Pokémon they just faced in the previous battle.

All Pokémon are level 30.
