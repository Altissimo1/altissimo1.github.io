The following tables contain the location(s) of all Poké Ball types in Ruby, Sapphire, and Emerald.

At any Poké Mart that sells Poké Balls, the player can get a Premier Ball for buying ten or more Poké Balls at one time.

Pickup

In Ruby and Sapphire, Pokémon with Pickup have a 10% chance of finding Ultra Balls. In Emerald, Pokémon with Pickup can find the following items:

  • Great Ball: 10% chance between levels 1-30, 30% chance between levels 31-40
  • Ultra Ball: 4% chance between levels 1-20, 10% chance between levels 21-80, 30% chance between levels 81-90
