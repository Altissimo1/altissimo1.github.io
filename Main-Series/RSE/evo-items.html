Ruby, Sapphire, and Emerald Evo Item Locations

The following lists and table contain the location of all evolutionary items, breeding items (incense), and the Everstone, in Ruby, Sapphire, and Emerald.

Treasure Hunter

The Treasure Hunter on Route 124 will trade the player an evolutionary stone in exchange for a colored Shard.

  • Red Shard for Fire Stone
  • Yellow Shard for Thunderstone
  • Blue Shard for Water Stone
  • Green Shard for Leaf Stone

Shards themselves can be found in the following locations:

  • Red Shard: Route 124, Route 127 underwater, held by wild Corsola (5%)
  • Yellow Shard: Route 124, Route 126 underwater, held by wild Chinchou (5%)
  • Blue Shard: Route 124, Route 126 underwater, held by wild Clamperl (5%)
  • Green Shard: Route 124 underwater, Route 126, held by wild Relicanth (5%)

Battle Tower

In the Ruby and Sapphire Battle Tower, a King's Rock is one of the rewards that the player can obtain for finishing a set of 7 battles when the player's total streak is 42 wins or higher.

Battle Frontier

In the Emerald Battle Frontier, King's Rocks can be purchased at the rightmost counter of the Exchange Service Corner in exchange for 64 BP.

Wild Pokémon

Some wild Pokémon can hold evolution items. The item can be obtained by capturing the Pokémon or using Thief, Covet, or Trick.

  • Wild Geodude and Graveler have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
  • Wild Horsea and Bagon have a 5% chance of holding a Dragon Scale.
  • Wild Magnemite and Magneton have a 5% chance of holding a Metal Coat.
  • Wild Solrock have a 5% chance of holding a Sun Stone.
  • Wild Lunatone have a 5% chance of holding a Moon Stone.
  • Wild Hariyama have a 5% chance of holding a King's Rock.

Pickup

Pokémon with the Pickup ability have a 1% chance of finding a King's Rock. In Emerald, the Pokémon with Pickup can only find King's Rocks if it is between levels 11 and 30.
