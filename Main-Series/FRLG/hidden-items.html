FireRed and LeafGreen Hidden Items

The following tables contain every hidden item and hidden Berry in FireRed and LeafGreen and its location.

Hidden Items

A Lava Cookie can be obtained near the hidden truck east of the S.S. Anne. The area requires Surf to get to. The player can get to this area by one of two methods: 1) trading a Pokémon with Cut from another game, and never collecting HM01 Cut on the S.S. Anne until after acquiring Surf, or 2) losing a battle on the S.S. Anne after acquiring HM01 Cut, and then not returning until after acquiring Surf.
