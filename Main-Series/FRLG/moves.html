FireRed and LeafGreen Move Locations

This page contains every TM, Move Tutor, and Move Relearner location in FireRed and LeafGreen.
All TMs, Move Tutors, and the Move Relearner have the same locations in all versions of FireRed and LeafGreen.

Move Relearner

The Move Relearner is located on Two Island. He can make a Pokémon remember a single move from its level-up movepool in exchange for either two Tinymushrooms or one Big Mushroom. Mushrooms can be found in the following locations:

  • Three Tinymushrooms and three Big Mushrooms can be found as hidden items on B1F of Mt. Moon. They may regenerate after the player takes 1500 steps.
  • The Nidoran♂ or Nidoran♀ the player can obtain in a trade in the Route 5-6 Underground Path holds a Tinymushroom.
  • A hidden Big Mushroom can be found on 5F of Pokémon Tower.
  • Wild Paras and Parasect have a 50% chance of holding a Tinymushroom and a 5% chance of holding a Big Mushroom.
