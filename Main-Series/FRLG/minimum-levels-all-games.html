FireRed and LeafGreen Minimum Levels (All Games)

This is a list of the lowest level at which you can obtain every Pokémon in FireRed and LeafGreen. It does not contain levels attainable via glitches. However, it does assume you have access to all of Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald, FireRed, LeafGreen, Colosseum, and XD.

This page was based off a spreadsheet sent to me by QuentinQuonce of Smogon. FireRed and LeafGreen information has been collated with my existing FRLG Minimum Levels page, but Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald, Colosseum, and XD information has not yet been fully compile. I will further review this page for accuracy and add links when I add locations information for those games.
