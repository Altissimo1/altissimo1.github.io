How Many Pokémon Can You Get Without Evolving or Breeding in Pokémon LeafGreen?

LeafGreen has two separate Pokédexes to complete: the Kanto Pokédex and the National Pokédex. The Kanto Pokédex corresponds to the Dex in the original Red, Green, Blue, and Yellow games (Pokémon 1-150 plus Mew), while the National Pokédex consists of the Kanto Pokédex plus the Pokémon introduced in Gold, Silver, & Crystal and Ruby, Sapphire, & Emerald (Pokémon 152-386). All Pokémon from the Kanto Pokédex, except for the event-exclusive Mew, are available between both FireRed and LeafGreen. Only a select few Pokémon from the National Pokédex are available in FireRed and LeafGreen. This page will cover both the Kanto and National Pokédexes.

The player can get up to 125 species total in the Kanto Pokédex and 171 in the National Pokédex in LeafGreen without trading, as described here. Without evolving or breeding any Pokémon, and only collecting evolutionary stages that the player can get in the wild, as a gift or purchase, or from an in-game trade, the player can get up to 100 species in the Kanto Pokédex and 122 in the National Pokédex, or 125 if they are playing the Switch re-release. The Pokémon unavailable without evolving or breeding are as follows:

Kanto Pokédex

Starter Evolutions (2) - 2 total
The starters are only available in their base forms, leaving their evolutions inaccessible.
Stone Evolutions (11) - 13 total
Raichu, Nidoqueen, Nidoking, Clefable, Ninetales, Wigglytuff, Poliwrath, Victreebel, Exeggutor, Starmie, and Eevee's evolutions are inaccessible.
Two Eevee evolutions (2) - 15 total
Without the ability to breed, the other two evolutions of Eevee are not accessible.
One of Hitmonlee/Hitmonchan (1) - 16 total
Without the ability to breed, the other of Hitmonlee or Hitmonchan is not accessible.
Fossils (1) - 17 total
The Fossils can only be revived into their base forms, leaving their evolutions inaccessible.
Other (8) - 25 total
Butterfree, Beedrill, Pidgeot, Dodrio, Weezing, Rhydon, Seadra, and Dragonite are inaccessible.

National Pokédex

Evolving from Kanto Pokémon (2) - 27 total
Crobat and Blissey evolve from Kanto Pokémon and are inaccessible.
Breeding from Kanto Pokémon (7) - 34 total
Pichu, Cleffa, Igglybuff, Tyrogue, Smoochum, and Magby must be bred from Kanto Pokémon and are inaccessible. Hitmontop evolves from Tyrogue and is also inaccessible.
Breeding from National Pokédex Pokémon (2) - 36 total
Wynaut must be bred from Wobbuffet, and Azurill from Marill or Azumarill, and is inaccessible.
Other (13) - 49 total
Furret, Ledian, Ariados, Togetic, Xatu, Azumarill, Skiploom, Jumpluff, Piloswine, Octillery, Donphan, Pupitar, and Tyranitar are inaccessible.
