Snowpoint Temple

Access to:
Exit on 1F: Snowpoint City

Utility:
In Diamond and Pearl, Strength is required to access a NeverMeltIce. In Platinum, Strength is required to access a Full Heal.

Access to:
Exit on 1F: Snowpoint City

Utility:
Strength is required to access a Never-Melt Ice.

Pokémon

  • Wild Sneasel have a 50% chance of holding a Grip Claw and a 5% chance of holding a Quick Claw.
  • Wild Graveler have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
  • Wild Steelix have a 5% chance of holding a Metal Coat.
  • Wild Smoochum and Jynx always hold an Aspear Berry.
