Mt. Coronet

Access to:
Western exit on 1F (south): Route 207
Western exit on 1F (north): Route 216
Western & eastern exits on 1F (north): Route 211
Eastern exit on 1F (south): Route 208
Accessible on 1F (north): Iceberg Ruins (Platinum only)
Summit: Spear Pillar

Places of interest:
Magneton will evolve into Magnezone and Nosepass will evolve into Probopass here due to the magnetic field.

Utility:
Surf, Strength, Defog, Rock Smash, Rock Climb, and Waterfall are required to fully navigate the area.
Surf can be used here to encounter wild Pokémon.

Feebas Tiles

Four of the water tiles on the lake on B1F will contain Feebas. The four tiles are randomly selected at the beginning of each day. This calculator can help identify the tiles they are on.

When fishing using any Rod on a 'Feebas tile', there is a 50% chance to encounter Feebas and a 50% chance that the encounter is taken from the normal encounter table, effectively halving all normal encounter rates.
