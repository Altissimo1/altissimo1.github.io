Gift

All Fossils can be revived at the Oreburgh Museum. However, Skull Fossils can only be found naturally in Diamond, while Armor Fossils can only be found naturally in Pearl. In Platinum, the player can get Skull Fossils if their Trainer ID is odd, and Armor Fossils if it is even. The other five Fossils can be obtained in all games after obtaining the National Pokédex.
