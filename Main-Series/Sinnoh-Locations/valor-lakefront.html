Valor Lakefront

Access to:
North: Route 214
West: Lake Valor
South: Route 213
East: Route 222

Places of interest:
Hotel Grand Lake: A Game Freak employee in the northwesternmost villa will give the player a certificate if they have seen all Sinnoh Pokédex Pokémon (excluding Manaphy in Diamond and Pearl only), and another one if they have 482 National Pokédex Pokémon - all of them excluding Mew, Ho-Oh, Lugia, Celebi, Jirachi, Deoxys, Phione, Manaphy, Darkrai, Shaymin, and Arceus - caught in the Pokédex.
Seven Stars Restaurant: The Seven Stars Restaurant at Hotel Grand Lake allows the player to face Trainers in Double Battles between 9:00 AM (09:00) and 11:00 PM (23:00) daily.

Utility:
Rock Climb is required to access TM85 Dream Eater. In Diamond and Pearl, Rock Climb is also required to access a Rare Candy. In Platinum, Rock Climb is also required to access a Sun Stone and an Iron.

Pokémon

  • Wild Staravia have a 5% chance of holding a Yache Berry.
  • Wild Kricketune have a 5% chance of holding a Metronome.
  • Wild Girafarig have a 5% chance of holding a Persim Berry.
  • Wild Bibarel have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Sitrus Berry.
  • Wild Geodude and Graveler have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
