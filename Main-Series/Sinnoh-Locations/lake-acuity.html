Lake Acuity

Access to:
South: Acuity Lakefront

Utility:
Surf is required to access TM14 Blizzard and Acuity Cavern, which contains Uxie. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
In Platinum, Rock Climb is required to access the entrance to Lake Acuity.

Access to:

Utility:

Pokémon

  • Wild Sneasel have a 5% chance of holding a Quick Claw.
  • Wild Snorunt have a 5% chance of holding a Babiri Berry.
  • Wild Bibarel have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Sitrus Berry.
  • Wild Chingling have a 5% chance of holding a Colbur Berry.
  • Wild Snover have a 5% chance of holding NeverMeltIce.
  • Wild Smoochum always hold an Aspear Berry.
  • Wild Solrock have a 5% chance of holding a Sun Stone.
  • Wild Lunatone have a 5% chance of holding a Moon Stone.
