Route 224

Access to:
Accessible on the route: Victory Road 1F Back (room 3)
North: Seabreak Path

Places of interest:
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Surf is required to access several items. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Access to:

Pokémon

  • Wild Buizel and Floatzel have a 5% chance of holding a Wacan Berry.
  • Wild Roselia and Tentacruel have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Shuckle always hold an Oran Berry.
  • Wild Beautifly and Dustox have a 5% chance of holding a Shed Shell.
  • Wild Chatot have a 5% chance of holding a Metronome.
  • Wild Duskull and Dusclops have a 5% chance of holding a Kasib Berry.
  • Wild Luvdisc have a 50% chance of holding a Heart Scale.
