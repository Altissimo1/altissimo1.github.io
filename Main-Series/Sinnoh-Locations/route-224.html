Route 224

Access to:
Accessible on the route: Victory Road 1F Back (room 3)
North: Seabreak Path

Places of interest:
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Srurf is required to access several items. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
