Route 222

Access to:
West: Valor Lakefront
East: Sunyshore City

Places of interest:
Honey Tree
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
In Diamond and Pearl, Rock Smash or Surf is required to access a Honey. In Platinum, Rock Smash or Surf is required to access a Full Restore, a Quick Ball, and a TinyMushroom.
Cut is required to access a Carbos.
Surf is required to access a Big Mushroom. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Pokémon

  • Wild Magnemite and Magneton have a 5% chance of holding a Metal Coat.
  • Wild Mr. Mime have a 5% chance of holding a Leppa Berry.
  • Wild Electabuzz have a 50% chance of holding an Electirizer.

  • Wild Skitty have a 5% chance of holding a Leppa Berry.
  • Wild Tentacool and Tentacruel have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Combee always hold Honey.
  • Wild Cherubi have a 5% chance of holding a Miracle Seed.
  • Wild Munchlax always hold Leftovers.
  • Wild Sharpedo have a 5% chance of holding a DeepSeaTooth.
