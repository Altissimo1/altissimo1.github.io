Fullmoon Island

Access to:
Accessible via the ferry: Canalave City

This area is only accessible after obtaining the National Pokédex and speaking with Sailor Eldritch in Canalave City about his son's condition. When Cresselia is interacted with here, she will flee and begin roaming Sinnoh.
