Eterna Forest

Access to:
Either exit: Route 205
Accessible in the area: Old Chateau

Places of interest:
The first time the player explores this area, Cheryl will request to accompany them. Every wild and Trainer battle that the player engages in while she is present will be a Double Battle. She has a level 15 (Diamond/Pearl) or 20 (Platinum) Chansey. The player's Pokémon will be healed after every encounter while she is present.
Eevee will evolve into Leafeon here due to the Moss Rock in the northeast.

Utility:
Cut is required to access both the exterior area of the forest and the Old Chateau, as well as an Insect Plate and an Ether inside the forest.

  • Wild Buneary have a 5% chance of holding a Chople Berry.

  • Wild Kricketot have a 5% chance of holding a Metronome.
  • Wild Combee always hold Honey.
  • Wild Cherubi have a 5% chance of holding a Miracle Seed.
  • Wild Munchlax always hold Leftovers.
