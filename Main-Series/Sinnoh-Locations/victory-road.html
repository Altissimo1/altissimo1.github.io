Victory Road

Access to:
Exits on 1F: Pokémon League
Exit on 1F Back (room 3): Route 224

Places of interest:
Marley is located in the back area of Victory Road, accessible from 1F after obtaining the National Pokédex. She will request to accompany the player to the exit to Route 224. Every wild and Trainer battle that the player engages in while she is present will be a Double Battle. She has a level 57 (Diamond/Pearl) or 59 (Platinum) Arcanine. The player's Pokémon will be healed after every encounter while she is present.

Utility:
Surf, Strength, Rock Smash, Waterfall, and Rock Climb are required to navigate the area.
Surf can be used here to encounter wild Pokémon.
