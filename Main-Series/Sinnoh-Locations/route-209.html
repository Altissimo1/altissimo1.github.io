Route 209

Access to:
North: Solaceon Town
West: Hearthome City
Accessible on the route: Lost Tower

Places of interest:
Honey Tree
THe Hallowed Tower, where the player can place an Odd Keystone to encounter Spiritomb, is located in the sandy area to the southeast.
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Surf is required to access TM19 Giga Drain. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Cut is required to access TM47 Steel Wing.
The Bicycle is required to access a Calcium.

Pokémon

  • Wild Chansey have a 50% chance of holding an Oval Stone and a 5% chance of holding a Lucky Egg.
  • Wild Roselia have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Duskull have a 5% chance of holding a Kasib Berry.
  • Wild Starly and Staravia have a 5% chance of holding a Yache Berry.
  • Wild Bibarel have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Sitrus Berry.
  • Wild Mime Jr. have a 5% chance of holding a Leppa Berry.
  • Wild Vulpix always hold a Rawst Berry.
  • Wild Miltank always hold Moomoo Milk.
  • Wild Combee always hold Honey.
  • Wild Cherubi have a 5% chance of holding a Miracle Seed.
  • Wild Munchlax always hold Leftovers.
