Route 212

Access to:
North: Hearthome City
East: Pastoria City
Accessible on the route: Pokémon Mansion and Trophy Garden

Places of interest:
Honey Trees
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Cut is required to access a Zinc, Scientist Shaun, and Parasol Lady Sabrina on the southern half. In Diamond and Pearl, Cut is also required to access an X Special on the southern half. In Platinum, Cut is required to access a Big Mushroom and a Hyper Potion on the southern half. Cut or Surf is required to access TM11 Sunny Day on the northern half.
Surf is required to access a Rose Incense, a Lum Berry, and a Tamato Berry on the northern half and TM84 Poison Jab on the southern half. In Diamond and Pearl, Surf is also required to access a Protein ont he southern half. In Platinum, Surf is also required to access an Iron on the northern half and a Max Ether on the southern half. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
