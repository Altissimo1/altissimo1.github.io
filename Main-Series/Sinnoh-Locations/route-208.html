Route 208

Access to:
West: Mt. Coronet 1F south
East: Hearthome City

Places of interest:
Honey Tree
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
In Diamond and Pearl, Rock Smash is required to access a Poké Ball. In Platinum, Rock Smash is required to access an Ether.
In Diamond and Pearl, Rock Smash and Rock Climb are required to access an Iron. In Platinum, they are required to access a Star Piece.
Rock Climb is required to access Hiker Alexander.
Surf is required to access Fisherman Cody. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Surf and Waterfall are required to access a Carbos.

Pokémon

  • Wild Bibarel have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Sitrus Berry.
  • Wild Zangoose have a 5% chance of holding a Quick Claw.
  • Wild Budew and Roselia have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Combee always hold Honey.
  • Wild Cherubi have a 5% chance of holding a Miracle Seed.
  • Wild Munchlax always hold Leftovers.
