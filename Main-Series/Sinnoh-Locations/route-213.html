Route 213

Access to:
North: Valor Lakefront
West: Pastoria City

Places of interest:
Honey Tree
Hotel Grand Lake
Dr. Footstep
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Rock Smash is required to access TM40 Aerial Ace. In Diamond and Pearl, it is also required to access an Awakening. In Platinum, it is also required to access a Hyper Potion.
Surf is required to access a Max Revive, a Big Pearl, a Water Stone, and several Trainers. In Diamond and Pearl, it is also required to access a Pearl. In Platinum, it is also required to access a Big Pearl. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Rock Climb is required to access a Protein, TM05 Roar, a Max Revive, and the Coin Toss Pokétch app. In Diamond and Pearl, it is also required to access a Max Revive. In Platinum, it is also required to access an HP Up.

Access to:
North: Valor Lakefront
West: Pastoria City

Places of interest:
Honey Tree
Hotel Grand Lake
Dr. Footstep
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Rock Smash is required to access an Awakening and TM40 Aerial Ace.
Surf is required to access a Max Revive, a Pearl, a Big Pearl, a Water Stone, several Trainers, and the three Swimmers and Tuber that give out Stickers. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Rock Climb is required to access two Max Revives, a Protein, TM05 Roar, and the Coin Toss Pokétch app.

Pokémon

  • Wild Buizel and Floatzel have a 5% chance of holding a Wacan Berry.
  • Wild Swellow have a 5% chance of holding a Charti Berry.
  • Wild Chatot have a 5% chance of holding a Metronome.
  • Wild Tentacool and Tentacruel have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Combee always hold Honey.
  • Wild Cherubi have a 5% chance of holding a Miracle Seed.
  • Wild Munchlax always hold Leftovers.
  • Wild Sharpedo have a 5% chance of holding a DeepSeaTooth.
