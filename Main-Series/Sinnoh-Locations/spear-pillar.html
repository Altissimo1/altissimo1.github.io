Spear Pillar

Access to:
Southern exit: Mt. Coronet
Accessible with the Azure Flute: Hall of Origin
Accessible after Giratina appears: Distortion World (Platinum only)

Places of interest:
Magneton will evolve into Magnezone and Nosepass will evolve into Probopass here due to the magnetic field.

Utility:
Surf, Strength, and Rock Climb are required to access this area from Mt. Coronet.
