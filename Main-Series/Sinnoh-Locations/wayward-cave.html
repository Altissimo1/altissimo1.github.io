Wayward Cave

Access to:
Exits on 1F: Route 206

Places of interest:
Mira is located at the northern dead end in the main area of the cave. She will request to accompany the player to the exit. Every wild and Trainer battle that the player engages in while she is present will be a Double Battle. She has a level 18 (Diamond/Pearl) or 26 (Platinum) Kadabra. The player's Pokémon will be healed after every encounter while she is present.

Utility:
Cut is required to access both entrances to the cave.
Rock Smash is required to navigate the main area of the cave.
The Bicycle is required to navigate the secret area of the cave.
In Diamond and Pearl, Strength is required to access B1F of the secret area of the cave.
Flash is recommended on 1F.

  • Wild Geodude have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
