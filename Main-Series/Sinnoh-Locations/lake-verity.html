Lake Verity

Access to:
South: Verity Lakefront

Utility:
Surf is required to access TM38 Fire Blast and Verity Cavern, which contains Mesprit. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Access to:
South: Verity Lakefront

Utility:
Surf is required to access TM38 Fire Blast and Verity Cavern, which contains Mesprit. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Pokémon

  • Wild Starly and Staravia have a 5% chance of holding a Yache Berry.
  • Wild Lunatone have a 5% chance of holding a Moon Stone.
  • Wild Solrock have a 5% chance of holding a Sun Stone.
  • Wild Bibarel have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Sitrus Berry.
