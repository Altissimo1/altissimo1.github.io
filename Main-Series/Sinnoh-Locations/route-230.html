Route 230

Access to:
West: Fight Area
East: Route 229

Places of interest:
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Surf is required to traverse the route. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Surf and Rock Smash are required to access a Rare Candy. In Diamond and Pearl, they are also required to access a Carbos and an HP Up. In Platinum, they are also required to access a Blue Shard, an Ultra Ball, and a Water Stone.
