Sendoff Spring

Access to:
South: Spring Path
Accessible in the area: Turnback Cave

Utility:
Rock Climb is required to access the water and the entrance to Turnback Cave.
Surf can be used here to encounter wild Pokémon.

Pokémon

  • Wild Bibarel have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Sitrus Berry.
  • Wild Staravia have a 5% chance of holding a Yache Berry.
  • Wild Lunatone have a 5% chance of holding a Moon Stone.
  • Wild Solrock have a 5% chance of holding a Sun Stone.
  • Wild Chingling and Chimecho have a 5% chance of holding a Colbur Berry.
  • Wild Graveler have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
  • Wild Dusclops have a 5% chance of holding a Kasib Berry.
