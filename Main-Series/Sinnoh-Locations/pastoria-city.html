Pastoria City

Access to:
West: Route 212
East: Route 213
Accessible in the city: Great Marsh

Places of interest:
Move Reminder: In exchange for a Heart Scale, the Move Reminder can teach a Pokémon a move that it was capable of learning at a lower level than its current level, but which is not currently in its moveset.
Pastoria Gym: The fourth (Diamond/Pearl) or fifth (Platinum) gym. It specializes in Water-types.
Poké Mart

Utility:
Surf is required to access a Mystic Water. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
