Iron Island

Access to:
Accessible via the ferry outside: Canalave City
Accessible on B3F: Iron Ruins (Platinum only)

Places of interest:
The first time the player explores the B2F western area, Riley will request to accompany them. Every wild and Trainer battle that the player engages in while he is present will be a Double Battle. He has a level 34 (Diamond/Pearl) or 41 (Platinum) Lucario. The player's Pokémon will be healed after every encounter while he is present.

Utility:
Surf can be used here to encounter wild Pokémon.

Pokémon

  • Wild Geodude and Graveler have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
  • Wild Mawile have a 5% chance of holding an Occa Berry.
  • Wild Steelix have a 5% chance of holding a Metal Coat.
  • Wild Tentacool, Tentacruel, and Qwilfish have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Finneon and Lumineon have a 5% chance of holding a Rindo Berry.
