Route 210

Access to:
West: Celestic Town
South: Solaceon Town
East: Route 215

Places of interest:
Honey Trees
Café Cabin
Move Tutor: An old lady in the house on the northern half of the route will teach Draco Meteor to Dragon-type Pokémon with high friendship.
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
The SecretPotion is required to clear the Psyduck blocking access to the northern half of the route.
Defog is recommended on the northern half of the route.
Rock Climb is required to access the Move Tutor's house and Dragon Tamer Patrick. In Diamond and Pearl, it is also required to access a Nugget. In Platinum, it is also required to access a Shiny Stone and a Zinc.
A Bicycle is required to access Ninja Boy Davido.
A Bicycle and Rock Smash are required to access TM30 Shadow Ball.
Surf can be used on the northern half to encounter wild Pokémon.
Surf and Waterfall are required to access a Meadow Plate and a Wave Incense.
