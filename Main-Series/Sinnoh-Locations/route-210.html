Route 210

Access to:
West: Celestic Town
South: Solaceon Town
East: Route 215

Places of interest:
Honey Trees
Café Cabin
Move Tutor: An old lady in the house on the northern half of the route will teach Draco Meteor to Dragon-type Pokémon with high friendship.
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
The SecretPotion is required to clear the Psyduck blocking access to the northern half of the route.
Defog is recommended on the northern half of the route.
Rock Climb is required to access the Move Tutor's house and Dragon Tamer Patrick. In Diamond and Pearl, it is also required to access a Nugget. In Platinum, it is also required to access a Shiny Stone and a Zinc.
A Bicycle is required to access Ninja Boy Davido.
A Bicycle and Rock Smash are required to access TM30 Shadow Ball.
Surf can be used on the northern half to encounter wild Pokémon.
Surf and Waterfall are required to access a Meadow Plate and a Wave Incense.

Pokémon

  • Wild Ponyta have a 5% chance of holding a Shuca Berry.
  • Wild Geodude have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
  • Wild Miltank always hold Moomoo Milk.
  • Wild Chansey have a 50% chance of holding an Oval Stone and a 5% chance of holding a Lucky Egg.
  • Wild Kricketune have a 5% chance of holding a Metronome.
  • Wild Mime Jr. have a 5% chance of holding a Leppa Berry.
  • Wild Staravia have a 5% chance of holding a Yache Berry.
  • Wild Roselia have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Bibarel have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Sitrus Berry.
  • Wild Zangoose have a 5% chance of holding a Quick Claw.
  • Wild Kecleon have a 5% chance of holding a Persim Berry.
  • Wild Bagon have a 5% chance of holding a Dragon Fang.
  • Wild Combee always hold Honey.
  • Wild Cherubi have a 5% chance of holding a Miracle Seed.
  • Wild Munchlax always hold Leftovers.
