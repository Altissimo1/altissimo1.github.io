Route 211

Access to:
West: Eterna City
East: Celestic Town
Accessible on the route: Mt. Coronet 1F north

Places of interest:
Honey Tree (eastern half)
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Rock Smash is required on the western half to access TM12 Taunt and Bird Keeper Alexandra.
Rock Smash and Strength are required in Mt. Coronet to access either half from the other half.
Rock Smash and Rock Climb are required on the eastern half to access TM29 Psychic. In Diamond and Pearl, they are also required to access a Zinc. In Platinum, they are also required to access a Calcium.
