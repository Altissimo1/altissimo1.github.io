Canalave City

Access to:
East: Route 218
Accessible from the city (via the ferry): Iron Island, Fullmoon Island, and Newmoon Island

Places of interest:
Canalave Library
Move Deleter
Port: The ferry in the port here can be used to travel to Iron Island, Fullmoon Island, and the event-exclusive Newmoon Island.
Canalave Gym: The sixth Gym. It specializes in Steel-types.

Utility:
