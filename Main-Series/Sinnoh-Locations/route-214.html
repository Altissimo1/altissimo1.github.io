Route 214

Access to:
North: Veilstone City
South: Valor Lakefront
East: Spring Path
Accessible on the route: Maniac Tunnel

Places of interest:
Honey Tree
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Surf is required to access a Rare Candy. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Pokémon

  • Wild Spoink have a 5% chance of holding a Tanga Berry.
  • Wild Geodude and Graveler have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
  • Wild Vulpix always hold a Rawst Berry.
  • Wild Ponyta have a 5% chance of holding a Shuca Berry.

  • Wild Girafarig have a 5% chance of holding a Persim Berry.
  • Wild Stunky, Poochyena, and Mightyena have a 5% chance of holding a Pecha Berry.
  • Wild Combee always hold Honey.
  • Wild Cherubi have a 5% chance of holding a Miracle Seed.
  • Wild Munchlax always hold Leftovers.
