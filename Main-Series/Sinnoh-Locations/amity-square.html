Amity Square

Access to:
Southern exit: Hearthome City

Access to:
Southern exit: Hearthome City

Overview

In Amity Square, the player can walk with one of their Pokémon outside of its Poké Ball. Only specific species of Pokémon can be walked with, indicated by the tables below. Pokémon have chances to pick up Berries and Accessories after a certain number of steps; the items that can be obtained this way are shown in the "Items" section below.

In Amity Square, the player can walk with one or more of their Pokémon outside of its Poké Ball. Only specific species of Pokémon can be walked with, indicated by the tables below. Pokémon have chances to pick up Berries, Stickers, and Poké Balls after a certain number of steps; the items that can be obtained this way are shown in the "Items" section below.
Once the player enters Amity Square for the first time, they will gain the ability to walk with any of their Pokémon outside of Amity Square.

A Poffin machine in the northern part of the area will allow the player to make Poffins with their Pokémon.
