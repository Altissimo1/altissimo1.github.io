Hall of Origin

Access to:
Accessible from the area: Spear Pillar

Places of interest:
Magneton will evolve into Magnezone and Nosepass will evolve into Probopass here due to the magnetic field.

Utility:
Surf, Strength, and Rock Climb are required to access this area from Mt. Coronet.

Accessing this area requires obtaining the Azure Flute and using it on every tile while walking north from the entrance. The Azure Flute was never legitimately distributed in Diamond, Pearl, or Platinum. The Azure Flute can be obtained with hacks or cheats, and the Hall of Origin itself is accessible in Diamond and Pearl with the use of a glitch.

Access to:
Accessible from the area: Spear Pillar

Places of interest:
Magneton will evolve into Magnezone and Nosepass will evolve into Probopass here due to the magnetic field.

Utility:
Surf, Strength, and Rock Climb are required to access this area from Mt. Coronet.

Accessing this area requires obtaining the Azure Flute by obtaining the National Pokédex and completing all Missions in Pokémon Legends: Arceus on the same system as the copy of Brilliant Diamond or Shining Pearl used to encounter Arceus here. The Azure Flute will appear in the player's room in Twinleaf Town, and must be played at Spear Pillar to access the Hall of Origin.
