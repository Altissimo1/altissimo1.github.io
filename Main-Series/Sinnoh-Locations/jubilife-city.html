Global Terminal

In Platinum, a Beauty on 1F of the Global Terminal will give away one Backdrop each day, once the player obtains the Fashion Case. The list of obtainable Backdrops are in the "Jubilife TV Lottery Corner" section below.

Jubilife TV Lottery Corner

Felicity will give out tickets with an ID number, which changes daily. If one of the player's Pokémon's IDs matches the number (partially or fully), the player will receive a prize. In Diamond and Pearl, the lottery can only be played once per day; in Platinum, it can be played multiple times until the player wins a prize.

In Diamond and Pearl, the Backdrop prize will award the player a random Backdrop they don't already have. Once all Backdrops have been obtained, the player can no longer win a prize for matching one digit. The list of Backdrops is given below this table.

Match Prize
Last digit Backdrop (Diamond/Pearl) / Ultra Ball (Platinum)
Last two digits PP Up
Last three digits Exp. Share
Last four digits Max Revive
All digits Master Ball
