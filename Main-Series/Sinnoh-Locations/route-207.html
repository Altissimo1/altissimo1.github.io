Route 207

Access to:
North: Route 206
South: Oreburgh City
East: Mt. Coronet 1F (south)

Places of interest:
Honey Tree
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
The Bicycle is required to access the northern part of the route, including Route 206 and the Mt. Coronet entrance, from the southern part.
In Diamond and Pearl, Surf and Rock Climb are required to access an Iron.
In Platinum, Surf, Strength, and Rock Climb are required to access a Timer Ball, a Red Shard, and an Iron.

Access to:

Pokémon

  • Wild Geodude have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
  • Wild Phanpy have a 5% chance of holding a Passho Berry.
  • Wild Kricketot have a 5% chance of holding a Metronome.
  • Wild Ponyta have a 5% chance of holding a Shuca Berry.
  • Wild Combee always hold Honey.
  • Wild Cherubi have a 5% chance of holding a Miracle Seed.
  • Wild Munchlax always hold Leftovers.
