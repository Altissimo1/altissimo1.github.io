Mr. Goods

Mr. Goods is a man standing on the street north of the Poffin House. He will give the player decorations for their Underground Secret Base when spoken to, if the player has achieved specific milestones.

Item Requirement

Tough Cup Win a Master Rank Tough Contest

Yellow Crystal Affect players with Traps 100 times in the Underground
