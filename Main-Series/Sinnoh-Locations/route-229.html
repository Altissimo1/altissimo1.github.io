Route 229

Access to:
North: Route 228
West: Route 230
South: Resort Area

Places of interest:
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Cut is required to access a Reaper Cloth and Protein.
Surf can be used here to encounter wild Pokémon.
In Diamond and Pearl, Cut and Surf are required to access a Big Mushroom. In Platinum, Cut and Surf are required to access a Thunderstone.
