Oreburgh Gate

Access to:
West: Route 203
East: Oreburgh City

Utility:
Rock Smash is required to access B1F.
Surf is required to access an Earth Plate and TM01 Focus Punch on B1F, and may be required to access TM31 Brick Break. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered on B1F with Surf.
The Bicycle may be required to access TM31 Brick Break on B1F.
Strength is required to access an Earth Plate and TM01 Focus Punch on B1F.
