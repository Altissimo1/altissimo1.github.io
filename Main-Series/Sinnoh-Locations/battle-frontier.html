The Battle Frontier is a location, accessible in Platinum, HeartGold, and SoulSilver, that houses five Battle Facilities where the goal is to defeat Trainers without losing. Each Battle Facility has a leader, known as the Frontier Brain, who can be battled after a given number of Trainers have been defeated, without the player losing to any one. Each Frontier Brain has two encounters in their facility. Winning the first Frontier Brain fight will give the player a Silver Print for that facility on their Vs. Recorder, and winning the second fight will upgrade the print to a Gold Print. Additionally, achieving 100 wins in the Battle Tower specifically will give the player a star for their Trainer Card, and winning certain modes in the Battle Tower will give the player's Pokémon Ribbons.
The list of facilities is as follows:

  • Battle Tower
  • Battle Arcade
  • Battle Castle
  • Battle Hall
  • Battle Factory

Although each facility has different rules, there are some restrictions that apply to all of them.

  • The player will not receive money, and their Pokémon will not receive experience. Instead, the player will receive BP, which is used as a currency for purchasable items in the Battle Frontier and, in HeartGold and SoulSilver, the Frontier Access.
  • Set Mode is enforced.
  • The moves Return and Frustration, when used by the opponent or rented Pokémon in the Battle Factory, will always have their maximum power.
  • Losing a battle will reset the player's streak to 0. Turning off the game mid-battle, or mid-round without saving, will count as a loss and reset the streak.
  • Pokémon encountered in battles will not be added to the Pokédex.
  • Defeating a Frontier Brain will give the player 20BP.
  • All challenges can be entered as a Single Battle, Double Battle, or Multi Battle. In the Battle Tower, Multis can be entered with either an AI-controlled partner Trainer or another real player (either local or via Wi-Fi). In the other facilities, only the option to join another real player is available. However, the Frontier Brain will only appear in Singles.
  • Enemy Pokémon will always take their Nature, Item, moves, and EVs from predetermined 'sets'.
  • Enemy Pokémon that can have two Abilities will always have a random Ability. Enemy Pokémon that can be male or female will have their gender randomly determined.
  • Any Pokémon holding a Soul Dew cannot enter.
  • Heat, Wash, Frost, Fan, and Mow Rotom will revert back to Rotom's default form when entered. All enemy Rotom will also be in their default form.
  • Some Pokémon cannot be entered altogether, and will never be encountered on an opponent's team:
Mewtwo Mew Spiky-Eared Pichu Lugia Ho-Oh
Celebi Kyogre Groudon Rayquaza Jirachi
Deoxys Dialga Palkia Giratina Phione
Manaphy Darkrai Shaymin Arceus Eggs

Pokémon Scratch-Off Corner

Three scratch-off cards can be purchased for 1BP. Each card has nine blocks, and the player can scratch three. Matching three of the same Pokémon, or two of the same and a Ditto, will give the player the prize shown on the top screen. A Nugget is always available as one of the prizes, and the other prizes will all be Berries, selected from the list below. If the player wins a Berry, they will win three of that Berry.

Qualot Berry Tamato Berry Wacan Berry Chople Berry
Coba Berry Pomeg Berry Hondew Berry Occa Berry
