Valley Windworks

Access to:
West: Route 205

Places of interest:
Honey Tree

Utility:
Surf is required to access a Max Elixir and TM24 Thunderbolt. In Platinum, Surf is also required to access an Electirizer. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Access to:
West: Route 205

Places of interest:
Honey Tree

Utility:
Surf is required to access a Max Elixir and TM24 Thunderbolt. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Pokémon

  • Wild Buizel have a 5% chance of holding a Wacan Berry.
  • Wild Elekid have a 50% chance of holding an Electirizer in Diamond and a 5% chance of holding an Electirizer in Pearl.
  • Wild Tentacool and Tentacruel have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Combee always hold Honey.
  • Wild Cherubi have a 5% chance of holding a Miracle Seed.
  • Wild Munchlax always hold Leftovers.
  • Wild Finneon and Lumineon have a 5% chance of holding a Rindo Berry.
  • Wild Shellder have a 50% chance of holding a Pearl and a 5% chance of holding a Big Pearl.
