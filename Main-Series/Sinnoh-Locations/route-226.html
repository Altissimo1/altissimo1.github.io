Route 226

Access to:
North: Route 227
West: Survival Area
East: Route 228

Places of interest:
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Rock Climb is required to access many items and Trainers, as well as the upper area of the Survival Area.
Surf is required to navigate the route. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Pokémon

  • Wild Fearow have a 5% chance of holding a Sharp Beak.
  • Wild Rattata and Raticate have a 5% chance of holding a Chilan Berry.
  • Wild Banette have a 5% chance of holding a Spell Tag.
  • Wild Mankey and Primeape have a 5% chance of holding a Payapa Berry.
  • Wild Graveler have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
  • Wild Tentacruel have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Horsea and Seadra have a 5% chance of holding a Dragon Scale.
  • Wild Relicanth have a 5% chance of holding a DeepSeaScale.
