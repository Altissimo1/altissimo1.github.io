Pokémon

  • Wild Magnemite have a 5% chance of holding a Metal Coat.
  • Wild Magmar have a 5% chance of holding a Magmarizer.
  • Wild Tentacool and Tentacruel have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Combee always hold Honey.
  • Wild Cherubi have a 5% chance of holding a Miracle Seed.
  • Wild Munchlax always hold Leftovers.
  • Wild Finneon and Lumineon have a 5% chance of holding a Rindo Berry.
  • Wild Shellder have a 50% chance of holding a Pearl and a 5% chance of holding a Big Pearl.

Mr. Fuego

In Platinum, the player can speak to Mr. Fuego to receive one Green Shard, Red Shard, Yellow Shard, and Blue Shard in exchange for a Star Piece. This can be repeated multiple times.
