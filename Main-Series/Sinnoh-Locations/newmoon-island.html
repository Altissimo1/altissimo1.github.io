Newmoon Island

Access to:
Accessible via the ferry: Canalave City

Accessing this area requires obtaining the Member Card and entering the locked inn in Canalave City, at which point the player will be automatically taken to Newmoon Island. After engaging Darkrai, the player will return to Canalave City, and can reach the island again by talking to Sailor Eldritch. The Member Card was only available via a Mystery Gift download for Platinum. The Member Card can be obtained with hacks or cheats, and Newmoon Island itself is accessible in Diamond and Pearl with the use of a glitch.
