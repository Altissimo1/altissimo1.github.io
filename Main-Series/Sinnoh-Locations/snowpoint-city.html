Snowpoint City

Access to:
West: Acuity Lakefront
Accessible in the city: Snowpoint Temple
Accessible from the city (via the S.S. Sinnoh/Spiral): Fight Area

Places of interest:
Move Tutor (Platinum only)
Trendy Phrase Man: A man in the northeastern house will provide one trendy phrase a day, until the player has obtained 32 phrases. These can be used in the easy chat system.
Port: The S.S. Sinnoh (Diamond/Pearl) or S.S. Spiral (Platinum) in the port here can be used to travel to Fight Area.
Snowpoint Gym: The seventh Gym. It specializes in Ice-types.
