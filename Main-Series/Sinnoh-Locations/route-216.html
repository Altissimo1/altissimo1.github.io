Route 216

Access to:
North: Route 217
East: Mt. Coronet 1F north

Places of interest:
The bed at the Snowbound Lodge can be used to heal the player's Pokémon.
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Rock Climb is required to access a Mental Herb, an HP Up, TM13 Ice Beam, and several Trainers. In Platinum, it is also required to access a Max Potion.

Pokémon

  • Wild Snover have a 5% chance of holding NeverMeltIce.
  • Wild Sneasel have a 50% chance of holding a Grip Claw and a 5% chance of holding a Quick Claw.
  • Wild Snorunt have a 5% chance of holding a Babiri Berry.
  • Wild Graveler have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
