Route 206

Access to:
North: Eterna City
South: Route 207
Accessible on the route: Wayward Cave

Places of interest:
Honey Tree
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
A Bicycle is required to access the route from either end.
Cut is required to access the area beneath Cycling Road, which contains several items, a Trainer, a Honey Tree, and the entrance to Wayward Cave.

Access to:
North: Eterna City
South: Route 207
Accessible on the route: Wayward Cave

Places of interest:
Honey Tree
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
A Bicycle is required to access the route from either end.
Cut is required to access the area beneath Cycling Road, which contains several items, a Trainer, a Honey Tree, and the entrance to Wayward Cave.

Pokémon

  • Wild Ponyta have a 5% chance of holding a Shuca Berry.
  • Wild Geodude have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
  • Wild Kricketot and Kricketune have a 5% chance of holding a Metronome.
  • Wild Nosepass have a 5% chance of holding a Hard Stone.
  • Wild Bronzor have a 5% chance of holding a Metal Coat.
  • Wild Stunky have a 5% chance of holding a Pecha Berry.
  • Wild Combee always hold Honey.
  • Wild Cherubi have a 5% chance of holding a Miracle Seed.
  • Wild Munchlax always hold Leftovers.
