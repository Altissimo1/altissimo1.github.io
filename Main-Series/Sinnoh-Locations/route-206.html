Route 206

Access to:
North: Eterna City
South: Route 207
Accessible on the route: Wayward Cave

Places of interest:
Honey Tree
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
A Bicycle is required to access the route from either end.
Cut is required to access the area beneath Cycling Road, which contains several items, a Trainer, a Honey Tree, and the entrance to Wayward Cave.
