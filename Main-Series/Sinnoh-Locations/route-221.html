Route 221

Access to:
West: Route 220
Accessible on the route: Pal Park

Places of interest:
Honey Tree
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Surf is required to access and traverse this route. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Access to:
West: Route 220
Accessible on the route: Ramanas Park

Places of interest:
Honey Tree
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Surf is required to access and traverse this route. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Pokémon

  • Wild Farfetch'd have a 5% chance of holding a Stick.
  • Wild Floatzel have a 5% chance of holding a Wacan Berry.
  • Wild Roselia, Tentacool, and Tentacruel have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Stunky and Skuntank have a 5% chance of holding a Pecha Berry.
  • Wild Girafarig have a 5% chance of holding a Persim Berry.
  • Wild Combee always hold Honey.
  • Wild Cherubi have a 5% chance of holding a Miracle Seed.
  • Wild Munchlax always hold Leftovers.
  • Wild Finneon and Lumineon have a 5% chance of holding a Rindo Berry.
  • Wild Clamperl have a 5% chance of holding a Big Pearl.

Pokémon

  • Wild Farfetch'd have a 5% chance of holding a Leek.
  • Wild Roselia, Tentacool, and Tentacruel have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Wingull and Pelipper have a 50% chance of holding a Pretty Feather.
  • Wild Combee have a 5% chance of holding Honey.
  • Wild Wurmple have a 50% chance of holding a Pecha Berry and a 5% chance of holding Bright Powder.
  • Wild Cherubi have a 5% chance of holding a Miracle Seed.
  • Wild Munchlax always hold Leftovers.
  • Wild Clamperl have a 50% chance of holding a Pearl and a 5% chance of holding a Big Pearl.
