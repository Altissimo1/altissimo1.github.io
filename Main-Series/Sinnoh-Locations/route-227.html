Route 227

Access to:
North: Stark Mountain
South: Route 226

Places of interest:
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Rock Climb is required to access Charcoal. In Diamond and Pearl, it is also required to access a Full Heal and a Protein. In Platinum, it is also required to access a Max Repel.
Surf can be used here to encounter wild Pokémon.

Access to:
North: Stark Mountain
South: Route 226

Places of interest:
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Rock Climb is required to access a Full Heal, Charcoal, and a Protein.
Surf can be used here to encounter wild Pokémon.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:

Pokémon

  • Wild Spinda have a 5% chance of holding a Chesto Berry.
  • Wild Graveler have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
  • Wild Numel and Camerupt always hold a Rawst Berry.
  • Wild Fearow have a 5% chance of holding a Sharp Beak.
  • Wild Weezing have a 5% chance of holding a Smoke Ball.
  • Wild Banette have a 5% chance of holding a Spell Tag.
  • Wild Magby have a 5% chance of holding a Magmarizer in Diamond and a 50% chance of holding a Magmarizer in Pearl.
  • Wild Poliwhirl have a 5% chance of holding a King's Rock.
