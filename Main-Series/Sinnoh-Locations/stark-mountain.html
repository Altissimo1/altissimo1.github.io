Stark Mountain

Access to:
South: Route 227

Places of interest:
Buck is located in the inner area of Stark Mountain. He will request to accompany the player to the chamber where Heatran is later encountered. Every wild and Trainer battle that the player engages in while he is present will be a Double Battle. He has a level 58 (Diamond/Pearl) or 63 (Platinum) Claydol. The player's Pokémon will be healed after every encounter while he is present.

Utility:
Rock Climb is required to access a Life Orb.
Strength is required to access an Escape Rope and a PP Up in the entrance. In Diamond and Pearl, Strength is also required to access a Burn Heal. In Platinum, it is also required to access a Full Heal.
Strength and Rock Smash are required to access the main cavern, as well as a Full Restore in the entrance. In Diamond and Pearl, Strength and Rock Smash are also required to access a Max Elixir in the entrance. In Platinum, they are also required to access a Star Piece in the entrance.

Pokémon

  • Wild Geodude and Graveler have a 5% chance of holding an Everstone.
  • Wild Numel and Camerupt always hold a Rawst Berry.
  • Wild Fearow have a 5% chance of holding a Sharp Beak.
  • Wild Koffing and Weezing have a 5% chance of holding a Smoke Ball.
  • Wild Banette have a 5% chance of holding a Spell Tag.
  • Wild Magby have a 5% chance of holding a Magmarizer in Diamond and a 50% chance of holding a Magmarizer in Pearl.
