Iron Ruins

Access to:
Exit: Iron Island B3F

This area is always accessible in Platinum, but will only contain Registeel if the player has a Regigigas from an event in their party. To access Registeel, the player must step on the seven dots on the floor and interact with the statue.

Pokémon

Static

Platinum
Pokémon Level Notes
Registeel 30 Only if the player has an event Regigigas in their party.
If defeated or run from, it reappears after defeating the Elite Four.
