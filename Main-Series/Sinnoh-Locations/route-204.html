Route 204

Access to:
North: Floaroma Town
South: Jubilife City
Accessible on the route: Ravaged Path

Places of interest:
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Rock Smash is required in Ravaged Path to access either half of the route from the other half.
Surf is required to access an HP Up and a Sea Incense on the southern half. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Cut is required to access a lady giving away TM78 Captivate on the northern half.
