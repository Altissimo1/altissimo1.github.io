Route 218

Access to:
West: Canalave City
East: Jubilife City

Places of interest:
Honey Tree
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Surf is required to cross the route and access the western half. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Access to:

The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:

Pokémon

  • Wild Floatzel have a 5% chance of holding a Wacan Berry.
  • Wild Ditto have a 50% chance of holding Quick Powder and a 5% chance of holding Metal Powder.
  • Wild Mr. Mime have a 5% chance of holding a Leppa Berry.
  • Wild Glameow have a 5% chance of holding a Cheri Berry.
  • Wild Chatot have a 5% chance of holding a Metronome.
  • Wild Tentacool and Tentacruel have a 65% chance of holding a Poison Barb
  • Wild Combee always hold Honey.
  • Wild Cherubi have a 5% chance of holding a Miracle Seed.
  • Wild Munchlax always hold Leftovers.
  • Wild Finneon and Lumineon have a 5% chance of holding a Rindo Berry.
