Ravaged Path

Access to:
Exits on 1F: Route 204

Utility:
Rock Smash is required to access most of the cave and go between the two exits.
Surf is required to access TM39 Rock Tomb, a Luck Incense, and TM03 Water Pulse. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
