Route 205

Access to:
West: Floaroma Town
West: Fuego Ironworks
East (from the southern half): Valley Windworks
East (from the eastern half): Eterna City
Accessible on the route: Eterna Forest

Places of interest:
Honey Trees
The Vs. Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.
The lady in the northern house will heal the player's Pokémon when spoken to.

Utility:
Surf is required to access Fuego Ironworks. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Cut is required to access the outer area of Eterna Forest from either half of this route.

Pokémon

  • Wild Buizel have a 5% chance of holding a Wacan Berry.
  • Wild Elekid have a 50% chance of holding an Electirizer in Diamond and a 5% chance of holding an Electirizer in Pearl.
  • Wild Slowpoke have a 5% chance of holding a Lagging Tail.
  • Wild Budew, Tentacool, and Tentacruel have a 5% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Kricketot have a 5% chance of holding a Metronome.
  • Wild Beautifly and Dustox have a 5% chance of holding a Shed Shell.
  • Wild Combee always hold Honey.
  • Wild Cherubi have a 5% chance of holding a Miracle Seed.
  • Wild Munchlax always hold Leftovers.
  • Wild Finneon and Lumineon have a 5% chance of holding a Rindo Berry.
  • Wild Shellder have a 50% chance of holding a Pearl and a 5% chance of holding a Big Pearl.
