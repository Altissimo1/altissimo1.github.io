Lake Valor

Access to:
East: Valor Lakefront

Utility:
Surf is required to access TM25 Thunder and Valor Cavern, which contains Azelf. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Access to:
East: Valor Lakefront

Utility:
Surf is required to access TM25 Thunder and Valor Cavern, which contains Azelf. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Pokémon

  • Wild Bibarel have a 50% chance of holding an Oran Berry and a 5% chance of holding a Sitrus Berry.
  • Wild Staravia have a 5% chance of holding a Yache Berry.
  • Wild Lunatone have a 5% chance of holding a Moon Stone.
  • Wild Solrock have a 5% chance of holding a Sun Stone.
  • Wild Lickitung have a 5% chance of holding a Lagging Tail.
  • Wild Chingling have a 5% chance of holding a Colbur Berry.

Static

All Versions
Pokémon Level Notes
Azelf 50 Only after completing the events at Spear Pillar.
In Diamond and Pearl, if run from, it reappears after leaving the area and returning.
In Platinum, if defeated or run from, it reappears after defeating the Elite Four.
