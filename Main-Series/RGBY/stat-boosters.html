Red, Green, Blue, and Yellow Stat Booster Locations

The following tables contain the location(s) of all Stat Boosters (HP Up, PP Up, Rare Candy, Protein, Carbos, Calcium, Iron) in Red, Green, Blue, and Yellow.

Celadon Department Store - 5F

Some stat boosters can be purchased from the rightmost vendor on 5F in Celadon Department Store. HP Ups are only sold here in Japanese Blue, Japanese Yellow, and all international releases of Red, Blue, and Yellow.
