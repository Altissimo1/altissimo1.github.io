Red, Green, Blue, and Yellow Pokémon Locations

The following table contains every Pokémon and all of its locations. Use the radio buttons above to switch between the Japanese games (RGBY) and the international games (RBY).

The following locations allow Fishing: Routes 6, 10, 11, 12, 13, 17, 18, 19, 20, 21, 22, 23, 24, and 25, Pallet Town, Viridian City, Cerulean City, Vermilion City, Vermilion Harbor, Celadon City, Fuchsia City, Cinnabar Island, Safari Zone (all areas), Seafoam Islands (B3F and B4F), and Cerulean Cave (1F and B1F).
Cerulean Gym also allows fishing. Its encounters are the same as Cerulean City's. In Yellow, only the Old and Good Rods work in the Gym.
