Events

Mew was distributed in Japan, Australia, the USA, Canada, the UK, the Republic of Ireland, Spain, France, Germany, and Belgium to Red, Green, Blue, and Yellow in various events held between 1996 and 2000.
Mew was distributed in the USA to Gold, Silver, and Crystal in an event held in November 2002.
Mew was distributed in Japan and the UK to Virtual Console copies of Red, Green, Blue, and Yellow in events held in 2016.
The lowest level possible for one of the Mew distributions was level 5.

Several Pokémon were also distributed at level 5 to Gold, Silver, and Crystal. All such Pokémon can be found at level 5 already in one or more games, so I have not listed them, as the events are long gone.

Glitches

The Mew glitch can be used with the enemy Ditto method to obtain all Gen 1 Pokémon (including Mew, naturally) at level 7.
Using Growl up to 6 times during the battle against the Pokémon whose Special stat is used to generate a Pokémon will lower the encountered Pokémon's level by 1 for each use, to a minimum of 1. Therefore, it is possible to get any Pokémon at level 1 using this glitch.
