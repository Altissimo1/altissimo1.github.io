How Many Pokémon Can You Get Without Evolving in Pokémon Red?

The player can get up to 124 species total in Red without trading, as described here. Without evolving any Pokémon, and only collecting evolutionary stages that the player can get in the wild, as a gift or purchase, or from an in-game trade, the player can get up to 102 species. Of the 124 possible species in Red, the Pokémon unavailable without evolving are as follows:

Starter Evolutions (2) - 2 total
The Starters are only available in their base forms, leaving their evolutions inaccessible.
Stone Evolutions (10) - 12 total
Nidoqueen, Nidoking, Clefable, Vileplume, Arcanine, Poliwrath, Cloyster, Exeggutor, Starmie, and two of Eevee's evolutions are inaccessible.
Fossils (1) - 13 total
The Fossils can only be revived into their base forms, leaving their evolutions inaccessible.
Other (9) - 22 total
Butterfree, Beedrill, Pidgeot, Primeape, Tentacruel, Rapidash, Gyarados, Dragonair, and Dragonite are inaccessible.
