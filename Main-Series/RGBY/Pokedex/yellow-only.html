How Many Pokémon Can You Get with Only Pokémon Yellow?

Assuming you do not use any glitches, the answer is 129. The Professor Oak Challenge Guide for Yellow can be found here, although I've made a shorter list of encounters below in case anything happens to the external guide.

The list of Pokémon you cannot get without trading, which equals 22 species, is as follows:

Raichu (1) - 1 total
You cannot evolve your starter Pikachu, and cannot get any other Pikachu, meaning Raichu is unobtainable without trading.
Trade Evolutions (3) - 4 total
Golem, Alakazam, and Gengar evolve by trade. Without the ability to trade, they are unobtainable. You can get Machamp via in-game trade.
Two Eevee evolutions (2) - 6 total
As you only receive one Eevee, you can only receive one of its evolutions.
One Fossil Pokémon (2) - 8 total
As you can only obtain one Fossil in Mt. Moon, you miss out on the other Fossil Pokémon (Omanyte or Kabuto) and its evolution.
One of Hitmonlee/Hitmonchan (1) - 9 total
As you can only obtain one of Hitmonlee or Hitmonchan, you miss out on the other.
Version Exclusives (12) - 21 total
The following Pokémon cannot be obtained without the use of glitches or trading: Weedle, Kakuna, Beedrill, Ekans, Arbok, Meowth, Persian, Koffing, Weezing, Jynx, Electabuzz, and Magmar.
Mythical/Event (1) - 22 total
Outside of glitches, Mew was only obtainable via events that are no longer active.
