Tanoby Ruins

Access to:
North: Sevault Canyon
Accessible in the area, from (north)west to east: Viapois Chamber, Rixy Chamber, Scufib Chamber, Dilford Chamber, Weepth Chamber, Liptoo Chamber, and Monean Chamber

Places of interest:
The VS Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Surf is required to traverse this area and access any of the Tanoby Chambers. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
