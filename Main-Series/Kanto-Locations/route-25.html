Bill's Sea Cottage
Misty appears on the eastern cape between 4:00-5:59 PM (16:00-17:59) after obtaining all 16 Badges, unless Suicune is present. She will give the player her Pokégear number when spoken to. After her Pokégear is obtained, she will appear here between 2:00-3:59 PM (14:00-15:59), and speaking to her with a Water-type Pokémon in the first slot will enable the player to take a picture with her.
