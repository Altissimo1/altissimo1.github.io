GO Park

Access to:
Southern exit: Fuchsia City

Overview

The GO Park, exclusive to Let's Go Pikachu and Let's Go Eevee, replaces the Safari Zone in Fuchsia City. It is used to obtain Pokémon sent from Pokémon GO. For a detailed explanation of how Pokémon are converted from the Pokémon GO data structures to those used by Let's Go, see Bulbapedia's page.

When a player has 25 Pokémon of a single species in the GO Park, they can play a minigame to receive Candies.

Any of the first 150 Pokémon (Bulbasaur through Mewtwo), Meltan, Melmetal, and any Alolan forms of the first 150 Pokémon can be transferred. Mew, despite being obtainable in Let's Go by other means, cannot be transferred. Non-standard Pokémon forms (such as Shadow or Purified Pokémon, or those with accessories) also cannot be transferred. Once transferred, a Pokémon cannot be returned to Pokémon GO.
