Cape Brink

Access to:
South: Two Island

Places of interest:
Move Tutor: The lady in the house can teach the player's fully-evolved starter Frenzy Plant (Venusaur), Blast Burn (Charizard), or Hydro Cannon (Blastoise). The player's starter must have maximum friendship for her to offer to teach the move, and it can only be learned one time.

Utility:
Surf is required to access a PP Max. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:

Pokémon

  • Wild Fearow have a 5% chance of holding a Sharp Beak.
  • Wild Meowth have a 5% chance of holding a Nugget.
