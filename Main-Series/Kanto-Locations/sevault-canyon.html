Sevault Canyon

Access to:
West: Canyon Entrance
South: Tanoby Ruins
Accessible in the area: Tanoby Key

Places of interest:
Speaking with the man in the house, and choosing to do the Chansey Dance, will heal the player's Pokémon.
The VS Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Strength and Rock Smash are required to access a King's Rock.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:

Pokémon

  • Wild Cubone and Marowak have a 5% chance of holding a Thick Club.
  • Wild Fearow and Skarmory have a 5% chance of holding a Sharp Beak.
  • Wild Meowth have a 5% chance of holding a Nugget.
  • Wild Onix and Graveler have a 5% chance of holding a Hard Stone.
