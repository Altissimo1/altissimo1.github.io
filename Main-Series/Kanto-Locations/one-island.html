One Island

Access to:
East: Kindle Road
South: Treasure Beach
Accessible from the city (via the Seagallop Ferries): Vermilion City, Two Island, Three Isle Port, Four Island, Five Island, Six Island, and Seven Island

Places of interest:
Pokémon Network Center: After restoring the Network Machine by delivering the Ruby and Sapphire, the player can use this Pokémon Center to connect to Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald, Colosseum, and XD.

Utility:
Surf is required to travel to Treasure Beach in the south. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Pokémon

  • Wild Shellder have a 50% chance of holding a Pearl and a 5% chance of holding a Big Pearl.
  • Wild Staryu have a 50% chance of holding Stardust and a 5% chance of holding a Star Piece.
