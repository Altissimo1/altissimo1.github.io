Pokémon Tower

Also known as Kanto Radio Tower in Gold, Silver, Crystal, HeartGold, and SoulSilver.

Access to:
Exit on 1F: Lavender Town

Places of interest:
5F contains a tile which heals the player's Pokémon.

Utility:
The Silph Scope is required to battle or catch any Pokémon, and to pass the Marowak on 6F.
