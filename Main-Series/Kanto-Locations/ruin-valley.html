Ruin Valley

Access to:
East: Water Path
Accessible in the area: Dotted Hole

Places of interest:
The VS Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Surf can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.
Cut is required to open the entrance to the Dotted Hole.
Strength is required to access a Sun Stone, an HP Up, and a Full Restore.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:

Pokémon

  • Wild Fearow have a 5% chance of holding a Sharp Beak.
  • Wild Meowth have a 5% chance of holding a Nugget.
