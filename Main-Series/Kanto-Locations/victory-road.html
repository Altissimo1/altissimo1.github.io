Places of interest:
Janine appears in the southern gate between 4:00-5:59 PM (16:00-17:59). She will give the player her Pokégear number when spoken to. After her Pokégear number is obtained, she will appear here between 6:00-7:59 PM (18:00-19:59), and speaking to her will enable the player to take a picture with her.

Utility:
Rock Smash is required to access a Zinc and TM79 Dark Pulse. Wild Pokémon and random items can also be found on 3F with Rock Smash.

Access to:
Either exit: Route 23

Places of interest:
The Master Trainer for Moltres is located on the ledge between the torches in the southern part of 2F after defeating the Elite Four. He cannot be battled, but will confer the title of Master Trainer on the player for Moltres if the player shows him a Moltres with at least 7500 CP.
The officer on 3F will heal the player's Pokémon.

Utility:
Strong Push is required to navigate this area.

Walking Pokémon

Walking Rock-type Pokémon can find Tiny Mushrooms, Big Mushrooms, or Fire Stones in the following locations:

  • On 1F, from a crevice in a rock in the northeast corner
  • On 3F, from a crevice in a rock on the north wall

Walking Fighting-type Pokémon can find Stardust, Star Pieces, or Nuggets in the following locations:

  • On 2F, along the northern wall
  • On 3F, along the northern wall in the southern corridor

Regenerating Items

Health Candies, Mighty Candies, Tough Candies, Smart Candies, Courage Candies, and Quick Candies can be found daily in the following areas:

  • On 1F, on a rock in the middle of the floor
  • On 2F, on a torch near the exit
  • On 3F, on a rock near the center of the floor
