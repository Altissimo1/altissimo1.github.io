Three Isle Path

Access to:
Either exit: Three Isle Port

Places of interest:
When first visiting Three Island, the path is incomplete. It is completed after the player obtains the National Pokédex. Once complete, it can be used to access a patch of grass in Three Isle Port. Two Nuggets are also obtainable here once it is completed.
