Route 19

Access to:
North: Fuchsia City
West: Route 20

Places of interest:
In Pokémon Yellow, there is a house north of the water, where a Pikachu with Surf can play a Surfing minigame. In the Virtual Console release, Pikachu does not need to know Surf to be allowed to play the minigame.

Utility:
Surf is required to traverse the route and travel to Route 20. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
