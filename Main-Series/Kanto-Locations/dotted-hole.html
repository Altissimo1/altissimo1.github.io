Dotted Hole

Access to:
Exit on 1F: Ruin Valley

Utility:
Cut is required to open the entrance to the Dotted Hole.

The Braille text in each of the rooms, once the player falls through the first hole, indicate which hole to fall through next. The order is up, left, right, down.
