Five Isle Meadow

Access to:
East: Memorial Pillar
West: Five Island
Accessible in the area: Rocket Warehouse

Utility:
Cut is required to access a Max Potion.
Surf is required to access a PP Up, as well as Memorial Pillar to the east. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Pokémon

  • Wild Sentret have a 5% chance of holding an Oran Berry.
  • Wild Meowth have a 5% chance of holding a Nugget.
