Places of interest:
Janine appears in the western gate between 4:00-5:59 PM (16:00-17:59). She will give the player her Pokégear number when spoken to. After her Pokégear number is obtained, she will appear here between 6:00-7:59 PM (18:00-19:59), and speaking to her will enable the player to take a picture with her.
Cameron appears here and can take photos on Sundays and Thursdays.
