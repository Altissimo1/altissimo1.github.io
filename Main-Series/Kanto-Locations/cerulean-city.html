Places of interest:
Move Tutor: The Tamer in the Pokémon Center will teach the player's Partner Pikachu Zippy Zap, or the player's Partner Eevee Bouncy Bubble, Buzzy Buzz, and Sizzly Slide. After speaking to the Tamer in Celadon City, the Tamer here will additionally offer Floaty Fall for Pikachu, and Glitzy Glow and Baddy Bad for Eevee. After speaking to the Tamer in Fuchsia City, the Tamer here will additionally offer Splishy Splash for Pikachu, and Sappy Seed, Freezy Frost, and Sparkly Swirl for Eevee.
Poké Mart
Cerulean Gym: The second Gym. It specializes in Water-types. The player must have a Pokémon at level 15 or higher to be allowed to enter.

Utility:
Sea Skim is required to access Cerulean Cave, Coach Trainer Harjit, and Psychic Avery in Cerulean City, and Beauty Camille and a PP Max on Route 4, by Sea Skimming through the city from Route 24.
