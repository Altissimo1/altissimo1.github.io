Access to:
South: Water Labyrinth
Accessible in the area: Lost Cave

Places of interest:
The VS Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Surf is required to access and traverse this area. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Selphy

After rescuing Selphy in the Lost Cave, she will return to her house. When spoken to, she will ask to see a specific Pokémon. If she is shown the Pokémon before the player has taken 250 steps, the player will receive a random item from the list below.
