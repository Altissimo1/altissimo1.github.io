Places of interest:
Brock appears at the northern entrance between 12:00-2:59 PM (12:00-14:59) after obtaining all 16 Badges. He will give the player his Pokégear number when spoken to. After his Pokégear number is obtained, he will appear here between 5:00-7:59 PM (17:00-19:59), and speaking to him will enable the player to take a picture with him. He can be traded with on Mondays, Thursdays, and Saturdays.

Utility:
Rock Climb is required to access a Calcium, a PP Max, and a Rock Incense.
