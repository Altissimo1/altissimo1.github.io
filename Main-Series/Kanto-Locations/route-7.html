Walking Pokémon

Walking Pokémon can find Nanab Berries, Silver Nanab Berries, and Golden Nanab Berries in the bush east of the Underground Path entrance.

Randomly Determined Items

A hidden Pretty Wing, Pearl, Big Pearl, or Nugget can be found in the following areas:

  • In the Underground Path, on the 15th red tile from the eastern exit
  • In the Underground Path, on the 23rd middle gray tile from the western exit

A hidden Repel, Super Repel, Max Repel, Lure, Super Lure, or Max Lure can be found in the Underground Path, on the 38th bottom gray tile from the western exit.
A hidden Fresh Water, Potion, Super Potion, or Hyper Potion can be found in the Underground Path, on the 31st blue tile from the eastern exit.
