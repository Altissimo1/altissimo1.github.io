Five Island

Access to:
North: Water Labyrinth
East: Five Isle Meadow
Accessible from the city (via the Seagallop Ferries): Vermilion City, One Island, Two Island, Three Isle Port, Four Island, Six Island, and Seven Island

Utility:
Surf is required to travel to Water Labyrinth in the north. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Pokémon

  • Wild Shellder have a 50% chance of holding a Pearl and a 5% chance of holding a Big Pearl.
  • Wild Staryu have a 50% chance of holding Stardust and a 5% chance of holding a Star Piece.
