Route 28

Access to:
North: Victory Road (through the gate)
West: Mt. Silver
South: Route 26 (through the gate)
East: Route 22 (through the gate)

Utility:
Cut is required at Mt. Silver to access TM47 Steel Wing and a Rare Candy.
Surf can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Access to:
North: Victory Road (through the gate)
West: Mt. Silver
South: Route 26 (through the gate)
East: Route 22 (through the gate)

Places of interest:
Janine appears in the eastern gate between 4:00 PM and 6:00 PM. She will give the player her Pokégear number when spoken to.

Utility:
Cut is required at Mt. Silver to access TM47 Steel Wing, TM35 Flamethrower, and a Rare Candy.
Surf can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:

Pokémon

  • In the Japanese and Korean versions of Gold and Silver, Ursaring appears instead of Donphan in Silver, and Donphan appears instead of Ursaring in Gold.
  • Wild Sneasel have a 2% chance of holding a Quick Claw.
  • Wild Dodrio have a 2% chance of holding a Sharp Beak.
  • Wild Poliwhirl have a 2% chance of holding a King's Rock.
  • Wild Tangela can flee.
