Places of interest:
Poké Mart
Fuchsia Gym: The fifth Gym. It specializes in Poison-types. The player must have caught 50 or more species of Pokémon to be allowed to enter.
Secret Technique: The Safari Zone Warden will teach the player's Partner Pokémon Strong Push after returning his Gold Teeth to him.
Secret Technique: The man with a Lapras will teach the player's Partner Pokémon Sea Skim.
Move Tutor: A Trainer in the Pokémon Center will teach the player's Partner Pikachu Zippy Zap, Floaty Fall, or Splishy Splash, or the player's Partner Eevee Bouncy Bubble, Buzzy Buzz, Sizzly Slide, Glitzy Glow, Baddy Bad, Sappy Seed, Freezy Frost, and Sparkly Swirl.

Utility:
Strong Push is required to access the Diglett who gives the player a daily Nugget in the Safari Zone Warden's house.
