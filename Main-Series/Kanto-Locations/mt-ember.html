Mt. Ember

Access to:
South: Kindle Road

Places of interest:
Move Tutor: A man in the outer area can teach one Pokémon Explosion.

Utility:
Surf is required to access this area.
Strength is reqired to navigate this area.
Rock Smash is required to access a Fire Stone. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Rock Smash.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:

Pokémon

  • Wild Fearow have a 5% chance of holding a Sharp Beak.
  • Wild Machoke have a 5% chance of holding a Focus Band.
