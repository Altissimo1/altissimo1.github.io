Walking Pokémon

Walking Ground-type Pokémon can find Stardust or Star Pieces in the following locations:

  • 1F, in the sand patch south of the northern entrance
  • 1F, in the sand patch south of Hiker Claus
  • B1F, in the sand patch near the ladder east of Poké Maniac Winston
  • B1F, in the sand patch southwest of Black Belt Steve

Walking Rock-type Pokémon can find items in the following locations:

  • 1F, from a crevice in a rock southeast of Camper Lenny. The item will be a Tiny Mushroom, Big Mushroom, or Fire Stone.
  • 1F, from a crevice in a rock southeast of Camper Leroy. The item will be a Tiny Mushroom, Big Mushroom, or Thunder Stone.
  • B1F, from a crevice in a rock in the southwest area. The item will be a Tiny Mushroom, Big Mushroom, or Water Stone.

Walking Fighting-type Pokémon can find Stardust, Star Pieces, or Nuggets in the following locations:

  • 1F, on the northern wall in the horizontal hallway south of the northern entrance
  • B1F, on the northern wall north of Black Belt Dudley
  • B1F, on the northern wall west of Poké Maniac Cooper
