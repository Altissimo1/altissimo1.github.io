Water Path

Access to:
North: Green Path
West: Six Island
South: Ruin Valley

Places of interest:
The VS Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Surf is required to access the northern half of the area and travel to Green Path, as well as to access a Dragon Scale in the southern part of the area. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:

Pokémon

  • Wild Sentret have a 5% chance of holding an Oran Berry.
  • Wild Fearow have a 5% chance of holding a Sharp Beak.
  • Wild Meowth have a 5% chance of holding a Nugget.
