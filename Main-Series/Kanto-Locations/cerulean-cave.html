Walking Pokémon

Walking Water-type Pokémon can find Pearls, Big Pearls, or Heart Scales at the edge of the pool of water on B1F.

Walking Pokémon can find Pearls or Big Pearls in the following locations:

  • 1F, from a glowing spot near the western ladder to 2F
  • 2F, from a glowing spot west of the large crystal at the center

Walking Pokémon can find Stardust or Star Pieces in the following locations:

  • 2F, from a glowing spot in the northeast
  • B1F, from a glowing spot northeast of the central plateau

Randomly Determined Items

2F has eleven spots where hidden items can be found. All spots are 'glowing' and have a 10%-30% (depending on the spot) chance of regenerating for every 256 steps the player takes. Five of the spots yield items in the Poké Ball family, five yield Berries, and one (in front of the large crystal at the center of the floor) yields Fossils and Max Revives. The exact locations of each spot can be found here, in the 'Cerulean Cave Locations' tab. The specific chance of finding each item type is shown in the tables below.
