Birth Island

Access to:
Accessible from the area (via the Seagallop Ferry): Vermilion City

Accessing this area requires the Auroraticket, which was only obtainable via a Mystery Gift download. The Auroraticket can be obtained with hacks or cheats, and Birth Island itself is accessible in Emerald with the use of a glitch.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:

Pokémon

When the island is first reached, there is a black triangle. The player must interact with the black triangle, causing it to move. The player must then interact with the triangle in its new location by taking the shortest path to the triangle. If the shortest path is not taken, the triangle will return to its base position.
Once the triangle has been interacted with enough times, it becomes red, and returns to the middle again. Interacting with it one final time will cause Deoxys to appear.
The path the player needs to take at each stage of the interaction is as follows:

  1. For the very first press, press A while standing south of the triangle.
  2. Go west 5 tiles, south 1, and press A.
  3. Go east 5 tiles, north 5, and press A.
  4. Go east 5 tiles, south 5, and press A.
  5. Go north 3 tiles, west 7, and press A.
  6. Go east 5 tiles and press A.
  7. Go west 3 tiles, south 2, and press A.
  8. Go south 1 tile, west 4, and press A.
  9. Go east 7 tiles and press A.
  10. Go west 4 tiles, south 1, and press A.
  11. Go north 4 tiles and press A.
