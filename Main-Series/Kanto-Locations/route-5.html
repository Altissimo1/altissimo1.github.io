Randomly Determined Items

A hidden Pretty Wing, Pearl, Big Pearl, or Nugget can be found in the following areas:

  • In the Underground Path, on the 23rd blue tile from the northern exit
  • In the Underground Path, on the 31st blue tile from the southern exit

A hidden Repel, Super Repel, Max Repel, Lure, Super Lure, or Max Lure can be found in the Underground Path, on the 31st red tile from the southern exit.
A hidden Fresh Water, Potion, Super Potion, or Hyper Potion can be found in the Underground Path, on the 12th red tile from the southern exit.
