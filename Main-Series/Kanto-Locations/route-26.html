Route 26

Access to:
North: Victory Road (through the gate)
West: Route 28 (through the gate)
South: Route 27
East: Route 22 (through the gate)

Places of interest:
The lady in the house next to the Ice Berry tree can heal the player's Pokémon.

Utility:
Surf can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Access to:
North: Victory Road (through the gate)
West: Route 28 (through the gate)
South: Route 27
East: Route 22 (through the gate)

Places of interest:
Janine appears in the northern gate between 4:00-5:59 PM (16:00-17:59). She will give the player her Pokégear number when spoken to. After her Pokégear number is obtained, she will appear here between 6:00-7:59 PM (18:00-19:59), and speaking to her will enable the player to take a picture with her.
Cameron appears here and can take photos on Tuesdays and Fridays.

Utility:
Surf can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Pokémon

  • Wild Dodrio have a 2% chance of holding a Sharp Beak.
  • Wild Shellder have a 23% chance of holding a Pearl and a 2% chance of holding a Big Pearl.
  • Wild Butterfree have a 2% chance of holding Silverpowder.
  • Wild Beedrill have a 2% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Quagsire can flee.
