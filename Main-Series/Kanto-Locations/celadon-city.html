Places of interest:
Celadon Gym: The fourth Gym. It specializes in Grass-types.

Fortune Teller: For 10,000, a Channeler in the Pokémon Center will allow the player to choose a Nature for wild Pokémon to have for the remainder of the day.
Secret Technique: After obtaining the Silph Scope, a Rocker in front of the Game Corner will teach the player's Partner Pokémon Sky Dash.
