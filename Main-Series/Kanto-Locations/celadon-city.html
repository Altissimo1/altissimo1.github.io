Erika appears around the Celadon City fountain between 3:00-4:59 PM (15:00-16:59) on Saturdays and Sundays after obtaining all 16 Badges. She will give the player her Pokégear number when spoken to. After her Pokégear number is obtained, she will appear her on Mondays, and speaking with her will enable the player to take a picture with her.
A man who can check the Hidden Power type of the player's Pokémon is located in the prize center for the Game Corner.

Access to:
West: Route 16
East: Route 7
Accessible in the city: Rocket Hideout

Places of interest:
Celadon Gym: The fourth Gym. It specializes in Grass-types. The player must show a "cute Pokémon" to be allowed to enter (the Partner Pikachu and Eevee work for this).
Game Freak Rooms: A Game Freak employee on the third floor of Celadon Condominiums will give the player a certificate if they have 150 Kanto Pokédex Pokémon - all of them excluding Mew - caught in the Pokédex.
Move Tutor: A Tamer in the Pokémon Center will teach the player's Partner Pikachu Zippy Zap and Floaty Fall, or the player's Partner Eevee Bouncy Bubble, Buzzy Buzz, Sizzly Slide, Glitzy Glow, and Baddy Bad. After speaking to the Tamer in Fuchsia City, the Tamer here will additionally offer Splishy Splash for Pikachu, and Sappy Seed, Freezy Frost, and Sparkly Swirl for Eevee.
Fortune Teller: For 10,000, a Channeler in the Pokémon Center will allow the player to choose a Nature for wild Pokémon to have for the remainder of the day.
Secret Technique: After obtaining the Silph Scope, a Rocker in front of the Game Corner will teach the player's Partner Pokémon Sky Dash.
