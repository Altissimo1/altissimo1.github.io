Walking Pokémon

Walking Pokémon can find Pinap Berries, Silver Pinap Berries, and Golden Pinap Berries in the yellow bush south of Camper Ruben on Route 16.
Walking Pokémon can find Nanab Berries, Silver Nanab Berries, and Golden Nanab Berries in the yellow bush southeast of the northernmost grass patch.

Randomly Determined Items

These items are hidden and will regenerate after every 256 steps taken by the player.

A Razz Berry, Razz Berry (3x), Nanab Berry, Nanab Berry (3x), Pinap Berry, or Pinap Berry (3x) can be found in the following areas:

  • In the yellow bush in the northwest corner
  • Between the two yellow bushes west of Jolteon
  • In the yellow bush east of Flareon
  • In the yellow bush at the northwest corner of the northernmost grass patch
  • In the yellow bush at the southwest corner of the northernmost grass patch
  • In the yellow bush south of the grass patch north of the pond
  • In the yellow bush east of Coach Trainer Grantley, south of the pond
  • In the yellow bush northeast of the southernmost grass patch
  • In the yellow bush north of the bench north of Gentleman Orson
  • In the yellow bush southeast of Picnicker Misaki

5 Razz Berries or a single Silver Razz Berry can be found in a yellow bush north of Jolteon and will regenerate for every 256 steps taken.
5 Nanab Berries or a single Silver Nanab Berry can be found between the two yellow bushes north of the grass patch north of the pond and will regenerate for every 256 steps taken.
5 Pinap Berries or a single Silver Pinap Berry can be found along the eastern fence, near the middle lamppost east of the pond and will regenerate for every 256 steps taken.
