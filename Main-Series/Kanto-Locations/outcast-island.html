Outcast Island

Access to:
South: Green Path
Accessible on the route: Altering Cave

Places of interest:
The VS Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Surf is required to access and traverse this area. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
