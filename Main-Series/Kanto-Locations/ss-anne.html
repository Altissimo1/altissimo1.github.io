S.S. Anne

Access to:
North exit: Vermilion City

Access to:

Access to:
North exit: Vermilion City

Places of interest:
A Lava Cookie can be obtained near the hidden truck east of the ship. The area requires Surf to get to. The player can get to this area by one of two methods: 1) trading a Pokémon with Cut from another game, and never collecting HM01 Cut on the S.S. Anne until after acquiring Surf, or 2) losing a battle on the S.S. Anne after acquiring HM01 Cut, and then not returning until after acquiring Surf.

Utility:
Surf is required to access the secret Lava Cookie.

Access to:
North exit: Vermilion City

Places of interest:
Secret Technique: The captain will teach the player's Partner Pokémon Chop Down.
