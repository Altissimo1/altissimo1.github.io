Blaine appears on the island on Tuesdays after obtaining all 16 Badges. He will give the player his Pokégear number when spoken to. After his Pokégear number is obtained, he will appear here on Fridays, and speaking to him will enable the player to take a picture with him.
After defeating Red, Cameron can take photos of the player with Blue here on Fridays.
