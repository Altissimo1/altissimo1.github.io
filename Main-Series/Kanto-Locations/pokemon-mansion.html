Places of interest:
The Master Trainers for Ditto, Mewtwo, Mew, Meltan, and Melmetal are located here. They cannot be battled, but will confer the title of Master Trainer on the player for their respective Pokémon species if the player shows them that species with a high enough CP. The CP values needed to receive the title are as follows:
Ditto: 4000
Mewtwo: 9000
Mew: 8000
Meltan: 4000
Melmetal: 8000
