Places of interest:
The beds on B1F can heal the player's Pokémon when interacted with.
The Master Trainers for Ditto, Mewtwo, Mew, Meltan, and Melmetal are located here. They cannot be battled, but will confer the title of Master Trainer on the player for their respective Pokémon species if the player shows them that species with a high enough CP. The CP values needed to receive the title, and the location of each Trainer, are as follows:
Ditto: 4000. The Trainer is located in the east room on 3F.
Mewtwo: 9000. The Trainer is located in the southern room with the statue on B1F.
Mew: 8000. The Trainer is located in the room with the tanks where the Secret Key is on B1F.
Meltan: 4000. The Trainer is located in the southwest part of B1F.
Melmetal: 8000. The Trainer is located in the northern room with the bookshelves on B1F.

Randomly Determined Items

Hidden Health Candies, Mighty Candies, Tough Candies, Smart Candies, Courage Candies, or Quick Candies can be found in the following areas:

  • On 1F, on the cracked tile southwest of the third pillar from the south on the east side of the hallway
  • On 2F, at the edge of the floor west of the southeastern ladder, accessible from 3F
  • On 3F, on the southwest part of the bookshelf in the southwest
  • On B1F, between the two beds, on the eastern dresser
  • On B1F, on the dresser west of Scientist Ivan
  • On B1F, in the northern room with the bookshelves, on a pile of books to the west
  • On B1F, in the west area, south of the southwestern tank
  • On B1F, in the southwestern area, on the eastern bookshelf
