Kindle Road

Access to:
North: Mt. Ember
West: One Island

Places of interest:
The VS Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.
Standing in the water in the Ember Spa will heal the player's Pokémon.

Utility:
Surf is required to traverse the area and travel to Mt. Ember. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Rock Smash is required to access an Ether and a Carbos. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Rock Smash.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:

Pokémon

  • Wild Fearow have a 5% chance of holding a Sharp Beak.
  • Wild Meowth have a 5% chance of holding a Nugget.
  • Wild Graveler have a 5% chance of holding a Hard Stone.
