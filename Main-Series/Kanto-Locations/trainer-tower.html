Trainer Tower

This page is about the map area called "Trainer Tower". For information on how the facility itself functions, check out this Trainer Tower page instead.

Access to:
South: Seven Island
Accessible in the area: The Trainer Tower facility

Places of interest:
The VS Seeker can be used in this area to rematch Trainers.

Utility:
Surf is required to access the northern half of the area. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
