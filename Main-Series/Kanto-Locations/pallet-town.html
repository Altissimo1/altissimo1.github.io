Professor Oak's Lab
Daisy will check the player's Pokémon's friendship and can groom one Pokémon to increase its friendship and Beauty stat between 3:00-3:59 PM (15:00-15:59) daily. She will also provide Blue's Pokégear number after the player's Pokémon have had a total of seven massages.
Cameron appears here and can take photos on Wednesdays, Thursdays, and Fridays.
After completing the National Dex (except for Mythical Pokémon), Cameron can take photos of the player with Professor Oak here.

If the player wins both battles, Eevee will evolve into Jolteon.
