All Fossils can be revived at the Pewter Museum. However, Helix and Claw Fossils can only be found naturally in HeartGold, while Dome and Root Fossils can only be found naturally in SoulSilver. Old Amber can be found in both games. Skull and Armor Fossils cannot be found in either game, and must be transferred from Diamond, Pearl, or Platinum.
