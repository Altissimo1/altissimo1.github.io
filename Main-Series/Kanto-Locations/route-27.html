Route 27

Access to:
West: New Bark Town
East: Route 26
Accessible on the route: Tohjo Falls

Utility:
Surf is required to access Tohjo Falls and Route 26, as well as to access a Rare Candy. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Surf and Whirlpool are required to access TM22 Solarbeam and Bird Keeper Jose.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Access to:

East: Route 26

Utility:

Pokémon

  • Wild Butterfree have a 2% chance of holding Silverpowder.
