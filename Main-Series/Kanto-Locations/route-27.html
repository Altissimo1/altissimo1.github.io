Route 27

Access to:
West: New Bark Town
East: Route 26
Accessible on the route: Tohjo Falls

Utility:
Surf is required to access Tohjo Falls and Route 26, as well as a Rare Candy. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Surf and Whirlpool are required to access TM22 Solarbeam and Bird Keeper Jose.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Access to:
West: New Bark Town
East: Route 26
Accessible on the route: Tohjo Falls

Utility:
Surf is required to access Tohjo Falls and Route 26, as well as a Nugget. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.

Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Pokémon

  • Wild Dodrio have a 2% chance of holding a Sharp Beak.
  • Wild Shellder have a 23% chance of holding a Pearl and a 2% chance of holding a Big Pearl.
  • Wild Butterfree have a 2% chance of holding Silverpowder.
  • Wild Beedrill have a 2% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
  • Wild Quagsire can flee.
