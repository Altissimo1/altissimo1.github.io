Places of interest:
In Mt. Moon Square, Clefairy can be seen dancing between 12 AM and 3:59 AM on Monday mornings, and again between 6 PM and 11:59 PM Monday nights. If the player views the dance in the morning, they will not see it again the same night. Interrupting the dance gives the player access to a Moon Stone.

Utility:
Rock Smash is required to access the Moon Stone left in Mt. Moon Square after the Clefairy leave.

Walking Pokémon

Walking Paras and Parasect can find Tiny Mushrooms or Big Mushrooms in the following locations:

  • B1F, in the area accessible from the easternmost ladder on 1F, east of the ladder to B2F
  • B1F, in the area accessible from the ladder near Youngster Josh on 1F, between the 1F and B2F ladders
  • B1F, in the area accessible from the northernmost ladder on 1F, south of the ladder to 1F
  • B2F, in the area accessible from the easternmost ladder on 1F, east of the Team Rocket Grunt
  • B2F, on the path south of the Fossils

Walking Fighting-types can find Stardust, Star Pieces, or Nuggets in the following locations:

  • 1F, on the wall southeast of Bug Catcher Kent
  • 1F, on the northern wall northwest of Lass Miriam
  • B2F, in the area accessible from the northernmost ladder on 1F, on the northern wall next to a crater
