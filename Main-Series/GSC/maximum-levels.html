Gold, Silver, and Crystal Maximum Levels

This page lists the highest possible level at which Pokémon can be obtained in Gold, Silver, and Crystal.

Only Pokémon that can be found in the wild, as gifts, or via purchasing are listed here, since the player can obviously evolve a Pokémon or complete an in-game trade at any level.

The three Kanto starters and their evolutions, all Kanto legendaries, and Omanyte, Omastar, Kabuto, and Kabutops are excluded from the table, as those Pokémon cannot be obtained normally in Gold, Silver, and Crystal.
