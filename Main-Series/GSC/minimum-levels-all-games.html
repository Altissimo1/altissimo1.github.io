Gold, Silver, and Crystal Minimum Levels (All Games)

This is a list of the lowest level at which you can obtain every Pokémon in Gold, Silver, and Crystal. It does not contain levels attainable via glitches. However, it does assume you have access to all of Red, Green, Blue, Yellow, Gold, Silver, Crystal, Pokémon Stadium, and Pokémon Stadium 2.
Some Pokémon can be found at slightly different lowest levels depending on whether the player has access to Japanese Blue or not. Use the radio buttons above to switch between the Japanese games (RGBY) and the international games (RBY).
