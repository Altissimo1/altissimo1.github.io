Generation 1 & 2 Games

The following table contains every Pokémon and the games in Generations 1 & 2 (Red, Green, Blue, Yellow, Gold, Silver, Crystal) that it can be obtained in. Use the radio buttons above to switch between the Japanese games (RGBY) and the international games (RBY). When the RGBY option is selected, the tables will display Teddiursa as appearing in Silver and Phanpy as appearing in Gold, to match the Japanese releases of those games.

For specific encounter locations in each game, see this page for Gold, Silver, and Crystal and this page for Red, Green, Blue, and Yellow.

Celebi can only be obtained without an event in the 3DS Virtual Console releases of Crystal.
