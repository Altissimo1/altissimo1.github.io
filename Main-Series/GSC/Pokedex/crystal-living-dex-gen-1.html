Completing a Living Dex With Gens 1 and 2 (Pokémon Crystal)

A "living dex" is a fan designation for when a player completes the Pokédex and has one of each species in the PC or the party at the same time. It's often considered to be much more difficult and time-consuming than a regular Pokédex because it often involves, for instance, playing the same game multiple times to get multiple copies of a Legendary Pokémon.

The method by which you can get a (mostly) completed Pokédex on Crystal is described here. This page will focus more on requirements specific to a living dex.

You will need access to at minimum three separate save files of Gold, Silver, or Crystal, across two different cartridges, as well as three save files of Red, Green, Blue or Yellow (two if you have access to Yellow) in order to obtain all starters. Since you can breed them, only three files are required in both cases. Your trade process should look something like this:

  1. Collect Johto starter #1 and trade it to the second copy of Gold, Silver, or Crystal.
  2. Collect Johto starter #2 and trade it to the second copy of Gold, Silver, or Crystal.
  3. Collect Johto starter #3 in either copy. Trade the other two starters around and breed them as needed.
  4. Start a save file on a copy of Red, Green, Blue, or Yellow. If it is not Yellow, collect Kanto starter #1 and one of the two Fossils; if it is Yellow, collect all three starters and one of the Fossils. Trade the starter(s) and Fossil to Gold, Silver, or Crystal.
  5. Collect the second Fossil. If your game is not Yellow, collect a second Kanto starter as well, and trade them to Gold, Silver, or Crystal.
  6. If your game is not Yellow, collect the third and final starter and trade it to Gold, Silver, or Crystal.

With access to Pokémon Stadium, you only need a single save file on Red, Green, Blue, or Yellow, as you can obtain the starters and Fossils by defeating the Elite Four and Rival. You will still need a copy in order to trade them to Gold, Silver, or Crystal, though.

Celebi

This is just to note that if you have access to a GS Ball Celebi, keep in mind that Gold and Silver cannot obtain it. if you wanted to, for instance, complete a living dex of Gold or Silver and Crystal simultaneously, you would need to get multiple Celebi in Crystal, requiring multiple save files that will each need to defeat the Elite Four.
