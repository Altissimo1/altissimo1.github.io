How Many Pokémon Can You Get Without Evolving or Breeding in Pokémon Crystal?

The player can get up to 206, or 207 with the Virtual Console, species total in Crystal without trading, as described here. Without evolving any Pokémon, and only collecting evolutionary stages that the player can get in the wild, as a gift or purchase, or from an in-game trade, the player can get up to 164/165 species. Of the 206/207 possible species in Crystal, the Pokémon unavailable without evolving are as follows:

Starter Evolutions (2) - 2 total
The Starters are only available in their base forms, leaving their evolutions inaccessible.
Stone Evolutions (17) - 19 total
Raichu, Nidoqueen, Nidoking, Clefable, Wigglytuff, Vileplume, Arcanine, Poliwrath, Victreebel, Cloyster, Exeggutor, Starmie, Eevee's evolutions, Bellossom, and Sunflora are inaccessible.
Baby Pokémon (5) - 24 total
Without the ability to breed, five of Pichu, Cleffa, Igglybuff, Smoochum, Elekid, and Magby are inaccessible.
Other (18) - 42 total
Pidgeot, Dewgong, Hitmonlee, Hitmonchan, Dragonite, Crobat, Togetic, Azumarill, Jumpluff, Espeon, Umbreon, Forretress, Magcargo, Piloswine, Houndoom, Hitmontop, Blissey, Tyranitar

If Celebi cannot be obtained, this number is instead 43 unobtainable Pokémon.
