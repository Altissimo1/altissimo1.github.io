Kurt

Kurt in Azalea Town makes custom Poké Balls in exchange for Apricorns. He will make one ball for each Apricorn provided, and each of the balls can be obtained multiple times. The balls that he can make are shown below.

Player's Mother

If the money the player has saved with their mother is an exact multiple of 2300, the player's mother may purchase a Poké Ball or Great Ball.

Mystery Gift

Great Balls can be obtained via Mystery Gift.

Bird Keeper Vance

In Crystal only, Bird Keeper Vance on Route 44 may call to offer a Poké Ball, Great Ball, or Ultra Ball if his number is registered in the Pokégear.
