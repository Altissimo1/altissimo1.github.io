Gold, Silver, and Crystal Held Item Locations

The following lists and table contain the location of all items that can have an effect on a Pokémon when held, except for Berries, which can be found here.

Bug-Catching Contest

Everstones can be obtained for winning second place in the Bug-Catching Contest.

Mystery Gift

The Scope Lens can be obtained via Mystery Gift.

Pokégear Calls

In Crystal only, Picnicker Tiffany can give away Pink Bows if her phone number is registered in the Pokégear.

Trainer Pokémon Held Items

Some Trainers have held items on their Pokémon. If the player's Pokémon uses Thief on the enemy Pokémon, and does not have a held item, the user Pokémon will be able to obtain the enemy Pokémon's held item permanently. The list of Trainers with stealable held items is as follows:

In-Game Trade

In Crystal only, the Dodrio that the player can receive in a trade in Blackthorn City holds a Smoke Ball.

In Crystal only, the Magneton that the player can receive in a trade in the Power Plant holds a Metal Coat.
