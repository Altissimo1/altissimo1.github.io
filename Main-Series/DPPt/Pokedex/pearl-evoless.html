How Many Pokémon Can You Get Without Evolving or Breeding in Pokémon Pearl?

The player can get up to 357 species total (395 with dual-slot encounters) in Pearl without trading, as described here. Without evolving or breeding any Pokémon, and only collecting evolutionary stages that the player can get in the wild, as a gift, or from an in-game trade, the player can get up to 242 species (270 with dual-slot encounters). Of the 357 possible species in Pearl, the Pokémon unavailable without evolving or breeding are as follows:

Starter Evolutions (2) - 2 total
The starters are only available in their base forms, leaving their evolutions inaccessible.
Stone Evolutions (22) - 24 total
Raichu, Nidoqueen, Nidoking, Clefable, Wigglytuff, Vileplume, Bellossom, Poliwrath, Victreebel, Cloyster, Exeggutor, Starmie, Vaporeon, Jolteon, Flareon, Togekiss, Sunflora, Mismagius, Gallade, Delcatty, Froslass, and Roserade are inaccessible.
Breeding Pokémon (8) - 32 total
Without the ability to breed, Koffing, Ledyba, Spinarak, Wynaut, Houndour, Taillow, Whismur, and Shuppet are inaccessible.
Fossils (5) - 37 total
The Fossils can only be revived into their base forms, leaving their evolutions inaccessible.
Other (78) - 115 total
Pidgeotto, Pidgeot, Crobat, Parasect, Persian, Rapidash, Slowbro, Magneton, Magnezone, Dodrio, Muk, Hypno, Kingler, Electrode, Marowak, Hitmonlee, Hitmonchan, Hitmontop, Lickilicky, Blissey, Mr. Mime, Jynx, Espeon, Umbreon, Leafeon, Glaceon, Snorlax, Dragonite, Furret, Togetic, Xatu, Ampharos, Azumarill, Jumpluff, Ambipom, Yanmega, Granbull, Weavile, Piloswine, Mamoswine, Mantine, Donphan, Linoone, Silcoon, Gardevoir, Masquerain, Breloom, Vigoroth, Slaking, Ninjask, Shedinja, Exploud, Hariyama, Probopass, Manectric, Swalot, Grumpig, Flygon, Altaria, Claydol, Milotic, Glalie, Walrein, Shelgon, Salamence, Metang, Metagross, Staraptor, Luxray, Wormadam, Mothim, Vespiquen, Cherrim, Drifblim, Lopunny, Gabite, Garchomp, and Lucario are inaccessible.
Dual-Slot Evolutions (10)
Even if dual-slot encounters are allowed, Butterfree, Beedrill, Ninetales, Arcanine, Electabuzz, Magmar, Forretress, Gliscor, Ludicolo, and Shiftry still cannot be obtained.
