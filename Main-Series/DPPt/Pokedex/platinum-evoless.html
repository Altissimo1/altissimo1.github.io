How Many Pokémon Can You Get Without Evolving or Breeding in Pokémon Platinum?

The player can get up to 374 species total (406 with dual-slot encounters) in Platinum without trading, as described here. Without evolving or breeding any Pokémon, and only collecting evolutionary stages that the player can get in the wild, as a gift, or from an in-game trade, the player can get up to 256 species (281 with dual-slot encounters). Of the 374 possible species in Platinum, the Pokémon unavailable without evolving or breeding are as follows:

Starter Evolutions (2) - 2 total
The starters are only available in their base forms, leaving their evolutions inaccessible.
Stone Evolutions (21) - 23 total
Raichu, Nidoqueen, Nidoking, Clefable, Wigglytuff, Vileplume, Bellossom, Poliwrath, Victreebel, Cloyster, Exeggutor, Starmie, Vaporeon, Jolteon, Flareon, Togekiss, Sunflora, Gallade, Delcatty, Roserade, and Froslass are inaccessible.
Breeding Pokémon (10) - 33 total
Without the ability to breed, Seel, Elekid, Magby, Ledyba, Spinarak, Wynaut, Taillow, Whismur, Shuppet, and Spheal are inaccessible.
Fossils (5) - 38 total
The Fossils can only be revived into their base forms, leaving their evolutions inaccessible.
Other (80) - 118 total
Pidgeotto, Pidgeot, Crobat, Parasect, Persian, Rapidash, Slowbro, Magnezone, Dodrio, Muk, Hypno, Kingler, Electrode, Marowak, Hitmonlee, Hitmonchan, Hitmontop, Lickilicky, Blissey, Tangrowth, Espeon, Umbreon, Leafeon, Glaceon, Snorlax, Dragonite, Furret, Lanturn, Togetic, Xatu, Ampharos, Skiploom, Jumpluff, Ambipom, Yanmega, Gliscor, Granbull, Weavile, Mamoswine, Mantine, Houndoom, Donphan, Pupitar, Tyranitar, Mightyena, Linoone, Gardevoir, Breloom, Vigoroth, Slaking, Ninjask, Shedinja, Exploud, Hariyama, Probopass, Lairon, Aggron, Manectric, Swalot, Sharpedo, Grumpig, Altaria, Claydol, Milotic, Glalie, Walrein, Shelgon, Salamence, Metang, Metagross, Staraptor, Luxray, Wormadam, Mothim, Vespiquen, Cherrim, Drifblim, Lopunny, Garchomp, and Lucario are inaccessible.
Dual-Slot Evolutions (7)
Even if dual-slot encounters are allowed, Butterfree, Beedrill, Ninetales, Arcanine, Forretress, Ludicolo, and Shiftry still cannot be obtained.
