Generation 4 Minimum Levels

This is a list of the lowest level at which you can obtain every Pokémon in Diamond, Pearl, Platinum, HeartGold, SoulSilver, and the Pokéwalker, excluding levels obtainable via Pal Park transfer from a GBA game. It does not contain levels attainable via glitches. However, it does assume you have access to all five games and the Pokéwalker.

Thanks to QuentinQuonce of Smogon for sending me a spreadsheet that made preparing this page much easier. I've cross-checked it with my own minimum level pages as well.
