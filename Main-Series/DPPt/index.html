Diamond, Pearl, and Platinum Home

Diamond, Pearl, and Platinum were released between 2006 and 2009 for the Nintendo DS. They are compatible with each other, as well as HeartGold and SoulSilver, also for the Nintendo DS. They are capable of communicating with the Nintendo Wii, allowing the player to use Pokémon from these games in Pokémon Battle Revolution. Using the Game Boy Advance slot of an original model DS, Pokémon from Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald, FireRed, and LeafGreen can be sent to these games, although they cannot be returned to any Game Boy Advance or GameCube game. Pokémon from Diamond, Pearl, and Platinum can be sent to Black, White, Black 2, and White 2, although they cannot be returned from these games.

The first 493 Pokémon are all programmed into the game. Between the three games, the player can access all Sinnoh Pokémon, as well as a wide variety of Kanto, Johto, and Hoenn Pokémon. Pokémon that are missing from this game can be traded in from HeartGold or SoulSilver, or transferred from Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald, FireRed, or LeafGreen.
