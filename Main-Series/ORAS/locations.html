Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire Pokémon Locations

The following table contains every Pokémon that can be obtained in Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire all of its locations. Use the radio buttons below to determine which Pokémon should be shown.

All Pokémon in Gens 1 through 5 can be transferred into Gen 6 by first transferring them into Pokémon Bank via the Poké Transporter, and then by putting them into a Gen 6 game. If a Pokémon is not in Omega Ruby or Alpha Sapphire, the table shown will only mention trading them from X or Y. The only Pokémon that require transfer from a previous generation are the Summer, Autumn, and Winter forms of Deerling and Sawsbuck.
