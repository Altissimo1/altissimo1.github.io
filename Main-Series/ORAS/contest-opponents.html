Contest Opponents

The following Pokémon are used in Pokémon Contests as potential opponents. Most of them can be found in more than one Contest category, and they all have different stats for each Contest category, although they keep the same moves in every category. The exception is Lisia, who has a different moveset for her Altaria depending on which Contest category she is faced in. The stat scores shown in the below tables indicate which Contest categories they can participate in.

  • Normal Rank
  • Super Rank
  • Hyper Rank
  • Master Rank

Stat Mechanics

Every opposing Pokémon has a stat in each of the five Contest categories (Cool, Beauty, Cute, Clever, Tough). The Pokémon with the highest stat will get a fancy background when they are introduced.

The numbers used in the tables below are not the exact values calculated by the game. These values have been 'converted' to the values of hearts that the player can get in the second round of contest judging, which may make them a little easier to comprehend.

The highest score that any Pokémon could have in a Contest category is 26.50. The highest score that any enemy Pokémon has is 19.40 (Lisia's Altaria, Ali, in the Beauty and Cute categories).

Some additional resources:

  • Inundata Sheets' linked document on Contests contains the Pokémon info I used for this page and a lot of information about Contests and Pokéblocks.
  • Bulbapedia has the precise formula used by the game to determine each Pokémon's score. To get the scores used in the below table, take the score as calculated according to Bulbapedia's formulas, and divide by 20.
