Pinball Ruby & Sapphire Scoring

This page was adapted from a document written by andu on the Pokémon Pinball Discord server, with his permission. The original document can be found here.

This page contains information on every action in Pinball Ruby & Sapphire that can provide points, and the amount of points provided. If you know of anything that gives points that isn't referenced here, feel free to contact andu or myself (and I'll let andu know too).

Slots

The slot machine can sometimes award the player with Small bonuses (100, 500, or 900 points) or Big bonuses (1,000,000-9,000,000 points).

Multipliers

The default multiplier is x1, meaning that all events on the board award the player with the exact amount of points shown in the tables below. It is possible to increase this multiplier, with the result that the amount of points the player gains is multiplied by the multiplier. There are three ways to increase the multiplier:

  1. The Multiplier switch, located on the edge of the ramp to the Poké Mart in the Ruby table and below Pelipper on the Sapphire table, will increase the multiplier when hit.
  2. The default Poké Ball has an inherent x1 multiplier. Upgrading to a Great Ball changes it to x2, Ultra Ball is x3, and Master Ball is x4.
  3. The Bonus Multiplier option in the slots can raise the player's bonus Multiplier by 1 through 5. This number is added on to the existing multiplier.

Rayquaza Bonus Stage

For defeating Rayquaza the first time, the total score the player will get from the stage is 100,999,999 points. For defeating it the second time (catching it), the score is 147,999,999 points.

End of Ball Bonus

The maximum total End of Ball Bonus is 19,111,950,000, if the player manages to complete each task in the table below 99 times and has a Bonus Multiplier of 99.

Kyogre Bonus Stage

For defeating Kyogre the first time, the total score the player will get from the stage is 57,500,000 points. For defeating it the second time (catching it), the score is 69,000,000 points.

Kyogre cannot be defeated while it is in its diving and surfacing phase. This means the player can get extra hits on it during this phase, allowing for a higher possible point total.
