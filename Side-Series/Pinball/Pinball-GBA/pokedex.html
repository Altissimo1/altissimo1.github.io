Ruins

The Ruins can only be accessed after the player travels seven times (in any direction).

When entering Catch 'em Mode by entering Sharpedo (Ruby field) or Wailmer (Blue field), the Pokémon generated will be chosen from either the 2-Light group or the 3-Light group. Which group is used depends on how many of the GET lights are lit on the right loop. If the player activates Catch 'em Mode via the slots, the game will use the 3-Light group if three GET lights are lit, and the 2-Light group otherwise.

See the Locations page for details about rarity mechanics.

A few additional notes:

  • If the player has Cascoon and Dustox registered in the Pokédex, evolving Wurmple will result in Silcoon. The opposite is true if the player has Silcoon and Beautifly. Otherwise, its evolution is random.
  • Clamperl's first evolution is random and its second is the other evolution.
  • Evolving Gloom on the Ruby field results in Vileplume, and evolving it on the Sapphire field results in Bellossom.
  • Pichu will only appear after catching 5 Pokémon in the current game.
  • Latias and Latios will only appear after registering 100 Pokémon as 'caught' in the Pokédex.
