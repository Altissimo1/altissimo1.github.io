These tables show which Pokémon are available in each field, and what their probability of appearing is.

The following two tables show which maps are available in each field in Groups 1 and 2. Group 1 maps can be selected when a game begins, and can be traveled to on the player's first and second move attempts. Group 2 maps can be traveled to on the third and fourth move attempts.

On the 5th map move, the player will move to Indigo Plateau, and the number of map moves made resets to 0, meaning the maps the player can move to reset as though starting a new board.

When entering Catch 'em Mode by entering Bellsprout (Red field) or Cloyster (Blue field), the Pokémon generated will either be from the 2-Light group or the 3-Light group. Which group is used depends on how many lights are lit on the right loop. If the player activates Catch 'em Mode via the slots, the game will pick a group at random; each group has a 50% chance of being chosen.
