Trozei List

The following table contains every Pokémon that can be obtained in Trozei and all of its locations.

To the best of current knowledge, Legendary Pokémon can only be obtained by using Espionage Mode with someone who has a Legendary on their Agent Card.

This does not include Pokémon that can be encountered in the Battle, Pair, or Espionage Modes, just due to a lack of information about those modes. If you can provide any insight, please contact me. Regardless, all Pokémon (except the Legendaries) can be obtained between the Adventure, Endless, and Forever Modes.
