Endless Mode

Endless Mode, accessible from the main menu, allows the player to play until they get a Game Over. There are 99 levels in Endless Mode. The higher the level number, the faster Pokémon will fall, and the more Pokémon will need to be cleared to complete the level. After completing level 99, the levels will repeat from 1.

Upon first playing the mode, the player can start from level 1 or any multiple of 5 up to and including 20. Every time the player successfully completes a level whose number is a multiple of 5, that level will become available for the player to start from when beginning Endless Mode.
