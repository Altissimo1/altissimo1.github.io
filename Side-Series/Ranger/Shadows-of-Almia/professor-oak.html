Professor Oak Challenge

A Professor Oak challenge is where you catch all available Pokémon before moving on to the next story event. I have not found a guide for this game, so this page may be the first. Please contact me if you know of an existing guide or know of inaccuracies in this guide.

A total of 270 Pokémon can be obtained in this game, including the three accessible in Ranger Net missions.
