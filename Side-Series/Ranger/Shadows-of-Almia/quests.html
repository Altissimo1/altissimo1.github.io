Quests

This page contains information about the Quests that can be taken in Shadows of Almia, including the location, requirements, and rewards for each quest. Use the box below to jump to a particular quest.

The Quests here are ordered according to when they appear to be taken by the player.



Calm Down Miltank

This Quest can be received from Little Tim at the Ranger Base in Vientown after completing Mission 2: Investigate the Marine Cave!. The player must complete this Quest to progress the game.

To complete this Quest, the player must catch Little Tim's Miltank.

The reward for completing this quest is the Grass Defense 1.

Under the Bridge

This Quest can be received from a Little Girl outside the northeastern house in Chicole Village after completing Calm Down Miltank.

To complete this Quest, the player must go to the bridge west of the Ranger School and see a Floatzel there.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Water Defense 1.

Is Combee Okay?

This Quest can be received from an Old Woman near the eastern exit to Vientown after completing Mission 3: Fight the Vien Forest Fire!.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Combee in Vien Forest and show it to the Old Woman.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Electric Defense 1.

Clear Some Fallen Logs

This Quest can be received from an Mr. Woodward in the southwestern house in Vientown after completing Mission 3: Fight the Vien Forest Fire!.

To complete this Quest, the player must clear two Fallen Logs in Vien Forest.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Long Line A.

Strange Crates Washed Ashore

This Quest can be received from a Boy in the northern part of Vientown after completing Mission 3: Fight the Vien Forest Fire!.

To complete this Quest, the player must clear two Wooden Crates on Nabiki Beach.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Normal Defense 1.

Clear the Crates!

This Quest can be received from a Man in the part of Vien Forest south of Pueltown after driving off Team Dim Sun during Mission 4: Destroy the Strange Machines!.

To complete this Quest, the player must clear two Crates deeper in Vien Forest.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Bug Defense 1.

Breeze Hill Sign Recovery

This Quest can be received from Webster near the southern exit to Vientown after reuniting Ollie with his mom.

To complete this Quest, the player must clear a Rock on Nabiki Beach south of Breeze Hill.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Dark Defense 1.

Please Save Cranidos

This Quest can be received from a Young Guy near the southern exit to Vien Forest after reuniting Ollie with his mom.

To complete this Quest, the player must catch Cranidos and Wartortle in the dead-end with the tree west of the Young Guy that gives the player this Quest.

The reward for completing this Quest is the captured Cranidos, who joins the player as a potential Partner Pokémon.

The Boy Who Cried Lumineon

This Quest can be received from the Fishing Kid on the southeastern dock in Pueltown after first visiting the Ranger Union.

To complete this Quest, the player must catch Lumineon in the Puel Sea. It is located in the area with the currents where the player captured Sharpedo during Mission 5: Recover a Key from Sharpedo!.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Rock Defense 1.

Eevee for Marital Bliss

This Quest can be received from the Old Man west of the Ranger Depot in Pueltown after first visiting the Ranger Union.

To complete this Quest, the player must catch an Eevee in the easternmost part of Puel Town, near where the ledge is that allows the player to jump from the higher level of the dock.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Steel Defense 1.

Go Talk to My Mom

This Quest can be received from Brook south of the bridge in Altru Park after first visiting the Ranger Union.

To complete this Quest, the player must visit Brook's mother. She is in the eastern part of the town, in the eastern house north of the fountain.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Flying Defense 1.

Nosepass! Must Have Nosepass!

This Quest can be received from a Man in the western part of Pueltown after learning how to ride Doduo.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Nosepass in Marine Cave and show it to the Man.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Ground Defense 1.

Who Ate My Lunch?!

This Quest can be received from a Man in the area two screens north of Vien Forest after learning how to ride Doduo.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Ambipom in the screen to the west and show it to the Man.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Power Plus A.

Oddish Research

This Quest can be received from Herbert on the bridge in Altru Park after learning how to ride Doduo.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture an Oddish in Vien Forest and show it to Herbert.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Fire Defense 1.

Please Save Turtwig

This Quest can be received from a Little Boy in front of the northeastern house in Chicole Village after learning how to ride Doduo.

To complete this Quest, the player must catch Turtwig on the northern cliff of Breeze Hill in Vientown

The reward for completing this Quest is the captured Turtwig, who joins the player as a potential Partner Pokémon.

Light the Leaves

This Quest can be received from Janice on the top floor of the Ranger School after receiving Mission 9: Support Sven at the Ruins!

To complete this Quest, the player must clear three piles of Fallen Leaves at the Ranger School in the main yard.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Time Assist A.

Cherrim, Cherrim, Come to Us♪

This Quest can be received from Nectar Victoria near the pond on Union Road after receiving Mission 9: Support Sven at the Ruins!

To complete this Quest, the player must bring Nectar Victoria a Cherrim. One can be found on Peril Cliffs.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Ghost Defense 1.

Team Dim Sun's Appetite

This Quest can be received from the Elder's Granddaughter in the southwestern house in Boyleland after receiving Mission 9: Support Sven at the Ruins!

To complete this Quest, the player must speak to the Team Dim Sun member at the hidden harbor in the deepest part of Volcano Cave.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Recovery A.

That Smell of Muk...

This Quest can be received from Oscar near the entrance to Volcano Cave in Boyleland after receiving Mission 9: Support Sven at the Ruins!

To complete this Quest, the player must bring Oscar a Muk. One can be found in Volcano Cave.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Poison Defense 1.

Please Save Shieldon

This Quest can be received from a Boy on Union Road after receiving Mission 9: Support Sven at the Ruins!

To complete this Quest, the player must catch a Shieldon on the northeastern screen of the Peril Cliffs.

The reward for completing this Quest is the captured Shieldon, who joins the player as a potential Partner Pokémon.

What Reporter Hans Forgot

This Quest can be received from the player's Dad on the farm in Chicole Village after receiving Mission 9: Support Sven at the Ruins!

To complete this Quest, the player must meet Reporter Hans outside the Altru Building in Altru Park, speak to him, and return to the player's Dad.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Psychic Defense 1.

Dim... Sun... Come Back...

This Quest can be received from a Lady on the overpass across the main area of Pueltown after completing Mission 9: Support Sven at the Ruins!

To complete this Quest, the player must capture two Murkrow in Pueltown. One is located in the northeastern part of the eastern area, south of Isaac's house, and the other is on the roof of the southwestern apartment complex in the western area.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Ice Defense 1.

Apologies to Vespiquen

This Quest can be received on Union Road from the Jam-maker in the cabin south of the entrance to the Peril Cliffs after completing Mission 9: Support Sven at the Ruins!

To complete this Quest, the player must capture two Combee and a Vespiquen near the northernmost tree on the first screen of Chroma Road.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Energy Plus.

Please Save Chimchar

This Quest can be received from the Explorer on Chroma Road next to the entrance to Chroma Highlands after completing Mission 9: Support Sven at the Ruins!

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Chimchar and two Beedrill in the Chroma Highlands.

The reward for completing this Quest is the captured Chimchar, who joins the player as a potential Partner Pokémon.

Lonely Chingling

This Quest can be received from the Lady in the western area of Pueltown after completing Mission 10: Get the Blue Gem!

To complete this Quest, the player must bring the Lady a Chingling. One can be found in Crysta Cave.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Dragon Defense 1.

Abomasnow Research

This Quest can be received from Herbert in the northwest corner of Shiver Camp after completing Mission 10: Get the Blue Gem!

To complete this Quest, the player must bring Herbert an Abomasnow. One can be found at Ice Lake.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Fighting Defense 1.

Melt the Ice!

This Quest can be received from Pamur in the northernmost hut in Shiver Camp after completing Mission 10: Get the Blue Gem!

To complete this Quest, the player must clear three blocks of Solid Ice in Crysta Cave.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Water Defense 2.

Please Save Piplup

This Quest can be received from a Research Team Member near the entrance to the Ice Lake in Hia Valley after completing Mission 10: Get the Blue Gem!

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Piplup and Houndoom in the southwestern part of Hia Valley.

The reward for completing this Quest is the captured Piplup, who joins the player as a potential Partner Pokémon.

Drifblim's Moving Service

This Quest can be received from an Old Man in the eastern part of Pueltown after completing Mission 11: Get the Red Gem!

To complete this Quest, the player must capture three Drifblim and show them to the Old Man. Drifblim can be found in Chroma Ruins and the eastern side of Volcano Cave.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Power Plus B.

Secure the Hidden Harbor

This Quest can be received from the Fishing Guide in the southwestern house in Boyleland after completing Mission 11: Get the Red Gem!

To complete this Quest, the player must clear two Fiery Walls in the west part of Volcano Cave.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Fire Defense 2.

Thaw the Frozen Door

This Quest can be received from a Research Team Member southwest of the entrance to Ice Lake in Hia Valley after completing Mission 11: Get the Red Gem!

To complete this Quest, the player must clear an Ice Wall in front of the western hut in Shiver Camp.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Normal Defense 2.

Snover's Mysterious Presence

This Quest can be received from the Explorer just inside the western entrance to Volcano Cave after completing Mission 11: Get the Red Gem!

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Snover in the eastern part of Volcano Cave.

The reward for completing this Quest is the captured Snover, who joins the player as a potential Partner Pokémon.

Unidentified Fleeing Omastar

This Quest can be received from the Angler on the southeastern dock in Pueltown after completing Mission 14: Get the Yellow Gem!

To complete this Quest, the player must capture an Omastar in the Sea of Wailord.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Latent Power A.

Cacturne Research

This Quest can be received from Herbert south of the muddy oasis in Haruba Village after completing Mission 14: Get the Yellow Gem!

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Cacturne in the Haruba Desert.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Grass Defense 2.

Mischievous Sneasel

This Quest can be received from Mrs. Winter in her cabin in Hia Valley after completing Mission 14: Get the Yellow Gem!

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Sneasel on the second floor of Almia Castle.

The reward for completing this Quest is the captured Sneasel, who joins the player as a potential Partner Pokémon.

Misdreavus Mischief Mystery!

This Quest can be received from an Explorer at the Chroma Highlands after completing Mission 14: Get the Yellow Gem!

To complete this Quest, the player must capture Misdreavus in Chroma Ruins.

The reward for completing this Quest is the captured Misdreavus, who joins the player as a potential Partner Pokémon.

My Precious Fishing Rod

This Quest can be received from the Angler in Pueltown after completing Mission 15: Protect the Ranger Union HQ!.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture Misdreavus in Chroma Ruins.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Psychic Defense 2.

Smell of Skuntank and Weezing

This Quest can be received from Oscar northwest of the Ranger Depot in Haruba Village after completing Mission 15: Protect the Ranger Union HQ!.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Skuntank and a Weezing and bring them both to Oscar. Skuntank can be found in Chroma Ruins and Oil FieldHideout, and Weezing can only be found in Almia Castle

The reward for completing this Quest is the Dark Defense 2.

Capture Gigaremo Units?

This Quest can be received from Henry in Haruba Village after completing Mission 15: Protect the Ranger Union HQ!.

To complete this Quest, the player must investigate the Hippowdon Temple and find the room with Claydol, south of the room with the spring plants.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Ghost Defense 2.

Elaine's Favorite

This Quest can be received from Ollie in Vientown next to the Ranger Base after completing Mission 15: Protect the Ranger Union HQ!.

To complete this Quest, the player must catch a Dragonair in Almia Castle, in the room with the Dratini.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Combo Bonus.
