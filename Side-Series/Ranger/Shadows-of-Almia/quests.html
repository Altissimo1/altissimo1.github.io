Quests

This page contains information about the 61 Quests that can be taken in Shadows of Almia, including the location, requirements, and rewards for each quest. Use the box below to jump to a particular quest.
Although there are 61 available Quests, the player will only ever complete 60 of them. This is because three of them are intended to give the player partner Pachirisu, Starly, and Munchlax, and the player will always have picked one of these as their Partner at the beginning of the game, so the player will not be offered the Quest corresponding to that Pokémon.

The Quests here are ordered according to when they appear to be taken by the player.



Calm Down Miltank

This Quest can be received from Little Tim at the Ranger Base in Vientown after completing Mission 2: Investigate the Marine Cave!. The player must complete this Quest to progress the game.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture Little Tim's Miltank.

The reward for completing this quest is the Grass Defense 1.

Under the Bridge

This Quest can be received from a Little Girl outside the northeastern house in Chicole Village after completing Calm Down Miltank.

To complete this Quest, the player must go to the bridge west of the Ranger School and see a Floatzel there.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Water Defense 1.

Is Combee Okay?

This Quest can be received from an Old Woman near the eastern exit to Vientown after completing Mission 3: Fight the Vien Forest Fire!.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Combee and bring it to the Old Woman. Combee can be found in Vien Forest or Chroma Road.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Electric Defense 1.

Clear Some Fallen Logs

This Quest can be received from an Mr. Woodward in the southwestern house in Vientown after completing Mission 3: Fight the Vien Forest Fire!.

To complete this Quest, the player must clear two Fallen Logs in Vien Forest.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Long Line A.

Strange Crates Washed Ashore

This Quest can be received from a Boy in the northern part of Vientown after completing Mission 3: Fight the Vien Forest Fire!.

To complete this Quest, the player must clear two Wooden Crates on Nabiki Beach.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Normal Defense 1.

Clear the Crates!

This Quest can be received from a Man in the part of Vien Forest south of Pueltown after driving off Team Dim Sun during Mission 4: Destroy the Strange Machines!.

To complete this Quest, the player must clear two Crates deeper in Vien Forest.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Bug Defense 1.

Breeze Hill Sign Recovery

This Quest can be received from Webster near the southern exit to Vientown after reuniting Ollie with his mom.

To complete this Quest, the player must clear a Rock on Nabiki Beach south of Breeze Hill.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Dark Defense 1.

Please Save Cranidos

This Quest can be received from a Young Guy near the southern exit to Vien Forest after reuniting Ollie with his mom.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture Cranidos and Wartortle in the dead-end with the tree west of the Young Guy that gives the player this Quest.

The reward for completing this Quest is the captured Cranidos, who joins the player as a potential Partner Pokémon.

The Boy Who Cried Lumineon

This Quest can be received from the Fishing Kid on the southeastern dock in Pueltown after first visiting the Ranger Union.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture Lumineon in the Puel Sea. It is located in the area with the currents where the player captured Sharpedo during Mission 5: Recover a Key from Sharpedo!.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Rock Defense 1. Completing this quest also allows the player to later take Unidentified Fleeing Omastar.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture an Eevee in the easternmost part of Pueltown, near where the ledge is that allows the player to jump from the higher level of the dock.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Steel Defense 1.

Murph's Ranger Contest

This Quest can be received from Murph in the Reference Room in the Ranger Union after completing Mission 16: Execute Operation Brighton!.

To complete this Quest, the player must bring Murph the seven Eeveelutions (Vaporeon, Jolteon, Flareon, Espeon, Umbreon, Leafeon, and Glaceon) at one time. Because the player's maximum friend Pokémon is seven, this means the player must be careful to get Eeveelutions that require Field Moves from other Pokémon earlier. Try the following order:

  1. Capture the Staraptor outside the Ranger Union and fly to the Chroma Ruins. Use Drifblim to reach the Umbreon in the Chroma Ruins.
  2. Leave the Ruins, capture the Staraptor, and fly to Boyleland. Enter the eastern entrance to Volcano Cave and use Torkoal to reach the Flareon.
  3. Leave Volcano Cave, capture the Staraptor, and fly to Almia Castle. Enter the castle and capture Vaporeon. (Vaporeon can also be found in Altru Tower.)
  4. Leave the castle and capture Staraptor and Empoleon. Use Empoleon to travel from the eastern dock outside the castle to the icy platform with Glaceon and capture it.
  5. Use the Staraptor and fly to Vientown. Go north into the Vien Forest and use Floatzel to reach Leafeon.
  6. Capture Staraptor and fly to Hippowdon Temple. Enter it and use Drifblim to reach Espeon.
  7. Leave the temple and capture the Jolteon in Haruba Desert.
  8. Take the boat back to Pueltown and go back to the Ranger Union on foot.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Time Assist B. Completing this quest also allows the player to later take Murph's Disappearing Act.

Staraptor High in the Sky

This Quest can be received from Wendy outside the Ranger Union after completing Mission 16: Execute Operation Brighton!.

To complete this Quest, the player must bring Wendy six Staraptor. One quick way to do this is:

  1. Catch the Staraptor next to Wendy and use it to fly to Hippowdon Temple. Capture the Staraptor east of the temple entrance.
  2. Go back to Haruba Desert on foot and capture the Staraptor there.
  3. Go to Haruba Village and speak to the captain to go to Boyleland, and capture the Staraptor there.
  4. Speak to the captain to go to Pueltown. Go south into Vien Forest and capture the Staraptor there.
  5. Go back to Pueltown, go north into Altru Park, and capture the Staraptor there.
  6. Go back to the Ranger Union on foot and capture the Staraptor there.
  7. Speak to Wendy.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Latent Power B.
