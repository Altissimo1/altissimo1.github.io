Quests

This page contains information about the Quests that can be taken in Shadows of Almia, including the location, requirements, and rewards for each quest. Use the box below to jump to a particular quest.

The Quests here are ordered according to when they appear to be taken by the player.



Calm Down Miltank

This Quest can be received from Little Tim at the Ranger Base in Vientown after completing Mission 2: Investigate the Marine Cave!. The player must complete this Quest to progress the game.

To complete this Quest, the player must catch Little Tim's Miltank.

The reward for completing this quest is the Grass Defense 1.

Under the Bridge

This Quest can be received from a Little Girl outside the northeastern house in Chicole Village after completing Calm Down Miltank.

To complete this Quest, the player must go to the bridge west of the Ranger School and see a Floatzel there.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Water Defense 1.

Is Combee Okay?

This Quest can be received from an Old Woman near the eastern exit to Vientown after completing Mission 3: Fight the Vien Forest Fire!.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Combee in Vien Forest and show it to the Old Woman.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Electric Defense 1.

Clear Some Fallen Logs

This Quest can be received from an Mr. Woodward in the southwestern house in Vientown after completing Mission 3: Fight the Vien Forest Fire!.

To complete this Quest, the player must clear two Fallen Logs in Vien Forest.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Long Line A.

Strange Crates Washed Ashore

This Quest can be received from a Boy in the northern part of Vientown after completing Mission 3: Fight the Vien Forest Fire!.

To complete this Quest, the player must clear two Wooden Crates on Nabiki Beach.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Normal Defense 1.

Clear the Crates!

This Quest can be received from a Man in the part of Vien Forest south of Pueltown after driving off Team Dim Sun during Mission 4: Destroy the Strange Machines!.

To complete this Quest, the player must clear two Crates deeper in Vien Forest.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Bug Defense 1.

Breeze Hill Sign Recovery

This Quest can be received from Webster near the southern exit to Vientown after reuniting Ollie with his mom.

To complete this Quest, the player must clear a Rock on Nabiki Beach south of Breeze Hill.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Dark Defense 1.

Please Save Cranidos

This Quest can be received from a Young Guy near the southern exit to Vien Forest after reuniting Ollie with his mom.

To complete this Quest, the player must catch Cranidos and Wartortle in the dead-end with the tree west of the Young Guy that gives the player this Quest.

The reward for completing this Quest is the captured Cranidos, who joins the player as a potential Partner Pokémon.

The Boy Who Cried Lumineon

This Quest can be received from the Fishing Kid on the southeastern dock in Pueltown after first visiting the Ranger Union.

To complete this Quest, the player must catch Lumineon in the Puel Sea. It is located in the area with the currents where the player captured Sharpedo during Mission 5: Recover a Key from Sharpedo!.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Rock Defense 1.

Eevee for Marital Bliss

This Quest can be received from the Old Man west of the Ranger Depot in Pueltown after first visiting the Ranger Union.

To complete this Quest, the player must catch an Eevee in the easternmost part of Puel Town, near where the ledge is that allows the player to jump from the higher level of the dock.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Steel Defense 1.

Go Talk to My Mom

This Quest can be received from Brook south of the bridge in Altru Park after first visiting the Ranger Union.

To complete this Quest, the player must visit Brook's mother. She is in the eastern part of the town, in the eastern house north of the fountain.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Flying Defense 1.

Nosepass! Must Have Nosepass!

This Quest can be received from a Man in the western part of Pueltown after learning how to ride Doduo.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Nosepass in Marine Cave and show it to the Man.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Ground Defense 1.

Who Ate My Lunch?!

This Quest can be received from a Man in the area two screens north of Vien Forest after learning how to ride Doduo.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Ambipom in the screen to the west and show it to the Man.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Power Plus A.

Oddish Research

This Quest can be received from Herbert on the bridge in Altru Park after learning how to ride Doduo.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture an Oddish in Vien Forest and show it to Herbert.

The reward for completing this Quest is the Fire Defense 1.

Please Save Turtwig

This Quest can be received from a Little Boy in front of the northeastern house in Chicole Village after learning how to ride Doduo.

To complete this Quest, the player must catch Turtwig on the northern cliff of Breeze Hill in Vientown

The reward for completing this Quest is the captured Turtwig, who joins the player as a potential Partner Pokémon.
