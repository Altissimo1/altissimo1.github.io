Obstacles

  • Empoleon's River Flow x2 must be used to traverse the lake leading to Almia Castle.
  • Tackle x3 is required to awaken Abomasnow. It can be found on Piloswine in Hia Valley.
  • Burn x1 is required to light the Torch to open the doors to Almia Castle. It can be found on Vulpix.
