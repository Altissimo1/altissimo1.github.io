Marine Cave

Access to:
Exit: Vientown (Nabiki Beach)

Mission 2: Investigate the Marine Cave!

Nosepass and Gastrodon must be captured to complete the mission.

Obstacles

  • There are two Wood Fences in the main area, requiring Taillow's or Glameow's Cut x1 to clear. Only one needs to be broken to complete the mission.
  • A Small Rock requires Crush x1 to clear. It leads to a wild Graveler.
  • Soak x1 is required to awaken Geodude.
  • A Rock requires Nosepass' Crush x2 to clear.
  • A Strange Machine requires Gastrodon's Soak x2 to clear.
  • Soak x2 is required to awaken Graveler.

Quest: Nosepass! Must Have Nosepass!

A Man in Pueltown requests to see a Nosepass. The only place where Nosepass can be found is in the Marine Cave.
