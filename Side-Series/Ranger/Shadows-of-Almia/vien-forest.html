  • A Fallen Tree requires Cut x3 to clear, which can be found on nearby Leafeon. It leads to a wild Weedle and Torterra.

Quest: Wish for the Vien Forest

Celebi can only be captured as part of this Quest.

Obstacles

  • River Flow can be used to traverse the river on the screen with Blastoise. The river leads to a section of the forest that contains Pikachu, Lopunny, Luxio, Kricketune, Forretress, Leafeon, Regirock, Roserade, and Celebi.
  • Doduo's Agility is probably required to catch up with Celebi.
