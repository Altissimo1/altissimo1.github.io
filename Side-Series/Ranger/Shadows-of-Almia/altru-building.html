Obstacles

  • A Steel Frame requires Cut x3 to clear, which can be found on nearby Absol and Scyther. It leads to a Team Dim Sum Admin and a wild Blaziken and Scizor.
  • Two rooms require Electabuzz' Flash to light up.
  • A Smoke Detector requires Burn x3 to clear, which can be found on nearby Blaziken.
  • Two Large Cages require Cut x4 to clear, which can be found on nearby Scizor and Absol. One houses a wild Electabuzz, and the other houses a wild Ampharos.
