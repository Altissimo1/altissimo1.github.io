Obstacles

  • Three Vines require Cut x1 to clear, which can be found on Fearow.
  • Doduo's Agility is probably required to catch Hippowdon.
  • Hippowdon's Sand Fill must be used to enter the Hippowdon Temple.

Quest: Cacturne Research

Herbert in Haruba Village requests to see a Cacturne. The only place where Cacturne can be found is in the Haruba Desert.

Obstacles

  • If not already cleared, one Vine requires Cut x1 to clear, which can be found on Fearow. It leads to wild Cacnea and Cacturne.
