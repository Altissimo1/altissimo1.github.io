Obstacles

  • Five Rocks require Crush x2 to clear, which can be found on nearby Pupitar.
  • Several light patches throughout the area require Drifblim's Elevate to lift through.
  • Multiple rooms require Electabuzz' Flash to light up.
  • Several patches of Soft Soil throughout the area require Sandshrew's Tunnel to dig through.
  • Two Sealed Doors require Psy Power x1 to clear, which can be found on nearby Duskull and Hypno. One leads to a wild Pupitar and the other leads to a wild Skuntank.
  • A Round Rock requires Tackle x3 to clear, which can be found on nearby Bastiodon.
  • Skuntank's Stink must be used to clear out the Team Dim Sun grunts.

Quest: Murph's Ranger Contest

Murph in the Ranger Union requests to see a Umbreon. The only place where Umbreon can be found is at the Chroma Ruins.

Obstacles

  • Several light patches throughout the area require Drifblim's Elevate to lift through.

Quest: Ponte's Path to Take

Ponte in the Chroma Highlands requests to see a Probopass. The only place where Probopass can be found is in the Chroma Ruins.

Obstacles

  • If not already cleared, a Sealed Door requires Psy Power x3 to clear. It leads to a wild Probopass.
  • Several light patches throughout the area require Drifblim's Elevate to lift through.
