Obstacles

  • A Gate requires Electrify x2 to clear, which can be found on nearby Luxio.
  • Four Power Generators require Electrify x3 to clear, which can be found on nearby Luxray.
  • Skuntank's Stink must be used to clear out the Team Dim Sun grunts.

Quest: Smell of Skuntank and Weezing

Oscar in Haruba Village requests to see a Skuntank. The only place where Skuntank can be found is at the Oil Field Hideout.
