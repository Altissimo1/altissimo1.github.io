Obstacles

  • Two red Strange Machines in the main part of the city require Soak x2 to clear, which can be found on Bibarel directly south in Vien Forest.
  • Two green Strange Machines in the western part of the city requires Burn x1 to clear, which can be found on Growlithe in the main part of the city.
  • A Gate in the western part of the city requires Electrify x2 to clear, which can be found on a Voltorb nearby after clearing the green Strange Machines.
  • A Gate in the eastern part of the city requires Electrify x2 to clear, which can be found on an Elekid in the apartment complex nearby.
  • A blue Strange Machine in the eastern part of the city requires Electrify x2 to clear, which can be found on an Elekid in the apartment complex nearby.
  • A green Strange Machine in the eastern part of the city requires Burn x1 to clear, which can be found on Growlithe in the main part of the city.
