Obstacles

  • Floatzel's River Flow x1 is required to access the area, and to navigate between two areas in the entrance screen. It can also be used to access Chingling from the second screen.
  • A block of Solid Ice requires Burn x2 to clear, which can be found on nearby Camerupt.

Quest: Lonely Chingling

A Lady in Pueltown requests to see a Chingling. The only place where Chingling can be found is in Crysta Cave.

Obstacles

  • River Flow is required to access Chingling from the second screen.

Quest: Melt the Ice!

Obstacles

  • Three blocks of Solid Ice appear while this Quest is active. They require Burn x2 to clear, which can be found on nearby Camerupt.
