Quest: Unidentified Fleeing Omastar

The Omastar captured here remains here permanently after completing this Quest.

 Omastar
  • Two underwater chasms in the far western area require Swim to traverse, which can be found on nearby Mantine. One of them leads to the wild Omastar.
  • If not already cleared, two Boulders require Crush x4 to clear, which can be found on nearby Kingdra. Clearing one is required to reach the Omastar.
  • If not already cleared, a Steel Frame requires Cut x3 to clear, which can be found on Starmie. It leads to a wild Wailmer.
