Ranger Net Missions

The story mode of Shadows of Almia provides the player with access to 267 Pokémon. However, the "Ranger Net" feature, accessible from the main menu, allows the player to access six missions that will provide them with access to an additional three Pokémon, as well as a Manaphy Egg, special Riolu, and special Darkrai. This page provides a quick overview of the six missions.

Obtaining the Missions

In all versions of the game, these missions were only obtainable via special Wi-Fi distributions between 2008 and 2009 (with the Dialga and Palkia missions extending until the shutdown of DS servers in 2014). These missions can be obtained currently with the use of specific tools.

As far as I can tell, these missions were never distributed for the Wii U Virtual Console. Even if they were, the Manaphy egg, Riolu, and Darkrai obtained through the missions would not have been transferable to any DS Pokémon game.

Missions

Special Mission: Recover the Manaphy Egg!

This was distributed between March 2008 and February 2009.

This mission, which takes place in Vientown, requires the player to retrieve a Manaphy Egg before Team Dim Sun can make off with it.

Completing this mission will provide the player with a Manaphy Egg. In the original release of the game, this Egg can be transferred to a copy of Diamond, Pearl, Platinum, HeartGold, or SoulSilver using the DS' wireless functionality. Only one Manaphy egg may be obtained per Shadows of Almia cartridge.

Special Mission: Rescue Kidnapped Riolu!

This was distributed in between March 2008 and February 2009.

This mission, which takes place at the Ranger School and then in the Oil Field Hideout, requires the player to chase Team Dim Sun in an effort to retrieve the Riolu they have kidnapped.

Completing this mission will provide the player with a Riolu with the special move Aura Sphere. In the original release of the game, this Riolu can be transferred to a copy of Diamond, Pearl, Platinum, HeartGold, or SoulSilver using the DS' wireless functionality. Only one Riolu may be obtained per Shadows of Almia cartridge.

 Riolu ♂ Steadfast Serious Lv.30 Aura Sphere Shadow Claw Bullet Punch Drain Punch

Special Mission: Liberate the Tower!

This was distributed between April 2008 and January 2009.

This mission, which takes place in the Altru Building and Altru Tower, requires the player to defeat the remnants of Team Dim Sun and their boss, who are calling themselves Team Debonairs, with the help of Darkrai.

Completing this mission will provide the player with a Darkrai. In the original release of the game, this Darkrai can be transferred to a copy of Diamond, Pearl, Platinum, HeartGold, or SoulSilver using the DS' wireless functionality. Only one Darkrai may be obtained per Shadows of Almia cartridge.

 Darkrai Bad Dreams Lv.50 Dark Void Dark Pulse Shadow Ball Double Team

Extra Mission: Dialga in Hia Valley!?

This was distributed between June 2008 and May 2014.

This mission, which takes place in Almia Castle, requires the player to find and capture an injured Dialga so it can be healed.

Completing this mission adds Dialga to the player's Browser; it is marked with a "Special" Browser number.

Extra Mission: Palkia in Haruba Desert!?

This was distributed between June 2008 and May 2014.

This mission, which takes place all over Almia due to Palkia's spatial distortions, requires the player to find and capture an injured Palkia so it can be healed.

Completing this mission adds Palkia to the player's Browser; it is marked with a "Special" Browser number.

Extra Mission: For the Bride and Shaymin!

This was distributed between July 2008 and March 2009.

This mission, which takes place in Pueltown, requires the player to protect Shaymin from Teasm Dim Sun.

Completing this mission adds Shaymin to the player's Browser; it is marked with a "Special" Browser number.
