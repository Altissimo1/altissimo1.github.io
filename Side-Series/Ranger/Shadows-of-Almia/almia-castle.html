Obstacles

  • Burn x1 is required to light three Torches to open the doors on the west side of the castle, which can be found on nearby Vulpix. One Torch unlocks a door allowing further access to Almia Castle, and the other two lead to a wild Mismagius.
  • Burn x4 is required to clear the Ice Block, which can be found on nearby Ninetales.
  • Psy Power x3 is required to clear the Sealed Door, which can be found on nearby Mismagius.
  • Though not a Target, two Riolu must be captured to clear the barrier blocking the way to the Blue Gem.
