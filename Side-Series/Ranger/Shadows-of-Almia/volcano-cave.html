Obstacles

  • Several steam vents throughout the area require Drifloon's Airlift to ride. Some shortcuts to leave the area also require Airlift.
  • One Sealed Door requires Psy Power x1 to clear, which can be found on Shuppet. It leads to a wild Muk.
  • A Rock requires Crush x2 to clear, which can be found on Muk.

Quest: Team Dim Sun's Appetite

Story Pokémon

 Charmander

Obstacles

  • Several steam vents throughout the area require Drifloon's Airlift to ride. Some shortcuts to leave the area also require Airlift.
  • Two Crates appear while this Quest is active. They require Crush x1 to clear, which can be found on nearby Muk and Stunky.

Quest: That Smell of Muk...

Oscar in Boyleland requests to see a Muk. The only place where Muk can be found is in Volcano Cave. Accessing it requires Psy Power x1 to clear a Sealed Door near the entrance to the hidden harbor.

Obstacles

  • Several magma rivers throughout the area require Torkoal's Magma Flow to traverse.
  • Two Hot Boulders require Soak x2 to clear, which can be found on nearby Gastrodon or Pelipper.
  • Two Rocks require Crush x2 to clear, which can be found on Hariyama or Graveler.
  • Two light patches require Drifblim's Elevate to lift through.
  • Graveler requires Soak x2 to awaken, which can be found on nearby Gastrodon.
  • Two Fiery Walls require Soak x3 to clear, which can be found on nearby Pelipper.
  • A Heavy Boulder requires Tackle x4 to clear, which can be found on Aggron.

Quest: Secure the Hidden Harbor

Obstacles
