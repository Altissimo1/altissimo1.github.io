Pokémon Ranger (Game)

The first game in the Pokémon Ranger series, itself called Pokémon Ranger, was released in 2006 in all regions except Europe and in 2007 in Europe. This game contains 213 Pokémon, and also provide access to a Manaphy egg that can be transferred to Diamond, Pearl, and Platinum. The game was re-released for the Wii U in 2016.
