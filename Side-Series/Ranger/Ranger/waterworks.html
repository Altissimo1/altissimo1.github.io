Obstacles

  • Two Metal Fences require Raticate's Cut to clear. Only one is required to complete the mission; the other leads to a wild Crawdaunt.
  • Two Posts require Tangela's Cross to cross. Only one is required to complete the mission; the other leads to some wild Pokémon.
  • The Waterworks Wall requires Meditite's Break to clear. It leads to some wild Pokémon.

Anytime After Mission 3

The Waterworks can be entered again after Mission 3 is over. The player cannot get all the Pokémon here until postgame, though.

Wild Pokémon

  • Accessing Feraligatr requires Cross and 2x Burn.
  • Accessing Squirtle requires Cross
  • Accessing Magneton and Oddish requires Break 1x.
  • Accessing Crawdaunt requires Cut 2x.
