Professor Oak Challenge

A Professor Oak challenge is where you catch all available Pokémon before moving on to the next story event. A guide for this game exists here, but there are some inaccuracies in addition to some incorrect counting, so I intend to present an accurate version here.

A total of 213 Pokémon can be obtained in this game, including the three accessible in Ranger Net missions.
