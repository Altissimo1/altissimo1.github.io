Obstacles

  • A Cavern Wall requires Crush x2 to clear. It leads to a Kirlia.
  • Wild Parasect require Gust x2 to awaken.
  • Two Posts require Cross to cross.
  • Four Boulders require Crush x2 to clear.
  • A Metal Fence requires Cut x2 to clear. Crossing both Posts, breaking all four Boulders, and cutting this Fence leads to Tangela and Registeel.
  • A Rockfall requires Crush x3 to clear. Crossing the first Post and breaking this rock leads to Dugtrio.

Special Mission: Gain Deoxys's Trust?!

The only required capture in this mission is Deoxys, which must first be captured in its Attack, Defense, and Speed forms before it can finally be captured in its Normal form. During the last battle, the final loop must be drawn around Normal Form to complete the capture.
