Ranger Net Missions

The story mode of Pokémon Ranger provides the player with access to 210 Pokémon. However, the "Ranger Net" feature, accessible from the main menu, allows the player to access four missions that will provide them with access to an additional three Pokémon and a Manaphy Egg. This page provides a quick overview of the four missions.

Obtaining the Missions

In the original Japanesse release of the game for the Nintendo DS, missions had to be downloaded as part of local event distributions. These event distributions all occurred in 2006 in various locations around Japan; therefore, the missions cannot be obtained in a Japanese copy of the game without hacking.

In the international releases of the game (both for the Nintendo DS and for the Wii U VC, they can instead be obtained by entering in passwords. To active the password entry, the player must hold R + X + Left while on the Ranger Net screen. All four missions will be obtained with the entry of one password, but they are only unlocked in the order they are listed below.

Missions

Recover the Precious Egg!

This was distributed in Japan between June and August 2006.

This mission, which takes place in Lyra Forest, requires the player to defeat the Go-Rock Squad, who have stolen a Manaphy Egg from Prof. Hastings.

Completing this mission will provide the player with a Manaphy Egg. In the original release of the game, this Egg can be transferred to a copy of Diamond, Pearl, Platinum, HeartGold, or SoulSilver using the DS' wireless functionality. Only one Manaphy egg may be obtained per Pokémon Ranger cartridge.

The Manaphy Egg is obtainable in the Wii U Virtual Console release of the game, but nothing can be done with it as that release cannot connect to any DS game.

Gain Deoxys's Trust?!

This was distributed in Japan between June and August 2006.

This mission, which takes place in Krokka Tunnel, requires the player to capture Deoxys multiple times in multiple areas around Krokka Tunnel. Deoxys uses all four of its Formes and can only be ultimately captured in its Normal Forme.

Deoxys is number R-211 in the Fiore Browser.

Rescue Celebi!

This was distributed in Japan in short events that occurred between July and September 2006.

This mission, which takes place in Lyra Forest, requires the player to defeat the Go-Rock Squad, who are trying to capture Celebi, and Celebi itself.

Celebi is number R-212 in the Fiore Browser.

Find Mew, the Mirage!

This was distributed in Japan between July and August 2006.

This mission, which takes place in Olive Jungle, requires the player to find and capture Mew as it runs from the player.

Mew is number R-213 in the Fiore Browser.
