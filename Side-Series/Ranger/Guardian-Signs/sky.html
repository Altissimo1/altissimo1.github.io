Between Missions 7 and 8

Every time the player goes to the sky, one of four patterns of Pokémon is loaded to place flying Pokémon around the area. Some Pokémon are exclusive to one pattern. The patterns are as follows:

Pattern 1

 Pidgey
Pidgeotto
Pidgeot
Starly
Staravia
Drifloon
Drifblim
Chatot

Pokémon can be found in the following locations:

  • Chatot near Dolce Island
  • Pidgey around Renbow Island
  • Pidgeotto and Drifblim around Mitonga Island
  • Drifloon between Mitonga Island and Faldera Island
  • Starly and Pidgeot around Sophian Island
  • Staravia around Mt. Layuda

Chatot and Pidgeot probably require Latias/Latios to capture.
