Obstacles

  • Chimchar is a hidden Pokémon that must be unveiled with Raikou or Entei's roar.
  • A Round Rock requires Tackle x2 to clear, which can be found on Phanpy. It leads to a wild Donphan.
  • A Heavy Rock requires Tackle x3 to clear, which can be found on Donphan.
  • A Rock Table requires Tackle x3 to clear, which can be found on Donphan.
  • A Rock Plate requires three Pokémon with Tackle x3 to clear, which can be found on many nearby Shelgon and a Donphan.
  • A Burning Rock requires two Pokémon with Soak x3 to clear, which can be found on three nearby Gastrodon.
  • A Volcanic Rock requires two Pokémon with Crush x3 to clear, which can be found on three nearby Golem and Primeape.
