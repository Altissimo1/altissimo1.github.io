Quests

This page contains information about the Quests that can be taken in Guardian Signs, including the location, requirements, and rewards for each quest. Use the box below to jump to a particular quest.

The Quests here are ordered according to when they appear to be taken by the player.



Pichu's Broken Ukulele

This Quest can be received from Booker in his house in Cocona Village after completing Mission 1: Drive the Pinchers out of the Woods!. The player must complete this Quest to progress the game.

To complete this Quest, the player must go to Dolce Island and investigate the large hollow tree.

The reward for completing this quest is 10 Ranger Points.

Recharge Pachirisu

This Quest can be received from a Little Girl next to the northeastern house in Cocona Village after catching Celebi in Cocona Plaza.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Pachirisu and bring it to the Little Girl. Pachirisu can be found on Lapras Beach, Teakwood Forest, and Rasp Cavern.

The reward for completing this quest is 10 Ranger Points.

A New Path in Teakwood Forest

This Quest can be received from an Old Man in Cocona Plaza after catching Celebi there.

To complete this Quest, the player must clear a Wood Fence in Teakwood Forest.

The reward for completing this quest is 10 Ranger Points.

The Elderly Couple's Argument

This Quest can be received from a Man next to the southwestern house in Cocona Village after catching Celebi in Cocona Plaza.

To complete this Quest, the player must first listen to the couple argue in the southwestern house, and then capture a a Geodude and bring it to the Man. Geodude can be found in Rasp Cavern.

The reward for completing this quest is 10 Ranger Points.

The Big One That Got Away

This Quest can be received from a Man on Lapras Beach after completing Mission 2: Salvage the Sunkern UFO!.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Luvdisc with a lure in the Coral Sea.

The reward for completing this quest is 10 Ranger Points.

The Son's Lunch

This Quest can be received from the Old Woman in the southwestern house in Cocona Village after completing Mission 3: Liberate the Wireless Tower!.

To complete this Quest, the player must take the lunch to the Young Man in the brown jacket at the Wireless Tower.

The reward for completing this quest is 10 Ranger Points. Completing it will automatically trigger the Quest My Lunch.

My Lunch

This Quest can be received from the Young Man in the brown jacket at the Wireless Tower after completing Mission 3: Liberate the Wireless Tower!. This Quest is obtained automatically on the conclusion of the Quest The Son's Lunch.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture Pidgey on the windy level of the Wireless Tower.

The reward for completing this quest is 10 Ranger Points.

The Sleepless Granny

This Quest can be received from the Old Woman in the house next to the cave entrance in Tilt Village after completing Mission 4: Investigate the Old Mansion!.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Noctowl and bring it to the Old Woman. Noctowl can be found outside the Old Mansion.

The reward for completing this quest is 10 Ranger Points.
