Quests

This page contains information about the Quests that can be taken in Guardian Signs, including the location, requirements, and rewards for each quest. Use the box below to jump to a particular quest.

The Quests here are ordered according to when they appear to be taken by the player.



Pichu's Broken Ukulele

This Quest can be received from Booker in his house in Cocona Village after completing Mission 1: Drive the Pinchers out of the Woods!. The player must complete this Quest to progress the game.

To complete this Quest, the player must go to Dolce Island and investigate the large hollow tree.

The reward for completing this quest is 10 Ranger Points.

Recharge Pachirisu

This Quest can be received from a Little Girl next to the northeastern house in Cocona Village after capturing Celebi in Cocona Plaza.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Pachirisu and bring it to the Little Girl. Pachirisu can be found on Lapras Beach, Teakwood Forest, and Rasp Cavern.

The reward for completing this quest is 10 Ranger Points.

A New Path in Teakwood Forest

This Quest can be received from an Old Man in Cocona Plaza after capturing Celebi there.

To complete this Quest, the player must clear a Wood Fence in Teakwood Forest.

The reward for completing this quest is 10 Ranger Points.

The Elderly Couple's Argument

This Quest can be received from a Man next to the southwestern house in Cocona Village after capturing Celebi in Cocona Plaza.

To complete this Quest, the player must first listen to the couple argue in the southwestern house, and then capture a a Geodude and bring it to the Man. Geodude can be found in Rasp Cavern.

The reward for completing this quest is 10 Ranger Points.

The Big One That Got Away

This Quest can be received from a Man on Lapras Beach after completing Mission 2: Salvage the Sunkern UFO!.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Luvdisc with a lure in the Coral Sea.

The reward for completing this quest is 10 Ranger Points.

The Son's Lunch

This Quest can be received from the Old Woman in the southwestern house in Cocona Village after completing Mission 3: Liberate the Wireless Tower!.

To complete this Quest, the player must take the lunch to the Young Man in the brown jacket at the Wireless Tower.

The reward for completing this quest is 10 Ranger Points. Completing it will automatically trigger the Quest My Lunch.

My Lunch

This Quest can be received from the Young Man in the brown jacket at the Wireless Tower after completing Mission 3: Liberate the Wireless Tower!. This Quest is obtained automatically on the conclusion of the Quest The Son's Lunch.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture Pidgey on the windy level of the Wireless Tower.

The reward for completing this quest is 10 Ranger Points.

The Sleepless Granny

This Quest can be received from the Old Woman in the house next to the cave entrance in Tilt Village after completing Mission 4: Investigate the Old Mansion!.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Noctowl and bring it to the Old Woman. Noctowl can be found outside the Old Mansion.

The reward for completing this quest is 10 Ranger Points.

Rolling Graveler

This Quest can be received from the Man east of the western entrance to Hinder Cape after learning about Signs from Leanne.

To complete this Quest, the player must go to Mt. Latolato and calm some Graveler there.

The reward for completing this quest is 10 Ranger Points and Graveler's Sign.

The Mild Mankey

This Quest can be received from the Lady in the southeasternmost house in Tilt Village after learning about Signs from Leanne.

To complete this Quest, the player must go to the Old Mansion and calm a Mankey there.

The reward for completing this quest is 10 Ranger Points and Mankey's Sign.

Cute, Cute Jumpluff

This Quest can be received from the Boy in the eastern part of Mitonga Road after completing Mission 5: Rescue Ben/Summer!

To complete this Quest, the player must go north on Mitonga Road and calm the Jumpluff there.

The reward for completing this quest is 15 Ranger Points and Jumpluff's Sign.

Mitonga Island's Odd Stones

This Quest can be received from Casey near the western exit to Tilt Village after completing Mission 5: Rescue Ben/Summer!

To complete this Quest, the player must speak to Casey to go to Noir Forest and use Entei to clear all the rocks there.

The reward for completing this quest is 10 Ranger Points.

Luggage for Renbow Island

This Quest can be received from the Lady on the eastern side of the Big Booker Bridge after completing Mission 5: Rescue Ben/Summer!

To complete this Quest, the player must deliver her parcel to Herb in the southeastern house in Cocona Village, and then deliver his parcel back to the Lady.

The reward for completing this quest is 15 Ranger Points.

Fly, Starly!

This Quest can be received from the Old Man in Cocona Plaza after completing Mission 5: Rescue Ben/Summer!

To complete this Quest, the player must speak to the Old Man to go to Teakwood Forest and then chase Starly toward the northern part of the forest.

The reward for completing this quest is 15 Ranger Points.

Save Bidoof!

This Quest can be received from the Little Boy next to the dais in Cocona Village after completing Mission 5: Rescue Ben/Summer!

To complete this Quest, the player must go to Teakwood Forest, outside the entrance to Rasp Cavern, and calm a Bidoof there.

The reward for completing this quest is 15 Ranger Points and Bidoof's Sign.

Trash the Pokémon Traps

This Quest can be received from the Old Woman near the western exit to Tilt Village after completing Mission 6: Foil Blue Eyes's Plans!

To complete this Quest, the player must destroy three Small Cages, which require Cut x1 to clear: set by the Pinchers. They are located in:

  1. Dangerous Cliff
  2. Old Mansion, near the pond to the east
  3. Mitonga Road, in the southeast

The reward for completing this quest is 15 Ranger Points.

Search for the Runaway Boy

This Quest can be received from the Lady in Tilt Village after completing Mission 6: Foil Blue Eyes's Plans!

To complete this Quest, the player must speak to her son in the Daybreak Ruins.

The reward for completing this quest is 15 Ranger Points.

Maddeningly Spicy!

This Quest can be received from the Girl in Curl Bay after completing Mission 6: Foil Blue Eyes's Plans!

To complete this Quest, the player must speak to the Lady to go to Latolato Trail and capture an agitated Mothim, Gible, Bibarel, and Stunky.

The reward for completing this quest is 15 Ranger Points.

Chase the Flying Tools

This Quest can be received from the Man in the house at the base of the Wireless Tower after completing Mission 7: Protect Moltres!

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Beldum near the top of the Wireless Tower.

The reward for completing this quest is 15 Ranger Points.

Happiny, Come Home!

This Quest can be received from the Man near Tilt Village on Mitonga Road after completing Mission 7: Protect Moltres!

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Happiny in Noir Forest and return it to the Man.

The reward for completing this quest is 20 Ranger Points.

The Boy's Quiz

This Quest can be received from the Boy next to Booker's house in Cocona Village after learning about Suicune's appearance in the Canal Ruins.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture a Drifloon in the Sky, add it to their party by talking to Supurna in Aqua Resort, and bring it to the Boy.

The reward for completing this quest is 20 Ranger Points.

Spin, Hitmontop, Spin!

This Quest can be received from the Man on the winding path across the river on Sophian Road after learning about Suicune's appearance in the Canal Ruins.

To complete this Quest, the player must capture the Man's Hitmontop.

The reward for completing this quest is 15 Ranger Points and Hitmontop's Sign.
