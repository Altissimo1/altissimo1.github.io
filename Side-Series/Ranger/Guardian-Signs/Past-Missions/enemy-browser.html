Guardian Signs Past Enemy Browser

The Browser, the Ranger series' equivalent to the Pokédex, does not match any ordering established by any National or Regional Pokédex in the main series. Pokémon below are ordered by their Browser number.

This page is intended to show the stats associated with enemy Pokémon: their friendship and agitated gauge numbers, their AP yield, and their potential Slate drops. Pokémon that can't be seen as wild Pokémon are excluded.

Friendship Gauge, Agitated Gauge, AP yields, and Slate drop chances come from the official Prima strategy guide for this game, and therefore may have some inaccuracies. If you have updates or corrections to make, please contact me.
Deoxys did not have values given in the Prima guide. I will have to do some research to identify its values.

Slate drops for clearing Missions are not mentioned with the boss Pokémon; those can be found on the Mission pages.
