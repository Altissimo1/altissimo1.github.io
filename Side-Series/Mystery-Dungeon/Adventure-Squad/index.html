Adventure Squad

There are three games in the Adventure Squad set: Wildfire, Tempest, and Radiant. Time and Darkness were released for the DS in 2009 in Japan. These games contain the first 493 Pokémon in the National Pokédex (Bulbasaur through Arceus). Although they were never released outside of Japan, a fan translation project provides English-language patches for the games. I use the names of the fan translation in my pages.
