Mable's Research

During Main Mission 03: A New Life in Lumiose City, the player will gain access to the first Wild Zone and Taunie or Urbain will encourage them to catch 5 species of Pokémon to participate in the Research Lab's promotional challenge. Upon catching the requested species and speaking with Mable in the Lab, the player will gain access to Mable's Research, which will allow them to complete Pokédex research tasks for points and rewards. This page aims to document the tasks and rewards associated with the research project.
