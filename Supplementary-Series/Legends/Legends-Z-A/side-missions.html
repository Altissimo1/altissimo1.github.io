Legends: Z-A Side Missions

This page contains information about the Side Missions that can be taken in Legends: Z-A, including the location, requirements, and rewards for each side mission. Use the box below to jump to a particular side mission.



Side Mission 001: A Big Ol' Bunnelby

This side mission is obtainable after speaking with Emma during Main Mission 05: The City in the Shadow of Prism Tower. It is obtained in Rouge Sector 6 from Trevelle near Rouge Plaza.

To complete this side mission, the player must:

  1. Speak with Trevelle in Rouge Sector 8, in the courtyard surrounded by the western building complex.
  2. Catch or defeat a level 10 alpha Bunnelby.

The reward for completing this side mission is 400 currency and 2 Fresh Waters.

Side Mission 002: A Use for an Evolution Stone!

This side mission is obtainable after speaking with Emma during Main Mission 05: The City in the Shadow of Prism Tower. It is obtained in Passage du Palais from Tracie near the western entrance.

To complete this side mission, player must trade Tracie a Pikachu in exchange for her Heracross, which is level 12.

The reward for completing this side mission is 800 currency, 5 Poké Balls, and 3 Potions.

Side Mission 003: Sableye in the Cemetery

This side mission is obtainable after speaking with Emma during Main Mission 05: The City in the Shadow of Prism Tower. It is obtained on Rouge Street from Moire near the western entrance to Dormez Bien Cemetery.

To complete this side mission, the player must:

  1. Catch or defeat a level 10 Sableye in the northeast clearing of Wild Zone 4. The player will receive the Cherished Ring after the battle.
  2. Return the Cherished Ring to Moire.

The reward for completing this side mission is 4,000 currency and 2 Revives.

Side Mission 004: A Break Time Battle

This side mission is obtainable after starting Main Mission 06: Reaching Rank X. It is obtained in Rouge Sector 1 from Sommi near the out-of-service Holovator west of Rouge Plaza.

To complete this side mission, the player must battle Sommi. He has a level 8 Pidgey, a level 8 Budew, and a level 10 Emolga.

The reward for completing this side mission is 1,200 currency, 3 Exp. Candies S, and 1 Fresh Water. Sommi also fixes the Holovator, allowing access to the roof of the building complex here and some of the buildings in Rouge Sector 3.

Side Mission 005: I'd Like to See an Ekans!

This side mission is obtainable after starting Main Mission 06: Reaching Rank X. It is obtained in Magenta Sector 1 from Ismene near the stalls.

To complete this side mission, the player must put an Ekans in their party and show it to Ismene.

The reward for completing this side mission is 500 currency, 3 Pecha Berries, 3 Cheri Berries, and 1 Soda Pop.

Side Mission 006: Long-Range Moves Have Style

This side mission is obtainable after starting Main Mission 06: Reaching Rank X. It is obtained in Bleu Sector 4 from Distan on the tennis court.

To complete this side mission, the player must battle Distan. He has a level 16 Binacle, a level 16 Fletchling, and a level 16 Skiddo.

The reward for completing this side mission is 1,000 currency and 1 Nugget.

Side Mission 007: A Feisty Chespin

This side mission is obtainable after starting Main Mission 06: Reaching Rank X. It is obtained in Académie Étoile from Axi in front of the academy building.

To complete this side mission, the player must battle a level 14 Chespin three times. The player cannot catch Chespin during these battles.

The reward for completing this side mission is 1,000 currency, 3 Exp. Candies XS, and 1 Exp. Candy S. The Chespin also joins the player.

Side Mission 008: Get Well, Fennekin

This side mission is obtainable after starting Main Mission 06: Reaching Rank X. It is obtained in Magenta Sector 2 from Branche in the grassy area.

To complete this side mission, the player must:

  1. Speak with the herbalist, an old man sitting at one of the outdoor tables in front of Café Classe on Vernal Avenue
  2. Investigate 5 twigs in Wild Zone 5. The 5 twigs are located in the eastern part of the Zone, in the grassy area with the Venipede and alpha Whirlipede that is accessible from the waterway in the western part of the Zone
  3. Return to Branche

The reward for completing this side mission is 1,200 currency, 3 Exp. Candies XS, and 1 Exp. Candy S. The revitalized Fennekin also joins the player; it is level 14.

Side Mission 009: A Challenge from Froakie

This side mission is obtainable after starting Main Mission 06: Reaching Rank X. It is obtained in Jaune Sector 8 from Surv on the grassy area next to North Boulevard.

To complete this side mission, the player must complete three scaffolding courses in a given amount of time. The first two courses are around the grassy area in Jaune Sector 8 and must be completed in 30 and 40 seconds, respectively. The third course is on North Boulevard north of Passage du Palais and must be completed in 50 seconds.

The reward for completing this side mission is 1,800 currency, 3 Exp. Candies XS, and 1 Exp. Candy S. The Froakie that the player is trying to beat also joins the player; it is level 14.

Side Mission 010: Skiddo's Fragrant Leaves

This side mission is obtainable after starting Main Mission 07: Reaching Rank W. It is obtained on Vernal Avenue from Odette in Promenade du Vent, outside Boutique Couture.

To complete this side mission, the player must put a Skiddo in their party and show it to Odette.

The reward for completing this side mission is 800 currency, 2 Sachets, and 5 Persim Berries.

Side Mission 011: The Kakuna Master

This side mission is obtainable after starting Main Mission 07: Reaching Rank W. It is obtained in Jaune Sector 1 from Montée under a tree.

To complete this side mission, the player must battle Montée. She has three Kakuna at levels 8, 8, and 9, and a level 11 Beedrill.

The reward for completing this side mission is 700 currency, 1 Silver Powder, and 5 Net Balls.

Side Mission 012: The Many Flowers of Flabébé

This side mission is obtainable after starting Main Mission 07: Reaching Rank W. It is obtained on South Boulevard from Vivace outside Coiffure Clips.

To complete this side mission, the player must show Vivace one of each of the five colors of Flabébé: red, orange, yellow, blue, and white.

The reward for completing this side mission is 1,500 currency and 1 Rare Candy. Additionally, showing each color of Flabébé unlocks a new color for use at any hair salon (red, orange, lemon yellow, blue, and white), and showing all five colors additionally unlocks the colors aqua, purple, and passion pink.

Side Mission 013: Stumped at the Fountain

This side mission is obtainable after starting Main Mission 07: Reaching Rank W. It is obtained in Jaune Sector 7 from Croquis in the courtyard.

To complete this side mission, the player must:

  1. Clear 1 bramble from the courtyard (using Ember)
  2. Speak with Croquis
  3. Clear 3 brambles from the courtyard
  4. Speak with Croquis
  5. Clear 6 brambles from the courtyard
  6. Speak with Croquis

The reward for completing this side mission is 1,000 currency and 3 Nest Balls.

Side Mission 014: Slurpuff's Café Visit

This side mission is obtainable after starting Main Mission 07: Reaching Rank W. It is obtained in Bleu Sector 10 from Meuse, the owner of the Shutterbug Café.

To complete this side mission, the player must go to Café Soleil on South Boulevard with Meuse's Slurpuff.

The reward for completing this side mission is 3,000 currency and 2 Whipped Dreams.

Side Mission 015: A Sensitive Audino

This side mission is obtainable after starting Main Mission 07: Reaching Rank W. It is obtained in Rouge Sector 1 from Hea in the alley southwest of Rouge Plaza.

To complete this side mission, the player must battle Hea. She has a level 20 Audino.

The reward for completing this side mission is 900 currency, 3 Revives, and 5 Super Potions.

Side Mission 016: The Budew Show

This side mission is obtainable after starting Main Mission 07: Reaching Rank W. It is obtained automatically when the player enters Vernal Pokémon Center in Aymlis Park from Jardin.

To complete this side mission, the player must:

  1. Add a Budew to their party
  2. Speak to Jardin again in the courtyard in Bleu Sector 8
  3. Agree to enter the Budew show and pay the entry fee (5,800)

The reward for completing this side mission is 10,000 currency (a net gain of 4,200 considering the entry fee) and 1 Miracle Seed.

Side Mission 017: A Shiny Mareep?

This side mission is obtainable after battling Lida during Main Mission 08: Reaching Rank V. It is obtained in Vert Sector 9 from Duve, north of the northeastern exit to Wild Zone 1.

To complete this side mission, the player must find the shiny Mareep in Wild Zone 1. It is on the eastern roof of the western building complex.

The reward for completing this side mission is 500 currency and 3 Pretty Feathers. The shiny Mareep itself can be captured following the conclusion of the side mission.

Side Mission 018: A Pan-tastic Pot of Tea

This side mission is obtainable after battling Lida during Main Mission 08: Reaching Rank V. It is obtained in Vert Sector 3 from Servando, the owner of Café Cyclone.

To complete this side mission, the player must put Pansage, Pansear, and Panpour in their party and show them to Servando.

The reward for completing this side mission is 1,000 currency, 3 HP Ups, and 1 White Herb. The Pantrio Tea menu item is also added to Café Cyclone's tea menu.

Side Mission 019: Poisonous, Paralyzing Strategies

This side mission is obtainable after battling Lida during Main Mission 08: Reaching Rank V. It is obtained in Vert Sector 3 from Venin in the courtyard.

To complete this side mission, the player must:

  1. Battle Lyse, who has a level 16 Electrike and a level 16 Budew
  2. Battle Venin, who has a level 16 Venipede and a level 16 Bellsprout

The reward for completing this side mission is 1,200 currency, 5 Potions, and 3 Poké Balls. Lyse also gives the player a Paralyze Heal after her battle, and Venin gives the player a Full Heal after hers.

Side Mission 020: A Berry Clever Plan

This side mission is obtainable after battling Lida during Main Mission 08: Reaching Rank V. It is obtained in Wild Zone 6 from Ticien on the tennis court.

To complete this side mission, the player must battle Ticien. He has a level 22 Swablu and a level 22 Meditite, both holding Oran Berries.

The reward for completing this side mission is 900 currency, 10 Oran Berries, and 2 Exp. Candies XS.

Side Mission 021: Spewpa in the Museum

This side mission is obtainable after battling Lida during Main Mission 08: Reaching Rank V. It is obtained in Lumiose Museum from Bundo, located left of the entrance.

To complete this side mission, the player must find twelve Spewpa in the museum. They are located in the following spots:

  1. In the corner behind the Lucario statue on 1F
  2. On the handrail of the stairs just behind Bundo and Wanda
  3. Between the couches on the stairwell just behind Bundo and Wanda
  4. On the modern Poké Ball display table on 2F
  5. On the 3D map of Lumiose on 2F
  6. On the couch near the 3D map of Lumiose on 2F
  7. On the crafting bench in the Hisui exhibit on 2F
  8. In the Hisui general store model on 2F
  9. In the Hisuian protagonists' clothing display on 2F
  10. In the Hisuian hairstyle exhibit on 2F
  11. On the Hisuian Commander's armor on 2F
  12. In front of the Hisuian hieroglyph mural on 2F

The reward for completing this side mission is 3,000 currency, 1 pair of Wise Glasses, and 3 Normal Gems. Bundo and Wanda also gift the player a level 9 Spewpa that will always evolve into a Marine pattern Vivillon.

Side Mission 022: A Call from Mable

This side mission is obtainable after battling Lida during Main Mission 08: Reaching Rank V. It is obtained via phone call when the player heals at any Pokémon Center following the battle with Lida, if the player has 30 or more species of Pokémon registered in the Pokédex.

To complete this side mission, the player must:

  1. Go to the Pokémon Research Lab
  2. Approach the Kanto starters on 3F
  3. Select one of the starters

The reward for completing this side mission is 1,000 currency, 3 Exp. Candies XS, and 2 Exp. Candies S. The player also receives the selected Kanto starter at level 5.

Side Mission 023: Underneath the Holovator

This side mission is obtainable after battling Taunie/Urbain during Main Mission 10: The Secrets of Mega Evolution. It is obtained on Vernal Avenue from Terra near the Holovator in Vert Sector 9.

To complete this side mission, the player must catch or defeat a level 28 Diggersby next to the Holovator in Vert Sector 9.

The reward for completing this side mission is 1,500 currency, 1 Soft Sand, and 1 Revive. The Holovator is also fixed, allowing access to the roof of the building complex.

Side Mission 024: An Abra Playmate

This side mission is obtainable after battling Taunie/Urbain during Main Mission 10: The Secrets of Mega Evolution. It is obtained in Académie Étoile from Bond inside the courtyard, near the entrance.

To complete this side mission, the player must trade Bond an Abra in exchange for his Riolu, which is level 25.

The reward for completing this side mission is 300 currency and 5 Exp. Candies XS.

Side Mission 025: Troublesome Patrons

This side mission is obtainable after battling Taunie/Urbain during Main Mission 10: The Secrets of Mega Evolution. It is obtained in Magenta Sector 4 from Fumi, the owner of Café Pokémon-Amie.

To complete this side mission, the player must:

  1. Receive the Tasty Trash item from Fumi.
  2. Lead the three Trubbish to the trash bins elsewhere in Magenta Sector 4.

The reward for completing this side mission is 1,200 currency, 2 Rare Candies, and 5 Dusk Balls.

Side Mission 026: Burn, Litleo, Burn

This side mission is obtainable after battling Taunie/Urbain during Main Mission 10: The Secrets of Mega Evolution. It is obtained on Autumnal Avenue from Armie outside Restaurant Le Yeah.

To complete this side mission, the player must battle Armie. He has a level 30 Litleo, a level 30 Numel, and a level 30 Houndoom.

The reward for completing this side mission is 900 currency, TM057 Will-O-Wisp, and 1 Fire Stone. Armie also gives the player a Burn Heal after the battle.

Side Mission 027: Restored from a Fossil

This side mission is obtainable after obtaining Main Missions 11, 12, and 13 during Main Mission 10: The Secrets of Mega Evolution. It is obtained on 2F of the Pokémon Research Lab from a scientist next to the elevator.

To complete this side mission, the player must:

  1. Purchase a Jaw or Sail Fossil from the Stone Emporium on Vernal Avenue. The scientist will give the player 20,000 currency to be used to purchase the Fossil.
  2. Return to the scientist and agree to have the Fossil revived.

The reward for completing this side mission is 2,500 currency, 1 Hard Stone, and 10 Kelpsy Berries. The player also receives the revived Fossil Pokémon at level 20, and can speak to the scientist to revive any Old Amber, Jaw Fossils, or Sail Fossils.

Side Mission 028: Who Says Normal Is Weak?

This side mission is obtainable after obtaining Main Missions 11, 12, and 13 during Main Mission 10: The Secrets of Mega Evolution. It is obtained in Bleu Sector 7 from Marnole in the courtyard.

To complete this side mission, the player must battle Marnole. He has a level 29 Audino, a level 29 Watchog, and a level 30 Lopunny.

The reward for completing this side mission is 3,000 currency and 1 Silk Scarf.

Side Mission 029: Full Course of Battles: One Star

This side mission is obtainable after obtaining Main Missions 11, 12, and 13 during Main Mission 10: The Secrets of Mega Evolution. It is obtained in Restaurant Le Nah from Rintaro at the host stand.

To complete this side mission, the player must battle three trainers in a row without being able to use items or heal between battles. The three trainers are:

  1. Audrey the Gourmet, with a level 30 Pansage and a level 30 Skiddo
  2. Thomas the Gourmet, with a level 30 Panpour and a level 30 Carvanha
  3. Thierry the Gourmet, with a level 30 Pansear and a level 30 Litleo

The reward for completing this side mission is 300 currency, TM085 Substitute, 2 Proteins, and 2 Irons. After completing the side mission, the player can also speak to Rintaro at any time to take the challenge again for 3,000 currency.

Side Mission 030: Show Me a Mega Camerupt!

This side mission is obtainable after starting Main Mission 14: Reaching Rank E. It is obtained on Vernal Avenue from Arden across the street from Coiffure Clips.

To complete this side mission, the player must battle Arden and use Mega Camerupt during the battle. Arden has a level 35 Watchog, a level 35 Krokorok, and a level 35 Sharpedo.

The reward for completing this side mission is 1,200 currency and 2 Fresh Waters. Arden also gives the player the "fired up" and "lovestruck" color contact options.

Side Mission 031: Show Me a Mega Sableye!

This side mission is obtainable after starting Main Mission 14: Reaching Rank E and completing Side Mission 030: Show Me a Mega Camerupt!. It is obtained on Vernal Avenue from Arden across the street from Coiffure Clips.

To complete this side mission, the player must battle Arden and use Mega Sableye during the battle. Arden has a level 35 Watchog, a level 35 Carbink, and a level 35 Krokorok.

The reward for completing this side mission is 3,000 currency and 2 Soda Pops. Arden also gives the player the "starry eyes" and "black void" color contact options.

Side Mission 033: Who Has the Bigger Magikarp?

This side mission is obtainable after starting Main Mission 14: Reaching Rank E. It is obtained in Vert Sector 4 from Kingsley at the end of the alley with the water southwest of Vert Plaza.

To complete this side mission, the player must show Kingsley a large Magikarp. I am unsure how large the Magikarp has to be, but I know that alpha Magikarp will work for this request (and Kingsley's dialogue may indicate an alpha is required). One can be found as a static encounter in Wild Zone 2.

The reward for completing this side mission is 1,500 currency, 1 Pearl, and 10 Exp. Candies S.

Side Mission 034: Moves That Put Up a Wall

This side mission is obtainable after starting Main Mission 14: Reaching Rank E. It is obtained in Jaune Sector 8 from Muro in the corner of the street north of the grassy area.

To complete this side mission, the player must battle Muro. He has a level 30 Kadabra and a level 30 Vivillon.

The reward for completing this side mission is 1,100 currency, TM031 Reflect, and 1 Lemonade.

Side Mission 035: Guidance from a Yoga Master

This side mission is obtainable after battling Naveen during Main Mission 14: Reaching Rank E. It is obtained on Jaune Street from Paix at the southeastern corner of Jaune Sector 1.

To complete this side mission, the player must find the yoga master, using the vague hints provided by Paix. The yoga master is located on top of the memorial in Rouge Plaza.

The reward for completing this side mission is 2,000 currency, 1 Twisted Spoon, and 5 Exp. Candies S.
