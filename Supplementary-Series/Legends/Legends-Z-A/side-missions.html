Legends: Z-A Side Missions

This page contains information about the Side Missions that can be taken in Legends: Z-A, including the location, requirements, and rewards for each side mission. Use the box below to jump to a particular side mission.



Side Mission 001: A Big Ol' Bunnelby

This request is obtainable after speaking with Emma during Main Mission 05: The City in the Shadow of Prism Tower. It is obtained in Rouge Sector 6 from Trevelle near Rouge Plaza.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Speak with Trevelle in Rouge Sector 8, in the courtyard surrounded by the western building complex.
  2. Catch or defeat a level 10 alpha Bunnelby.

The reward for completing this request is 400 currency and 2 Fresh Waters.

Side Mission 002: A Use for an Evolution Stone!

This request is obtainable after speaking with Emma during Main Mission 05: The City in the Shadow of Prism Tower. It is obtained in Passage du Palais from Tracie near the western entrance.

To complete this request, player must trade Tracie a Pikachu in exchange for her Heracross, which is level 12.

The reward for completing this request is 800 currency, 5 Poké Balls, and 3 Potions.

Side Mission 003: Sableye in the Cemetery

This request is obtainable after speaking with Emma during Main Mission 05: The City in the Shadow of Prism Tower. It is obtained on Rouge Street from Moire near the western entrance to Dormez Bien Cemetery.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Catch or defeat a level 10 Sableye in the northeast clearing of Wild Zone 5. The player will receive the Cherished Ring after the battle.
  2. Return the Cherished Ring to Moire.

The reward for completing this request is 4,000 currency and 2 Revives.

Side Mission 004: A Break Time Battle

This request is obtainable after starting Main Mission 06: Reaching Rank X. It is obtained in Rouge Sector 1 from Sommi near the out-of-service Holovator west of Rouge Plaza.

To complete this request, the player must battle Sommi. He has a level 8 Pidgey, a level 8 Budew, and a level 10 Emolga.

The reward for completing this request is 1,200 currency, 3 Exp. Candies S, and 1 Fresh Water. Sommi also fixes the Holovator, allowing access to the roof of the building complex here and some of the buildings in Rouge Sector 3.

Side Mission 005: I'd Like to See an Ekans!

This request is obtainable after starting Main Mission 06: Reaching Rank X. It is obtained in Magenta Sector 1 from Ismene near the stalls.

To complete this request, the player must put an Ekans in their party and show it to Ismene.

The reward for completing this request is 500 currency, 3 Pecha Berries, 3 Cheri Berries, and 1 Soda Pop.

Side Mission 006: Long-Range Moves Have Style

This request is obtainable after starting Main Mission 06: Reaching Rank X. It is obtained in Bleu Sector 4 from Distan on the tennis court.

To complete this request, the player must battle Distan. He has a level 16 Binacle, a level 16 Fletchling, and a level 16 Skiddo.

The reward for completing this request is 1,000 currency and 1 Nugget.

Side Mission 007: A Feisty Chespin

This request is obtainable after starting Main Mission 06: Reaching Rank X. It is obtained in Académie Étoile from Axi in front of the academy building.

To complete this request, the player must battle a level 14 Chespin three times. The player cannot catch Chespin during these battles.

The reward for completing this request is 1,000 currency, 3 Exp. Candies XS, and 1 Exp. Candy S. The Chespin also joins the player.

Side Mission 008: Get Well, Fennekin

This request is obtainable after starting Main Mission 06: Reaching Rank X. It is obtained in Magenta Sector 2 from Branche in the grassy area.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Speak with the herbalist, an old man sitting at one of the outdoor tables in front of Café Classe on Vernal Avenue
  2. Investigate 5 twigs in Wild Zone 5. The 5 twigs are located in the eastern part of the Zone, in the grassy area with the Venipede and alpha Whirlipede that is accessible from the waterway in the western part of the Zone
  3. Return to Branche

The reward for completing this request is 1,200 currency, 3 Exp. Candies XS, and 1 Exp. Candy S. The revitalized Fennekin also joins the player; it is level 14.

Side Mission 009: A Challenge from Froakie

This request is obtainable after starting Main Mission 06: Reaching Rank X. It is obtained in Jaune Sector 8 from Surv on the grassy area next to North Boulevard.

To complete this request, the player must complete three scaffolding courses in a given amount of time. The first two courses are around the grassy area in Jaune Sector 8 and must be completed in 30 and 40 seconds, respectively. The third course is on North Boulevard north of Passage du Palais and must be completed in 50 seconds.

The reward for completing this request is 1,800 currency, 3 Exp. Candies XS, and 1 Exp. Candy S. The Froakie that the player is trying to beat also joins the player; it is level 14.

Side Mission 010: Skiddo's Fragrant Leaves

This request is obtainable after starting Main Mission 07: Reaching Rank W. It is obtained on Vernal Avenue from Odette in Promenade du Vent, outside Boutique Couture.

To complete this request, the player must put a Skiddo in their party and show it to Odette.

The reward for completing this request is 800 currency, 2 Sachets, and 5 Persim Berries.

Side Mission 011: The Kakuna Master

This request is obtainable after starting Main Mission 07: Reaching Rank W. It is obtained in Jaune Sector 1 from Montée under a tree.

To complete this request, the player must battle Montée. She has three Kakuna at levels 8, 8, and 9, and a level 11 Beedrill.

The reward for completing this request is 700 currency, 1 Silver Powder, and 5 Net Balls.

Side Mission 012: The Many Flowers of Flabébé

This request is obtainable after starting Main Mission 07: Reaching Rank W. It is obtained on South Boulevard from Vivace outside Coiffure Clips.

To complete this request, the player must show Vivace one of each of the five colors of Flabébé: red, orange, yellow, blue, and white.

The reward for completing this request is 1,500 currency and 1 Rare Candy. Additionally, showing each color of Flabébé unlocks a new color for use at any hair salon (red, orange, lemon yellow, blue, and white), and showing all five colors additionally unlocks the colors aqua, purple, and passion pink.

Side Mission 013: Stumped at the Fountain

This request is obtainable after starting Main Mission 07: Reaching Rank W. It is obtained in Jaune Sector 7 from Croquis in the courtyard.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Clear 1 bramble from the courtyard (using Ember)
  2. Speak with Croquis
  3. Clear 3 brambles from the courtyard
  4. Speak with Croquis
  5. Clear 6 brambles from the courtyard
  6. Speak with Croquis

The reward for completing this request is 1,000 currency and 3 Nest Balls.

Side Mission 014: Slurpuff's Café Visit

This request is obtainable after starting Main Mission 07: Reaching Rank W. It is obtained in Bleu Sector 10 from Meuse, the owner of the Shutterbug Café.

To complete this request, the player must go to Café Soleil on South Boulevard with Meuse's Slurpuff.

The reward for completing this request is 3,000 currency and 2 Whipped Dreams.

Side Mission 015: A Sensitive Audino

This request is obtainable after starting Main Mission 07: Reaching Rank W. It is obtained in Rouge Sector 1 from Hea in the alley southwest of Rouge Plaza.

To complete this request, the player must battle Hea. She has a level 20 Audino.

The reward for completing this request is 900 currency, 3 Revives, and 5 Super Potions.

Side Mission 016: The Budew Show

This request is obtainable after starting Main Mission 07: Reaching Rank W. It is obtained automatically when the player enters Vernal Pokémon Center in Aymlis Park from Jardin.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Add a Budew to their party
  2. Speak to Jardin again in the courtyard in Bleu Sector 8
  3. Agree to enter the Budew show and pay the entry fee (5,800)

The reward for completing this request is 10,000 currency (a net gain of 4,200 considering the entry fee) and 1 Miracle Seed.

Side Mission 017: A Shiny Mareep?

This request is obtainable after battling Lida during Main Mission 08: Reaching Rank V. It is obtained in Vert Sector 9 from Duve, north of the northeastern exit to Wild Zone 1.

To complete this request, the player must find the shiny Mareep in Wild Zone 1. It is on the eastern roof of the western building complex.

The reward for completing this request is 500 currency and 3 Pretty Feathers. The shiny Mareep itself can be captured following the conclusion of the side mission.

Side Mission 018: A Pan-tastic Pot of Tea

This request is obtainable after battling Lida during Main Mission 08: Reaching Rank V. It is obtained in Vert Sector 3 from Servando, the owner of Café Cyclone.

To complete this request, the player must put Pansage, Pansear, and Panpour in their party and show them to Servando.

The reward for completing this request is 1,000 currency, 3 HP Ups, and 1 White Herb. The Pantrio Tea menu item is also added to Café Cyclone's tea menu.

Side Mission 019: Poisonous, Paralyzing Strategies

This request is obtainable after battling Lida during Main Mission 08: Reaching Rank V. It is obtained in Vert Sector 3 from Venin in the courtyard.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Battle Lyse, who has a level 16 Electrike and a level 16 Budew
  2. Battle Venin, who has a level 16 Venipede and a level 16 Bellsprout

The reward for completing this request is 1,200 currency, 5 Potions, and 3 Poké Balls. Lyse also gives the player a Paralyze Heal after her battle, and Venin gives the player a Full Heal after hers.

Side Mission 021: Spewpa in the Museum

This request is obtainable after battling Lida during Main Mission 08: Reaching Rank V. It is obtained in Lumiose Museum from Bundo, located left of the entrance.

To complete this request, the player must find twelve Spewpa in the museum. They are located in the following spots:

  1. In the corner behind the Lucario statue on 1F
  2. On the handrail of the stairs just behind Bundo and Wanda
  3. Between the couches on the stairwell just behind Bundo and Wanda
  4. On the modern Poké Ball display table on 2F
  5. On the 3D map of Lumiose on 2F
  6. On the couch near the 3D map of Lumiose on 2F
  7. On the crafting bench in the Hisui exhibit on 2F
  8. In the Hisui general store model on 2F
  9. In the Hisuian protagonists' clothing display on 2F
  10. In the Hisuian hairstyle exhibit on 2F
  11. On the Hisuian Commander's armor on 2F
  12. In front of the Hisuian hieroglyph mural on 2F

The reward for completing this request is 3,000 currency, 1 pair of Wise Glasses, and 3 Normal Gems. Bundo and Wanda also gift the player a level 9 Spewpa that will always evolve into a Marine pattern Vivillon.

Side Mission 022: A Call from Mable

This request is obtainable after battling Lida during Main Mission 08: Reaching Rank V. It is obtained via phone call when the player heals at any Pokémon Center following the battle with Lida, if the player has 30 or more species of Pokémon registered in the Pokédex.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Go to the Pokémon Research Lab
  2. Approach the Kanto starters on 3F
  3. Select one of the starters

The reward for completing this request is 1,000 currency, 3 Exp. Candies XS, and 2 Exp. Candies S. The player also receives the selected Kanto starter at level 5.
