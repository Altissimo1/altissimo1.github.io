Lumiose Map

An interactive map of Lumiose, displaying items, Colorful Screws, and ladder locations. Click an item or Colorful Screw icon to see a short description of its location below the map.

This is a work in progress and, currently, only shows the locations of all items obtainable when the open world opens up during Mission 05. I plan to add more icons as I go, and also add icons for Holovators, Pokémon Centers, Fly locations, and (when I am able to properly source this information) wild Pokémon.
