Pokémon Legends: Z-A

Pokémon Legends: Z-A was released in October 2025 for the Switch, with an upgraded version available for the Switch 2. A DLC package, Mega Dimension, was released in December 2025. The mechanics used to catch Pokémon are similar to the previous Legends game, Legends: Arceus, and the battle mechanics are very different from any other Pokémon game released up to this point. The game uses its own unique Pokédexes: the Lumiose Pokédex for Pokémon in the base game, and the Hyperspace Pokédex for Pokémon added in the Mega Dimension DLC. There are 232 Pokémon in the Lumiose Pokédex and an additional 130 in the Hyperspace Pokédex.
