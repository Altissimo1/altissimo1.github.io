Recipes

Throughout the game, the player can obtain recipes that will allow them to craft items using raw materials, which themselves can either be found in the overworld or purchased from shops. The player can craft at the workbenches located at Anvin's shop and at each of the wilderness areas' camps, which will allow them to make items using all materials in their inventory and storage, and with the Crafting Kit, which will only allow them to use items currently in their inventory.

This page gives the locations and progress requirements of all recipes.
