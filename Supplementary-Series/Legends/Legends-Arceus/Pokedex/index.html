Legends: Arceus Pokédex Pages

The subpages linked here detail some numbers and requirements linked to completing the Pokédex in Legends: Arceus.

How Many Pokémon Can You Get with One Game?

Unlike other Pokémon games, Legends: Arceus actually makes it possible to get all Pokémon without trading. This is also the ultimate goal of the Professor Oak Challenge.

How Many Pokémon Can You Get Without Evolving?

In Mystery Dungeon, I often strive to recruit as many Pokémon as possible at the evolutionary stage I want them at, no matter their spawn rate, and evolve only the ones I can't get otherwise. This page outlines what Pokémon can be obtained with a similar approach to Legends: Arceus.

Completing a Living Dex

Although all Pokémon can be obtained, not all forms can be obtained at one time. This page discusses the highest possible number of Pokémon that can be obtained in a living dex, and what would be required to finish the living dex.
