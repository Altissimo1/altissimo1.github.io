A list of Path of Solitude opponents in Legends: Arceus, and their Pokémon's stats and moves. This YouTube playlist by Alpha Clément appears to have strategies for all fights, and it looks like there is an effort to show them all being completed with minimum effort (e.g. low-level Pokémon, not maxing every stat), but I have yet to review or test these. Once I have, I will see about updating this page with text descriptions of strategies and potentially uploading some of my own videos.

Some Path of Solitude battles are required in order to complete Requests 108-122. The specific Pokémon whose battles are required are listed on this page as well.

Attacks with a plus sign + next to their name indicate mastery.

Arceus

In Arceus' Path of Solitude, the three Pokémon shown below will all be sent out at the same time.

The player can choose to use a Plate on Arceus prior to the battle to change Arceus' type and the type of Judgment to that type. If the Legend Plate is used, Arceus will become Normal-type. When Judgment is used with the Legend Plate, a type is selected that is super-effective on the opposing Pokémon's type(s), and Arceus' type will change before the attack is executed to provide STAB to Judgment. The type will be determined as follows:

  • If any type is doubly super-effective against the target Pokémon, that type will be used.
  • Otherwise, any type that is singly super-effective against the target will be used.
  • If multiple types tie for super-effectiveness, the type that is immune to (prioritized) or best resists the target's primary type will be chosen.
  • If, after the above step, multiple types still tie, then the type that is immune to (prioritized) or best resists the target's second type will be chosen.
  • If, after the above step, multiple types still tie, a qualifying type will be chosen at random.
