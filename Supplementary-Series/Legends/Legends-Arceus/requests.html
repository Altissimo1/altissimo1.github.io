Legends: Arceus Requests

This page contains information about the 122 Requests that can be taken in Legends: Arceus, including the location, requirements, and rewards for each request. Use the box below to jump to a particular request.

Sometimes, if the player completes a request that is required to unlock another request, and the player has already met the requirements for the next request, the request may not be available immediately. It seems to be the case that catching Pokémon (10-20) may be sufficient to unlock the following request.



Request 1: Wurmple Can Evolve

This request is obtainable after completing Mission 3: The Basics of Crafting. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Beauregard, a Security Corps member located outside the entrance to the Galaxy Hall.

To complete this request, the player must give Beauregard a (non-Alpha) Wurmple. He will keep the Wurmple forever.

The reward for completing this request is 3 Dazzling Honeys. Completing this request also allows the player to later take Request 10: Wurmple's Evolved!.

The player can speak to Beauregard to see Wurmple evolve into Cascoon. To trigger Wurmple's evolution, the player must start Mission 5: A Request from Mai and catch 15 Pokémon after starting the mission. Seeing Wurmple's evolution is required to unlock Request 10: Wurmple's Evolved!.

Request 2: Adorable Starly

This request is obtainable after completing Mission 3: The Basics of Crafting. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Marli, a Supply Corps member located near the General Store on Floaro Main Street.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Get Starly to rank 10 in the Pokédex and show the completed entry to Marli.
  2. Show Marli a Starly.

The reward for completing this request is 10 Poké Balls.

Request 3: What Do Shinx's Ears Look Like?

This request is obtainable after completing Mission 3: The Basics of Crafting. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Toshi, a villager located near the eastern gate.

To complete this request, the player must show Toshi a Shinx.

The reward for completing this request is 3 Potions.

Request 4: Big Buizel, Little Buizel

This request is obtainable after completing Mission 3: The Basics of Crafting. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Dorian, a Security Corps member located next to the southern gate.

To complete this request, the player must give Dorian a Buizel whose height is greater than 0.8m (2'8"). Due to an oversight, some slightly smaller Buizel can be offered to complete this quest. Alpha Buizel will always be large enough to fulfill the requirement. Dorian will keep the Buizel forever.

The reward for completing this request is 5 Oran Berries and 1 Exp. Candy S.

Request 5: What it Takes to Be Awesome

This request is obtainable after starting Mission 5: A Request from Mai. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Sho, a villager located under the tree north of the bridge near the eastern gate. He does not have a request marker.

To complete this request, the player must give Sho 5 Poké Balls.

The reward for completing this request is 3 Grit Dusts.

Request 6: Mushroom Cake Marketing

This request is obtainable after starting Mission 5: A Request from Mai. It is obtained in Obsidian Fieldlands from Morel, a villager located at the Fieldlands camp.

To complete this request, the player must give Morel 1 Springy Mushroom.

The reward for completing this request is the recipe for Mushroom Cakes. Morel also gives the player 5 Cake-Lure Bases. Completing this request also allows the player to later take Request 33: What a Massive Mushroom!.

Request 7: Playing with Drifloon

This request is obtainable after starting Mission 5: A Request from Mai and catching a Drifloon. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Laventon's blackboard.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Speak with Miki, a Security Corps member located near the southern gate.
  2. Change the village's time to evening.
  3. Investigate near the cabin at Prelude Beach.
  4. Return to Miki.

The reward for completing this request is 1 Stardust. Completing it also unlocks the Sky Pattern at Anthe's clothing store, and fulfills the "Investigated whether Drifloon truly does play with kids" task in Drifloon's Pokédex entry.

Request 8: Bothersome Bidoof

This request is obtainable after starting Mission 5: A Request from Mai. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Laventon's blackboard.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Speak with Tsumugi, a Security Corps member located near the eastern gate.
  2. Add a Bidoof to your active party and find three Bidoof in three locations around the village: west of the southwesternmost building in the village, next to the player character's quarters, and in the southeasternmost corner within the village walls.

The reward for completing this request is 1 Rare Candy. Completing it also unlocks the Blank Pattern at Anthe's clothing store, and fulfills the "Investigated the Bidoof that bothered the village" task in Bidoof's Pokédex entry.

Request 9: Zubat's Eyes

This request is obtainable after starting Mission 5: A Request from Mai. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Laventon's blackboard.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Speak with Clarissa, a Security Corps member east of the farm.
  2. Show her a Zubat.

The reward for completing this request is 2 Aux Powers. Completing it also fulfills the "Investigated Zubat's knack for navigating in the dark" task in Zubat's Pokédex entry.

Request 10: Wurmple's Evolved!

This request is obtainable after completing Request 1: Wurmple Can Evolve, seeing Beauregard's Wurmple evolve into Cascoon, catching 10 Pokémon after witnessing the evolution, and registering both Silcoon and Cascoon in the Pokédex. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Laventon's blackboard.

Seeing Beauregard's Wurmple evolve requires completing Request 1: Wurmple Can Evolve, starting Mission 5: A Request from Mai, and catching 15 Pokémon after starting Mission 5.

To complete this request, the player must show Beauregard a Silcoon.

The reward for completing this request is 1 Exp. Candy S. Completing it also fulfills the "Investigated how Silcoon and Cascoon differ" task in Cascoon's Pokédex entry.

Speaking with Beauregard after completing the quest will cause Cascoon to evolve into Dustox. I am not sure what conditions need to be met to trigger this evolution, although I suspect catching Dustox is one of them.

Request 11: The Timbre of the Fields

This request is obtainable after starting Mission 5: A Request from Mai. It is obtained in Obsidian Fieldlands from Yeo, a villager located at the Fieldlands camp.

To complete this request, the player must get Kricketot to rank 10 in the Pokédex and show the completed entry to Yeo.

The reward for completing this request is 3 Vivichokes. Completing it also unlocks the Insect Pattern at Anthe's clothing store.

Request 12: A Perfect Pickling Stone

This request is obtainable after starting Mission 5: A Request from Mai. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Radisa, a villager located on 2F of Galaxy Hall.

To complete this request, the player must give Radisa a Geodude. She will keep the Geodude forever.

The reward for completing this request is 3 Honey Cakes and 1 Exp. Candy S. Completing it also unlocks the Earth Pattern at Anthe's clothing store and allows the player to later take Request 53: Octillery's Ink.

Request 13: Trees that Bear Berries

This request is obtainable after starting Mission 5: A Request from Mai. It is obtained in Obsidian Fieldlands from Bjorn, an Agriculture Corps member located around Deertrack Path.

To complete this request, the player must give Bjorn 3 Cheri Berries.

The reward for completing this request is 15 Poké Balls.

Request 14: Berry Helpful

This request is obtainable after starting Mission 5: A Request from Mai. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Andra, a villager located near the eastern gate.

To complete this request, the player must give Andra 5 Oran Berries.

The reward for completing this request is 3 Revives. Completing this request also allows the player to later take Request 21: Back-Alley Mr. Mime.

Request 15: Balloon Race in the Fieldlands

This request is obtainable after obtaining the Celestica Flute during Mission 7: The Frenzy of the Lord of the Woods. It is obtained in Obsidian Fieldlands from the Balloon Guy located at the Heights Camp.

To complete this request, the player must complete the Fieldlands Balloon Race minigame with a total of 17 or more balloons burst.

The reward for completing this request is 1 Rare Candy and 15 Feather Balls. The player will also receive rewards separately for beating the minigame, with the exact rewards depending on the player's score. If the player has the free Daybreak update, completing this request also allows the player to later take Request 105: The Ultimate Balloon Race.

Request 16: Strange Happenings at Midnight

This request is obtainable after completing Mission 5: A Request from Mai and catching a Pichu. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Sanqua, a Construction Corps member located north of the southern gate.

To complete this request, the player must investigate Sanqua's house at night.

The reward for completing this request is 7 Nanab Berries.

Request 17: To Bloom or Not to Bloom

This request is obtainable after completing Mission 5: A Request from Mai. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Kichi, an Agriculture Corps member located near the farm.

To complete this request, the player must get Cherrim to rank 10 in the Pokédex and show the completed entry to Kichi.

The reward for completing this request is 5 Vivichokes.

Request 18: Please! Make Me a Pokéshi Doll!

This request is obtainable after starting Mission 7: The Frenzy of the Lord of the Woods. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Anvin, the owner of the Craftworks.

To complete this request, the player must craft a Pokéshi Doll and give it to Anvin. At the beginning of the request, Anvin will give the player the recipe for the Pokéshi Doll.

The reward for completing this request is 1 Pokéshi Doll.

Request 19: A Peculiar Ponyta

This request is obtainable after defeating Irida during Mission 7: The Frenzy of the Lord of the Woods and catching a Ponyta. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Laventon's blackboard.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Speak with Yota, a villager located on the eastern bridge.
  2. Catch the shiny Ponyta that appears on Horseshoe Plains in Obsidian Fieldlands.
  3. Return to Yota.

The reward for completing this request is 5 Razz Berries. Completing it also fulfills the "Investigated a sighting of an unusual Ponyta" task in Ponyta's Pokédex entry.

Request 20: The Mysterious Will-o'-the-Wisp

This request is obtainable after defeating Irida during Mission 7: The Frenzy of the Lord of the Woods. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Paira, a villager located southeast of the central bridge.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Catch the Chimchar that appears on Windswept Run in Obsidian Fieldlands.
  2. Return to Paira.

The reward for completing this request is 2 Exp. Candy S'. Completing it also unlocks the Flame Pattern at Anthe's clothing store.

Request 21: Back-Alley Mr. Mime

This request is obtainable after defeating Irida during Mission 7: The Frenzy of the Lord of the Woods, catching a Mr. Mime, and completing Request 14: Berry Helpful. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Laventon's blackboard.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Speak with Andra, a villager located southwest of the central bridge.
  2. Interact with Mr. Mime in three locations: between buildings in the village, behind Galaxy Hall, and near the eastern gate. In all cases, invisible walls mean that Mr. Mime can only be approached from specific directions.

The reward for completing this request is 2 Aux Guards. Completing it also unlocks the Mind Pattern at Anthe's clothing store and fulfills the "Investigated the suspicious movements of Mr. Mime" task in Mr. Mime's Pokédex entry.

Request 22: Eerie Apparitions in the Night

This request is obtainable after completing Mission 7: The Frenzy of the Lord of the Woods. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Vessa, who will present the request to the player in an unavoidable cutscene wherein the player must also collect the first Wisp in Jubilife Village.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Collect all 107 Wisps found in Jubilife Village and the game's five wilderness areas. My maps can assist with tracking the wisps the player has collected.
  2. Speak with Vessa near the river in Jubilife Village.
  3. Meet with Vessa at the Shrouded Ruins in Crimson Mirelands, at night.
  4. Collect the final Wisp on the Odd Keystone on the small hill.

The reward for completing this request is 3 Twice-Spiced Radishes. Completing it also spawns a level 60 Spiritomb at the Shrouded Ruins that the player can capture. This will also unlock Spiritomb as a rare spawn in that location, and allow Spiritomb to appear as a base species for Massive Mass Outbreaks.

Additionally, Vessa will give the player rewards for collecting certain numbers of Wisps when spoken to. The rewards she gives are as follows:

  • 5 Wisps: Oval Stone
  • 10 Wisps: Rare Candy
  • 20 Wisps: Seed of Mastery
  • 30 Wisps: Dusk Stone
  • 40 Wisps: Exp. Candy L
  • 50 Wisps: Linking Cord
  • 60 Wisps: 5 Grit Pebbles
  • 70 Wisps: Reaper Cloth
  • 80 Wisps: 3 Seeds of Mastery
  • 90 Wisps: 2 Exp. Candy XL's
  • 100 Wisps: 3 Grit Rocks

Request 23: Getting Ahold of New Wares

This request is obtainable after starting Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Choy, the owner of the General Store.

To complete this request, the player must give 3 Hearty Grains to Tao Hua, the captain of the Supply Corps, and return to Choy. Tao Hua is located in the basement of Galaxy Hall.

The reward for completing this request is that new items are added to the General Store's stock: Heavy Balls, Feather Balls, Super Potions, Smoke Bombs, Mushroom Cakes, and Honey Cakes. Completing this request also allows the player to later take Request 43: More New Wares.

Request 24: Inspiration from Hippopotas

This request is obtainable after starting Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament and speaking with Anthe, the clothier, at least once. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Anthe.

To complete this request, the player must show Anthe both a male and female Hippopotas.

The reward for completing this request is that new items are added to Anthe's clothing store. The new items are Patterned Kimonos and Patterned Bottoms with the following patterns: Oribe pattern (I, II, and III), Stars (I, II, and III), Checkered (I, II, and III), and Large dots (I and II). Completing this request also allows the player to later take Request 45: Shellos of the East and West.

Request 25: The Pokémon in the Woodland Photo

This request is obtainable after starting Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament and taking a picture at the Photography Studio. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Dagero, the owner of the Photography Studio.

To complete this request, the player must show Dagero a Buneary.

The reward for completing this request is that the Monochromatic lens filter is added to the available photography filters. Completing this request also allows the player to later take Request 44: The Pokémon in the Nighttime Photo.

Request 26: Aim for the Big Leagues!

This request is obtainable after starting Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Taggart, a villager located south of the central bridge.

To complete this request, the player must complete the target practice minigame at the Practice Field with a score of 10,000 points or greater.

The reward for completing this request is 15 Great Balls and 1 Nugget. The player will also receive rewards separately for beating the minigame, with the exact rewards depending on the player's score.

Request 27: Help Wanted: Plowing the Fields

This request is obtainable after starting Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Miller, an Agriculture Corps member located at the farm.

To complete this request, the player must give Miller a Ground-type Pokémon. He will keep the Pokémon forever, but the player can speak to him to substitute a different Ground-type Pokémon.

The reward for completing this request is that Colza will now offer Vegetable and Mushroom harvests at the farm. Completing this request also allows the player to later take Request 42: Help Wanted: Watering the Fields.

Request 28: Measuring Your Compatibility

This request is obtainable after starting Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Bellamy, a villager located near the eastern fence.

To complete this request, the player must show Bellamy a Pokémon with high friendship.

The reward for completing this request is 1 Rare Candy.

Request 29: The Search for Bitter Leaves

This request is available after starting Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Shinon, a villager located south of the central bridge.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Speak with Anise, a Medical Corps member located in Galaxy Hall.
  2. Give Anise a Petilil. She will keep the Petilil forever.

The reward for completing this request is 3 Fine Remedies and 5 Hopo Berries.

Request 30: A Beautiful Rose...

This request is available after starting Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament. It is obtained in Crimson Mirelands from Berra, a villager located at the Mirelands camp.

To complete this request, the player must get Roselia to rank 10 in the Pokédex and show the completed entry to Berra.

The reward for completing this request is 5 Grain Cakes and 2 Exp. Candy S'. Completing it also unlocks the Meadow Pattern at Anthe's clothing store.

Request 31: Setting Up the Bogbound Camp

This request is available after starting Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament. It is obtained in Crimson Mirelands from Maldon, a Construction Corps member located near the Sludge Mound.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Speak with Odo, a Construction Corps member at the site of the Bogbound camp.
  2. Catch or defeat 3 level 20 Stunky.

The reward for completing this request is the new Bogbound camp. Completing it also allows the player to later take Request 36: Watering with Care and Request 40: The Charm Lost in the Swamp.

Request 32: The Headache-Stricken Psyduck

This request is available after starting Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament. It is obtained in Crimson Mirelands from Martia, a Diamond Clan member located at the Diamond Settlement.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Speak with Tomma, a Medical Corps member in Galaxy Hall.
  2. Give Martia the Secret Medicine.

The reward for completing this request is 1 Max Revive.

Request 33: What a Massive Mushroom!

This request is available after starting Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament, catching a Parasect, and completing Request 6: Mushroom Cake Marketing. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Laventon's blackboard.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Speak with Morel, a villager in a house south of the central bridge.
  2. Give him a Parasect. He will keep the Parasect forever.

The reward for completing this request is 5 Candy Truffles. Completing it also fulfills the "Investigated the mushroom growing on Parasect" task in Parasect's Pokédex entry and allows the player to later take Request 76: Mushroom Hunting with Swinub.

Request 34: Croagunk's Curative Poison

This request is available after completing Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament and catching a Croagunk. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Laventon's blackboard.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Speak with Pesselle, a Medical Corps member in Galaxy Hall.
  2. Give her a Croagunk. She will keep the Croagunk forever.

The reward for completing this request is 3 Full Heals and 3 Exp. Candy S'. Completing it also unlocks the Toxic Pattern at Anthe's clothing store, fulfills the "Investigated Croagunk poison's medicinal properties" task in Croagunk's Pokédex entry, and allows the player to later take Request 72: Pesselle's Easy Errand.

Request 35: Battling with Pachirisu

This request is available after unlocking Ursaluna as a rideable Pokémon during Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament and catching a Pachirisu. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Laventon's blackboard.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Speak with Ren, a Security Corps member at the Mirelands camp.
  2. Defeat him in a battle using only Pachirisu. He uses a level 30 Ursaring.

The reward for completing this request is 3 Sitrus Berries and 1 Grit Gravel. Completing it also unlocks the Zap Pattern at Anthe's clothing store and fulfills the "Investigated strategies for battling with Pachirisu" task in Pachirisu's Pokédex entry.

Request 36: Watering with Care

This request is available after starting Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament, catching a Sudowoodo, and completing Request 31: Setting Up the Bogbound Camp. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Laventon's blackboard.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Speak with Odo, a Construction Corps member at the Bogbound camp.
  2. Show Odo a Pokémon with the move Water Pulse.
  3. Defeat a level 26 Sudowoodo.

The reward for completing this request is 15 Heavy Balls and 3 Exp. Candy S'. Completing it also unlocks the Stone Pattern at Anthe's clothing store and fulfills the "Investigated the causes behind a listless Sudowoodo" task in Sudowoodo's Pokédex entry.

Request 37: The Fragrance of Nostalgic Herbs

This request is available after quelling Lilligant during Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Risa, a villager in the basement of Galaxy Hall.

To complete this request, the player must show Risa a Tangela.

The reward for completing this request is 5 Smoke Bombs and 2 Exp. Candy S'.

Request 38: Gone Astray... in the Mirelands

This request is available after quelling Lilligant during Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Zeke, a villager in Galaxy Hall.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Use Ursaluna to find Wanda in Crimson Mirelands. She is located near the river between Gapejaw Bog and the southern mass of land that contains the Holm of Trials.
  2. Return to Zeke.

The reward for completing this request is 3 Stardust. Completing it also allows the player to later take Request 58: Gone Astray... in the Coastlands.

Request 39: All about Magikarp

This request is available after completing Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament and catching a Magikarp. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Ceci, a villager located between the General Store and Galaxy Hall, during the daytime.

To complete this request, the player must get Magikarp to rank 10 in the Pokédex and show the completed entry to Ceci.

The reward for completing this request is a Rare Candy. Completing it also unlocks the Splash Pattern at Anthe's clothing store.

Request 40: The Charm Lost in the Swamp

This request is available after completing Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament and Request 31: Setting Up the Bogbound Camp. It is obtained in Crimson Mirelands from Yojiro, a Security Corps member located at the Bogbound camp.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Find Yojiro's charm in the Gapejaw Bog. It is visible as a sparkle in the bog, around where the static alpha Hippowdon is.
  2. Catch or defeat a level 34 Hippowdon.

The reward for completing this request is 5 Iron Chunks and 1 Grit Gravel.

Request 41: An Elegant Tail

This request is available after completing Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Asabei, a villager located in the southwest part of the village.

To complete this request, the player must show Asabei a Glameow.

The reward for completing this request is 2 Aux Evasions and 2 Grit Gravels.

This request is available after completing Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament and Request 27: Help Wanted: Plowing the Fields. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Miller, an Agriculture Corps member located at the farm.

To complete this request, the player must give Miller a Pokémon with a Water-type move. He will keep the Pokémon forever, but the player can speak to him to substitute a different Pokémon with a Water-type move.

The reward for completing this request is that Colza will now offer Berry harvests (both regular and special) at the farm. (It's possible the special Berry harvest requires the free Daybreak update, but I am not able to check.) Completing this request also allows the player to later take Request 60: Help Wanted: Rock Smashing in the Fields.

Request 43: More New Wares

To complete this request, the player must give 3 Pop Pods to Tao Hua, the captain of the Supply Corps, and return to Choy. Tao Hua is located in the basement of Galaxy Hall.

The reward for completing this request is that new items are added to the General Store's stock: Great Balls, Hyper Potions, Full Heals, Stealth Sprays, Scatter Bangs, and Grain Cakes. Completing this request also allows the player to later take Request 61: Even More New Wares.

Request 44: The Pokémon in the Nighttime Photo

To complete this request, the player must show Dagero a Duskull.

The reward for completing this request is that the Dreamy lens filter is added to the available photography filters. Completing this request also allows the player to later take Request 62: The Pokémon in the River Photo.

Request 45: Shellos of the East and West

Request 46: Setting Up the Coastlands Camp

This request is available after starting Mission 10: The Lordless Island. It is obtained in Cobalt Coastlands from Gully, a Supply Corps member located in the pass between Aipom Hill and Bathers' Lagoon.

To complete this request, the player must:

  1. Speak with Gully again at the site of the Coastlands camp in Sand's Reach.
  2. Find Yorrich, a Construction Corps member located on the eastmost 'finger' of the Sand's Reach hand-shaped landform.
  3. Catch or defeat a level 34 Chatot.
  4. Speak with Gully again.

The reward for completing this request is the new Coastlands camp. Completing it also allows the player to later take Request 47: Balloon Race in the Coastlands, Request 55: Poor, Peckish Piplup, and Request 68: A Nosepass to Guide the Way.

Request 47: Balloon Race in the Coastlands

To complete this request, the player must complete the Coastlands Balloon Race minigame with a total of 24 or more balloons burst.

The reward for completing this request is 1 Nugget and 10 Ultra Balls. The player will also receive rewards separately for beating the minigame, with the exact rewards depending on the player's score. If the player has the free Daybreak update, completing this request also allows the player to later take Request 105: The Ultimate Balloon Race.

Request 48: The Taste of Home

This request is available after completing Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Floaro, a Construction Corps member located in a house south of the central bridge.

To complete this request, the player must bring Floaro 3 Hearty Grains, 2 Hopo Berries, and 1 Razz Berry.

This request is available after starting Mission 10: The Lordless Island. It is obtained in Cobalt Coastlands from Hiko, a villager located between Windbreak Stand and Veilstone Cape.

The reward for completing this request is 5 Scatter Bangs and 1 Exp. Candy M.

Request 50: Double the Tails, Double the Fun

To complete this request, the player must show Netta a Finneon.

The reward for completing this request is 1 Star Piece.

Request 51: Coming Up Roses

This request is available after unlocking Basculegion as a rideable Pokémon during Mission 10: The Lordless Island. It is obtained in Obsidian Fieldlands from Hiemo, a Diamond Clan member located on the northwest part of Ramanas Island.

To complete this request, the player must show Hiemo a Scyther.

The reward for completing this request is 1 Seed of Mastery.

Request 52: Eevee's Evolutions

This request is available after unlocking Basculegion as a rideable Pokémon during Mission 10: The Lordless Island, catching an Eevee, and completing Request 48: The Taste of Home. It is obtained in Jubilife Village from Laventon's blackboard.

  3. Craft a Jubilife Muffin and feed it to the Umbreon.

The reward for completing this request is 1 Rare Candy. Floaro will also give the player their choice of a Water, Fire, or Thunder Stone. Completing it also unlocks the Dread Pattern at Anthe's clothing store and fulfills the "Investigated more about how Eevee evolves" task in Eevee's Pokédex entry.

Request 53: Octillery's Ink
