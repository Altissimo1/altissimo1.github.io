Legends: Arceus Evo Item Locations

The following lists contain the location of all evolutionary items in Legends: Arceus.

Wisp Rewards

Collecting Wisps as part of Request 22: Eerie Apparitions in the Night will allow the player to get rewards from Vessa in Jubilife Village, based on the number of Wisps collected. Four of the rewards are evolution items.

  • 5 Wisps - Oval Stone
  • 30 Wisps - Dusk Stone
  • 50 Wisps - Linking Cord
  • 70 Wisps - Reaper Cloth

Attackable Objects

Some attackable objects (ores, leaf piles, and snow piles) in the wilderness areas can drop evolutionary stones when attacked.

  • Fire Stone: Brown Ores in Obsidian Fieldlands, Crimson Mirelands, and Cobalt Coastlands
  • Thunder Stone: Black Ores in Obsidian Fieldlands, Crimson Mirelands, and Cobalt Coastlands
  • Water Stone: Blue Ores in Obsidian Fieldlands, Crimson Mirelands, and Cobalt Coastlands
  • Shiny Stone: Brown Ores in Coronet Highlands and Alabaster Icelands
  • Dusk Stone: Black Ores in Coronet Highlands and Alabaster Icelands
  • Dawn Stone: Blue Ores in Coronet Highlands and Alabaster Icelands
  • Leaf Stone: Leaf piles in Crimson Mirelands, Cobalt Coastlands, and Coronet Highlands
  • Ice Stone: Snow piles in Alabaster Icelands

Space-Time Distortions

Evolutionary items are among the items that can appear on the ground during a space-time distortion. All evolutionary items have a 0.27% chance of appearing.

Upgrade Dubious Disc Electirizer Magmarizer Protector Linking Cord
Water Stone Fire Stone Thunder Stone Leaf Stone Moon Stone Sun Stone
Dusk Stone Shiny Stone Dawn Stone Ice Stone

Ursaluna Dig Spots

When digging with Ursaluna in any of the wilderness areas, Ursaluna has a chance to find a Sun Stone, Moon Stone, Black Augurite, or Peat Block. The chance of each item being dug up depends on the area.

Ginter's Shop

Ginter, a member of the Ginkgo Guild, sets up shop in Jubilife Village. He offers three items at a time and his inventory changes every time the player catches 20 Pokémon. Wild Pokémon

All wild Pokémon have a chance to drop items when defeated or captured. Several have a small chance of dropping an evolution item. All of these Pokémon must not be alpha to be able to drop these items.

Item Dropped by Rate
Black Augurite Graveler, Golem 8%
Metal Coat Scizor, Steelix, Magnemite, Magneton, Magnezone 7%

Reaper Cloth Duskull, Dusclops, Dusknoir 8%
Razor Fang Gligar, Gliscor 8%
Razor Claw Sneasel (both forms), Sneasler, Weavile 8%

Requests

Several Requests provide the player with evolutionary items, either as rewards for completing the request or as gifts from people during the request.

  • Request 52: Eevee's Evolutions: Choice of one of Water, Fire, or Thunder Stone
  • Request 64: Getting to Know Ghosts: Linking Cord
  • Request 67: The Clefairy's Moonlit Dance: A Moon Stone appears on the ground after the dance finishes
  • Request 68: A Nosepass to Guide the Way: Black Augurite
  • Request 73: Which Is the Real Burmy?: Leaf Stone
  • Request 74: A Bit of Help from Blissey: Shiny Stone
  • Request 82: Traces of a Lost Village: Dawn Stone
  • Request 85: At Home Under the Eaves: Sun Stone
  • Request 86: Gone Astray... in the Icelands: Peat Block, Ice Stone
  • Request 102: Daybreak (Daybreak update only): Dawn Stone
  • Request 106: Adaman's Hope (Daybreak update only): Leaf Stone
  • Request 107: Irida's Wish (Daybreak update only): Ice Stone
