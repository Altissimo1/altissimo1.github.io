Collectibles Maps

Interactive maps that will display Unown, wisp, Old Verse, and dig spot locations for each of the locations in Pokémon Legends: Arceus.

  • Jubilife Village | コトブキムラ
  • Obsidian Fieldlands | 黒曜の原野
  • Crimson Mirelands | 紅蓮の湿地
  • Cobalt Coastlands | 群青の海岸
  • Coronet Highlands | 天冠の山麓
  • Alabaster Icelands | 純白の凍土
