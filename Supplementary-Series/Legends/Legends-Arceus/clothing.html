Legends: Arceus Clothing Locations

The following tables contain the location of all clothing objects in Legends: Arceus.

Anthe's Shop

The majority of clothing items in the game are purchased from the clothier, Anthe, in Jubilife Village. Most of her inventory is tied to the completion of requests, missions, or Pokédex entries; the requirements for all clothing items are shown below.

Other

A handful of other clothing items are obtained in other ways than buying them from Anthe's shop.

Starting clothing

When the player begins the game, the outfit the player character is using is referred to as the Casual Tee (a Two-Piece Suit), and their footwear is Sandals. Both of these are available to be changed into after the player is forced to change into the Survey Corps outfit.

Survey Corps outfit

During Mission 2: The Galaxy Team's Entry Trial, Cyllene gives the player the Survey Corps Uniform (a Two-Piece Suit) and Survey Corps Sandals, and requests the player change immediately. Upon returning to Galaxy Hall, the player is also given a Survey Corps Cap.

Everyday clothing

Upon leaving Galaxy Hall after receiving the Survey Corps uniform, Anthe gifts the player an Everyday Kimono and Everyday Trousers, both in the Nickel pattern.

Ginter

In the free Daybreak update, the player can buy Slippers from Ginter for 5000; these are referred to in his inventory as Unusual Shoes.

Color contacts

When changing outfits in the player's quarters, the player has the option to change their eye color using color contacts. The available colors are: Hazel, Gray, Green, Pale blue, Black, Yellow, Pink, Purple, Brown, Ultramarine, Yellow-green, Lavender ice, Poppy red, and White.

Event

Some clothing items were only made available as part of Mystery Gift distributions or with save data for other Pokémon Switch games on the system. While the Mystery Gift distributions are no longer available, items requiring save data can still be accessed; they are listed here because they require additional software the player may not always have access to.

Event clothing can be obtained by speaking with Anthe.

Mystery Gift

  • Baneful Fox Mask (White lily color): 28 Jan 2022 - 09 May 2022
  • Baneful Fox Mask (Shiny color): 22 Jun 2022 - 01 Jun 2023
  • Fancy Kimono & Fancy Bottoms (Garchomp pattern): 28 Jan 2022 - 16 May 2022
  • Fancy Kimono & Fancy Bottoms (Growlithe pattern): 28 Jan 2022 - 09 May 2022

Save data bonus

  • Let's Go Pikachu or Let's Go Eevee: Festival Mask (Pikachu & Eevee patterns)
  • Brilliant Diamond or Shining Pearl: Modern Jacket, Modern Trousers, & Modern Shoes
  • Sword or Shield: Fancy Kimono & Fancy Bottoms (Shaymin pattern)

Additionally, Darkrai has a Fancy Kimono & Fancy Bottoms set that can be unlocked by capturing it. Darkrai itself is only available with save data for Brilliant Diamond or Shining Pearl.
