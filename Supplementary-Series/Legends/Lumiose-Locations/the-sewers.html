The Sewers

The sewers are accessible initially from an entrance in Bleu Sector 7 during Main Mission 19: Reaching Rank D. After completing this mission, the player must unlock an entrance in the underground path in Bleu Sector 1 by accepting Side Mission 057: The Camerupt Entrepreneur from Hérup.
