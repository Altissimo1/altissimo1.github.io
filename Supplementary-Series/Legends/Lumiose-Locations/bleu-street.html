Bleu Street

The northeastern part of Bleu Street is incorporated as part of Wild Zone 5.

Access to:
West: Bleu Sector 2, Bleu Sector 6, Bleu Sector 8
East: Bleu Sector 1, Bleu Sector 5, Bleu Sector 7
Northeast terminus: Centrico Plaza
Southwest terminus: North Boulevard
