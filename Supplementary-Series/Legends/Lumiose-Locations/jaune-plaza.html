Jaune Plaza

The grassy part of the plaza makes up Wild Zone 8. This Wild Zone only appears after completing Main Mission 09: Chase That Mysterious Pokémon!.

Access to:
North: Jaune Sector 5
West: Jaune Sector 3
Southwest & northeast: Jaune Street
Southwest: Jaune Sector 2
Southeast: Jaune Sector 6
East: Jaune Sector 9

Items

Lumiose Galette stalls

Two stalls in the grassy part of the plaza sell Lumiose Galettes for 350.

When the area becomes a Wild Zone, these stalls are abandoned.

Berry stalls

There are three Berry stalls, all located in the grassy part of the plaza.

When the area becomes a Wild Zone, two stalls are abandoned, and a new stall with a new inventory appears. The wares of these two stalls are given on Wild Zone 8's page.
