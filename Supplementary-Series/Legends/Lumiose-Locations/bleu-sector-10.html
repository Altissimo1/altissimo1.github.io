Bleu Sector 10

Most of Bleu Sector 10 is incorporated as part of Wild Zone 10. This Wild Zone only appears after completing Main Mission 09: Chase That Mysterious Pokémon!.

Access to:
North: Estival Avenue
West: South Boulevard
Southwest: Bleu Sector 9
Southeast: Bleu Sector 6
East: Espace Vide
