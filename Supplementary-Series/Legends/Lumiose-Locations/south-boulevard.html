Racine Construction

A Racine Construction employee outside their office will trade the player's Colorful Screws for Canari Plushes, which can give bonuses. There are five colors of plushes with different effects.

  • Red Canari Plush: Boosts the EXP the player gains
  • Gold Canari Plush: Boosts the prize money the player receives in the Z-A Royale
  • Pink Canari Plush: Increases the amount of Mega Shards received when breaking Mega Crystals
  • Green Canari Plush: Increases the player's resistance to wild Pokémon attacks
  • Blue Canari Plush: Improves the player's odds of catching wild Pokémon

Different levels of plush cost different amounts of Colorful Screws.

1 3
2 5
3 8
