A hair salon is located here. Getting hair styling costs 3,000.

Style option Available options
Hair styles Hi-top fade, Undercut pixie, Flyaway short cut, Slicked back, Simple short hair, Half-up chignon, Simple bob, Low chignon, Asymmetrical bob, Ponytail, Braids, Long and wavy, Side ponytail, Pigtails
Bangs Middle part, Side part, Extra long, Blunt
Colors (used for Hair color, Color blocking, Balayage, and Eyebrow color) Brown, Black, Gold, Dark brown, Pitch black, Platinum, Ash, Milk tea, Dark red, Dark blue, Olive, Violet, Scarlet, Teal, Blue-gray
Eyebrow shape Thick, Average, Thin

For each color of Flabébé shown to Vivace during Side Mission 012: The Many Flowers of Flabébé, a new hair color option is added to the salon: red, blue, orange, white, and lemon yellow. Completing the request additionally adds aqua, purple, and passion pink.
