Saison Canalside & Saison Canal

The part of the canal between Vert Sector 6 and Jaune Sector 10 is incorporated as part of Wild Zone 11. This Wild Zone only appears after completing Main Mission 14: Reaching Rank E.

Access to:
North: Jaune Sector 2, Jaune Sector 6, Jaune Sector 10, Jaune Sector 12
South: Vert Sector 1, Vert Sector 3, Coulant Waterway, Vert Sector 6
Northwest terminus: Centrico Plaza
Southeast terminus: North Boulevard and South Boulevard
