Jaune Sector 7

The Café Ultimo building on North Boulevard, including its roof, is incorporated as part of Wild Zone 15. This Wild Zone only appears after completing Main Mission 19: Reaching Rank D.

Access to:
North & east: North Boulevard
West: Hibernal Avenue
Southwest: Jaune Sector 4
South: Jaune Sector 5
South: Jaune Sector 8
