Magenta Sector 6

The southernmost alley, between Magenta Sectors 5 and 6, is incorporated as part of Wild Zone 9. This Wild Zone only appears after completing Main Mission 09: Chase That Mysterious Pokémon!.

Access to:

Northwest: Electrical Substation
West: South Boulevard
South: Magenta Sector 5
East: Magenta Sector 3
