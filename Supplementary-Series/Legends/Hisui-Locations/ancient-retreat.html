Ancient Retreat

The Ancient Retreat is a small house inhabited by Cogita. It is first accessed during Mission 13: Disaster Looming, and serves as the player's base of operations during the period in which they are expelled from the Galaxy Team.

Items

Shop

The player can buy items from Volo between completing Mission 13: Disaster Looming and crafting the Red Chain. The items sold here are the same as those sold in the general store in Jubilife Village, and require the same requests to be completed to expand the inventory.
