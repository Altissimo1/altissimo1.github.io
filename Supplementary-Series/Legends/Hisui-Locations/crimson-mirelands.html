Crimson Mirelands

Crimson Mirelands is the second wilderness area that can be accessed in Legends: Arceus. It is first accessed during Mission 8: Arezu's Predicament.

Survey Corps Rank 2 is required to enter the area.

Miss Fortunes

The Miss Fortunes may occasionally be present around Crimson Mirelands and can be battled. Per Bulbapedia, all three members' teams can be various levels depending on the player's progress through the story. However, I am not sure if it is possible to see all these levels; the earliest I have heard about them being present is during the red sky event between Missions 13 and 18. If you have any information about when they begin to show up, please contact me.

Collectibles

The map located here shows the location of all Old Verses, Ursaluna dig spots, Wisps, and Unown that can be found in the overworld here.

There are four Old Verses (1, 2, 9, and 13), 20 Wisps, and five Unown (A, D, K, M, and V).
