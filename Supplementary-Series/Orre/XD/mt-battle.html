Mt. Battle Trainers

After defeating Cooltrainer Bardo and speaking with Vander at Mt. Battle, the player can choose to take on the Mt. Battle Challenge, where they will have to defeat 100 Trainers to reach the top. If the player defeats all 100 Trainers in a row, and uses the same team for all 100 battles, their Pokémon will receive the Earth Ribbon, and they will be able to choose one of the three Johto Starters. Beating the challenge again with the same restrictions a second and third time allows the player to get the other two Johto Starters.

All of the battles are Double Battles, even in the case where the opposing Trainer only has one Pokémon.
