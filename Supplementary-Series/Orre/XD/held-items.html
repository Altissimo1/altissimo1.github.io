XD Held Item Locations

The following tables and lists contain the location of all items that can have an effect on Pokémon when held, including Berries.

Pickup

Pokémon with the Pickup ability can find the following items:

  • King's Rock (1% chance between levels 11-30)
  • White Herb (1% chance between levels 41-60)
  • Leftovers (1% chance between levels 81-100)

Berry Man

The old man with a Taillow near the eastern waterfall in Agate Village will give away a Berry when spoken to. I believe he will give away one per day (the date and time is tracked by the GameCube automatically), but have not confirmed this.
