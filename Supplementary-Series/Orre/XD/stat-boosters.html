XD Stat Booster Locations

The following tables contain the location(s) of all Stat Boosters (HP Up, PP Up, Rare Candy, Protein, Carbos, Calcium, Iron, Zinc) in XD.

Pickup

Pokémon with the Pickup ability can find the following items:

  • Rare Candy (4% chance between levels 21-40, 10% chance between levels 41-100)
  • Protein (4% chance between levels 31-50, 10% chance between levels 51-100)
  • HP Up (4% chance between levels 51-70, 10% chance between levels 71-100)
  • PP Up (4% chance between levels 81-100)

Phenac City Poké Mart

The Poké Mart in Phenac City sells HP Ups, Proteins, Irons, Carboses, Calciums, and Zincs for 9800 apiece.

Battle CDs

Some Poké Balls can be obtained for completing Battle CDs.

Item Battle CD
Rare Candy Battle CDs 22, 40, and 41
PP Up Battle CDs 36 and 37
Timer Ball Battle CDs 27 and 28
