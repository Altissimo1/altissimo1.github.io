XD Evo Item Locations

The following tables contain the location of all evolutionary items in XD.

Pickup

Pokémon with the Pickup ability have a 1% chance of finding King's Rocks between levels 11-30, although no Pokémon in XD evolves using one.

Held Item

Shadow Magneton, obtainable at the Cipher Key Lair, holds a Metal Coat.

Mt. Battle Shop

King's Rocks can be purchased for 8000 Poké Coupons at the Mt. Battle shop, although no Pokémon in XD evolves using one.

Gateon Port

The Sailor in the Machine Parts Shop in Gateon port will give the player one of Eevee's evolutionary items (Water Stone, Thunderstone, Fire Stone, Sun Shard, and Moon Shard) when spoken to. Only one can be obtained from him. The Sun Shard and Moon Shard will not evolve any Pokémon other than Eevee.
