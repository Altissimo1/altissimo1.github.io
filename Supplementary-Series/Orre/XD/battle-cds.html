Battle CD 26: A Slaking Pair

Location: Purchase in the Battle CD Room after clearing CDs 09, 13, and 17

This is a Double Battle with a limit of 8 turns. Because enemy Pokémon will use Protect the first turn they are available, the player's Slaking will loaf the turns they are not Protecting. To deal with this, follow the steps below:

  1. Have the male Slaking attack (blocked by Protect) while the female Slaking is switched for Exploud.
  2. Have the male Slaking loaf while Exploud uses Roar on Kecleon.
  3. Switch the male Slaking for the female Slaking while Exploud uses Roar (blocked by Protect).
  4. Have the female Slaking use Double-Edge on Blissey while Exploud uses Roar (which will fail).
  5. Have the female Slaking loaf while Exploud is switched for the male Slaking.
  6. Have the female Slaking use Double-Edge on Kecleon while the male Slaking uses Shadow Ball on Dusclops.

The player will receive an Ultra Ball for completing this CD.

This is a Double Battle. It seems to be random whether Kyogre and Kingdra will double-target Quagsire with Ice Beam or Lanturn with Surf, so you may have to get lucky in guessing correctly initially. You can either use Quagsire Earthquake + Lanturn Protect, or Quagsire Protect + Lanturn Thunder, and switch between them until one Pokémon is defeated.

The player will receive TM18 Rain Dance for completing this CD.
