XD Poké Ball Locations

The following tables and lists contain the location(s) of all Poké Ball types in XD.

Pickup

Pokémon with the Pickup ability can find the following items:

  • Great Ball (10% chance between levels 1-30, 30% between levels 31-40)
  • Ultra Ball (4% chance between levels 1-20, 10% between levels 21-80, 30% between levels 81-90)
