XD Ribbons

This page contains the location and requirements for every Ribbon available in XD. The ribbons listed here are ones that can be obtained in XD directly.

Ribbon Location Notes
National Ribbon Obtained on any Shadow Pokémon when they are Purified at the Relic Stone in Agate Village or at the Purify Chamber in Pokémon HQ Lab
Earth Ribbon Obtained on all Pokémon in the party upon completion of the Mt. Battle challenge The Earth Ribbon will only be obtained if all 100 battles are completed in one go, and the Pokémon in the party are not changed.
