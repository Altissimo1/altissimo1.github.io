Quick Battle Trainers

Quick Battle allows the player to battle a Trainer or another human player using a pre-set team of Pokémon. There are four difficulty options to choose from, and each one has ten possible teams associated with it. Both Trainers' teams are taken from the ten teams.

Abilities and genders are random.

  • Easy
  • Normal
  • Hard
  • Ultimate
