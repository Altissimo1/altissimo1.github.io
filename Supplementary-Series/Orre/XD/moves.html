Agate Village Move Tutor

The Move Tutor is located in the cave at the western part of Agate Village. Each move can only be taught one time.

Not all moves are available at the start of the game. The requirements for unlocking each move are shown in the table below.

Mew Move Tutor

This Move Tutor appears in the lobby of Mt. Battle after beating Greevil at Citadark Isle. He can tutor Mew any move it could learn by TM, HM, or Move Tutor in any other Generation 3 game, plus Faint Attack, Fake Out, Hypnosis, Night Shade, Role Play, and Zap Cannon. Each move costs 5000 Poké Coupons.
