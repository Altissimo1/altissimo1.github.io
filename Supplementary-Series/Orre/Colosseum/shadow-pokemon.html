Colosseum Shadow Pokémon

The following table contains every Shadow Pokémon that can be obtained in Colosseum and its level, moves, and held item. For the locations of Shadow Pokémon, see this page.

All Shadow Pokémon have a random nature, Ability, gender, and IVs.

If Qwilfish is not Snagged during the first battle it can be Snagged in, it will not hold its Poison Barb in subsequent battles. If Suicune is Snagged at the Deep Colosseum, it will know Hydro Pump instead of Surf.
