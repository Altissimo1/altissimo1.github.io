Colosseum Ribbons

This page contains the location and requirements for every Ribbon available in Colosseum. The ribbons listed here are ones that can be obtained in Colosseum directly.

Ribbon Location Notes
National Ribbon Obtained on any Shadow Pokémon when they are Purified at the Relic Stone in Agate Village
Earth Ribbon Obtained on all Pokémon in the party upon completion of the Mt. Battle challenge in Story Mode The Earth Ribbon will only be obtained if all 100 battles are completed in one go, and the Pokémon in the party are not changed.
