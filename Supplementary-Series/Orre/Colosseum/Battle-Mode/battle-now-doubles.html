Battle Now (Doubles)

Battle Now allows the player to battle a Trainer using a pre-set team of Pokémon in either a Single or Double Battle. There are four difficulty options to choose from, and each one has eight possible teams associated with it. The enemy Trainer's team will also be taken from one of these eight teams.

  • Easy
  • Normal
  • Hard
  • Ultimate
