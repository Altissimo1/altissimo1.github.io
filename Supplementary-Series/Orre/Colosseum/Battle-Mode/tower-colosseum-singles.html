Tower Colosseum Trainers (Singles)

This is a list of opposing Trainers available in the Tower Colosseum Singles challenge in Colosseum's Battle Mode. All Pokémon here are level 100.

If the player's Pokémon are lower than level 100, their levels will not be raised to 100. The player also cannot use any Shadow Pokémon, and must prepare a full team of six Pokémon.

The player cannot use two Pokémon of the same species or two Pokémon with the same held item.

The reward for beating this challenge is 3,000 Poké Coupons.
