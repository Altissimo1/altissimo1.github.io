Orre Colosseum Trainers (Doubles)

This is a list of opposing Trainers available in the Orre Colosseum Doubles challenge in Colosseum's Battle Mode. This challenge has two level options: Level 50 and Level 100.

  • Level 50
  • Level 100

Level 50 Challenge

This challenge is only available after the player has beaten Doubles Mode for Phenac Stadium, Pyrite Colosseum, and Under Colosseum.

The player's Pokémon have to be level 50 or lower in order to enter this challenge. If they are lower than level 50, their levels will not be raised to 50. The player also cannot use any Shadow Pokémon, and must prepare a full team of six Pokémon.

The player cannot use two Pokémon of the same species or two Pokémon with the same held item.

The reward for beating this challenge is 2000 Poké Coupons.
