Under Colosseum Trainers (Doubles)

This is a list of opposing Trainers available in the Under Colosseum Doubles challenge in Colosseum's Battle Mode. All Pokémon here are level 50.

The player's Pokémon have to be level 50 or lower in order to enter this challenge. If they are lower than level 50, their levels will not be raised to 50. The player also cannot use any Shadow Pokémon, and must prepare a full team of six Pokémon.

The player cannot use two Pokémon of the same species or two Pokémon with the same held item.

The reward for beating this challenge is 1,500 Poké Coupons.
