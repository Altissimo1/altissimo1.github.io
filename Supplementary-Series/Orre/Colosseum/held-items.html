Colosseum Held Item Locations

The following tables and lists contain the location of all items that can have an effect on Pokémon when held, including Berries.

Pickup

Pokémon with the Pickup ability have a 1% chance of picking up King's Rocks. Of the Pokémon with Pickup, only Aipom is available without trading with Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald, FireRed, or LeafGreen.

Shadow Pokémon

Several Shadow Pokémon hold type-boosting items when Snagged. The list is given below.

Note that, if Qwilfish is not Snagged the first time it is encountered, it will not be holding the Poison Barb on subsequent encounters, rendering the Poison Barb permanently missable.

Mt. Battle Shop

The shop in Mt. Battle sells a large selection of held items and some Berries, which can be purchased in exchange for Poké Coupons. The items sold here can also be purchased in the Poké Coupon Exchange available from the menu of Battle Mode.

Berry Man

The old man with a Taillow near the eastern waterfall in Agate Village will give away a Berry when spoken to. I believe he will give away one per day (the date and time is tracked by the GameCube automatically), but have not confirmed this.
