Colosseum Stat Booster Locations

The following tables contain the location(s) of all Stat Boosters (HP Up, PP Up, Rare Candy, Protein, Carbos, Calcium, Iron, Zinc) in Colosseum.

Pickup

Pokémon with the Pickup ability have a 10% chance of picking up Rare Candies, a 5% chance of picking up Proteins, and a 4% chance of picking up PP Ups. Of the Pokémon with Pickup, only Aipom is available without trading with Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald, FireRed, or LeafGreen.

Agate Village Poké Mart

The Poké Mart in Agate Village sells HP Ups, Proteins, Irons, Carboses, Calciums, and Zincs for 9800 apiece.
