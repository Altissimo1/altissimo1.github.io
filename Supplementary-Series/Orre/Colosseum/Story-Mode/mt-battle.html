Mt. Battle (Story Mode)

After defeating Dakim at Mt. Battle, the player can choose to take on the Mt. Battle Challenge, where they will have to defeat 100 Trainers to reach the top. Reaching the top will award every Pokémon in the party with an Earth Ribbon, if each party member was present during all 100 battles. A Time Flute can also be obtained in Zone 100, providing additional incentive to complete the challenge.

Ho-Oh is not obtained by defeating this version of Mt. Battle. It is obtained by defeating the Battle Mode version, using only Pokémon from Story Mode, after purfiying all Shadow Pokémon. Trainers for that version of Mt. Battle can be found here and here.

Defeating each Area rewards the player with Poké Coupons, which can be exchanged for items in the lobby. The rewards for each area are given at the beginning of each area's data.

In order to obtain the Earth Ribbon, the player must fight all 100 battles in one go, and not change the Pokémon in their party.
