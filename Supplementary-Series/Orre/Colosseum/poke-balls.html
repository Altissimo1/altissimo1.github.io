Colosseum Poké Ball Locations

The following tables and lists contain the location(s) of all Poké Ball types in Colosseum.

Pickup

Pokémon with the Pickup ability have a 10% chance of picking up Ultra Balls. Of the Pokémon with Pickup, only Aipom is available without trading with Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald, FireRed, or LeafGreen.

Outskirt Stand Shop

The shop at Outskirt Stand is the only place in Colosseum where Poké Balls can be purchased.

In addition to the Poké Balls listed in the below table, the player will receive a Premier Ball for buying ten or more Poké Balls at once.

Outskirt Stand Poké Balls
Item Price Notes
Great Ball 600 Only after defeating Team Snagem Wakin in Phenac City.
Poké Ball 200 Only after defeating Team Snagem Wakin in Phenac City.
Ultra Ball 1200 Only after Team Cipher attacks Mt. Battle.
Net Ball 1000 Only after Team Cipher attacks Mt. Battle.
Nest Ball 1000 Only after Team Cipher attacks Mt. Battle.
Timer Ball 1000 Only after Team Cipher attacks Mt. Battle.
