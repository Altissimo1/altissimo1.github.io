DNA Samples

To enter Ein's room, the player must find 3 DNA Samples around the main wing and analyze them with the DNA Analyzer. There are nine possible locations for the DNA Samples, though only three will be selected.

  • One on B1F, in the northeast corner of the southwest room
  • Two on B1F, in the room with the cells southwest of the door unlocked with the Down St. Key
  • One on B2F, in the room with the stairway leading to the room containing the Down St. Key
  • Two on B2F, in the room guarded by Cipher Peon Lesar
  • One on B2F, east of the two adjacent elevators
  • Two on B2F, in the room north of the two adjacent elevators
