DNA Samples

To enter Ein's room, the player must find 3 DNA Samples around the location and analyze them with the DNA Analyzer. There are nine possible locations for the DNA Samples, though only three will be selected.

  • One in the northwest corner of the room west of the door unlocked with the Down St. Key
  • One in the room with the stairway leading to the room containing the Down St. Key
  • Two in the room to the west after the door unlocked with the Down St. Key
  • Two in the room guarded by Cipher Peon Lesar
  • One east of the two adjacent elevators
  • Two in the room north of the two adjacent elevators
