Orre Colosseum

The Orre Colosseum is unlocked after defeating Greevil at Citadark Isle. This Colosseum is intended to be much more challenging than the others in XD. Here, Trainers' Pokémon's levels match the player's highest-leveled Pokémon, but they will not go lower than level 60.

Defeating the first round is required to start the Pokémon voice sidequest in Agate Village.

The rewards for each round are given at the beginning of each round's data.
