Mt. Battle

This page covers the Trainers that can be fought the first time the player enters, when the area is taken over by Team Cipher, and the items that can be found or purchased. For a list of Trainers that can be battled in the proper Mt. Battle challenge, see this page. For lists of Trainers that the player can face in Battle Mode, see this page for Singles and this page for Doubles.

Ho-Oh can only be obtained in Battle Mode. Further details about it are given on the two Battle Mode pages linked above.

This page covers the Trainers that can be fought the first time the player enters, when only three Trainers can be fought, and the items that can be found or purchased. For a list of Trainers that can be battled in the proper Mt. Battle challenge, see this page.

Places of interest:
Mew Move Tutor: The Move Tutor in the lobby appears after beating Greevil at Citadark Isle. He can tutor Mew any move it could learn by TM, HM, or Move Tutor in any other Generation 3 game, plus Faint Attack, Fake Out, Hypnosis, Night Shade, Role Play, and Zap Cannon. Each move costs 5000 Poké Coupons.

Gift

The Johto Starters can only be obtained by beating all 100 Trainers in a row, and using the same party for all battles. The player can choose one upon winning the challenge, and can obtain the others by beating the challenge a second and third time with the same restrictions.
