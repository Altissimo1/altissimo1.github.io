Mt. Battle

This page covers the Trainers that can be fought the first time the player enters, when the area is taken over by Team Cipher, and the items that can be found or purchased. For a list of Trainers that can be battled in the proper Mt. Battle challenge, see this page. For lists of Trainers that the player can face in Battle Mode, see this page for Singles and this page for Doubles.

Ho-Oh can only be obtained in Battle Mode. Further details about it are given on the two Battle Mode pages linked above.
