Poké Spots

The Poké Spots are three locations in Orre where wild Pokémon can be found. In order to run into a wild Pokémon, the player must leave one or more Poké Snacks at the location, and they will be alerted on their P*DA if a snack is being eaten. If the player returns to the Poké Spot quickly, they can encounter the Pokémon and attempt to catch it.
There is a 10% chance that the Munchlax owned by the Herb Shop owner from Gateon Port will appear instead of a wild Pokémon, in which case the player will receive ten Poké Snacks and an item as an apology.
After clearing the S.S. Libra, there is also a 30% chance the player will find the runaway Bonsly. The player must catch the Bonsly and return it to its Trainer at ONBS to receive a Bonsly Card for Battle Bingo.
