Stargazer Colosseum

This colosseum becomes available to the player after they clear Courtyard Colosseum. In this colosseum, the player must defeat six Trainers, followed by the Colosseum Leader, in Knockout Battles.

The first time the player challenges this colosseum, they will have to face Double Battles, using the Level 30 Open ruleset.

In Double Battles, the player and opponent will each enter with six Pokémon, and must choose four to participate in the battle with.
