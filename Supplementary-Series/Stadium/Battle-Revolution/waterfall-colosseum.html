Waterfall Colosseum

This colosseum becomes available to the player after they clear Main Street Colosseum. In this colosseum, the player must defeat six Trainers, followed by the Colosseum Leader, in Team Battles.

The first time the player challenges this colosseum, they will have to face Single Battles, using the Level 30 Open ruleset, on Rank 1.

In Single Battles, the player and opponent will each enter with six Pokémon, and must choose five to participate in the battle with.

Completing this Colosseum for the first time unlocks Neon Colosseum and Crystal Colosseum.
