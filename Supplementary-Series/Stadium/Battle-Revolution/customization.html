Customization

The player can customize their Trainer character with a variety of options including clothing, Trainer title, and Battle Pass. This page shows which customizations are available and what requirements they have to unlock.

  • Battle Passes
  • Trainer Titles
  • Gear

Battle Passes

Battle Passes are the cards that show the player's Trainer character and the six Pokémon on their team. There are a variety of designs available for the Pass itself that can be unlocked by meeting certain requirements.

Rental Passes

Six designs are only available on Rental Passes.

Outfits

These are obtained the first time the player defeats the Colosseum Leader wearing the outfit in a Level 50 All battle. There is a 1/8192 chance that the Leader wears a Shiny-colored version of the outfit. If the player defeats them while they have the Shiny outfit, and it is also the player's first time defeating them, the player will get the Shiny version of the outfit.
