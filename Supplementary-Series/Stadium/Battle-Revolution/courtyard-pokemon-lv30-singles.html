Courtyard Colosseum Pokémon (Lv.30 Open, Singles)

These Pokémon can be selected for opposing Trainers' teams in Courtyard Colosseum for Lv. 30 Open Single Survival Battles. Survival Battles are only available in this colosseum after completing Stargazer Colosseum for the first time.

All Pokémon have random IVs and Natures. Their level will match the level of the player's highest-leveled Pokémon, or be set to 30 if the player's Pokémon are all lower than 30. The "minimum level" column in these tables indicates the lowest level at which the Pokémon can be encountered on an opposing Trainer's team.

Moves marked as "random level-up moves" should be only taken from the moves the Pokémon can learn at or below the level at which they are fought. Information about TMs included in the different random TM groups can be found here.

Enemy Trainers seem to start with lower-ranked Pokémon and move to higher-ranked ones over the course of the challenge, but I don't know the specifics at this time.
