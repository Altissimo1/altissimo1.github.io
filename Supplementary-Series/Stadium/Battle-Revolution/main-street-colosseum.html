Main Street Colosseum

This is one of the two colosseums available to the player at the start of the game. In this colosseum, the player must defeat six Trainers, followed by the Colosseum Leader, in Knockout Battles.

The first time the player challenges this colosseum, they will have to face Single Battles, using the Level 30 Open ruleset, on Rank 1.

In Single Battles, the player and opponent will each enter with six Pokémon, and must choose three to participate in the battle with.

Completing this Colosseum for the first time unlocks Waterfall Colosseum.
