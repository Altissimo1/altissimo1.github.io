Stadium 2 Earl's Pokémon Academy

Earl's Pokémon Academy is a mode in Stadium 2 where the player can listen to lectures, take quizzes, and play mock battles to learn more about various battle mechanics and interactions. This page contains information about the parties that the player and opponent use during the mock battles.

The player can only use the rental teams determined for every mock battle, but they will still have to choose three of the six Pokémon to use in the battle. In order to "pass" a battle, the player must not only win the battle, but also choose the correct three Pokémon based on the mechanic the battle is based around.

Most Pokémon only have one or two attacks. I've cleaned up the tables by removing move slot columns with no entries.
