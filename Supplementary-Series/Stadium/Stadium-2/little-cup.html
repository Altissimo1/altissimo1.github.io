Little Cup

The Little Cup is a challenge cup in Pokémon Stadium 2, where only un-evolved Pokémon at level 5 can be used. The cup features two difficulty modes, Round 1 and Round 2.

In the original release, the player can choose to use either a team selected from rental Pokémon, a team consisting of Pokémon from a connected copy of Red, Green, Blue, Yellow, Gold, Silver, or Crystal, or a mix of both. In the Nintendo Switch rerelease, only rental Pokémon can be used.

The rules in this cup are as follows:

  • The player will select three of their six Pokémon to battle with.
  • Pokémon must be level 5 exactly.
  • Only the lowest evolutionary stage of an evolutionary family can be used. Pokémon that cannot evolve cannot be used.
  • If one Pokémon on the opposing team is asleep, any moves that can put a second opposing Pokémon to sleep will fail to do so. The player can still inflict a second sleep status on their own party using Rest.
  • If one Pokémon on the opposing team is frozen, any moves that can freeze a second opposing Pokémon will fail to do so.
  • If the player and the opponent both have only one Pokémon left, and one Pokémon uses Selfdestruct, Explosion, Destiny Bond, or Perish Song to knock out both Pokémon, the player who used the self-KO move will lose the match.
  • Two Pokémon of the same species cannot be entered.
  • Two Pokémon holding the same item cannot be entered.
  • Sonicboom and Dragon Rage have no effect if used.
