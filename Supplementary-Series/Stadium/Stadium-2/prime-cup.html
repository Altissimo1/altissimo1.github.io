Stadium 2 Prime Cup

The Prime Cup is a challenge cup in Pokémon Stadium 2, with no species or level restrictions. The cup features two difficulty modes, Round 1 and Round 2.

The player can choose to use either a team selected from rental Pokémon, a team consisting of Pokémon from a connected Game Boy game, or a mix of both.

The rules in this cup are as follows:

  • The player will select three of their six Pokémon to battle with.
  • If one Pokémon on the opposing team is asleep, any moves that can put a second opposing Pokémon to sleep will fail to do so. The player can still inflict a second sleep status on their own party using Rest.
  • If one Pokémon on the opposing team is frozen, any moves that can freeze a second opposing Pokémon will fail to do so.
  • If the player and the opponent both have only one Pokémon left, and one Pokémon uses Selfdestruct, Explosion, Destiny Bond, or Perish Song to knock out both Pokémon, the player who used the self-KO move will lose the match.
  • Two Pokémon of the same species cannot be entered.
  • Two Pokémon holding the same item cannot be entered.

Rentals

Rental Pokémon can be given Przcureberry, Burnt Berry, Ice Berry, Bitter Berry, Mint Berry, or Berry.

Mew and Celebi are only available as rentals during Round 2.

In Round 2, Pikachu has Surf instead of Headbutt, and Psyduck has Amnesia instead of Psych Up.

These rentals are also the ones used for the Anything Goes mode of Free Battle, but Mew and Celebi can never be rented, and Pikachu and Psyduck always have their Round 1 moves.
