Stadium 1 Pika Cup

The Pika Cup is a challenge cup in Pokémon Stadium 1, where only Pokémon between level 15 and 20 can be used. The cup features two difficulty modes, Round 1 and Round 2.

The player can choose to use either a team selected from rental Pokémon, a team consisting of Pokémon from a connected Game Boy game, or a mix of both.

The rules in this cup are as follows:

  • The player will select three of their six Pokémon to battle with.
  • Pokémon must be between level 15 and level 20. The combined levels of the three chosen Pokémon cannot exceed 50.
  • Mewtwo and Mew cannot be entered, even if they are between levels 15 and 20.
  • If one Pokémon on the opposing team is asleep, any moves that can put a second opposing Pokémon to sleep will fail to do so. The player can still inflict a second sleep status on their own party using Rest.
  • If one Pokémon on the opposing team is frozen, any moves that can freeze a second opposing Pokémon will fail to do so.
  • If the player and the opponent both have only one Pokémon left, and one Pokémon uses Selfdestruct or Explosion to knock out both Pokémon, the player who used Selfdestruct or Explosion will lose the match.
  • Two Pokémon of the same species cannot be entered.

It is normally impossible to get several species of Pokémon at a low enough level to use them in this cup. The long-range trainer glitch in Red, Green, Blue, and Yellow allows the player to get any Pokémon at very low levels. Even with this glitch, though, Mewtwo and Mew cannot be entered. Outside of this glitch, a list of lowest levels at which Pokémon can be found is here, and a version that does not require access to trading, Gold, Silver, or Crystal is here.
