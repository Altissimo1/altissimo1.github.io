Nintendo Cup '97

The Nintendo Cup '97 is a challenge cup in the Japanese release of Pokémon Stadium 1, where Pokémon between level 50 and 55 can be used. The cup features two difficulty modes, Round 1 and Round 2. It is loosely based on the same cup from Stadium 0.

In the original release, the player can choose to use either a team selected from rental Pokémon, a team consisting of Pokémon from a connected copy of Red, Green, Blue, or Yellow, or a mix of both. In the Nintendo Switch rerelease, only rental Pokémon can be used.

The rules in this cup are as follows:

  • The player will select three of their six Pokémon to battle with.
  • Pokémon must be between level 50 and level 55. The combined levels of the three chosen Pokémon cannot exceed 155.
  • Mew and Mewtwo cannot be entered, even they are between levels 50 and 55.
  • If one Pokémon on the opposing team is asleep, any moves that can put a second opposing Pokémon to sleep will fail to do so. The player can still inflict a second sleep status on their own party using Rest.
  • If one Pokémon on the opposing team is frozen, any moves that can freeze a second opposing Pokémon will fail to do so.
  • If the player and the opponent both have only one Pokémon left, and one Pokémon uses Selfdestruct or Explosion to knock out both Pokémon, the player who used Selfdestruct or Explosion will lose the match.
  • Two Pokémon of the same species cannot be entered.
