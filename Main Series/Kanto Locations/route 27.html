Route 27

Access to:
West: New Bark Town
East: Route 26
Accessible on the route: Tohjo Falls

Utility:
Surf is required to access Tohjo Falls, and to head east on the route after Tohjo Falls, as well as to access TM232 Solarbeam and a Rare Candy. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Whirlpool is required to access TM22 Solarbeam and a Rare Candy.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:






Pokémon

Wild Dodrio have a 2% chance of holding a Sharp Beak.
Wild Shellder have a 23% chance of holding a Pearl and a 2% chance of holding a Big Pearl.
Wild Butterfree have a 2% chance of holding a Silverpowder.
Wild Beedrill have a 2% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
Wild Quagsire can flee.
