Viridian City

Access to:
North: Route 2
West: Route 22
South: Route 1

Places of interest:
Poké Mart: Sells Poké Balls, Antidotes, Parlyz Heals, and Burn Heals after you deliver Oak's Parcel. In Yellow, it also sells Potions.
Viridian Gym: The eighth gym. It specializes in Ground-types.

Utility:
Cut or Surf is required to collect TM42 Dream Eater from an NPC in the southwest.

Access to:
North: Route 2
West: Route 22
South: Route 1

Places of interest:
Trainer House: The player can battle Trainers they have used Mystery Gift with here once per day. If the player has not used Mystery Gift, they can battle Cal.
Poké Mart: Sells Ultra Balls, Hyper Potions, Full Heals, Revives, Antidotes, Parlyz Heals, Awakenings, Burn Heals, and Flower Mail.
Viridian Gym: The eighth Kanto gym. It does not specialize in any type.

Utility:
Cut or Surf is required to collect TM42 Dream Eater from an NPC in the southwest. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
