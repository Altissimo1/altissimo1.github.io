Access to:

Places of interest:
In Mt. Moon Square, Clefairy can be seen dancing between 8:00 PM Monday nights and 4:00 AM Tuesday mornings. Interrupting the dance gives the player access to a Moon Stone.
In Mt. Moon Square, a shop sells Fresh Waters, Lemonades, Soda Pops, Portraitmail, Repels, and Poké Dolls. It is open from 10:00 AM to 5:59 PM every day.

Utility:
Rock Smash is required to access the Moon Stone left in Mt. Moon Square after the Clefairy leave.
