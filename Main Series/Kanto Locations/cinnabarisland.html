Cinnabar Island

Access to:
North: Route 21
East: Route 20
Accessible on the island: Pokémon Mansion

Places of interest:
Poké Mart: Sells Ultra Balls, Great Balls, Hyper Potions, Max Repels, Escape Ropes, Full Heals, and Revives
Cinnabar Gym: The seventh gym. It specializes in Fire-types.
Pokémon Lab: The Pokémon Lab will restore Fossils.

Utility:
Surf is required to access Cinnabar Island the first time, as well as to travel to Route 20 or 21
