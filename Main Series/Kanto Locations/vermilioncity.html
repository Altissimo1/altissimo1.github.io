Vermilion City

Access to:
North: Route 6
East: Route 11
Accessible in the city: S.S. Anne

Places of interest:
Poké Mart: Sells Poké Balls, Super Potions, Ice Heals, Awakenings, Parlyz Heals, and Repels.
Vermilion Gym: The third gym. It specializes in Electric-types.

Utility:
Cut or Surf is required to access the gym.
Surf is required to access a Max Ether.
