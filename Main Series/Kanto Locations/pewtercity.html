Pewter City

Access to:
South: Route 2
East: Route 3

Places of interest:
Poké Mart: Sells Poké Balls, Potions, Escape Ropes, Antidotes, Burn Heals, Awakenings, and Parlyz Heals.
Pewter Gym: The first gym. It specializes in Rock-types.

Utility:
Cut is required to collect the Old Amber from the back entrance to Pewter Museum.
