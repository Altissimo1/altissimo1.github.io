Celadon City

Access to:
West: Route 16
East: Route 7
Accessible in the city: Rocket Hideout

Places of interest:
Celadon Gym: The fourth gym. It specializes in Grass-types.
Rocket Game Corner
Game Freak Rooms: An NPC in the Game Freak Rooms in Celadon Mansion will give you a certificate if your Pokédex has 150 species.
Celadon Department Store
2F: Left side sells Great Balls, Super Potions, Revives, Super Repels, Antidotes, Burn Heals, Ice Heals, Awakenings, and Parlyz Heals. Right side sells TM32 Double Team, TM33 Reflect, TM02 Razor Wind, TM07 Horn Drill, TM37 Egg Bomb, TM01 Mega Punch, TM05 Mega Kick, TM09 Take Down, and TM17 Submission.
4F: Sells Poké Dolls, Fire Stones, Thunderstones, Water Stones, and Leaf Stones.
5F: Left side sells X Accuracies, Guard Specs, Dire Hits, X Attacks, X Defends, X Speeds, and X Specials. Right side sells HP Ups, Proteins, Irons, Carboses, and Calciums. In Japanese Red and Green, HP Ups are not sold.
Roof: The Vending Machines sell Fresh Waters, Soda Pops, and Lemonades. Each drink can be exchanged with the girl for a TM. One drink needs to be given to the guard at any Saffron City gate to enter Saffron City and progress.
Celadon Game Corner: In addition to Pokémon, the prize center sells TM23 Dragon Rage, TM15 Hyper Beam, and TM50 Substitute.

Utility:
Surf is required to collect TM41 Softboiled from an NPC north of the pond.
Cut is required to access Celadon Gym and navigate it.
