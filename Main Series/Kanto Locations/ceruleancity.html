Cerulean City

Access to:
North: Route 24
West: Route 4
South: Route 5
East: Route 9
Accessible in the city: Cerulean Cave

Places of interest:
Poké Mart: Sells Poké Balls, Potions, Repels, Antidotes, Burn Heals, Awakenings, and Parlyz Heals. In Yellow, it also sells Escape Ropes.
Cerulean Gym: The second gym. It specializes in Water-types.
Bike Shop

Utility:
Surf is required to access Cerulean Cave and a trainer on Route 4 by Surfing through the city from Route 24.
