Fuchsia City

Access to:
West: Route 18
South: Route 19
East: Route 15
Accessible in the city: Safari Zone

Places of interest:
Poké Mart: Sells Ultra Balls, Great Balls, Super Potions, Revives, Full Heals, and Super Repels. In Yellow, Hyper Potions are sold instead of Super Potions.
Fuchsia Gym: The fifth gym. It specializes in Poison-types.

Utility:
Strength is required to obtain the Rare Candy in the Warden's house.
