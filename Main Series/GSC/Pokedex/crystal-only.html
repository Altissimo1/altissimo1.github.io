How Many Pokémon Can You Get with Only Pokémon Crystal?

Assuming you do not use any glitches, the answer is 207 if you are using the Virtual Console release of Crystal (allowing access to Celebi), or 206 otherwise. The Professor Oak challenge guide for Crystal can be found here, although I've made a shorter list of encounters below in case anything happens to the external guide.

The list of Pokémon you cannot get without trading, which equals 44 or 45 species, is as follows:

Two Starters (6) - 6 total
As you can only select one starter Pokémon, the other two starters, and their evolutions, are unavailable; this totals 6 Pokémon.
Kanto Starters (9) - 15 total
None of the Kanto starters can be obtained without trading with Gen 1 games.
Kanto Legendaries (4) - 19 total
Articuno, Zapdos, Moltres, and Mewtwo are unobtainable without trading with Gen 1 games.
Kanto Fossils (4) - 23 total
Omanyte, Omastar, Kabuto, and Kabutops are unobtainable without trading with Gen 1 games.
Trade Evolutions (10) - 33 total
Golem, Machamp, Alakazam, Gengar, Politoed, Slowking, Steelix, Scizor, Kingdra, and Porygon2 evolve by trade. Without the ability to trade, they are unobtainable.
Version Exclusives (10) - 43 total
The following Pokémon, and their evolutions, cannot be obtained without the use of glitches or trading: Mareep, Vulpix, Mankey, Girafarig, and Remoraid.
Mythical/Event (1/2) - 44 or 45 total
Outside of glitches, Mew was only obtainable via events that are no longer active. Celebi can be obtained in the Virtual Console release of Crystal, but not on the original cartridge release. However, they were never needed to get the certificate from the Game Freak employee.
