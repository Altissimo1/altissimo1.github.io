Completing a Living Dex With Gens 1 and 2 (Pokémon Gold)

A "living dex" is a fan designation for when a player completes the Pokédex and has one of each species in the PC or the party at the same time. It's often considered to be much more difficult and time-consuming than a regular Pokédex because it often involves, for instance, playing the same game multiple times to get multiple copies of a Legendary Pokémon.
With Gen 2, the Legendary Pokémon are, fortunately, not a concern, and with Gen 1, neither is the lack of evolutionary stones.

The method by which you can get a completed Pokédex with Gen 1 on Gold is described here. This page will focus more on requirements specific to a living dex.

Because Gen 2 lacks version-exclusive legendaries and generally lacks mutually exclusive Pokémon period, and because of the introduction of breeding, completing a living dex in Gen 2 actually requires comparatively little effort. You will need access to at minimum three separate Gen 2 save files, on two different copies, and three Gen 1 save files or, with access to Yellow, two Gen 1 save files. Your process should look something like this:

  1. Collect Johto Starter #1 and trade it to the second Gen 2 game.
  2. Collect Johto Starter #2 and trade it to the second Gen 2 game.
  3. Collect Johto Starter #3. Trade the other starters back to the first Gen 2 game and breed them to complete the living dex.
  4. Start a Gen 1 save file. If it is not Yellow, collect Kanto Starter #1 and Fossil #1; if you are playing Yellow, collect all three starters and Fossil #1. Trade them to Gen 2.
  5. Collect Kanto Starter #2 (if not playing Yellow) and Fossil #2 and trade them to Gen 2.
  6. If not playing Yellow, collect Kanto Starter #3 and trade it to Gen 2.

If you have access to Pokémon Stadium, however, you can get all of these by defeating the Elite Four and the Rival and then trade them.

Celebi

This is just to note that if you have access to a GS Ball Celebi with Crystal, you can obviously trade it to Gold to add it to Gold's living dex. However, if you wanted to, for instance, complete a living dex of Gold and Crystal simultaneously, you would need to get multiple Celebi in Crystal, requiring multiple save files that will each need to defeat the Elite Four.
