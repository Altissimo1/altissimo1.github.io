Route 40

Access to:
North: Battle Tower (Crystal)
South: Route 41
East: Olivine City

Utility:
Surf is required to traverse most of the route and travel to Route 41 in the south. Wild Pokémon can also be found with Surf.
Rock Smash can be used to access a hidden Hyper Potion and encounter wild Pokémon.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:






Pokémon

Wild Staryu have a 23% chance of holding a Stardust and a 2% chance of holding a Star Piece.
Wild Shuckle have a 25% chance of holding a Berry.
