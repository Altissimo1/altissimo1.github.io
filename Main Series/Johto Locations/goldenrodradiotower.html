Goldenrod Radio Tower

Access to:
Exit on 1F: Goldenrod City

Places of interest:
Buena's Password: Buena's show airs in Crystal on channel 10.5. Between 6 PM and 12 AM, a password is given. Providing the correct password to Buena will give you 1 point. With your points, you can purchase Ultra Balls, Full Restores, Nuggets, Rare Candies, Proteins, Irons, Carboses, Calciums, and HP Ups. At 30 points, you can register Buena's phone number in your Pokégear.
