Battle Tower

Access to:
South: Route 40

Details:
The Battle Tower is only available in international releases of Crystal. In the original Japanese release, it was only available via connecting to Mobile System GB, and in the Virtual Console Japanese release, it is disabled.
When challenging the Battle Tower, the player will face seven Trainers in a row and receive a reward for completing the challenge. The reward will be 5 of Calcium, Carbos, HP Up, Iron, or Protein.
The levels of the enemy Trainers' Pokémon will be between 10 and 100, in intervals of 10. The level 10-50 challenges are only available before defeating the Elite Four.
A full list of enemy Pokémon, and their stats, can be located here.
The following restrictions apply:

  • All Pokémon must be equal to, or less than, the level of the challenge entered.
  • All Pokémon must hold a different item.
  • No items can be used by Trainers during the battle.
  • Mewtwo, Mew, Lugia, Ho-Oh, and Celebi can only be used in challenges of level 70 or higher.
