Azalea Town

Access to:
West: Ilex Forest
East: Route 33
Accessible in the city: Slowpoke Well

Places of interest:
Azalea Gym: The second gym. It specializes in Bug-types.
Kurt's House: Kurt will make special Apricorn balls if you bring him Apricorns: Love Balls for Pink, Level Balls for Red, Moon Balls for Yellow, Friend Balls for Green, Lure Balls for Blue, Heavy Balls for Black, and Fast Balls for White.
Poké Mart: Sells Charcoals, Poké Balls, Potions, Super Potions, Escape Ropes, Repels, Antidotes, Parlyz Heals, and Flower Mail.

Utility:
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.
