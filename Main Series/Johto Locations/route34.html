Route 34

Access to:
North: Goldenrod City
South: Ilex Forest

Places of interest:
Pokémon Day Care: In international versions of Pokémon Crystal, you can receive the Odd Egg here.

Utility:
Surf is required to access three Trainers, a Rare Candy, and a Nugget (Crystal). Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:

Odd Egg (International Crystal only)

The Odd Egg will hatch into into one of the following Pokémon. It will always be female (except for Tyrogue), and know Dizzy Punch. If it is not shiny, its IVs will be all 0's, and it will have a Hidden Power type of Fighting with 31 power. If it is shiny, its IVs will be 2/10/10/10, and it will have a Hidden Power type of Grass with 49 power.
The hatched Pokémon has a 14% chance of being shiny, but not all species are equally possible. The precise percentages are given below.
