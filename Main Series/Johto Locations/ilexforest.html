Ilex Forest

Access to:
North: Route 34
East: Azalea Town

Utility:
Cut is required to pass between the northern and southern areas.
Surf and Headbutt can both be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:






Pokémon

Wild Butterfree have a 2% chance of holding a Silverpowder.
Wild Beedrill have a 2% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
Wild Paras have a 23% chance of holding a Tinymushroom and a 2% chance of holding a Big Mushroom.
Celebi has a 2% chance of holding a Miracleberry.
