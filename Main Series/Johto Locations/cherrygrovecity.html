Cherrygrove City

Access to:
North: Route 30
East: Route 29

Places of interest:
Poké Mart: Sells Poké Balls, Potions, Antidotes, Parlyz Heals, and Awakenings. Only sells Poké Balls after you first receive them from Professor Elm's Aide in New Bark Town.

Utility:
Surf is required to access the man who gives you the Mystic Water. Wild Pokémon can also be found here with Surf.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:
