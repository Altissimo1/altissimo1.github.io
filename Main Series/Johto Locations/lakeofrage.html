Lake of Rage

Access to:
South: Route 43

Utility:
Surf is required to access the Red Gyarados and obtain the Red Scale. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Cut is required to access Wesley, a Rare Candy, a Max Ether, TM10 Hidden Power, amd TM43 Detect.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:






Pokémon

Wild Butterfree have a 2% chance of holding a Silverpowder.
Wild Beedrill have a 2% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
