Whirl Islands

Access to:
Accessible from any exit: Route 41

Utility:
Surf is required to travel to access any of the Whirl Islands, as well as navigate the Southwest cave and B2F. Wild Pokémon can be also encountered in these two areas, as well as Lugia's room, with Surf.
Whirlpool is required to access any of the Whirl Islands.
Waterfall is required on B2F to return from Lugia's room, but is not required to go to Lugia's room.
Strength can be used to move around more freely in the southern half of B1F.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:






Pokémon

Wild Horsea and Seadra have a 2% chance of holding a Dragon Scale.

Walking

Northwest, Northeast, Southwest, and Southeast caves, and the vertical tunnel area when exiting from the ladder southeast of the entrance to Lugia's room
