Goldenrod City

Access to:
North: Route 35
South: Route 34
Accessible in the city: Goldenrod Tunnel
Accessible in the city: Goldenrod Radio Tower
Accessible from the city: Saffron City (via Magnet Train)

Places of interest:
Pokémon Communication Center: In the original Japanese Crystal, the GS Ball could be obtained here, the Egg Ticket could be exchanged for the Odd Egg, and online trading was possible.
Bike Shop
Goldenrod Game Corner: In addition to Pokémon, the prize center sells TM25 Thunder, TM14 Blizzard, and TM38 Fire Blast.
Name Rater
Friendship Checker
Goldenrod Gym: The third gym. It specializes in Normal-types.
Goldenrod Department Store
2F: Upper cashier sells Potions, Super Potions, Antidotes, Parlyz Heals, Awakenings, Burn Heals, and Ice Heals. Lower cashier sells Poké Balls, Great Balls, Escape Ropes, Repels, Revives, Full Heals, Poké Dolls, and Flower Mail.
3F: Sells X Speeds, X Specials, X Defends, X Attacks, Dire Hits, Guard Specs, and X Accuracies.
4F: Sells Proteins, Irons, Carboses, Calciums, and HP Ups.
5F: Sells TM41 Thunderpunch, TM48 Fire Punch, TM33 Ice Punch, TM02 Headbutt (after first obtaining it in Ilex Forest), and TM08 Rock Smash (after first obtaining it on Route 36).
6F: The Vending Machines sell Fresh Waters, Soda Pops, and Lemonades.

Odd Egg (Japanese Crystal only)

The Odd Egg will hatch into into one of the following Pokémon. It will always be female (except for Tyrogue), and know Dizzy Punch. If it is not shiny, its IVs will be all 0's, and it will have a Hidden Power type of Fighting with 31 power. If it is shiny, its IVs will be 2/10/10/10, and it will have a Hidden Power type of Grass with 49 power.
In the Japanese games, the hatched Pokémon has a 50% chance of being shiny.
