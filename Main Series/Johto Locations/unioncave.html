Union Cave

Access to:
Northern exit: Route 32
Southern exit: Route 33
Exits on B1F: Ruins of Alph

Utility:
Surf is required to navigate most of B1F and B2F and access the Ruins of Alph. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered on all three floors with Surf.
Strength is required to access the southern Ruins of Alph exit on B1F.
