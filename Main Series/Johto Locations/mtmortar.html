Mt. Mortar

Access to:
Exits on 1F (front): Route 42

Utility:
Surf is required to access several areas, items, and a few Trainers, including Blackbelt Kiyo. Wild Pokémon can also be found with Surf.
Waterfall is required to access several areas, items, and a few Trainers, including Blackbelt Kiyo.
Strength is required to access several items, and can be used in place of Surf and Waterfall to access one trainer in Crystal.
