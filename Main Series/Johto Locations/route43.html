Route 43

Access to:
North: Lake of Rage
South: Mahogany Town

Utility:
Surf is required to access a Bitter Berry. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered with Surf.
Cut is required to access a Bitter Berry.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Going through the gate house before finishing Team Rocket HQ will cost you 1000.
