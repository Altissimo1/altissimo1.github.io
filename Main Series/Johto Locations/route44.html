Route 44

Access to:
West: Mahogany Town
Accessible on the route: Ice Path

Utility:
Surf is required to access the grassy patch, a Max Revive and an Elixer. Wild Pokémon can also be found here with Surf.
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:






Pokémon

Wild Poliwhirl have a 2% chance of holding a King's Rock.
Wild Tangela and Heracross can flee.
