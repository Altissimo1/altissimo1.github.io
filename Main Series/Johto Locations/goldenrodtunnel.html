Goldenrod Tunnel

Known as Goldenrod Underground in Gold, Silver, and Crystal.

Access to:
Either exit on 1F: Goldenrod City

Places of interest:
Barber shop: On all days except Mondays, one of two brothers will give your Pokémon a haircut, increasing its friendship.
Bargain shop: On Monday mornings, a salesman will sell discounted Nuggets, Pearls, Big Pearls, Stardusts, and Star Pieces. Each item can only be purchased once a week.
Herb shop: On Saturdays and Sundays, a woman will sell Energypowder, Energy Roots, Heal Powder, and Revival Herbs.
Goldenrod Department Store's basement can be accessed here.
