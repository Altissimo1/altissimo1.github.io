Dark Cave

Access to:
Route 31/46 side, western exit: Route 31
Route 31/46 side, eastern exit: Route 46
Route 31/46 side, northern exit: Route 45 side
Route 45 side, eastern exit: Route 45
Route 45 side, western exit: Route 31/46 side

Utility:
Surf is required to access the Route 45 side from the Route 31/46 side. However, ledges prevent access to the whole area. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Surf is required to access most of the Route 45 side, including TM13 Snore, a Revive, and the man who gives you Blackglasses. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered here with Surf.
Rock Smash is required to access an Elixer, a Dire Hit (Crystal only), a Hyper Potion, and a Full Heal on the Route 31/46 side. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered with Rock Smash.
Flash is recommended.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:






Pokémon

Hiker Anthony on Route 33 will alert you to Dunsparce swarms on the Route 31/46 side if you have his Pokégear number.
Wild Geodude and Graveler have a 2% chance of holding an Everstone.
Wild Shuckle have a 25% chance of holding a Berry.
Wild Teddiursa may flee.
