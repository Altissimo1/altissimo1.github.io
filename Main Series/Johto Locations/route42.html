Route 42

Access to:
West: Ecruteak City
East: MahogAnytime Town
Accessible on the route: Mt. Mortar

Utility:
Surf is required to access a Max Potion, Super Potion, and Apricorn trees, as well as to pass from west to east without going through Mt. Mortar. Wild Pokémon can also be found here with Surf.
Cut is required to access Apricorn trees.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:

Wild Fearow have a 2% chance of holding a Sharp Beak.
Wild Heracross can flee.
