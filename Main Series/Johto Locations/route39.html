Access to:
South: Olivine City
East: Route 38

Places of interest:
Moomoo Farm: You can buy Moomoo Milk here after healing Moomoo.

Utility:
Headbutt can be used to encounter wild Pokémon.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:






Pokémon

Wild Farfetch'd have a 2% chance of holding a Stick.
Wild Miltank have a 25% chance of holding a Moomoo Milk.
Wild Butterfree have a 2% chance of holding a Silverpowder.
Wild Beedrill have a 2% chance of holding a Poison Barb.
Wild Magnemite can flee.
