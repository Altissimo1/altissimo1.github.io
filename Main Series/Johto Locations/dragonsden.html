Dragon's Den

Access to:
Exit on 1F: Blackthorn City

Utility:
Surf is required to reach the Dragon Shrine, a Trainer, and several items. Wild Pokémon can also be found here with Surf.
Whirlpool is required to reach the back of the Dragon Shrine and several items.

Choose display option for Pokémon tables:






Pokémon

Wild Dratini and Dragonair have a 2% chance of holding a Dragon Scale.
Wild Dragonair can flee.
