Ruins of Alph

Access to:
North: Route 36
East: Route 32
Accessible in the area: Union Cave

Utility:
Surf is required to access the second, third, and fourth puzzles, as well as the Trainer and the grass patch. Wild Pokémon can also be encountered with Surf.
Flash is required to access the hidden room of the second puzzle in Crystal.
Strength is required to access the fourth puzzle.

Puzzles
There are four block puzzles that can be solved. Solving each puzzle unlocks specific Unown letters inside the Ruins of Alph. In Crystal, each puzzle has a hidden room behind it, which contain items and can be accessed by fulfilling certain conditions.
Puzzle 1 (Kabuto): Accessible from the cave near the Route 36 exit. Solving the puzzle unlocks Unown letters A-K. In Crystal, the hidden room is accessible by using an Escape Rope in front of the far wall.
Puzzle 2 (Aerodactyl): Accessible from the cave to the south, which requires Surf to reach. Solving the puzzle unlocks the Unown letters S-W. In Crystal, the hidden room is accessible by using Flash in front of the far wall.
Puzzle 3 (Ho-Oh): Accessible via Union Cave. It requires using Surf in Union Cave to access. Solving the puzzle unlocks the Unown letters X-Z. In Crystal, the hidden room is accessible if Ho-Oh is the first Pokémon in the player's party.
Puzzle 4 (Omanyte): Accessible via Union Cave. It requires using Surf and Strength in Union Cave to access. Solving the puzzle unlocks the Unown letters L-R. In Crystal, the hidden room is accessible if the player has a Water Stone in their inventory.
