RGBY Locations

The following table contains every Pokémon and all of its locations.

The following locations allow Fishing: Routes 6, 10, 11, 12, 13, 17, 18, 19, 20, 21, 22, 23, 24, and 25, Pallet Town, Viridian City, Cerulean City, Vermilion City, Vermilion Harbor, Celadon City, Fuchsia City, Cinnabar Island, Safari Zone (all areas), and Seafoam Islands (B3F and B4F)
Cerulean Gym also allows Fishing. Its encounters are the same as Cerulean City's. In Yellow, only the Old and Good Rods work.
