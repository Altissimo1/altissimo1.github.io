Support the Site

If you are interested in supporting my work or contributing to this site in some capacity, here are some things I'd be interested in.

Monetary

I am one person and I work on this as a labor of love. I don't intend to ever have ads on here, nor do I intend to gate any of my content behind a paywall. That said, if you like the work I'm doing and want to support me, please consider a donation.

Ko-Fi

If a sufficient number/amount of donations are received, I want to use the money for some or all of the following (in no particular order):

  • Pay someone to assist with developing for mobile devices
  • Pay someone to assist with improving the look and accessibility of the site
  • Pay people to assist with writing and preparing my planned content, improving the speed at which I can make updates
  • Pay people who know more than I do about some games to help with content there, allowing me to spend less time on it myself
  • Pay people to make translations of pages

Content

Do you have a web tool that you've developed? Do you have a Google Docs guide to something Pok√©mon-related, that doesn't have a permanent home? Do you have an article or interesting bit of prose you'd like to post somewhere? If so, get in touch or submit a pull request - this is the kind of thing I want to be able to host on the site on behalf of others.

As an existing example, the Mystery Dungeon Wonder Mail generator was a tool developed by the speedrunning community that was originally distributed as a local file you could run to open in your browser. I offered instead to post it on my website to give it some more exposure and a more permanent home, and the developers agreed. If you've been working on something and want to share it with the world, definitely contact me or submit a pull request and we can discuss putting it up here.

Translations

I'd like for the content here to reach more than just an English-speaking audience, but English is the only language I speak. If you'd like to see this site in your native language, contact me and we can discuss it - I'm willing to pay you for your time as a translator, if you're willing to be one.
