Content Goals

A (probably incomplete) list of things I want to get on this site eventually.

Main Series

  • Locations pages for all games
  • Pokédex and Pokémon location pages for all games, with information for what is achievable using a variety of compatible games
  • Utility, TM, evolution item, and hidden item pages for all games
  • Minimum and maximum levels
  • Strategies for Master Trainers in Let's Go

Supplementary Series

  • Enemy and rental Pokémon movesets for Battle Revolution
  • Information about things that can be obtained in Battle Revolution and sent to main series games
  • Some Pokédex and locations information relating to Colosseum and XD
  • Strategies for Path of Solitude in Legends Arceus

Side Series

  • Level-up, TM, and tutor movesets for all Mystery Dungeon games
  • Pokémon locations in Mystery Dungeon games
  • Anything to do with Pokémon Conquest
  • I want to have some content for Snap and New Snap, but not sure what yet
