Altissimo's Pokémon Site

I have data dumps and prose about things I find interesting regarding Pokémon games.

I also compile links to resources elsewhere that I find useful. Here are some generalized resources. More specific resources are linked on more specific pages.

Legends: Z-A Information

I plan to order a physical copy of Legends: Z-A from the Pokémon website as soon as it is available. This may mean it takes a few days to ship, and I'll also be out of town on the weekend of the 18th, so I probably won't get to play it immediately. I am ordering the Switch 2 version.

I welcome and encourage any submissions regarding documentation, data mined information, or guides that you might want to submit to this site or link to. I've set up a page here to hold both on-site and off-site resources in the initial information blitz of the game.

Your documentation doesn't need to match my formatting style or even have an HTML page to be added to this repo. While I do intend on making sure all of my own documentation follows a certain style format and is publicly accessible, that doesn't mean yours has to be held to the same standard. Don't be afraid to contribute anything you want to see reflected here in the long term, no matter what it is.

Even if you don't submit information directly to this repository, I plan to monitor the Smogon thread for data mines and documentation and link to Smogon's posts on the page above.

Additionally, feel free to contact me to discuss any questions, concerns, requests, etc. that you have!

Games

Main Series: "Core" series games. Currently contains pages for the following games:

  • Red, Green, Blue, and Yellow
  • Gold, Silver, and Crystal
  • Ruby, Sapphire, and Emerald
  • FireRed and LeafGreen
  • HeartGold and SoulSilver
  • Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire
  • Let's Go Pikachu and Let's Go Eevee

Supplementary Series: A term I made up to describe games that don't have the exact same gameplay loop as core games, but can trade and interact with them. Currently contains pages for the following games:

  • Legends: Arceus
  • Stadium series
  • Orre games (Colosseum and XD)

Side Series: Other Pokémon games with altogether different mechanics. Currently contains pages for the following games:

  • Pinball series
  • Mystery Dungeon series
  • Ranger series
  • Trozei / Link series
