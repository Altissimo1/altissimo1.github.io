An Extra Ball is probably the most valuable thing to achieve while playing a round of pinball. When the player has an active "Extra Ball", losing a ball below the bottom of the screen will not result in a lost ball life. Up to 10 Extra Balls can be active at a time. This means that the player could lose a ball 10 straight times without losing a ball life. If an 11th Extra Ball would have been received, the player is awarded 10 million points instead. When an Extra Ball is active, the "AGAIN" light above the flippers is lit.

There are many ways to receive an Extra Ball, and all of these methods apply ONLY to a single ball life, except for the Slot Reward. (When the player loses a ball life, these associated counters reset back to 0.)

Catch 'em Mode

The player receives an Extra Ball every 10 Pokemon caught.

Slot Reward

An Extra Ball is a rare reward from the Slot roulette. It only appears as a reward option in the 25th unique Slot reward set; see Slots.

Bonus Multiplier

Both Red and Blue fields use a Bonus Multiplier system, which is increased when the pinball hits certain field objects. An Extra Ball is received whenever the Bonus Multiplier value is increased to x25, x50, or x75. The Slot roulette's Bonus Multiplier reward can also increase the Bonus Multiplier, which will follow the same rules for granting an Extra Ball.

Pikachu Saver

Every 10 times the Pikachu Saver is used, an Extra Ball is received. This counter has a maximum of 100, so 10 Extra Balls can be received from this.

Blue Field

  • Every 25 times Slowpoke is entered, an Extra Ball is received. This counter has a maximum of 100, so 4 Extra Balls can be received from this.
  • Every 25 times Cloyster is entered, an Extra Ball is received. This counter has a maximum of 100, so 4 Extra Balls can be received from this.
  • Every 10 times Poliwag OR Psyduck are triggered for their 3rd hit, an Extra Ball is received. This counter has a maximum of 100, so 10 Extra Balls can be received from this.

Red Field

  • Every 25 times Bellsprout is entered, an Extra Ball is received. This counter has a maximum of 100, so 4 Extra Balls can be received from this.
  • Every 10 times the left-side OR right-side Diglett are triggered for their 3rd hit, an Extra Ball is received. This counter has a maximum of 100, so 10 Extra Balls can be received from this.
