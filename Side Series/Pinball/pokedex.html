Pokédex

When entering Catch 'em Mode by entering Bellsprout (Red field) or Cloyster (Blue field), the Pokémon generated will either be from the Common group or the Rare group. Which group is used depends on how many lights you have lit on the right loop: 2 lights will select from the Common table, and 3 lights will select from the Rare table. If you activate Catch 'em Mode via the slots, the game will pick a group at random; each group has a 50% chance of being chosen.
