On the 5th map move, you will move to Indigo Plateau, and the number of map moves made resets to 0, meaning the maps you can move to reset as though starting a new board.

When entering Catch 'em Mode by entering Bellsprout (Red field) or Cloyster (Blue field), the Pokémon generated will either be from the 2-Light group or the 3-Light group. Which group is used depends on how many lights you have lit on the right loop. If you activate Catch 'em Mode via the slots, the game will pick a group at random; each group has a 50% chance of being chosen.
